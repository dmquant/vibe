4. **Stress-test ranking: Guizhou has the highest risk of weakened implicit support, followed by Jiangsu, then Zhejiang.** Jiangsu and Zhejiang would face visible cash gaps under extreme refinancing contraction, but they still have fiscal scale, financial resources, and asset monetization options. Guizhou’s bond maturity scale is small, so the model shows a low bond cash gap; however, this reflects years of bond-market contraction and risk migration into non-standard liabilities and payables, not low underlying credit risk.
