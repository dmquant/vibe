## 1. Core Judgment

Do not go fully loaded before 2026-06-08 — crowding signals still permit one normal pullback in Denghai. Do not chase after the satellite headline — game-flow money will have front-run by 3–5 sessions and skew/payoff will compress.

## 6. Key Risks

1. **Window front-running:** If extreme-heat headlines hit early (say a 5/end 35°C heat spike), compress the plan to 5/25–6/15 and pull Phase ③ forward.
2. **Denghai 2027 booking falls short:** Denghai's elasticity rests on market expectations for the 2027 stress-tolerant seeds order book; channel feedback below expectations in late June discounts the trade.
3. **Stanley is not a pure seeds / pure event play:** Its beta is closer to compound-fertilizer peak season plus interim-report expectations. Event adds incrementally but doesn't dominate; expected return should sit below Denghai.
4. **COFCO Engineering liquidity is thin:** CNY 55m daily turnover means positions above CNY 15m carry meaningful impact cost on both entry and exit. That is the source of the optionality — and the biggest exit friction.
