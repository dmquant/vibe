# A-Share Strategy: Wind All-A Surpasses 2015 Peak — Institutional "Quality" vs. Leveraged "Heat"
**Date:** 2026-05-14
**Analyst:** ashare-strategist (A-Share Strategist)

## Executive Summary
On May 14, 2026, the Wind All-A Index (万得全A) officially closed above the historic peak of June 12, 2015 (7,257.79). While the price level mirrors the 2015 "Leveraged Bull," the underlying liquidity structure and market depth have fundamentally transformed. The current rally, characterized by >3 trillion RMB daily turnover and 9 consecutive days of Northbound inflows, is driven by institutional "Quality Liquidity" (ETFs) rather than the retail-driven "Wild Leverage" of 2015.

## 1. Liquidity Structure: ETF "Quality" vs. Margin "Quantity"
The core difference between the current peak and the 2015 peak lies in the **Source of Incremental Capital**.

| Metric | June 12, 2015 (Peak) | May 14, 2026 (Today) | Comparison/Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wind All-A Index** | 7,257.79 | **> 7,260** | Price parity, different depth. |
| **Daily Turnover** | ~1.97 Trillion RMB | **> 3.0 Trillion RMB** | Liquidity density is 50% higher. |
| **Margin Balance** | 2.23 Trillion RMB | **Controlled (< 2.0T)** | Leverage is no longer the main engine. |
| **Margin Requirement** | ~50% (often bypassed) | **100% (Strict)** | High entry barrier for leverage. |
| **Key Inflow Driver** | Retail Margin / Umbrella Trusts | **Broad-based ETFs / Foreign Capital** | Transition to institutional-led flows. |

### 2. Leverage Analysis: The "Cooling" 100% Requirement
In 2015, the "Margin Balance / Free-Float Market Cap" ratio exceeded 4%, signaling extreme fragility. Today, with the margin requirement raised to 100%, the leverage ratio is significantly lower. The 2026 margin inflow forecast of only 450 billion CNY indicates that the market's "blood" is no longer borrowed; it is organic capital from passive index funds and global allocation.

### 3. Market Depth and Turnover Efficiency
The A-share market has expanded from ~2,800 stocks in 2015 to over 5,500 today. 
*   **Cap Distribution:** 2015 was a "Small-Cap Bubble" (ChiNext PE ~100x). 2026 shows higher concentration in large-cap CSI 300/A500 names via ETFs.
*   **Turnover Heat:** While 3 trillion RMB is an absolute record, the "Turnover/Total Market Cap" ratio is lower than the 2015 frenzy, suggesting the market is not yet "overcooked."

## 4. Remaining Upside and Risk Factors
*   **Remaining Space:** Given the low leverage and high institutional participation, the valuation re-rating (估值修复) has higher sustainability. We estimate "Remaining Space" to be 10-15% for broad indices before reaching "Dangerous Overcrowding" (拥挤度警戒线).
*   **Risks:** Short-term technical pullbacks are likely if turnover persists above 3.5T without further policy catalysts.

## Conclusion
The breach of the 2015 high is a symbolic milestone for the "Institutionalization" of A-shares. Investors should favor ETF-heavy sectors (TMT, New Energy, Financials) while monitoring the margin requirement policy as a signal for the next tactical shift.
