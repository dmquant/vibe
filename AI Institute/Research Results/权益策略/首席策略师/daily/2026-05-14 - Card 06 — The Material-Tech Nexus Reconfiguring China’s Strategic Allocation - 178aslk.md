## 1. Core Judgment: From "Background Risk" to "Endogenous Variable"

In legacy valuation models, mineral concentration, rare earth licensing, and tariffs were treated as stochastic noise in the denominator (risk premium). As of **2026-05-14**, these factors have evolved into deterministic variables in the numerator.

### Valuation Paradigm Shift:
- **Legacy Model**: Valuation = Earnings Growth / (Risk-Free Rate + **Geopolitical Discount**)
- **New Model**: Valuation = (Fundamental Earnings + **Sovereign Premium × Supply Chain Control**) / (Risk-Free Rate + Residual Compliance Risk)

**Sovereign Premium**: Rewards companies that possess "veto power" or "irreplaceable certification" within processed minerals, specialty chemicals, or biotechnology supply chains.

---

## 2. Cross-Industry Synthesis: Synergy of Materials, Tech, and Pharma

### A. Decoupling and Recomposition of Materials & Tech (Responding to Card 05)
Card 05 correctly noted that strategic demand does not equal pricing power (e.g., lithium prices dropping >80% since 2023). This card further posits: **Valuation premiums are no longer granted for "raw material extraction" but for "processing and certification franchises."**
- **Semiconductor Materials**: Multiples for photoresists, electronic gases, and large wafers should exceed those of design firms. Design firms are vulnerable to US EDA/IP restrictions, while material breakthroughs serve as the gatekeeper for "terminal value."
- **0.1% Threshold Effect**: As foreign limits on Chinese-origin materials (such as the 0.1% threshold in FEOC rules) become normalized, entities with "de-risked" certification paths or "fully closed-loop" domestic cycles will gain a double premium.

### B. "Clean Stack" Premium in Biotechnology (Responding to Cards 03-04)
Cards 03-04 quantified a 20-40% discount on BD NPV. This card argues that this discount is essentially a loss of **supply chain sovereignty**.
- **Allocation Strategy**: Overweight "BIOSECURE-clean" biotechs with autonomous drug substance manufacturing.
- **Valuation Logic**: These assets should be valued not just as "innovative drugs" but as "strategic security assets." Their WACC should be revised downward by 200-300bps to reflect resilience in extreme decoupling scenarios.

## 4. Modeling Guidelines: Three Key Correction Factors

Research teams should mandate the inclusion of the following "Sovereign Correction Coefficients" in their models:

1.  **Material Security Score**: Evaluates upstream raw material dependency on "non-friendly countries." For every 10% reduction in dependency, the target P/E should be raised by 1-2x.
2.  **Licensing Moat Multiple**: Whether the company holds export licenses for rare earths, graphite, or sensitive technologies. Such firms should be valued as "franchises" rather than generic manufacturers.
3.  **Tariff Resilience**: Simulating margin retention under a doubling of Section 232/301 tariffs. Retention rates >80% warrant a valuation premium.
