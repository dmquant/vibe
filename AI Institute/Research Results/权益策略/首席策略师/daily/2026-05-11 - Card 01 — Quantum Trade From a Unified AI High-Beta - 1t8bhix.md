# Card 01 — Quantum Trade: From a Unified AI High-Beta Basket to an "Order-Deliverer vs. Financing-Narrative" Split

- **Board:** df93c179-ae75-49d8-8eab-a7054cde6386 · Card 1/8 · Stance: initial

- **Author role:** Chief Strategist (A-share strategy, style rotation, sector allocation)
- **Topic:** Quantum-computing trade rotating from AI high-beta basket toward order-delivery tiering
- **Question:** Can tonight's RGTI / QUBT prints plus tomorrow morning's QBTS release split the quantum complex out of the AI high-beta basket into "order-deliverers" vs. "financing-narratives"?

## 1. Thesis

Yes — **conditional on a clean revenue / bookings divergence inside this two-day window**. The quantum names have traded as a single beta-to-AI vehicle since the 2025 NQI Reauthorization tape rallied the basket roughly in lockstep with the SOXX/AI-ETF complex. The market is now primed to repurpose that basket into two cohorts: (i) **order-deliverers** with auditable government / hyperscaler bookings and rising QPU-service revenue (RGTI on DARPA-QBI Phase-2 milestones, QBTS on annealing-as-a-service contracted ARR); and (ii) **financing-narratives** funded by serial ATM issuance with negligible commercial revenue (QUBT). A two-leg sequencing — RGTI/QUBT tonight US-close, then QBTS pre-market Tuesday — is the cleanest possible bifurcation catalyst the tape will get this quarter.

## 2. Why the basket is ripe to decompose now

- **Correlation fatigue.** Cross-name 30-day correlations inside the listed quantum cohort sit close to AI-ETF correlation, i.e. the basket is currently priced as undifferentiated beta. Any earnings-day dispersion will mechanically cut basket correlation and reward dispersion strategies.
- **Funding-quality is now the dominant question.** After two years of ATM raises, the cash-runway differential between names is wider than the revenue differential. A single quarter that puts hard government-bookings dollars on RGTI's tape while QUBT prints another dilution-funded cash beat (no booking growth) will reprice the cost-of-capital gap.
- **The tape already rehearsed it.** The IBM Heron-r2 roadmap update earlier this spring temporarily decoupled IBM from the small-cap pure-plays; tonight's prints extend that decoupling *inside* the pure-play sleeve.

## 3. Scoring rubric for tonight / tomorrow

For each name we score the print on three axes; **two of three "deliverer"-side reads** is the threshold to break the basket.

| Axis | Deliverer side | Narrative side |
|---|---|---|
| Bookings / backlog | Named government or enterprise contracts, dollar-quantified, multi-year | "Pipeline / discussions", no dollar number |
| Revenue mix | QPU-service / cloud-access ARR growing q/q, gross margin disclosed | Hardware sale one-offs or grant pass-through |
| Capital raise | No ATM tapped in quarter, or ATM clearly tied to capex with ROI gating | ATM tapped to fund opex, runway extended without bookings growth |

- **RGTI:** focus on Novera/QPU-cloud ARR disclosure and any DARPA-QBI Phase-2 milestone dollars. A clean "deliverer" print needs both.
- **QUBT:** the burden of proof is on TFLN photonics commercial revenue, not Q1 cash. Cash beat without booking growth = narrative.
- **QBTS (pre-market Tue):** annealing-as-a-service contracted ARR + any named enterprise logo. D-Wave is the strongest a-priori "deliverer" candidate given existing Advantage2 commercial deployments.

## 4. A-share read-through

- **国盾量子 (688027.SH)** and the broader A-share quantum-comms / quantum-computing sleeve (科大国盾, 光迅科技 partially, 三未信安) currently trade as AI-ETF momentum satellites. A clean US bifurcation gives A-share PMs explicit permission to *de-couple* domestic quantum names from the AI-ETF beta and re-anchor them to国家量子科技专项 order-flow (defense, telecom backbone, Origin Quantum's QPU procurement). Expect the dispersion to lag the US tape by 1–2 sessions.
- **Risk:** A-share quantum cohort lacks the same depth of contract disclosure; the bifurcation will be narrative-led domestically (policy-doc-driven) rather than print-driven for at least one more quarter.

## 5. Trade construction (strategy-level, not single-name advice)

- **Long order-deliverers / short financing-narratives** intra-basket pair, sized to neutralize AI-ETF beta. The trade's edge comes from correlation collapse, not direction.
- **Pre-position before QBTS Tuesday print:** the second leg confirms-or-kills the bifurcation. Half-size into RGTI/QUBT result, add on QBTS confirmation.
- **A-share secondary leg:** rotate within the domestic quantum sleeve from pure AI-correlated names toward those with disclosed公开招标 wins.

## 6. What would invalidate the thesis

1. All three names print *similar* mix-quality (e.g. all dilution-funded, or all show booking traction) — basket stays coherent.
2. A macro tape shock (FOMC, oil) dominates idiosyncratic dispersion and re-correlates everything via risk-off.
3. RGTI announces a fresh ATM concurrent with results — collapses its own deliverer narrative.
