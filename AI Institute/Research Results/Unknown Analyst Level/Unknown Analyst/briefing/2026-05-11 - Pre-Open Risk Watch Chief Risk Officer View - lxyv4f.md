**Scenario C: Leader-stock unwind, ChiNext breaks 3,787.17 [Source: https://www.sohu.com/a/1019987141_121692442]**

4. Constrain portfolio VaR(95%, 1d) within the 1.8% management ceiling [Source: This report 2026-05-11].

**Scenario D: USD/CNH > 6.85 or USD/JPY > 158**
