# Sentiment and Flow Snapshot

Date anchor: 2026-05-11 [source: shell `date +%Y-%m-%d`, queried 2026-05-11]. Because the latest complete post-close A-share and Hong Kong datasets are mainly for 2026-05-08 [source: Eastmoney, Securities Times, Sina Finance, Caizhong, queried 2026-05-11], this snapshot uses 2026-05-08 [source: Eastmoney, Securities Times, Sina Finance, Caizhong, queried 2026-05-11] as the latest full trading day.

## Key Conclusions

- Sentiment is hot, but not indiscriminately euphoric: Shanghai and Shenzhen turnover was RMB 30,486bn, down RMB 954bn from RMB 31,440bn in the prior session, and stayed above RMB 3tn for three consecutive sessions [source: Sina Finance/Tanzhen Finance, queried 2026-05-11, https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-05-08/doc-inhxerwi4225025.shtml]. The latest China Securities sentiment model was in a 77-79 range, above the 75 high-sentiment zone but still below the 90 euphoria area [source: Sina Finance/China Securities research, queried 2026-05-11, https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-05-05/doc-inhwvyti6563765.shtml].
- Leverage is the clearest pro-cyclical signal: A-share margin balance reached RMB 27,864.36bn, up RMB 297.24bn on the day and equal to 2.58% of free-float market cap; margin trading turnover was RMB 3,353.96bn, or 10.58% of A-share turnover [source: Sina Finance/Shanghai Securities News, queried 2026-05-11, https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-05-08/doc-inhxcust4380501.shtml].
- ETF flows show profit-taking and rotation: stock-index ETFs saw a one-day outflow of RMB 221.75bn and a five-session cumulative outflow of RMB 1,209.14bn [source: Stockstar ETF, queried 2026-05-11, https://stock.stockstar.com/RB2026050900005148.shtml], while CSI 300 ETF products still attracted RMB 21.95bn [source: Caizhong, queried 2026-05-11, https://m.caizhongshe.cn/article-3394137721368299328.html].
- Southbound flows bought the Hong Kong dip aggressively: southbound net buying was HKD 13.168bn, with HKD 68.314bn bought and HKD 55.146bn sold, while the Hang Seng Index fell 0.87% to 26,393.71 [source: Eastmoney/Securities Times DataBao, queried 2026-05-11, https://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202605083731822774.html].
- Northbound “net buying” is no longer directly observable: since 2024-08-19, the Stock Connect disclosure regime mainly publishes aggregate turnover after the close and no longer publishes daily buy and sell amounts for northbound trading [source: Eastmoney Stock Connect page, queried 2026-05-11, https://data.eastmoney.com/hsgtV2/hsgtDetail/scgk.html]. The latest northbound turnover was RMB 357.042bn, converted from NF_DEAL_AMT=357041.96 in RMB 10k units [source: Eastmoney RPT_MUTUAL_DEALAMT API, queried 2026-05-11].
