## Priority Conclusions

- The previous session was a low-open structural rebound, not a broad indiscriminate rally: the Shanghai Composite closed at 4,242.57, up 0.67%; the Shenzhen Component closed at 16,089.75, up 1.67%; the ChiNext Index closed at 4,038.33, up 2.63%; and ChiNext reached an intraday high of 4,041.99 [Source: China News/JW View 2026-05-13].
- Turnover stayed high but marginally contracted: Shanghai and Shenzhen turnover was about RMB 3,240.014bn, down about RMB 2.642bn from the previous session [Source: China News/JW View 2026-05-13]. That argues against chasing a crowded open; wait for evidence of absorption after the first round of profit-taking.
- The live trade remains “AI compute hardware + power/grid + high-dividend financials as ballast.” Electronics saw RMB 11.892bn of main-board-style active net inflow, communications RMB 3.935bn, computers RMB 2.215bn, and utilities RMB 1.926bn [Source: Securities Times DataBao 2026-05-13].
- Leveraged money is increasingly crowded in growth chains: as of 2026-05-12, total margin financing and securities lending balance across Shanghai, Shenzhen, and Beijing was RMB 2,850.744bn, rising for 5 consecutive trading days and up RMB 135.536bn over that stretch; electronics margin financing balance increased by RMB 47.008bn during the period [Source: Securities Times DataBao 2026-05-13].
- Today’s core variables are the Trump-Xi Beijing talks and tech-chain sentiment from Jensen Huang joining the trip. Reuters reported that the talks are set to run from Thursday to Friday, and Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang is also traveling with Trump [Source: Reuters/Investing.com 2026-05-13].

## Positioning and Pacing

- Keep overall exposure moderately above neutral, but do not force full risk. If the Shanghai Composite holds above 4,260 and electronics, communications, and utilities continue to receive active net inflows, expand offensive exposure toward AI hardware and power [Source: this report’s positioning rule 2026-05-14; fund-flow direction from Securities Times DataBao 2026-05-13].
- If AI hardware gaps up in unison, wait for the first real pullback. Yesterday, 181 stocks had more than RMB 100mn of main-fund net inflow, so crowding is already meaningful [Source: Securities Times DataBao 2026-05-13].
- In the power chain, trade only four logics: compute-power demand, green-power direct supply, grid equipment, and earnings improvement. Pure limit-up names are intraday trading vehicles; after Datang Power’s 6-day limit-up streak, sentiment value is larger than risk-reward [Source: Securities Times DataBao 2026-05-13].
- After total margin balance rose for 5 consecutive trading days, if electronics and communications show heavy-volume stalling, cut high-beta exposure first and keep only holdings with earnings or order verification [Source: Securities Times DataBao 2026-05-13].
- On lockup supply pressure, avoid low-volume spikes in recently high-unlock names. The 39.01% unlock ratio for Howsun, 16.93% for Tiangong Technology, and 14.79% for Hongjing Technology argue for continued avoidance of high-unlock-ratio names with high turnover [Source: NBD/Wind 2026-05-13].
