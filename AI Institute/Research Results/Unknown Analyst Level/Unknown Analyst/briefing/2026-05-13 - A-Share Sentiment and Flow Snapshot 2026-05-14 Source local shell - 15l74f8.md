# A-Share Sentiment and Flow Snapshot | 2026-05-14 [Source: local shell `date +%Y-%m-%d` 2026-05-14]

The anchor date is 2026-05-14 [Source: local shell `date +%Y-%m-%d` 2026-05-14], and the previous trading day is 2026-05-13 [Source: Peakstone Labs post-close dashboard 2026-05-13]. Bottom line: sentiment has moved from recovery into a hot zone, but flows are not broad-based; they are concentrated in AI hardware, power/grid, memory/CPO, and a narrow set of very high-turnover leaders.

## Priority Conclusions

- The sentiment temperature gauge reads 86/100 [Source: this report's model using Peakstone Labs 2026-05-13 limit-up 20-day percentile of 98%, limit-down 20-day percentile of 12%, advancer ratio of 60.1%, and turnover of RMB 32642.1 hundred-million yuan, calculated 2026-05-14], putting the market in a greed-leaning zone; holding leadership makes sense, but chasing a uniform gap-up does not.
- For northbound flows, daily net buying is no longer a stable verifiable disclosure item under the adjusted Stock Connect reporting regime; the verifiable metric is 2026-05-13 northbound turnover of RMB 3932.61 hundred-million yuan, equal to 12.14% of total Shanghai-Shenzhen turnover [Source: East Money Choice 2026-05-13].
- Southbound flows showed clear profit-taking: on 2026-05-13, southbound turnover was HKD 1231.62 hundred-million and net selling was HKD 67.72 hundred-million [Source: Securities Times DataBao 2026-05-13], diverging from the hotter A-share growth-stock tape.
- Leverage is the core incremental fuel in this sentiment upswing: as of 2026-05-12, margin-financing and securities-lending balances across Shanghai, Shenzhen, and Beijing stood at RMB 28507.44 hundred-million yuan, rising for 5 consecutive trading days and adding RMB 1355.36 hundred-million yuan over that run [Source: Securities Times DataBao 2026-05-13].
- Contrarian signals are already visible: the MA5 concentration of turnover in the top 5% of stocks rose to 45.63% [Source: Peakstone Labs 2026-05-13], while equity ETFs saw RMB 271.03 hundred-million yuan of net outflows on 2026-05-12 [Source: China Fund News/Securities Times 2026-05-13], suggesting allocation money is taking profits into strength.
