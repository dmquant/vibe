### II. Multi-Dimensional Fund Flow Analysis

#### 1. Margin Trading (Leverage) — Historic Fever
The margin balance has surpassed the 2015 peak, reflecting an exceptionally high risk appetite among retail and institutional leveraged players.
*   **Latest Balance:** As of May 12 close, total margin & securities lending balance stood at ~**2.85 trillion yuan** (Margin balance at 2.83T).
*   **Today's Dynamics:** Margin buying continues to account for **over 10%** of total market turnover.
*   **Focus Areas:** Capital is heavily concentrated in AI hardware, CPO (Zhongji Innolight), and semiconductors (Cambricon, Montage Technology).

#### 2. Main Force (Institutional) — Structural Rotation
Institutional capital is shifting aggressively between sectors.
*   **Top Inflows:** Computing power, smart grids, and AI infrastructure.
*   **Top Outflows:** Pork/Agri sector, traditional financials (ICBC, China Pacific Insurance).
*   **Rationale:** Investors are rotating out of defensive "dividend assets" to fully embrace high-growth technology themes.

#### 3. Northbound Capital (Foreign) — High-Level Rebalancing
Foreign investors remain structurally active, buoyed by RMB appreciation expectations (Goldman Sachs targets 6.5 vs USD).
*   **Trend:** A mix of profit-taking and strategic accumulation.
*   **Key Picks:** SMIC (00981.HK) and CNOOC (00883.HK) continue to see net purchases.

#### 4. ETF Fund Flows — Passive Entry
*   **Total AUM:** Total ETF assets in the market have reached **6.2 trillion yuan**.
*   **Characteristics:** Active ETFs (introduced in 2025) are gaining traction, with tech and advanced manufacturing ETFs recording significant net inflows today.

### III. Key Conclusions & Outlook

1.  **Sentiment Indicator:** Market is in the "Greed" zone. The ChiNext Index breaking above 4,000 points signifies a high level of consensus on the new technological cycle.
2.  **Drivers:** Marginal improvements in geopolitical expectations (Trump’s China visit) coupled with ample domestic liquidity have created a powerful tailwind.
3.  **Risk Warning:** With the margin balance at a historic 2.8 trillion yuan extreme, investors must be wary of "deleveraging cascades" if the market starts to consolidate.
