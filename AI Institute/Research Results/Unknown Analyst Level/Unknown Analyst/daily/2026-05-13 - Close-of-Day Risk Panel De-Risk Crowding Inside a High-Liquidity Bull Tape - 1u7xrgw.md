## Layered Risk Assessment

### Macro Risk

- **Diplomatic event risk is the first tail variable.** The Trump China visit runs from 2026-05-13 to 2026-05-15 [source: https://english.www.gov.cn/news/202605/11/content_WS6a012e12c6d00ca5f9a0addc.html]. The market has partly priced expectations around trade, AI chips and supply-chain improvement; if announcements stay at the principle level, event VaR in high-flying technology shares rises.
- **Monetary conditions are not a systemic stress point, but they are no longer a one-way tailwind.** The PBOC conducted 5 hundred-million yuan of reverse repos today at 1.4%, with a net withdrawal of 255 hundred-million yuan [source: aastocks 2026-05-13]. This is not tight in isolation, but against 32,642 hundred-million yuan of turnover [source: https://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0513/874496.shtml], marginal liquidity can easily be interpreted as a high-level cooling signal.

### Liquidity And Leverage Risk

- **Market liquidity remains green, but trading liquidity is yellow.** Turnover has stayed above RMB 3 trillion for the 6th consecutive trading day [source: https://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0513/874496.shtml], so the exit channel still exists; however, 28,338.58 hundred-million yuan of margin balance [source: https://finance.caixin.com/2026-05-12/102443295.html] means synchronized de-risking can appear quickly in a drawdown.
- **Stress threshold:** if tomorrow’s turnover falls below RMB 3 trillion [source: https://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0513/874496.shtml] and ChiNext falls back below 4,000 [source: 04_technical_map.md 2026-05-13], cut the net equity exposure cap from 70% to 60% [source: CRO internal execution rules 2026-05-13].

### Sector And Theme Risk

- **AI compute / CPO / server chain: yellow-to-red.** Foxconn Industrial Internet (601138.SH) closed at 70.84 yuan, with market value of 1.41 trillion yuan and full-day turnover above 278 hundred-million yuan [source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-05-13/doc-inhxtvfi7521007.shtml]. Leadership strength is not the issue; the issue is that second-tier names may lack fundamental support when liquidity fades.
