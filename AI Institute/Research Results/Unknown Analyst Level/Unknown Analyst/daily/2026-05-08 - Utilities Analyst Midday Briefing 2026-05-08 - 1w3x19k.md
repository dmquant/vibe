## 1. Morning Confirmed Facts & Data

### 1.1 Power Market: Policy Dividends and Marketization Acceleration
*   **"Multi-user Green Power Direct Connection" Policy:** The NEA has explicitly allowed renewable energy to directly supply multiple users via dedicated lines. Currently, 24 provinces have issued supporting policies, with the first batch of 99 projects approved, involving an installed capacity of **34.05 GW**.
*   **Spot Trading Mechanism Reform:** Provinces like Shandong, Guangdong, and Zhejiang are substantially moving forward with canceling fixed time-of-use pricing. Electricity prices for users directly participating in trading will be determined by real-time clearing prices in the spot market.
*   **Computing-Power Synergies:** On May 7, 2026, China's first "Green Electricity for Computing" project was officially launched, aiming to achieve 100% green power supply for AI data center clusters.
*   **Thermal Power Surge:** To ensure power supply stability, the commissioning scale of thermal power in Q1 2026 surged by more than **400%** year-on-year.

### 1.2 Carbon Market: Expansion and Reset
*   **Sectoral Expansion Initiated:** The national ETS has officially launched the entry of steel, cement, and aluminum smelting industries, increasing the covered emissions to approximately **8 billion tons**.
*   **"CEA-2025" Transition:** The market has initiated an allowance reset, with key emitting units converting old vintages from 2019-2024 into the CEA-2025 vintage.
*   **CCER Methodologies Implemented:** 12 new methodologies (including water electrolysis for hydrogen and mangrove restoration) have officially taken effect, and the CCER market has entered the countdown for issuance.

### 1.3 Physical Supply Chain Bottlenecks
*   **Transformer Shortage:** Lead times for Large Power Transformers (LPT) have extended to **140-160 weeks**.
*   **GOES Constraints:** Grain-Oriented Electrical Steel (GOES) supply has entered a "quota era," particularly for high-end domain-refined grades, with capacity highly concentrated in a few mills like Baowu and Nippon Steel.
