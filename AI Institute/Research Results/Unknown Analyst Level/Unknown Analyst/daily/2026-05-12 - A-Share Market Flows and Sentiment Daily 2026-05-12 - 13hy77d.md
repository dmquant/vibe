## 3. Crowding and Contrarian Signals
- **Crowding Alert:** The turnover share of Electronics and Computer sectors remains elevated. Main-force outflows of over **25.5 billion RMB** [Source: CLS 2026-05-12] indicate short-term overcrowding and potential "stampede" risks.
- **Contrarian Signal:** Securities ETF (512880) received counter-trend inflows, with a cumulative net inflow of over **2 billion RMB** [Source: NBD 2026-05-12] in the last 10 days, as capital bets on a brokerage rally following a breakout above 4,200 points.
- **Risk Control Warning:** Brokerages like Orient Securities announced new risk management standards effective May 18, including a **115% immediate liquidation line** [Source: Sina Finance 2026-05-12], a major signal for cooling sentiment.

## 4. Investor Behavior Observations
1. **Retail Leveraging:** The excessive growth in margin balance (daily increase of 30B+) indicates active retail participation, which often precedes increased volatility.
2. **Institutional Profit-taking:** Large-scale redemptions in broad-base ETFs (e.g., 6.54B from 510300) suggest institutional investors are taking defensive measures around the 4,200 level [Source: Securities Times 2026-05-12].
3. **Capital Migration:** Funds are shifting from TMT "concept" plays towards "AI Infrastructure + Power Grid" hardware implementation scenarios.

## 5. Risk Appetite Outlook for Tomorrow
- **Neutral to Cautious:** With over 100 billion RMB in main-force outflows, the market needs time to digest profit-taking pressure.
- **Support Levels:** If the 4,200-point psychological level is not reclaimed quickly, it may trigger more proactive deleveraging from margin traders.
- **Key Monitoring Points:** Whether Northbound flows remain positive and if the growth rate of the margin balance begins to slow down.
