# A-Share Theme Rotation Daily: Holiday Session, Review Based on the Last Trading Day

## Priority Conclusions

- 2026-05-04 fell within the Labor Day market holiday; the Shanghai Stock Exchange notice says the market is closed from 2026-05-01 through 2026-05-05 and resumes normal trading from 2026-05-06 [Source: Shanghai Stock Exchange, queried 2026-05-04]. This report’s “today’s theme rotation” therefore uses the latest trading-day tape and flow data from 2026-04-30 [Source: Tonghuashun Finance Research Center, queried 2026-05-04].
- The pre-holiday theme leadership was “AI compute chips set the direction, semiconductors broadened and absorbed capital, commercial aerospace and robotics provided beta, and energy metals caught up.” STAR 50 closed at 1571.07, up 5.19% [Source: Tonghuashun Finance Research Center, queried 2026-05-04], while the semiconductor industry rose 3.3668% on turnover of 3407.14 hundred-million yuan [Source: Tonghuashun Finance Research Center, queried 2026-05-04], showing that hard technology remained the preferred offensive exposure into reopening.
- Strong themes did not mean a broad rally. Shanghai and Shenzhen turnover was 27409 hundred-million yuan, up 1508 hundred-million yuan from the previous trading day [Source: The Paper, queried 2026-05-04], but only 54 industry indexes rose while 36 fell [Source: Tonghuashun Finance Research Center, queried 2026-05-04]; the top-gaining industries were energy metals at 4.678%, semiconductors at 3.3668%, and automation equipment at 2.109% [Source: Tonghuashun Finance Research Center, queried 2026-05-04], while the worst-performing industries were games at -1.6735%, steel at -1.4109%, and power at -1.4077% [Source: Tonghuashun Finance Research Center, queried 2026-05-04].
- Trading implication: the reopening question is not “whether to stay constructive on technology,” but “whether heavy semiconductor turnover can be absorbed and broaden into commercial aerospace, robotics, energy metals, and lower-positioned equipment.” If semiconductor turnover falls below the pre-holiday 3407.14 hundred-million yuan benchmark [Source: Tonghuashun Finance Research Center, queried 2026-05-04] and no broad-index or lower-positioned themes take over, high-beta exposure should be reduced first.
