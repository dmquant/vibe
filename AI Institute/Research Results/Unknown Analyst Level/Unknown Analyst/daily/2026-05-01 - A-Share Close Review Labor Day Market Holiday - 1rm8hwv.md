# A-Share Close Review: Labor Day Market Holiday

## Priority Conclusions

- Today was not an A-share trading day. The Shanghai Stock Exchange notice says the Labor Day market holiday runs from May 1 (Friday) to May 5 (Tuesday), with normal trading resuming on May 6 (Wednesday)[Source: https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml]. This report therefore does not treat May 1[Source: https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml] as a closing session; it reviews April 30[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml], the final trading day before the holiday.
- The pre-holiday session was not a broad rally. It was a structural tape: stable headline indices, strong hard tech, and clear stock-level dispersion. STAR 50 rose 5.19% and SSE 50 rose 0.61%, while CSI 300 fell 0.06% and ChiNext fell 0.27%[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml].
- Turnover remained elevated, with no obvious cash-market retreat. Shanghai and Shenzhen turnover reached 27592 hundred million yuan, up 1503 hundred million yuan from the prior session, and more than 2800 stocks advanced across the market[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml]. On the Wind count, Shanghai, Shenzhen and Beijing had 2871 advancers, 2459 decliners and 173 unchanged stocks[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6182188.shtml].
- The main line narrowed from broad growth to hard tech with earnings validation. Semiconductors traded 3407.14 hundred million yuan and rose 3.37%, making the group both one of the most active and one of the strongest sectors of the day[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml].
- Strategy: do not chase prices during the holiday. Before the next cash session, the priority is to review first-quarter earnings quality, separate crowding across the AI hardware chain, and prepare buy-on-weakness plans rather than chase the holiday reopening.

- SSE 50 gained 0.61%[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml], showing that large-cap blue chips helped stabilize the headline tape; BSE 50 fell 0.12%[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml], so risk appetite did not fully spread to micro and small caps.

STAR 50 rose 25.05% for the month[Source: https://cn.dailyeconomic.com/2026/04/30/147770.html], confirming that growth beta led the month, even as the final session showed crowding and differentiation.

## Main Drivers

- Domestic compute was the leading driver. Cailian Press noted that the domestic compute chain surged on the back of the AI-chip chain; Cambricon hit a 20cm limit-up, made a new high after 8 months, and retook the top-priced-stock position[Source: https://cn.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/article-3340397].
- Earnings and orders strengthened the consensus. Cailian Press reported that VeriSilicon’s new orders increased by nearly 40 hundred million yuan over 9 days, with AI compute accounting for more than 90% of those new orders[Source: https://cn.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/article-3340397].
- Sector money became more selective. Semiconductors saw 69.31 hundred million yuan of net inflow, while communications equipment saw 68.85 hundred million yuan of net outflow[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkui1330852.shtml]. This was not a simple buy-everything-tech session; money was being reallocated inside AI hardware.
- The monthly backdrop reinforced momentum leadership. In April, among Shenwan Level-1 industries, electronics rose 26.79%, communications rose 22.23%, and food and beverage fell 1.12%[Source: https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892568.html], widening the performance gap between growth and consumption.

## Institutional Read: Market Character

I would define the current A-share tape as a structural uptrend with high turnover, a strong main line, and no broad-market bull confirmation yet. The evidence is straightforward: turnover stayed at 27592 hundred million yuan[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml], but index performance was split, with STAR 50 up 5.19%, ChiNext down 0.27%, and CSI 300 down 0.06%[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml]. At the sector level, 54 industries rose and 36 fell[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml], so breadth was acceptable but the money-making effect remained concentrated.

China Merchants Securities said A-share risk appetite recovered quickly over the past month, while sectors showed “extreme crowding” and “deep valuation divergence”; it suggested focusing May allocation on first-quarter earnings validation and continuing prosperity in electronics, power equipment, defense, nonferrous metals, basic chemicals and utilities[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-05-01/doc-inhwkpte5519261.shtml]. Everbright Securities argued that after earnings season, disclosure uncertainty should fall and A-shares may extend a range-bound uptrend, with structural focus on hard tech, export chains and resources where fundamentals are improving[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-05-01/doc-inhwkpte5519261.shtml].

## Tomorrow’s Strategy Cadence

Tomorrow remains inside the Labor Day market holiday. The closure runs from May 1 (Friday) to May 5 (Tuesday), and normal trading resumes on May 6 (Wednesday)[Source: https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml]. The cadence is:

- Positioning: keep core pre-holiday exposure; do not infer the A-share reopening direction mechanically from offshore holiday moves.
- Review: separate the three high-turnover chains instead of treating them as one trade. Semiconductors traded 3407.14 hundred million yuan, communications equipment traded 1548.01 hundred million yuan, and batteries traded 1502.26 hundred million yuan[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml]; after the holiday, watch whether volume can still absorb supply.
- Entry discipline: in the first reopening session, prefer waiting for divergence; do not extrapolate STAR 50’s one-day 5.19% move[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml] in a straight line.
- Watchlist: domestic AI chips, semiconductor equipment, optical communications, defense electronics and energy metals; avoid high-position themes driven only by events and lacking earnings support.
- Risk control: if post-holiday turnover falls materially below 27592 hundred million yuan[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml] and STAR 50 cannot maintain relative strength, shift offensive exposure from chasing to taking profits into strength.
