## Style And Sector Rotation

- The main line was “strong STAR-board growth, split traditional growth, value as support.” STAR 50 rose 5.19% [Source: Tonghuashun 2026-04-30 https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml], while ChiNext fell 0.27% [Source: Tonghuashun 2026-04-30 https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml]. The market was not simply buying growth; it was buying growth backed by earnings and order validation.
- By sector, energy metals, semiconductors, robotics, aerospace equipment, military electronics, decoration materials and new metal materials led; hotels and catering, fiberglass and gaming lagged [Source: Sina Finance / Greater Bay Area Finance Media 2026-04-30 https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkui1330852.shtml]. This maps to a structure of technology offense against weakness in consumer services and some cyclical pockets.
- By theme, the AI-compute chip chain, GPU, semiconductors, commercial aerospace, superhard materials, humanoid robots, memory, lithium ore and space photovoltaics were active; batteries, gas and precious metals corrected [Source: The Paper 2026-04-30 https://m.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_33083715].

## Main Drivers

- Earnings and order validation drove the semiconductor chain. CLS reported that Cambricon’s first-quarter net profit rose 185% year on year [Source: CLS 2026-04-30 https://www.cls.cn/detail/2361107], that AI-compute-related orders accounted for over 90% of VeriSilicon’s new orders [Source: CLS 2026-04-30 https://www.cls.cn/detail/2361107], and that GigaDevice’s first-quarter net profit rose 523% year on year [Source: CLS 2026-04-30 https://www.cls.cn/detail/2361107].
- Capital rotated from pre-holiday defensive areas into offensive areas. The Paper cited Central China Securities as saying that dividend-defensive sectors previously favored by risk-hedging capital weakened, while technology and growth became the main market drivers [Source: The Paper 2026-04-30 https://m.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_33083715].
- The holiday effect raises uncertainty around tomorrow’s repricing. Because the market was closed from 2026-05-01 through 2026-05-05 [Source: Shanghai Stock Exchange announcement 2026-04-23 https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml], external information will be remapped into A shares when trading resumes on 2026-05-06 [Source: Shanghai Stock Exchange announcement 2026-04-23 https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml].

## Institutional Read On Market Character

- This is not a broad index-rally market; it is a structural market with high turnover, a strong main line and fast rotation. Shanghai-Shenzhen-Beijing turnover of RMB 27595 hundred million [Source: Sina Finance / Greater Bay Area Finance Media 2026-04-30 https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkui1330852.shtml] can support theme diffusion, but the gap between 2871 advancers [Source: The Paper 2026-04-30 https://m.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_33083715] and 2459 decliners [Source: The Paper 2026-04-30 https://m.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_33083715] was not extreme, which means capital was still selecting for business momentum and delivery.
- Shenwan Hongyuan argued that funding conditions in May-June are unlikely to tighten in a trend-like way, but are more likely to move from “extremely loose” in April toward “neutral to loose” [Source: The Paper 2026-04-30 https://m.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_33083715]. That keeps valuation expansion possible, but makes turnover persistence and earnings delivery more important.
- China Securities said AI hardware remains the direction with the strongest explosive potential, while crowding in technology is elevated and some “high-to-low rotation” and rebalancing are also reasonable choices [Source: The Paper 2026-04-30 https://m.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_33083715]. The strategy should shift from “buy the sector” to “buy earnings elasticity and positional advantage within the sector.”

## Strategy Cadence For Tomorrow

- Start with turnover confirmation. If reopening turnover is materially below the pre-holiday Shanghai-Shenzhen-Beijing turnover reference of RMB 27595 hundred million [Source: Sina Finance / Greater Bay Area Finance Media 2026-04-30 https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkui1330852.shtml], the risk of chasing a technology gap-up rises.
- Keep core exposure to the technology main line, but avoid indiscriminate chasing. Prioritize semiconductor equipment, AI compute, optical communication, PCB, memory and advanced packaging where orders and profit delivery are visible; de-emphasize pure sentiment trades.
- Keep the portfolio balanced. On the growth side, focus on semiconductors, compute, commercial aerospace and humanoid robots; on the defensive and rebalance side, watch energy security, strategic metals, high-dividend financials and selected policy-recovery chains. If reopening breadth falls back below the pre-holiday benchmark of 2871 advancers [Source: The Paper 2026-04-30 https://m.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_33083715], reduce chase exposure.
- Risk control should take priority over post-holiday front-running. After STAR 50 rose 5.19% [Source: Tonghuashun 2026-04-30 https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml] in the last pre-holiday session, tomorrow’s key signal is leader follow-through and second-line diffusion, not the opening move itself.
