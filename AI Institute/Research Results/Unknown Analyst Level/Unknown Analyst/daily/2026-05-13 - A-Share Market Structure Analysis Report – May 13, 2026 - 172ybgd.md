# A-Share Market Structure Analysis Report – May 13, 2026

## 1. Market Overview

Today (Wednesday, May 13, 2026), the A-share market exhibited a clear "growth outperformance" pattern. Driven by anticipation of the high-stakes Trump-Xi summit and the fundamental logic of the AI industry, the ChiNext Index surged, comfortably closing above the psychologically important 4,000-point mark.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shanghai Composite (SHCOMP)** | 4,244.00 | +0.69% | Opened lower but rallied; heavyweights lagged while energy/infra supported. |

| **ChiNext Index (ChiNext)** | 4,038.33 | +2.63% | Hit an 11-year high; resonance between AI and "New Quality Productive Forces." |

## 2. Turnover Concentration

Turnover was heavily concentrated in top-tier growth sectors, reflecting a typical "structural bull market."

- **Sector Concentration**: AI infrastructure, Low-Altitude Economy, and "New Quality Productive Forces" sectors accounted for approximately 45% of total market turnover. Turnover rates in compute power leasing, advanced packaging, and domestic software all exceeded 8%.
- **Stock-Level Highlights**: High-turnover stocks were focused on tech leaders (e.g., SMIC, Inspur Electronic) and power infrastructure (Datang Power, Shanghai Electric). Datang International Power hit its +10% daily limit on favorable energy transition policy news.
- **Capital Flow**: Both Northbound (foreign) and major domestic institutional capital accelerated into ChiNext blue chips in the afternoon, while traditional heavyweights like Insurance and Banking saw net outflows.

## 3. Gainers/Losers Distribution

Despite the major indices closing in the green, internal structural divergence remained sharp.

- **Advance/Decline Ratio**: Approximately 3,800 stocks advanced while 1,400 declined, a ratio of nearly 2.7:1.
- **Limit Ups**: Over 120 stocks reached the daily limit up (including ST stocks), primarily in TMT, Defense, and Utilities.
- **Decline Statistics**: Around 80 stocks fell more than 5%, mostly concentrated in "Dividend Play" stocks that had previous excessive gains and financial heavyweights with disappointing earnings.

## 4. Volume & Price Characteristics

- **Turnover Volume**: Total turnover for the Shanghai and Shenzhen markets reached **RMB 2.21 trillion**, a slight increase from the previous trading day, indicating high market sentiment.
- **Intraday Trend**: Markets opened lower due to geopolitical tensions and volatility in US equities but trended upward driven by tech. Sentiment peaked in the afternoon as more positive details regarding the upcoming "Trump-Xi Summit" surfaced.
- **Valuation Levels**: With ChiNext back at 4,000 points, overall valuations have entered a historically high range (PE-TTM approx. 65x), reflecting extremely high market expectations for future earnings growth.

## 5. Conclusions & Strategic Outlook

- **Core Drivers**: Geopolitical de-escalation expectations (Trump-Xi Summit) + AI industry breakthroughs + Energy infrastructure expansion.
- **Market Sentiment**: Optimistic. The breakout in the ChiNext index has significantly boosted risk appetite.
- **Risk Warnings**:
    1. Persistent weakness in heavyweight sectors (Financials, Real Estate) may cap the upside for the broader indices.
    2. Rapid valuation expansion in tech stocks suggests caution against potential profit-taking at these highs.
    3. If the May 14–15 summit results fall short of expectations, significant market volatility could ensue.
