## Main Session Drivers

- The AI hardware narrative broadened from chips into transmission and substrates. Nvidia’s cooperation with Corning strengthened expectations for high-speed optical interconnects and CPO, while PCB/CCL tightness also lifted sentiment in electronic base components [Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/stock/bxjj/2026-05-07/doc-inhxaaii2876039.shtml].
- Risk appetite rotated from defensive assets into high-beta growth. Technology growth, optical communication, compute leasing, and semiconductors were strong, while oil and gas, coal, and chemicals corrected; the funding pattern looked like a shift from high-dividend defense to high-elasticity growth [Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-05-07/doc-inhxaaii2830803.shtml].
- This was not indiscriminate buying at the stock level. Optical fiber, CPO, PCB, compute leasing, and AI applications were active, while industrial gases and oil and gas weakened at the same time. The market was ranking industrial certainty rather than simply levering up to buy everything [Source: https://www.sohu.com/a/1019391041_362042].

## Institutional Read: Market Character

The tape is a “high-volatility strong market with a clear main line.” At the index level, the Shanghai Composite gained only 0.48% [Source: Eastmoney push2 quote API, queried 2026-05-07], but CSI 1000 gained 1.63% [Source: Eastmoney push2 quote API, queried 2026-05-07], which shows the profit effect came mainly from growth beta and thematic diffusion, not from a slow large-cap bull move.

For this type of market, the key question is not whether the index can immediately push higher. The key is whether the main line can change hands during intraday disagreement. Turnover was still RMB 3.14 trillion [Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-05-07/doc-inhxahrf8305269.shtml], but it was down RMB 828.79 hundred-million-yuan from the previous trading day [Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-05-07/doc-inhxahrf8305269.shtml], so the next session is more likely to test bid quality than reward straight-line sentiment extrapolation.
