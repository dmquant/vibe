# A-Share Flows and Sentiment Daily (2026-04-30)[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30; National Business Daily 2026-04-30]

## Priority Conclusions

On the final pre-holiday trading day, risk appetite did not fade, but it shifted from broad index participation to “hard-tech crowding + high-level divergence + hedging flows.” The Shanghai Composite closed at 4112.16, up 0.11%[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close], while STAR 50 closed at 1571.07, up 5.19%[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close]. Yet main-force capital in Shanghai and Shenzhen recorded a net outflow of 296.59亿元[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close], meaning price strength was driven by a limited set of high-recognition themes rather than broad capital inflow.

The sentiment thermometer reads 62/100[Source: author calculation based on Securities Times 2026-04-30 close data: 2671 advancers, 2362 decliners, 97 limit-up stocks, 55 limit-down stocks, turnover of 27409.17亿元, STAR 50 +5.19%, and main-force net outflow of 296.59亿元], a warm but not euphoric zone. The next trading day is 2026-05-06[Source: Sina Finance 2026-04-23 SSE Labor Day holiday schedule]. Post-holiday risk appetite will likely be determined by whether semiconductors, domestic AI compute and commercial space can absorb supply; if volume remains high while main-force outflows persist, momentum chasing in crowded tech should be reduced.

## Crowding and Contrarian Signals

- Electronics ranked among the stronger industries[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close], yet main-force capital in electronics posted a net outflow of 65.53亿元[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close]. This is the key crowding signal: strong price action with net selling indicates divergent turnover, not one-way incremental demand.
- STAR 50 rose 5.19%[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close] on turnover of 1434亿元[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close], while Cambricon turnover exceeded 284亿元[Source: East Money 2026-04-30 close]. Capital clearly used hard-tech leaders as pre-holiday crowding positions; without post-holiday volume confirmation, intraday volatility can rise.
- Margin balance stood at 27323.02亿元[Source: Shanghai Securities News / Sina Finance 2026-04-30] and was the third-highest in history[Source: Shanghai Securities News / Sina Finance 2026-04-30]. This is positive feedback for sentiment, but also a contrarian risk if the tech main line weakens.
- Gold ETF Huaan received 3.98亿元 of main-force net inflow[Source: Sina Finance / Jiemian 2026-04-30], showing investors had not abandoned hedges. Risk appetite was not “all-in offense”; it was “tech offense plus gold hedge.”

## Investor Behavior Shifts

First, leveraged capital became more aggressive. On 2026-04-29, margin balance increased by 38.66亿元 from the prior trading day[Source: Shanghai Securities News / Sina Finance 2026-04-30], and margin traders added 14.51亿元 to nonferrous metals[Source: Shanghai Securities News / Sina Finance 2026-04-30], showing that retail and high-beta capital still wanted exposure to cyclical and growth momentum.

Second, institutions and main-force capital rotated within strong stocks. Dragon-Tiger List institutional seats recorded a combined net sell of about 5368.37万元[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close], but VeriSilicon saw institutional net buying of about 3.35亿元[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close], while Cambricon saw institutional net selling of about 6.81亿元[Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close]. This is not a withdrawal from technology; it is a shift from overextended core names toward lower-position or more tradable alternatives.

Third, ETF investors favored theme baskets. Satellite ETF Yongying (159206) recorded 18.54亿元 of cumulative inflows over the past 30 trading days[Source: Sina Finance / Jiemian 2026-04-30], while ChiNext AI ETF Huabao (159363) recorded 3.81亿元 over the past 7 days[Source: Jiemian 2026-04-30]. Investors want main-line exposure but prefer to diversify away from single high-priced stocks.
