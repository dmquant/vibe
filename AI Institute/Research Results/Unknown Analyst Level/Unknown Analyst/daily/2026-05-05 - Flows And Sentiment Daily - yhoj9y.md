## Priority Conclusions

- Risk appetite was not broad-based; it was a structural rotation into technology, out of some blue-chip ETF exposure, and away from defensive holdings. STAR-board main-force funds saw a net inflow of 78.39亿元 [Source: Securities Times / DataBao 2026-04-30 https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html], while the broader two-market main-force balance was a net outflow of 384.07亿元 [Source: Securities Times / DataBao 2026-04-30 https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html].
- Northbound trading was closed today [Source: HKEX Stock Connect Calendar 2026 https://www.hkex.com.hk/-/media/HKEX-Market/Mutual-Market/Stock-Connect/Reference-Materials/Trading-Hour%2C-Trading-and-Settlement-Calendar/2026-Calendar_pdf_e.pdf]. On 2026-04-30, Northbound turnover was 3506.82亿元, with Shanghai Connect at 1688.77亿元 and Shenzhen Connect at 1818.06亿元 [Source: Stockstar Northbound Flow 2026-04-30 https://m.vip.stockstar.com/Northflow/index.html]. Since 2024-08-19, the post-close disclosure framework for Shanghai/Shenzhen Connect primarily publishes total turnover, number of trades, ETF turnover and top active securities turnover, so official daily net-buying is no longer a directly usable daily directional signal [Source: Yicai 2024-07-26 https://m.yicai.com/news/102208280.html].
- Margin money remained active but was not adding risk indiscriminately. As of 2026-04-29, Shanghai-Shenzhen financing balance totaled 27038.92亿元, up 36.64亿元 from the prior session [Source: Sina Finance / Shanghai Securities News 2026-04-30 https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwftwi0399587.shtml?froms=ggmp]. On 2026-04-30, Shanghai-market margin net buying was -75.26亿元 [Source: Stockstar Margin Trading 2026-04-30 https://quote.stockstar.com/Financing/].
- Sentiment was warmer but crowded. Pre-holiday tradable A shares had 2877 advancers, representing 52.70% of the universe, and 2461 decliners [Source: Securities Times / DataBao 2026-04-30 https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html]. The session had 98 limit-up stocks and 52 limit-down stocks [Source: JRJ / Sohu 2026-04-30 https://www.sohu.com/a/1016833558_114984]. That supports a short-term sentiment repair, not a risk-free broad rally.

## Crowding And Contrarian Signals

- The strongest contrarian signal is that the index did not broadly rally, while capital concentrated heavily in technology. Guosen Securities said AI hardware and new energy are relatively crowded but not yet at an extreme, while AI applications and resource names have cooled to moderate levels and consumer/property remains cold [Source: The Paper 2026-04-30 https://m.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_33083715].
- China Securities said AI hardware remains the highest-explosive-potential area, while technology crowding is elevated and structural overheating risk exists; high-to-low rotation and rebalancing are also reasonable [Source: The Paper 2026-04-30 https://m.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_33083715].
- In ETF financing, on 2026-04-29 HFT CSI Short-term Bond ETF saw 24.92亿元 of financing net buying, Southern CSI 500 ETF saw 6.48亿元, and Harvest SSE STAR Chip ETF saw 1.86亿元; Fullgoal ChinaBond 7-10Y Policy Financial Bond ETF saw 1.85亿元 of financing net selling, while E Fund ChiNext ETF saw 1.55亿元 [Source: Ifeng Finance / Securities Times 2026-04-30 https://finance.ifeng.com/c/8sjuahIsmqX]. Leveraged ETF trading is therefore split across cash management, broad-beta elasticity and chip exposure rather than pointing in one direction.
- At the stock level, Cambricon had 38.38亿元 of net inflow, China Greatwall 24.89亿元 and Accelink 14.59亿元; Foxconn Industrial Internet had 29.49亿元 of net outflow, Eoptolink 18.27亿元 and Hengtong Optic-Electric 14.31亿元 [Source: Securities Times / DataBao 2026-04-30 https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html]. Clear divergence inside the AI/compute chain means “technology” should not be treated as a single trade after the holiday.

## Investor Behavior Shifts

- Foreign-flow monitoring has shifted from “net buying/net selling” toward “turnover intensity and active names.” This is a disclosure-regime change, not evidence that cross-border capital suddenly disappeared [Source: Yicai 2024-07-26 https://m.yicai.com/news/102208280.html].
- Main-force money moved from large index weights toward STAR assets with earnings validation. CSI 300 constituents saw a main-force net outflow of 178.78亿元 [Source: Securities Times / DataBao 2026-04-30 https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html], while the STAR board saw a net inflow of 78.39亿元 [Source: Securities Times / DataBao 2026-04-30 https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html].
- ETF investors rebalanced within broad-beta exposure: CSI 500 ETF and CSI 1000 ETF received subscriptions, while CSI 300 ETF and SSE 50 ETF were redeemed [Source: National Business Daily 2026-04-30 https://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-04-30/4375813.html].
- Margin traders did not chase in one direction. On 2026-04-30, the Shanghai market recorded 1357亿 of margin buying, 1433亿 of repayment and -75.26亿 of net margin buying [Source: Stockstar Margin Trading 2026-04-30 https://quote.stockstar.com/Financing/], showing some leveraged capital locked in gains before the holiday.

## Risk Appetite For Tomorrow

Base case: trading resumes on 2026-05-06 [Source: Shanghai Stock Exchange 2026-04-23 https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml], and risk appetite is likely to stay constructive, but it needs confirmation from turnover, breadth and main-line follow-through. If reopening turnover is close to or above the pre-holiday Shanghai-Shenzhen-Beijing level of 27595亿 [Source: Sina Finance / Greater Bay Area Finance Media 2026-04-30 https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkui1330852.shtml], and advancers exceed the pre-holiday 2877-name benchmark [Source: Securities Times / DataBao 2026-04-30 https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html], technology can continue to broaden. If turnover is materially below 27595亿 [Source: Sina Finance / Greater Bay Area Finance Media 2026-04-30 https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkui1330852.shtml] and limit-up names fall below the pre-holiday 98-name benchmark [Source: JRJ / Sohu 2026-04-30 https://www.sohu.com/a/1016833558_114984], pre-holiday chase money and ETF financing positions may become short-term supply.
