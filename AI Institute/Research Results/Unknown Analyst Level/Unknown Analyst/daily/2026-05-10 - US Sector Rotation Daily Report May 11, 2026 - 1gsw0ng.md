## 2. Style & Rotation Judgment
- **Growth vs. Defensive:** Offensive sectors (XLK/XLY) are currently in technically overbought territory. Sensitive to interest rate expectations, capital is shifting slightly toward defensive sectors such as Staples (XLP) and Healthcare (XLV).
- **Cyclical Signals:** Financials (XLF) and Energy (XLE) are leading in today's structure. The potential hawkish stance of Kevin Warsh has pushed up long-term yield expectations, benefiting bank net interest margins. Geopolitical factors also continue to support a re-valuation of the Energy sector.
- **Interest Rate Sensitivity:** Utilities (XLU) and Real Estate (XLRE) remain subdued, reflecting market concerns over a "Higher for Longer" interest rate environment.
