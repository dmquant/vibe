# Theme And Sector Rotation Daily

> Date anchor: 2026-05-05 [Source: local shell date 2026-05-05]. A shares were closed for the Labor Day holiday today; the Shanghai Stock Exchange notice states that trading is closed from 2026-05-01 through 2026-05-05 and resumes on 2026-05-06 [Source: https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml]. Therefore, “today’s rotation” has no fresh intraday trading print, and the trading baseline is the previous pre-holiday session on 2026-04-30 [Source: https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml].

## Priority Conclusions

- The key rotation was not broad-index beta; it was “hard-tech offense, blue-chip ETF redemptions, and weakness in defensive and some crowded high-position trades.” STAR 50 rose 5.19%, Shanghai-Shenzhen-Beijing turnover reached 27595亿, up 1504亿 from the previous session [Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkui1330852.shtml]. Yet main-force funds still net sold 384.07亿元 across the two markets, while the STAR board alone saw 78.39亿元 of net inflow [Source: https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html].
- Winners were concentrated in AI-compute chips, semiconductors, commercial aerospace, humanoid robotics and energy metals; laggards were concentrated in the battery chain, gas, precious metals, plus hotels and catering, fiberglass and gaming [Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6182188.shtml; Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkui1330852.shtml].
- Trading implication: do not equate “strongest before the holiday” with “chaseable after the holiday.” The key reopening tests are whether turnover can approach the pre-holiday 27595亿, whether advancers can again exceed the pre-holiday 2871, and whether the STAR board can sustain its 78.39亿元 relative inflow advantage [Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkui1330852.shtml; Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6182188.shtml; Source: https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html].

## Trading Conclusion

The pre-reopening core view is: hard tech remains the main line, but the trade has moved from “buy AI concepts” to “buy earnings, orders and policy execution.” AI-compute chips and semiconductors are the first tier; commercial aerospace and embodied AI are potential diffusion directions; energy metals are the resource-security branch. Quantum, biotech and Web3/data infrastructure do not yet have pre-holiday flow confirmation. If reopening turnover and breadth confirm together, keep core technology exposure and add lower-position diffusion; if the market only gaps up without volume, reduce chase exposure.
