## I. Priority Conclusions (Highest-Urgency Items This Evening)

1. **Critical (pre-open direction):** Three analysts have given **mutually incompatible** reads on where capital rotates after the holiday — chief-strategist bets on liquidity returning to internet giants (Tencent 0700.HK / Alibaba 9988.HK), tmt-analyst favors the AI-application layer (Kingsoft Office, Tonghuashun), and chief-quant warns that the momentum factor has hit its 5-year historical extreme (top 5% crowding) with crash-risk implications. 3. **Medium-high urgency (framing of A-share 4,000):** china-macro's whiteboard (Card 02) characterises the Shanghai Composite at 4,000 as a "fragile high-altitude equilibrium sustained by retail FOMO, with institutions actively rebalancing out." The risk panel (05_risk_panel.md) issues a Yellow / structurally-bullish signal. Both reports are referenced by downstream analysts, but they point to **opposite portfolio implications** — misalignment here will produce systematic risk-assessment errors through the first week of reopening.
