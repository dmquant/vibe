### LGFVs And Debt Resolution

- The national debt-resolution framework remains based on `2 万亿元` per year in hidden-debt swaps for `2024-2026`, plus `8000 亿元` per year in new special bonds for debt resolution during `2024-2028`; for `2026`, this is best treated as an approximately `2.8 万亿元` resource pool. [S2][S3][S4]
- `1Q26` local-government bond issuance was `3.1059 万亿元`; hidden-debt swap refinancing special bonds were `9604 亿元`, and special new special bonds exceeded `1719 亿元`. [S2]
- As of `2026-04-17`, issuance used to swap existing hidden debt was about `1.13 万亿元`, roughly `40%` of the `2026` debt-resolution quota of `2.8 万亿元`. [S3][S4]
- Weak-region issuance has been directly confirmed: Tianjin `357.48 亿元`, Liaoning `45.7 亿元`, Gansu `35.4759 亿元` plus `6.9688 亿元`, Guizhou “26贵州07” `66.8757 亿元`, and Yunnan “26云南债08” has listed. [S4]
- The Qingdao signal looks more like pressure spilling over from Shandong district/county platforms, not a reversal in Qingdao municipal credit: in `1Q26`, Shandong had `27` bond-issuing LGFVs with continuing commercial-bill overdue records, or `50.00%` of the sample; district/county platforms accounted for `36` names, or `66.67%`; overdue entities had `589.12 亿元` of outstanding bonds maturing within one year, or `40.18%`. [S4]
- The Wuhan signal is currently weaker than Qingdao's commercial-bill overdue chain, and is more about high-cost non-bank financing and maturity-refinancing pressure: Wuhan Urban Construction had `1400.63 亿元` of interest-bearing debt at end-`2024`, with `147.30 亿元` due within one year; other interest-bearing debt was `689.89 亿元`, or `49.26%`; Wuhan Chegu City Development disclosed `35.825244 亿元` of non-bank/trust/leasing borrowings, with some rates at `4.75%-5.90%`. ### Small Banks And Capital Instruments

- There is no public evidence that Inner Mongolia Rural Commercial Bank or the former Jingu Rural Commercial Bank has formally missed principal or interest; “silent credit event” is the more accurate description. [S6]
- The trigger chain is “reform merger - debt assumption - financial-statement reconstruction - unavailable rating materials.” Inner Mongolia Rural Commercial Bank was newly formed through the merger of the former regional rural-credit union, `93` county-level rural-credit legal entities, and `26` village banks; `120` institutions were dissolved, and creditors' rights and debts were assumed by Inner Mongolia Rural Commercial Bank. [S6]
- For “21 金谷农商永续债,” with size of `5 亿元`, the key economic-loss triggers are coupon cancellation and write-down clauses, not ordinary-bond cross-default; the issuer may cancel coupons, and unpaid interest does not accumulate. [S6]
- Cross-regional contagion risk is “limited but not negligible”: direct risk is constrained by instrument size and local resolution resources, but information opacity can depress small-bank capital-bond valuations and interbank credit appetite. [S6]
