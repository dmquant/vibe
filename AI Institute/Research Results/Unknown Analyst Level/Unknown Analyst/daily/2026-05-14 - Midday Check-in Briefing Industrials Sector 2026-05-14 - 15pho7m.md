## 1. Confirmed Facts and Data (Morning Session)

### 1.1 Macro and Sector Fundamentals
- **Manufacturing Resilience:** April Manufacturing PMI recorded **50.3**, marking three consecutive months of expansion. Despite external demand uncertainty, production for the "New Three" (EVs, batteries, solar) remains robust.
- **Carbon Market Activity:** CEA (National Carbon Emission Allowance) closed at **RMB 82.56/ton**, with cumulative turnover exceeding RMB 60 billion.
- **FX Impact:** USD/CNY broke **7.35** and is moving toward the **7.35-7.40** policy defense zone. Industrials face a mix of export benefit expectations and input cost pressures for imported raw materials.

### 1.2 Industrials and Advanced Manufacturing Dynamics
- **"First Commercial Aerospace Manufacturing Stock":** Topu CNC is currently undergoing its global IPO, with an expected listing on May 20. The company ranks first in market share for 5-axis CNC machines for aerospace.
- **Compute-Power Synergy:** AI infrastructure is driving energy demand. Datang Power (601991) recorded its 7th consecutive limit-up, becoming the market leader.
- **Defense and Autonomous Systems:** Shield AI and Thunder Tiger reached a strategic cooperation to integrate Hivemind autonomous flight software into Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs).
- **Capacity Expansion:** Northrop Grumman and Collins Aerospace are expanding manufacturing capacity at their Florida bases.

### 1.3 Market Sentiment and Liquidity
- **Volatile High Turnover:** A-share morning turnover reached **RMB 2.28 trillion**. Social media is focused on rumors of Trump's visit and Jensen Huang (NVIDIA CEO) potentially accompanying him.
- **TMT Crowding:** Sector holdings reached the **32%** warning line, with turnover accounting for **36.7%** of the total market.
