## Priority Conclusions

- **Travel and hotels: search heat is not the same as booking heat.** The morning read stands: the gap between OTA search and payment conversion looks more like a channel-structure shift driven by AI comparison shopping, member-direct diversion, and prepaid room vouchers than a plain demand collapse.[R1][R2]
- **Low-end consumer pressure is visible in high-frequency data, but it is not yet a cyclical credit crunch.** Alt-data flagged a 180bp MoM increase in 30+ day delinquencies at small fintech lending platforms during the first 10 days of May; global-macro’s review classifies consumer credit as moving from “normalization” to “early stress,” with no contagion yet into prime / near-prime, HY OAS around 320–340bp, and no complete tightening chain.[R2][R3]

The reported regulatory move from “100% non-refundable” to “tiered cancellation penalties” needs hard evidence.[R1]

The current narrative is driven by a community model around “深蕾科技入主 + Broadcom/长鑫 agency + potential asset injection,” not by verified listed-company earnings.[R4]
