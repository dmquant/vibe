## 3. Detailed Factor Analysis

### 3.1 Momentum Factor
Momentum continues to deliver significant alpha. With the Shanghai Composite reaching decade highs and maintaining a 14-day winning streak, the market is characterized by strong trend-following behavior. Sectors linked to the **15th Five-Year Plan**, such as semiconductors, computing power, and optical modules, are driving both technical and fundamental momentum.

### 3.2 Quality Factor
As Q1 2026 earnings reports are finalized, investors are prioritizing firms with high ROE, stable cash flows, and "anti-involution" pricing power. Financials and high-tech sectors with proven profitability are seeing consistent inflows, revitalizing the Quality factor.

### 3.3 Crowding Risk Analysis
*   **High Crowding:** AI+ Initiative, Semiconductors. Institutional consensus is heavily concentrated in computing hardware, domestic chip substitution, and industrial AI. Crowding levels in these themes are approaching two-year highs.
*   **Low Crowding:** Traditional Cyclical Value, High-Dividend Stocks. Despite attractive valuation metrics, these sectors remain under-loved as capital rotates toward the "Technology-First" national strategy.

- **Long Positioning:** Maintain a "High-Quality Momentum" tilt. Focus on assets with both strong trend indicators and confirmed earnings recovery. Avoid chasing pure high-beta stocks without fundamental support.

- **Rotation Opportunity:** Monitor the lagging "Value-Quality" composite factors for potential catch-up plays as AI-related liquidity eventually spills over into adjacent sectors.
