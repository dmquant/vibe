## 1. Confirmed Morning Facts & Key Data

### 1.1 Equity Markets: Profit Taking and Massive Turnover
- **A-Share Performance:** Classic "High Open, Low Close" pattern. As of midday, the Shanghai Composite (SHCOMP) stood at 4199.19 (-1.02%), while the ChiNext Index fell by 1.93%.
- **Trading Volume:** Combined turnover reached **2.28 trillion CNY** in the half-day session, showing significant volume expansion and heavy rotation.
- **Theme Shift:** Driven by Trump's China visit and the presence of tech titans like Musk and Huang, market expectations shifted from "Self-Reliance" to "Globalization Cooperation." Power and Chemical sectors strengthened on AI infra demand, while the previously hot Semiconductor and Trust sectors saw heavy profit-taking.

### 1.2 Macro & FX: Divergence Intensifies
- **RMB Pressure:** USD/CNY spot is trending towards the 7.33–7.40 stress zone.
- **Data Divergence:** US April PPI rose 1.4% MoM, triggering a re-pricing of rate hike expectations. China’s April CPI rose only 0.4% YoY. The deepening yield inversion (US 10Y @ 4.46% vs CN 10Y @ 2.15%) is the primary driver for capital outflows.
- **Supportive Factors:** China's April PMI (50.3) remains in expansion, and the trade surplus is estimated at $78.2B, providing a fundamental floor.

### 1.3 Commodities: Safe Haven vs Inflation
- **Spot Gold:** Strongly broke above the **$4,700/oz** threshold.
- **Carbon Market:** National CEA closed at 82.56 CNY/ton with active trading.
