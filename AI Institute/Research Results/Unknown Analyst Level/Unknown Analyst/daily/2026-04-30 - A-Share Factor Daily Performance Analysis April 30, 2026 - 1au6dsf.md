## Quant View & Strategy
- **Style Allocation:** Maintain an overweight stance on **Quality** and **Value**. Following the earnings season, the market enters a "fundamental vacuum" where stocks with robust ROE and dividend support offer superior defensiveness.
- **Risk Warning:** The failure of the Momentum factor often signals a transition in market leadership. Avoid theme-based stocks with excessive prior gains.
- **Outlook:** Monitor how the market reprices macro recovery expectations after the holiday and look for stabilization in Momentum factors after their mid-term mean reversion.
