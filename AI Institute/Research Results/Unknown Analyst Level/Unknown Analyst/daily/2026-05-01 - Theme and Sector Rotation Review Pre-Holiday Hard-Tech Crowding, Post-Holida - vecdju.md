# Theme and Sector Rotation Review: Pre-Holiday Hard-Tech Crowding, Post-Holiday Confirmation Required

## Trading-Day Calibration and Priority Conclusions

- May 1, 2026 is an A-share Labor Day market holiday; the Shanghai Stock Exchange notice says trading is suspended from May 1, 2026 (Friday) to May 5, 2026 (Tuesday), and resumes as usual on May 6, 2026 (Wednesday)[来源：https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml]. Therefore this report does not describe May 1 as a new market close; it reviews the last pre-holiday trading day, April 30, 2026[来源：https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml].
- Strength ranking: AI compute chips and semiconductors led; energy metals and upstream lithium provided beta; commercial space, humanoid robots, memory, CPO/PCB were the main diffusion trades; media/gaming, power, steel, and parts of the battery chain were the main adjustment areas.
- Trading implication: STAR 50 rose 5.19%, far stronger than CSI 300 at -0.06% and ChiNext at -0.27%[来源：https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml]. Funds were not buying all growth assets; they were crowding into hard-tech names with earnings, orders, and policy catalysts.
- Key risk: semiconductor turnover reached 3407.14亿元, communications equipment turnover reached 1548.01亿元, and battery turnover reached 1502.26亿元[来源：https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml]. Such high turnover must be absorbed after the holiday; if volume contracts after the May 6 open, leading themes can shift from uptrend to high-level churn[来源：https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml].

## Strategy Conclusion

The last pre-holiday trading day was not a broad bull market; it was a high-turnover, strong-main-line, narrow-diffusion hard-tech rally. AI compute chips and semiconductors were the top line, energy metals and commercial space provided beta diffusion, innovative drugs were an event-window alternative, and quantum technology plus Web3 were not first-tier trades. The first post-holiday trading day is May 6, 2026[来源：https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml]. The key is not the theme label, but whether funds can keep absorbing semiconductor turnover of 3407.14亿元[来源：https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml], STAR Market main-fund net inflow of 78.39亿元[来源：https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html], and total market turnover of 27592亿元[来源：https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml].
