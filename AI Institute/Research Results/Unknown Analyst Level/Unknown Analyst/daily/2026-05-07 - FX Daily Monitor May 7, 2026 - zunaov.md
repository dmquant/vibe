## Interest Rate Differential Analysis

Policy divergence remains the dominant theme, though spreads are beginning to compress:

1.  **Federal Reserve (Fed):** The target range for the federal funds rate stands at **3.50% - 3.75%**. Following a "hawkish hold" in April, the Committee remains divided. However, the market is already pricing in potential shifts as Powell prepares to step down.
2.  **European Central Bank (ECB):** The deposit rate remains at **2.00%**. Reduced inflationary pressure from falling oil prices has lowered the immediate need for further cuts, providing a floor for the Euro.
3.  **Spread Impact:** The US-Germany 10Y yield spread has narrowed to approximately **150bps**, fueling the rotation back into Euro-denominated assets.
