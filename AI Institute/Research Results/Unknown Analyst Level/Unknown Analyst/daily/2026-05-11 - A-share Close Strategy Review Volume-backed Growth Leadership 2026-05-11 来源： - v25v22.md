## Market Character

The session can be summarized as “structural diffusion within a strong trend.” Turnover reached 35,388.61亿元 [来源：腾讯行情 2026-05-11 查询], and more than 3,100 stocks advanced [来源：https://www.nbd.com.cn/articles/2026-05-11/4386825.html], confirming both sentiment and liquidity. But the Beijing Stock Exchange 50 fell 0.70% [来源：腾讯行情 2026-05-11 查询], which means capital was not chasing every micro-cap pocket; it concentrated in tech hardware and selected cyclicals with verifiable momentum, earnings mapping, and adequate capacity.

Institutionally, this looks more like an acceleration phase in an earnings-led market than a full-market valuation rerating. As long as turnover stays elevated and tech hardware continues to absorb incremental capital, index momentum can persist. If high-position hard-tech names begin to show shrinking turnover and intraday divergence, low-position catch-up trades may rotate in, while the win rate of chasing pure sentiment names will fall.
