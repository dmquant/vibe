## Key Conclusions

Today was not a one-way index rally. It was a structural session: hard-tech multiples expanded sharply while broad indices digested gains. STAR 50 rose 5.19%[Source: 新浪财经/格隆汇 2026-04-30 close], materially outperforming ChiNext at -0.27%[Source: 每日经济新闻 2026-04-30 close]. Within growth, capital rotated further from lithium batteries and AI applications into domestic AI chips, semiconductor equipment, memory and CPO. Market turnover stayed high at 27595亿[Source: 新浪财经/湾区财经传媒 2026-04-30 close], up 1504亿[Source: 新浪财经/湾区财经传媒 2026-04-30 close] from the prior session, which points to concentration rather than risk-off liquidation.

Trading conclusion: on the final pre-holiday session, investors crowded into high-beta technology names with visible earnings validation. The index backdrop remains healthy, but after a rise in theme concentration, the better post-holiday cadence is “confirm first, then add,” not broad-based chasing. ## Main Drivers

First, semiconductor fundamentals were repriced. After several semiconductor companies reported first-quarter results, the market traded AI compute orders, domestic chip volume ramp-up and semiconductor-equipment localization as one coherent theme. Strength in Cambricon, VeriSilicon and other leaders helped lift STAR 50 by 5.19%[Source: 财联社/新浪财经 2026-04-30 close review].

Second, policy expectations supported risk appetite. The Shenzhen Housing and Construction Bureau issued an optimized property-policy notice on 2026-04-29[Source: Shenzhen Government Online 2026-04-29], effective from 2026-04-30[Source: Shenzhen Government Online 2026-04-29]. Eligible households may buy one additional commodity home in Futian District, Nanshan District and Xin'an Subdistrict of Bao'an District[Source: Shenzhen Government Online 2026-04-29]. This helped the property chain rebound tactically, but the market’s turnover structure still shows technology as the main line.

Third, pre-holiday positioning became clearer. Total market turnover reached 27595亿[Source: 新浪财经/湾区财经传媒 2026-04-30 close] and advancers numbered 2907[Source: 每日经济新闻 2026-04-30 close], while broad indices diverged. That combination says capital did not leave; it clustered around areas with earnings delivery, strong industrial trends and sufficient institutional capacity.

## Institutional Read: Market Character

The current market can be described as “slow-bull index, fast-rotating main lines, crowded core assets.” The Shanghai Composite held near 4112.16[Source: 新浪财经/湾区财经传媒 2026-04-30 close] and turnover remained elevated, suggesting low systemic risk. But STAR 50’s 5.19%[Source: 新浪财经/湾区财经传媒 2026-04-30 close] gain alongside ChiNext’s -0.27%[Source: 新浪财经/湾区财经传媒 2026-04-30 close] decline also shows that the key battleground inside growth has shifted from beta to alpha.

For portfolio construction, the split does not justify cutting exposure aggressively, but it does require tighter structure. Core holdings should sit in semiconductor equipment, domestic AI compute, CPO and memory, supported by a lower-position policy-repair sleeve. For themes that have risen too fast without earnings confirmation, wait for post-holiday absorption before adding.
