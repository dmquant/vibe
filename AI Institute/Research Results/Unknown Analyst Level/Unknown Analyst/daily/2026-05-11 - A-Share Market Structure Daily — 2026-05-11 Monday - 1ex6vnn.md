## 5. Conclusions & What to Track
**Calls**
1. The structural lead is unchanged: STAR 50 + ChiNext are where leveraged money is concentrating; the main board (SSE, SZSE) is following rather than leading. Position ranking "hard tech > growth > cyclicals > defensives" still works.
2. With margin debt pressing RMB 2.8 trn and brokers raising liquidation lines, the tape's sensitivity to leverage is rising — the tail risk of a single-day "good news in, fast give-back" gap is materially higher than two weeks ago.
3. The US backdrop is moderately, not broadly, risk-on — XLK/XLC strong but XLF/XLU lagging — so A-share strength remains structural rather than systemic.

**Tracking**
- Tomorrow's open: whether STAR 50 holds 1700 and ChiNext holds 3900 — these are the psychological lines that mark whether growth leadership shifts gear or pauses.
- Flows: whether margin balance prints above RMB 2.8 trn and whether northbound net inflows continue. If margin stalls while indices keep rising, leadership is shifting from incremental capital to existing-stock churn — and structural risk steps up.
