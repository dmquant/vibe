## Executive Summary

Sentiment on major Chinese financial social platforms has rebounded significantly today, characterized by a dual-track focus on "Tech Growth" and "Resource Defense." A major portfolio shift by high-profile investor Duan Yongping sparked a heated debate between value and growth investing philosophies. While market turnover remains robust at approximately 3 trillion RMB, a "fear of heights" regarding tech valuations is beginning to surface among retail and institutional commentators.

## Platform-Specific Breakdown

### 1. Xueqiu & Eastmoney (Stock Bar)
*   **Duan Yongping's Shift to Pop Mart:** Renowned investor Duan Yongping (ID: 大道无形我有型) publicly disclosed selling China Shenhua to buy Pop Mart (HK: 09992). This triggered massive discussions on the rotation from high-dividend defensive plays to consumption-driven growth stocks, signaling a potential sentiment shift toward the recovery of discretionary spending.
*   **The First "Double-Gain" Fund of 2026:** The *GF Vision Choice* fund achieved a year-to-date return exceeding 112%, heavily weighted in memory chips and optical communications. This has fueled extreme enthusiasm for the semiconductor sector on Eastmoney forums.
*   **DeepSeek’s 50 Billion RMB Financing:** News of DeepSeek’s massive fundraising plan went viral across tech and investment groups, reinforcing the AI sector’s status as the definitive investment theme for the year.

### 2. Weibo (Trending Topics)
*   **#China-US Trade Consultation#:** High anticipation for the upcoming talks on May 12-13. Financial influencers are interpreting this as a potential "marginal improvement" in policy expectations, boosting sentiment for the export-oriented and trade-related sectors.
*   **#Mainstream AI Apps Start Charging#:** Rumors that TikTok's AI assistant "Doubao" will implement fee-based services have shifted the public discourse from "AI utility" to "AI monetization," driving increased interest in listed AI application companies.
*   **#Table Tennis World Championship 12th Consecutive Title#:** National pride has translated into a surge of mentions for "Guochao" (domestic trend) brands like Li-Ning and Anta.
