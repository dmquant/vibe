# Flows And Sentiment Daily

## Priority Conclusions

- Today is not a new A-share closing session. The Shanghai Stock Exchange notice says the Labor Day market holiday runs from May 1 to May 5, with normal trading resuming on May 6[Source: https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml]. This report therefore reviews April 30[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml], the final trading day before the holiday.
- Risk appetite is best described as hot trading, narrow leadership and elevated leverage. Shanghai and Shenzhen turnover reached 27592 hundred million yuan, up 1503 hundred million yuan from the previous trading day[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml], but main-force money posted a marketwide net outflow of 384.07 hundred million yuan, with ChiNext at -100.63 hundred million yuan and CSI 300 constituents at -178.78 hundred million yuan[Source: https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html].
- STAR Market remains the core crowding zone. Main-force money into STAR Market was +78.39 hundred million yuan[Source: https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html], while STAR 50 rose 5.19%[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml]. Offensive money has not left the market, but it is concentrating inside hard tech.
- Northbound flow should not be written under the old “net buying” convention. After the Stock Connect disclosure adjustment, exchanges disclose items such as northbound turnover and trade count after the close[Source: https://www.szse.cn/aboutus/trends/news/t20240412_606835.html]. On April 30, northbound turnover was 3506.82 hundred million yuan, with Shanghai Connect at 1688.77 hundred million yuan and Shenzhen Connect at 1818.06 hundred million yuan[Source: https://hk.stockstar.com/northflow/].
- Sentiment is warm but breadth is not in full squeeze mode. On April 30, there were 79 limit-up stocks, 14 failed limit-ups and an 85% seal rate[Source: https://k.sina.cn/article_7857201856_1d45362c001904x19q.html]. Among tradable A-shares, 2877 advanced, equal to 52.70%, while 2461 declined[Source: https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html].

## Implications For Tomorrow And The Next Trading Day

Tomorrow, May 2, is still an A-share market holiday[Source: https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml], so risk-appetite judgment should be applied to the May 6 reopening[Source: https://www.sse.com.cn/disclosure/announcement/general/c/c_20260423_10816345.shtml].

- Base case: Post-holiday risk appetite should remain positive, but not broad-based. Support comes from 27592 hundred million yuan of turnover[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml], 78.39 hundred million yuan of STAR Market main-force net inflow[Source: https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html], and 2229.31 hundred million yuan of April equity ETF net inflow[Source: https://k.sina.com.cn/article_7857201856_1d45362c001904hfcm.html].
- Cooling condition: If post-holiday turnover falls materially below 27592 hundred million yuan[Source: https://finance.sina.com.cn/roll/2026-04-30/doc-inhwhkue6193447.shtml] and semiconductors cannot absorb the prior 3407.14 hundred million yuan turnover base[Source: https://news.10jqka.com.cn/20260430/c676416615.shtml], high-position hard-tech exposure should shift from chasing to taking profits.
- Continued offense condition: If STAR Market keeps receiving main-force inflow and northbound turnover remains near the high-activity level of 3506.82 hundred million yuan[Source: https://hk.stockstar.com/northflow/], the growth leadership can extend.
- Contrarian watchpoint: If limit-up count stays above 79 and seal rate stays above 85%[Source: https://k.sina.cn/article_7857201856_1d45362c001904x19q.html], but advancers do not rise meaningfully beyond 2877[Source: https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3892165.html], sentiment heat would be stronger than market breadth.
