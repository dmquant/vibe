# A-Share Theme and Sector Rotation Daily (2026-04-30) [Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close; Eastmoney 2026-04-30 market wrap]

## Priority Conclusions

The final trading day before the holiday was not a broad “growth rally”; it was a concentrated re-rating of domestic AI-compute chips and the semiconductor supply chain on the STAR Market. The STAR 50 closed at 1571.07, up 5.19% [Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close], materially outperforming ChiNext at 3677.15, down 0.27% [Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close]. Yet main-board and Shenzhen market main-force funds recorded a net outflow of RMB 296.59亿元 [Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close], which means price strength was driven by a small group of high-recognition hard-tech names rather than a broad injection of new money.

Trading implication: domestic AI-compute chips, advanced packaging, memory chips, and satellite internet/commercial aerospace should remain the main watchlist. But the first post-holiday trading day is 2026-05-06 [Source: Sina Finance 2026-04-23 SSE Labor Day holiday schedule], so high-level tech needs confirmation of buying support first. If electronics and communications keep seeing main-force outflows, chasing strength will lose edge quickly. If defense, commercial aerospace, and semiconductor equipment keep seeing broader inflows, the rotation can shift from “leader crowding” to “supply-chain catch-up.”

## Emerging Directions and Trading Implications

1. **AI hardware remains the strongest consensus trade**: Cambricon turnover exceeded RMB 284亿元, while Foxconn Industrial Internet, GigaDevice, and Zhongji Innolight each exceeded RMB 180亿元 [Source: Eastmoney 2026-04-30 market wrap]. Funds are crowding into liquid leaders across AI chips, optical modules, memory, and advanced packaging. If post-holiday turnover cannot sustain the move, leader volatility will rise.
2. **Commercial aerospace is receiving a second confirmation from flows**: Satellite ETF Yongying (159206) saw cumulative net inflow of RMB 18.54亿元 over the past 30 trading days [Source: Sina Finance / Jiemian 2026-04-30], while defense main-force inflow reached RMB 15.82亿元 [Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close]. Space infrastructure, satellite internet, and aerospace materials deserve continued monitoring.
3. **Policy-repair chains should only be traded as event trades**: Shenzhen’s housing policy took effect on 2026-04-30 [Source: Shenzhen Government Online 2026-04-29], and real estate saw RMB 5.13亿元 of main-force net inflow [Source: Securities Times 2026-04-30 close]. These trades need transaction-data validation before spreading heavily into building materials, home furnishing, or banks.
4. **AI applications face regulatory re-pricing**: The Cyberspace Administration of China launched a 4-month “Qinglang” campaign to clean up disorder in AI applications [Source: People’s Daily Online 2026-04-30]. Low-quality AI content, anthropomorphic interaction, and AIGC marketing names face valuation constraints; compliant tools, enterprise/government AI, and security governance are better-positioned beneficiaries.
5. **Quantum and Web3 are not intraday mainlines yet**: China’s national optical-wavelength quantum standard was approved on 2026-04-24 [Source: DoNews 2026-04-24], which is a medium- to long-term technology-infrastructure catalyst. No verifiable A-share mainline flow signal was found for Web3/digital assets today [Source unknown], so it remains a policy watch item rather than a post-holiday attack line.
