# Capital Flows and Market Sentiment Daily (2026-05-08)

## Executive Summary

Today's market was characterized by a "tug-of-war" between three primary forces: aggressive foreign buying, domestic institutional profit-taking, and a surge in retail leverage. Northbound capital recorded its 10th consecutive day of net inflows [Source: 163.com 2026-05-08], with a cumulative 10-day total exceeding 45 billion RMB [Source: 163.com 2026-05-08], underscoring foreign dominance in pricing core A-share assets. In contrast, domestic "Main-force" capital saw a net outflow of 31.626 billion RMB [Source: 163.com 2026-05-08], primarily through profit-taking in high-flying "hard tech" sectors such as semiconductors and optical communication. Most notably, the total margin balance has climbed to a significant 2.7864 trillion RMB [Source: stcn.com 2026-05-08], with the financing balance hitting a historical record of 2.7663 trillion RMB [Source: stcn.com 2026-05-08]. This suggests that market risk appetite has been pushed to the extreme by leveraged funds, necessitating caution regarding potential liquidation pressure during high-level volatility.
