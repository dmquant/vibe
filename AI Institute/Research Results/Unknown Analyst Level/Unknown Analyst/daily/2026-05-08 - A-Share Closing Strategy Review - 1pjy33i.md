## Main Drivers

First, robotics had industry data behind it. Cailian Press cited customs data showing that in the first quarter, China exported RMB 11.32 billion of separately listed robot products to 148 countries and regions, while industrial robot exports reached RMB 3.16 billion, up 42% year on year [来源：https://www.cls.cn/detail/2365833]. This helps explain why robotics found bids even on an index-consolidation day.

Second, commercial aerospace and defense moved into an event window. Cailian Press reported that institutions expect the second quarter to potentially begin a wave of reusable-rocket maiden flights and noted upcoming domestic satellite batch tenders [来源：https://www.cls.cn/detail/2365833]. Cailian Press' homepage showed defense up 2.63% [来源：https://www.cls.cn/]. These themes are driven more by order expectations and catalysts than by traditional index weight, which gives them stronger short-term elasticity.

Third, the communications and AI complex split into optical connectivity, 6G and AI applications rather than broad AI-hardware beta. MIIT recently approved trial-frequency use in the 6GHz band for 6G testing [来源：https://www.cls.cn/detail/2365833], while Cailian Press showed communications up 1.86% [来源：https://www.cls.cn/]. This means capital is not abandoning AI; it is moving inside the AI value chain toward lower-position and fresher catalysts.

Fourth, high turnover with limited index progress means positioning matters more than direction. Shanghai and Shenzhen turnover was about RMB 3.05 trillion [来源：东方财富 push2his 2026-05-08 查询], yet STAR 50 fell 2.29% [来源：东方财富 push2his 2026-05-08 查询] while BSE 50 rose 2.24% [来源：东方财富 push2his 2026-05-08 查询]. Capital was clearly being redistributed.

## Institutional Read

The session was a high-turnover market in strong liquidity conditions. The index tape was not weak, but the reward-to-risk for chasing was lower. Turnover remained high, but returns depended on rotation speed and position discipline. Technology growth remains the core opportunity set, but the STAR 50's 2.29% one-day decline [来源：东方财富 push2his 2026-05-08 查询] shows crowded high-level trades now carry a cost. CSI 1000 rising 0.31% [来源：东方财富 push2his 2026-05-08 查询] and BSE 50 rising 2.24% [来源：东方财富 push2his 2026-05-08 查询] show that investors still want risk, but they prefer event-driven, less crowded and higher-elasticity assets.

So the market is not turning systemically bearish. It is moving from index beta to thematic alpha. The strategy focus should shift from calling the index direction to identifying the rotation order among defense aerospace, robotics, optical communications, AI applications, precious metals and shipping.
