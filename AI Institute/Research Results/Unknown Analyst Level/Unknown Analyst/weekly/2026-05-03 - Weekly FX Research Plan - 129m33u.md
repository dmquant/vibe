# Weekly FX Research Plan
**Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)**

## 1. Core Scope
The FX markets this week are driven by the confluence of central bank decisions, China's macro data, and the Labor Day holiday:
- **Apr 29 (Wed): US FOMC Interest Rate Decision.** Assessing the Fed's latest stance on the inflation path and any revisions to rate-cut guidance for the year. This directly determines the Dollar Index (DXY) anchor.
- **Apr 30 (Thu): China Official PMI for April.** The strength of Manufacturing and Non-manufacturing PMIs will validate the domestic recovery momentum, impacting the internal driver for CNY/CNH.
- **May 1 (Fri): US Non-Farm Payrolls (NFP).** Labor market tightness will provide the ultimate verification for the Fed's forward path.
- **May 1 (Fri): China Labor Day Holiday begins.** Domestic markets are closed. Focus on CNH volatility and its deviation from the fixing in a low-liquidity environment.
- **Weekly: JPY Intervention Risks.** Monitoring USD/JPY performance near the 155-160 range and whether the Bank of Japan (BoJ) moves beyond verbal intervention.
- **Cross-border Flows:** Observing Carry Trade rebalancing following the FOMC outcome.

## 2. Key Analytical Questions
1. **How does the Fed's hawk/dove tilt reshape the USD?** Is the Fed signaling "Higher for longer," putting pressure on non-USD currencies?
2. **Do China's April PMIs support CNY stabilization?** If PMIs stay consistently above the 50-mark, can they offset yield differential pressures?
3. **What is the impact of the "Holiday Effect" on CNH?** How do external shocks feedback into CNY expectations via the offshore market while onshore is closed?
4. **Has JPY depreciation hit the "tripwire" for carry trade unwinding?** What stage is the tug-of-war between yield gaps and intervention risks?
5. **Does Eurozone inflation data solidify June rate-cut expectations?** The extent of EUR/USD suppression due to Fed-ECB policy divergence.

## 3. Data Requirements
- **FX Series:** Daily closes for USD/CNY (Midpoint & Spot), USD/CNH, DXY, USD/JPY, EUR/USD.
- **Macro Indicators:** China April Official Mfg/Non-mfg PMIs; US April NFP, Unemployment, Wage Growth; Fed Funds Target Rate.
- **Yield Spreads:** 10Y US Treasury vs. 10Y China Government Bond (CGB) spread.
- **Volatility:** USD/CNH Implied Volatility.
- **Event Logs:** Keywords and takeaways from Fed Chair Powell's press conference.

## 4. Proposed Visualizations
1. **CNY Dynamics:** USD/CNY Fixing vs. USD/CNH spot (highlighting spread widening around the holiday).
2. **G10 FX Performance Heatmap:** Ranking of G10 currency returns against the USD for the week.
3. **Yield Differential vs. FX Correlation:** Overlay of 10Y spread changes vs. USD/CNY price action.
4. **PMI Trend Matrix:** 6-month trend comparison of China's Manufacturing vs. Non-manufacturing PMIs.

## 5. Report Outline
- **I. Executive Summary:** Core weekly narrative (Hawkish Fed, solid data, holiday closure).
- **II. The Macro Anchor:** USD pricing logic post-FOMC.
- **III. CNY Observation:** PMI quality and offshore market dynamics during the holiday.
- **IV. Regional Focus:** JPY intervention clouds and Euro policy divergence.
- **V. Risk Outlook:** Potential volatility risks as markets return post-holiday.
