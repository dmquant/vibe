1.  **CXO Resilience Assessment**: Has the Q1 order funnel for top CXO firms shown substantial contraction due to US policy risks? Can non-US market growth effectively hedge potential losses?

3.  **VBP Expansion Risks**: Predicted impact of 10th batch rumors on the profit margins of medical device leaders (e.g., intervention and orthopedic segments).
4.  **Innovative Drug Monetization**: Does the frequency of out-licensing deals indicate that domestic Biotech valuations have bottomed out, or that Global Big Pharma reliance on Chinese assets is increasing?
