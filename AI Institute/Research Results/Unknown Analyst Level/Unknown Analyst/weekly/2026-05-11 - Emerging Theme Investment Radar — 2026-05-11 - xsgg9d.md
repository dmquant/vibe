## 2. Theme Deep Dives

### 2.1 Low-Altitude Economy / eVTOL — **Overweight**

**Thesis**: CAAC completed the type/production certification template through EH216-S and AE200 in 2025. In 2026H2, Shenzhen, Hefei and Guangzhou enter the transition window from "manned demonstration" to "scheduled commercial routes," opening dual revenue streams (B2G municipal contracts + B2C short-range tourism). For the first time the theme has a measurable revenue line — not just MOUs.

**Preferred exposures**:
- OEMs: EHang (EH); A-share supply chain in composites, motors, flight control.
- Infrastructure: Vertiport builders + low-altitude ATC system vendors — the under-appreciated "picks-and-shovels" layer.
- Batteries: high energy-density (>350 Wh/kg) semi-solid-state suppliers.

**Catalysts (12 months)**:
1. 2026Q3: First scheduled manned routes approved in Shenzhen / Hefei.
2. 2026Q4: National standard for low-altitude ATC finalized.
3. 2027Q1: First overseas deliveries (Middle East, SE Asia).

**Key risk**: A single accident can re-rate the entire theme; battery safety redundancy may slow ramp.

---

### 2.2 Biotech: 3rd-gen GLP-1 + AI Pharma — **Overweight**

**Thesis**:
- **3rd-gen GLP-1** (oral small molecule + tri-agonist GLP-1/GIP/GCG) hits its pivotal Phase 3 readout window in 2026H1. If Lilly's retatrutide and Novo's amycretin extend the >24% weight-loss trend seen in mid-2025 interims, expect a second EPS upgrade cycle. China — Hutchmed, Innovent, Hengrui — should see BD/out-licensing value re-rated for me-better assets.
- **AI Pharma**: The Recursion–Exscientia entity, Insilico, and peers now have 4–5 AI-designed candidates in Phase 2. 2026 is the proof-of-concept year for the first "AI-native" molecules; one positive readout would systematically lift platform valuation multiples across the subsector.

**Preferred names**:
- US: LLY, NVO (core), VKTX (second-line obesity), RXRX (AI pharma proxy).
- CN/HK: Innovent (1801.HK), Hengrui, WuXi AppTec (CXO beneficiary of AI-pharma outsourcing demand).

**Catalysts**:
1. 2026Q2-Q3: retatrutide Phase 3 topline.
2. 2026Q3: First AI-designed molecule Phase 2 readout (Insilico ISM001-055 in IPF).
3. 2026Q4: FDA finalizes guidance on AI-assisted drug review.

**Risks**: US — CMS coverage decision for obesity drugs remains the single biggest swing factor. AI Pharma — first Phase 2 failure would trigger a Davis double-kill across the sub-theme.

---

### 2.3 AI — **Neutral, intra-theme rotation**

**Core view**: The training-compute narrative (NVDA H/B foundry chain) has priced in 2027 capacity; marginal upside is narrowing. Capital is rotating from "training" toward **inference + power + monetization**.

**Rotation map**:
1. **Inference silicon + ASIC**: AVGO, MRVL, Groq (private), Cambricon, Hygon. Inference TCO is the 2026-2027 main battleground.
2. **Power + datacenter**: CEG, VST, TLN (US nuclear); domestic nuclear operators and UHV grid equipment. AI datacenter power gap projected at 80–120 GW by 2027.
3. **AI monetization**: Shifting from SaaS price-uplift (Microsoft Copilot ARPU) toward Agent-as-a-Service metering. Watch PANW, CRWD on SecOps Agent uptake.

**Underweight**: Pure GPU foundry chain (priced through), pure LLM model companies (no durable moat).

**Catalysts**:
1. NVDA Rubin shipments from 2026Q2 — incremental upside lands.
2. New BIS export-control round mid-2026 (14nm→7nm boundary).
3. Domestic DeepSeek V4 / Qwen 4 closing on GPT-5-class capability, fueling app-layer.

---

### 2.4 Space Economy — **Neutral**

**Thesis**: SpaceX Starship V3 completed its first fully reusable mission (booster + ship recovery) in 2026Q1. Theoretical $/kg-to-orbit drops to roughly $200 (vs. Falcon 9 at $1,500) — an industry-economics inflection, but commercial harvesting is 18–24 months out.

**Preferred exposures**:
- **Sat-internet downstream**: Iridium, Globalstar, AST SpaceMobile (D2D), and A-share names tied to China Satellite Network Group.
- **Launch services**: RKLB (Neutron first flight in 2026H2).
- **Defense + space situational awareness**: AVIO Electronics (CN), Maxar / BlackSky.

**Risks**: Valuation is narrative-sensitive. A single Starship failure could trigger ~20% drawdowns; China's Qianfan/GW constellation may slip on schedule.

---

### 2.5 Quantum Computing — **Watch**

**Core view**: Technical milestones are accelerating (IBM Heron R3 156-qubit at <1e-3 error rate; IonQ Tempo demo in 2026Q4; Google surface-code distance-7 demo). But **commercial revenue remains government/research-funded**. Pure commercial ROI is at least 24–36 months from threshold.

**Suggested approach**: Small thematic sleeve (1–2% NAV) via QTUM ETF or an IONQ/RGTI/QBTS basket — do not concentrate in a single name.

**Key checkpoints**:
1. H2 2026: First "commercially useful" quantum-advantage algorithm in materials/chemistry.
2. Logical-qubit count crossing 100 (currently ~12).

## 3. Cross-Theme Observations

### 3.1 Common variables
- **Power**: AI, quantum, space ground stations, and low-altitude vertiports all need incremental power. Bullish for nuclear operators, grid equipment, and storage.
- **Capex cycle**: 2026 is the peak year for thematic capex AND the inflection year from "promised capex" to "delivered revenue." Any theme with <60% revenue-conversion ratio risks systemic de-rating.

### 3.2 Style positioning notes
- Overweight themes (low-altitude, biotech) are mid-cap, high-β growth — strict position sizing required.
- Neutral themes (AI, space) are large-cap, suitable as core holdings with tactical adds/trims around rotation.
- Quantum is currently an option-like position, not a thematic holding.
