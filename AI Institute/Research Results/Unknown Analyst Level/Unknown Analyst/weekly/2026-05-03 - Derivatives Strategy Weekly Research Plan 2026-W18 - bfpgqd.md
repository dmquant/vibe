# Derivatives Strategy Weekly Research Plan (2026-W18)

**Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)**

## 1. Scope
This week, the market was driven by a powerful confluence of macro policy and micro earnings, putting extreme pressure on derivative pricing and volatility regimes.

- **FOMC Meeting (Apr 28-29):** The Fed's interest rate decision and Chair Powell's press conference, setting the tone for the yield curve and cross-asset volatility (MOVE Index).
- **"Super Wednesday" Earnings Wave (Apr 29):** Microsoft (MSFT), Amazon (AMZN), Meta (META), and Alphabet (GOOGL) reporting on the same day, causing severe shocks to Nasdaq-100 (NDX) IV surfaces and Skew.
- **Apple (AAPL) Earnings (Apr 30):** The tech bellwether's impact on S&P 500 (SPX) Gamma distribution and hedging demand for mega-cap weights.
- **US April Employment Report / NFP (May 1):** Macro job data driving a re-pricing of June FOMC rate expectations and fueling 0DTE option volumes.
- **International Labor Day Market Closures (May 1):** Closures in China, HK, and Europe for the "Golden Week" or May Day, leading to thin liquidity during Asian hours and volatility dislocations in China ADRs.
- **Volatility Term Structure Evolution:** The shift between contango and backwardation in VIX futures pre/post-FOMC, and the "Vol Crush" effect following the earnings clusters.

## 2. Key Analytical Questions
1. **How did the FOMC statement alter the VIX futures term structure?** Did we see a mean reversion after front-month volatility was overbid?
2. **Did the simultaneous release of four tech giants' earnings create a "volatility clustering" effect?** How did tail-risk hedging costs evolve post-Wednesday's close?
3. **Where are the post-earnings Gamma Flip levels for AAPL and MSFT?** Is there a sufficient margin of safety for Short Vol strategies?
4. **Following the NFP release, how did Fed Funds futures shift pricing for the June meeting?** Analyze positioning changes in rate-sensitive options (e.g., TLT).
5. **To what extent did international holiday closures suppress global volatility arbitrage flows?** Observe anomalies in CNH and China ADR volatility.

## 3. Data Needs
- **Underlying Indices/Assets:** SPX, NDX, SOX, TLT, AAPL, MSFT, AMZN, META, GOOGL.
- **Volatility Indicators:** VIX, VVIX, SKEW Index, MOVE Index, 1M/3M IV Surface.
- **Option Chain Data:** Open Interest (OI), Volume, and Delta/Gamma exposure (GEX) across major strikes.
- **Macro Pricing:** CME FedWatch rate probabilities, NFP Estimates vs. Actuals.
- **Cross-Border Data:** China-related ETFs (KWEB, FXI) Implied Vol vs. Realized Vol.
