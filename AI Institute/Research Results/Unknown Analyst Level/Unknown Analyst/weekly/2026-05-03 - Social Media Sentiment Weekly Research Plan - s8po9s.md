# Social Media Sentiment Weekly Research Plan

**Coverage window:** 2026-04-27 (Monday) → 2026-05-01 (Friday)  
**Market Status Note:** 2026-05-01 (Friday) is the Labor Day public holiday; Chinese A-shares and Hong Kong markets are expected to be closed. This plan focuses on trading sentiment from April 27 to April 30 and holiday sentiment monitoring on May 1.

## 1. Research Scope
This week represents a critical intersection of the earnings season finale and macro-policy positioning. We will focus on the following 6 material developments:

1. **2025 Annual & 2026 Q1 Earnings Finale (Apr 27-30):** As the end-of-April deadline approaches, both "earnings landmines" and "hidden gems" are concentrated. Monitor Xueqiu's Hot Stock List for spikes in discussion volume due to earnings surprises or misses.
2. **Politburo Meeting Policy Signals (Apr 28):** Market expectations for Q2 economic policy and capital market reforms. Analyze rank fluctuations of "Central-Enterprise Valuation (Zhongtegu)" and "New Quality Productive Forces" on Eastmoney's Popularity Ranking.
3. **DeepSeek V4 Global Developer Conference Effect (Apr 28):** Rumors of architecture updates driving sentiment in domestic AI applications and computing power chains. Observe the latest consensus on AI commercialization under Weibo Finance topics.
4. **Pre-holiday Liquidity & Risk-off Sentiment (Apr 29-30):** Capital reallocation patterns before the long break. Analyze the "Hold Stocks vs. Hold Cash" psychology through investor polls.
5. **Consumption Recovery Expectations (Apr 30-May 1):** Labor Day travel booking data triggering discussions in social services, retail, and Baijiu (liquor) sectors.
6. **Growth vs. Dividend Style Rotation (Weekly):** A comparison of discussion frequency between defensive high-dividend assets and volatile AI-growth assets as the month ends.

## 2. Key Analytical Questions
1. **Policy Sensitivity:** Does the intensity of discussion regarding "Real Estate Support" or "New Quality Productive Forces" from the Politburo meeting translate into buying intent the following day?
2. **Earnings Sentiment:** Among the final three days of disclosures, which stocks show a "Negative Sentiment Reversal" (i.e., rebounding discussions after bad news is priced in) on Xueqiu?
3. **AI Sentiment Sustainability:** Compared to previous hype, is the discussion around DeepSeek V4 leaning towards "Hardcore Tech Evolution" or "Speculative Valuation Gaming"?
4. **Holiday Effect:** How does the ratio of "Frugal Travel" vs. "Revenge Spending" in Weibo trending topics affect expectations for the May consumption sector opening?
5. **Retail Positioning:** Which specific sub-sectors do the newcomers in the Eastmoney Top 10 Popularity Ranking belong to?
