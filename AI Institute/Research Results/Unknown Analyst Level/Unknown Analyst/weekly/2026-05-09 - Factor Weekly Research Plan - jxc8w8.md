## 2. Questions the Weekly Must Answer

1. Was growth/momentum outperformance driven by mega-cap/AI concentration, or did sector and stock breadth improve too?
2. Did the April labor data strengthen the soft-landing case, or was it a mix of headline resilience and weaker internal quality?
3. What is the marginal impact of the oil and gasoline shock on energy, consumer discretionary, transportation, low-margin companies, and low-volatility factors?
4. In earnings season, is the quality factor being rewarded for real margin/cash-flow resilience, or is the market still paying up for growth?
5. Why did value lag growth: sector allocation, the rate/oil mix, or weaker earnings revisions?
6. Is lower or stable volatility credible, or are index highs and concentrated weights masking tail risk?

1. **One-page conclusions**: Present 5-7 takeaways covering the factor story, key evidence, and next-week risks.
2. **Market and style performance**: Quantify index, sector, style ETF, and equal-weight versus cap-weight moves to answer whether momentum was concentrated.
