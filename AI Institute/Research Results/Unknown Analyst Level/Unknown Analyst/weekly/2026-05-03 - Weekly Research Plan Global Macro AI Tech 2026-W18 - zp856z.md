# Weekly Research Plan: Global Macro & AI Tech (2026-W18)

**本周覆盖范围 / Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)**

## 1. Scope of Coverage

The market this week is driven by three primary narratives: the FOMC meeting, the non-farm payroll (NFP) data, and the AI price war ignited by DeepSeek V4.

1.  **DeepSeek V4 Global Disruption (Cont. April 27):** Following its release on April 24, V4-Pro has pressured OpenAI's GPT-5.5 with a 97% cost advantage. Research will focus on the reshaping of the "inference cost curve" and the valuation shock to the Nasdaq AI sector.
2.  **Fed FOMC Policy Decision (April 28–29):** The Fed held rates steady, but Chair Powell's press conference mentions of "AI-driven total factor productivity (TFP) gains" possibly shifting the long-term neutral rate (R-star).
3.  **Big Tech Earnings Season (April 28–30):** Microsoft (MSFT), Alphabet (GOOGL), and Amazon (AMZN) released Q1 2026 results. Analysis will focus on whether AI Capex ROI is being diluted by the DeepSeek-led price collapse.
4.  **China Official PMI Data (April 30):** The NBS released April Manufacturing PMI. We must assess whether the domestic recovery ahead of the "Golden Week" holiday is strong enough to support an A-share rebound in Q2.
5.  **U.S. April Employment Report / NFP (May 1):** Falling on the first Friday of May, this data will determine the second wave of market re-pricing following the FOMC.
6.  **Labor Day Market Holidays (May 1):** Mainland China, Hong Kong, and Singapore markets are closed; watch for volatility in offshore FX (USD/CNH) due to thin liquidity.

## 2. Key Analytical Questions

1.  **Valuation Anchors in an AI Price War:** Will DeepSeek V4's extreme pricing force OpenAI and Anthropic into a "commoditization" phase earlier than expected? How does this affect hardware demand projections for firms like NVIDIA (NVDA)?
2.  **New Paradigm for Monetary Policy:** Does Powell’s commentary on "AI Productivity" imply the Fed might tolerate higher inflation targets to allow room for economic structural transformation?
3.  **Sustainability of Big Tech Capex:** In an environment of collapsing inference costs, do the hundreds of billions of dollars in data center investments by giants like Microsoft face impairment risks?
4.  **Quality of China's Macro Recovery:** Does the divergence between new export orders and production indices in the April PMI signal a further rise in global protectionism?
