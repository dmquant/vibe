# HK/US Weekly Research Plan (Week 18, 2026)

**Coverage Window:** 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)
**Analyst:** HK/US Strategist
**Market Holiday Note:** Friday, 2026-05-01 is International Labor Day. Hong Kong markets (HKSE) and A-shares are closed; US markets (NYSE/NASDAQ) remain open for regular trading.

## 1. Material Developments

1.  **US Core PCE Price Index Release (May 1):** As the Fed's preferred inflation gauge, this data will set the tone for rate cut expectations at the June FOMC meeting.
2.  **Big Tech Earnings Week (AAPL & AMZN):** Apple and Amazon report this week. The market is hyper-focused on the monetization of AI CAPEX and the progress of AI integration on the hardware side.
3.  **Pre-holiday De-risking in HK Connect (Apr 27 - Apr 30):** Approaching the May Day long weekend, Southbound capital has shown significant profit-taking tendencies. Key focus on flows for Tencent (700.HK) and Meituan (3690.HK).
4.  **Yen Volatility and Carry Trade Unwinding (Apr 28):** With the US-Japan yield gap remaining wide, the Yen hit a psychological threshold again, sparking global asset pricing volatility and shifts in carry trade logic.
5.  **Divergence in AI Compute Sector:** As DeepSeek V4 and other open-source models penetrate global markets, investors are re-evaluating valuation premiums for Nvidia and the high-performance computing chain.
6.  **ADR-HK Premium Narrowing:** With HK markets closing early for the holiday, potential volatility in ADR discounts relative to HK shares is expected during Friday's US session.

## 2. Analytical Questions

1.  **Valuation Validation:** Do the financial results of US Tech giants justify current record-high valuations? Specifically, has the revenue contribution from AI exceeded expectations?
2.  **Policy Outlook:** Does the Core PCE data suggest inflation stickiness? How does this alter market expectations for the "Higher for Longer" interest rate regime?
3.  **Liquidity Shift:** Is the Southbound capital retreat ahead of the holiday a temporary hedge or a sign that the mid-April rebound momentum is exhausted?
4.  **Arbitrage Dynamics:** While HK is closed on Friday, how will ADR price action guide the opening of the HK market next Monday?
