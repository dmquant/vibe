# A-Share Strategist Weekly Research Plan (W1 May 2026)

**Coverage Window: 2026-05-04 (Mon) → 2026-05-08 (Fri)**

## 1. Material Developments
1. **Labor Day Holiday & Post-Holiday Rally (May 4-6)**: The market was closed on Monday and Tuesday for the Labor Day holiday. Trading resumed on Wednesday (May 6) with a massive gap-up, fueled by pent-up sentiment and strong Caixin Manufacturing PMI (52.2).
2. **SHCOMP Hits 11-Year High (May 8)**: The Shanghai Composite Index closed Friday at 4,180 points, its highest level since 2015. This marked the fifth consecutive weekly gain, signaling extreme bullishness.
3. **Margin Balance Surpasses 2.79 Trillion RMB (May 7)**: The total margin balance reached a new nominal historical high, reflecting accelerated entry of leveraged capital. Exchanges have raised minimum margin requirements to 100% to mitigate systemic risks.
4. **Massive Lockup Expiry for Nexchip (May 6)**: Nexchip (688249.SH) saw 819.4 million shares (approx. 25.9B RMB) unlocked on the first trading day. Despite the pressure, the semiconductor sector showed unexpected resilience.
5. **Strait of Hormuz Conflict & Oil Volatility (May 7)**: Escalating tensions in the Middle East pushed Brent oil above $100/barrel briefly, before cooling on rumors of a maritime agreement. Geopolitical risk became the primary driver of intra-week volatility.
6. **Offshore Yuan Breaks 6.8 Level (May 7)**: USD/CNH hit a three-year peak (below 6.8), reflecting strong global demand for Chinese assets but posing currency headwinds for export-oriented sectors.
7. **Low-Altitude Economy Standards (May 1)**: New national standards for drones took effect during the holiday, sparking a limit-up rally in related tech stocks upon trading resumption.
8. **April Trade Data Outperformance (May 9)**: Data released Saturday showed exports growing 14.1% YoY and imports surging 25.3%, significantly beating forecasts and providing a strong fundamental backdrop for the next week.

## 2. Analytical Questions
1. **Leverage Risk Assessment**: With the margin balance at record highs and the index at 4,180, has the market entered a "mad bull" danger zone? How does the current margin-to-market-cap ratio compare to the 2015 peak?
2. **Tech Sector Stress Test**: How does the Nexchip lockup expiry reshape the valuation logic for semiconductors? Is there a "buy the news" opportunity following the massive release?
3. **The Double-Edged Sword of Yuan Strength**: Which sectors (e.g., Aviation, Paper) are major beneficiaries of the sub-6.8 level? Will CNH strength eventually dampen the momentum seen in the April trade data?
4. **Geopolitics & PPI Expectations**: How will the Hormuz standoff affect May PPI forecasts? To what extent will rising energy costs squeeze margins for mid-stream and down-stream manufacturers?
5. **Pre-Summit Positioning**: With the Xi-Trump summit in Beijing scheduled for mid-May, how is the market pricing in expectations regarding trade and export controls?
