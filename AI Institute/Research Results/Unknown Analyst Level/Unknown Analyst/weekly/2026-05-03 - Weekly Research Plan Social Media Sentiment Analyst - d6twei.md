# Weekly Research Plan: Social Media Sentiment Analyst

**Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Monday) → 2026-05-01 (Friday)**

> **Note:** May 1, 2026 (Friday) is the Labor Day holiday in Mainland China; markets are closed, and it is a non-trading day.

---

## 1. Scope
Core events involving social media and market interaction during the final week of April 2026:

1.  **Closing of A-share Annual/Q1 Report Disclosures (April 27 - April 30)**:
    - *What*: Over 2,000 listed companies released their financial results in the last four days.
    - *Why it matters*: Discussion volumes on Xueqiu and Eastmoney reached their annual peak. Focus on the impact of earnings "bombs" (misses) vs. beats on retail sentiment.
2.  **DeepSeek V4 Launch & AI Sector Sentiment (April 28)**:
    - *What*: DeepSeek released its next-generation model, V4, with performance metrics sparking intense debate in tech and investment circles.
    - *Why it matters*: Heat levels for AI/TMT sectors surged on Weibo and Xueqiu. Analyze the sentiment divergence between "domestic computing power" and "AI applications."
3.  **April Official PMI Data Release (April 30)**:
    - *What*: The National Bureau of Statistics released April's Manufacturing and Non-manufacturing PMI.
    - *Why it matters*: Adjustment of social media expectations for Q2 macro recovery. Observe the clash between "fundamentalists" and "sentiment-driven" viewpoints.
4.  **Labor Day Consumption & Tourism Expectations (April 29 - April 30)**:
    - *What*: Booking data showed a surge in outbound and "county-level" (rural) tourism.
    - *Why it matters*: How trending topics on Xiaohongshu and Weibo translate into sentiment premiums for the Tourism and Catering sectors in the secondary market.
5.  **Pre-holiday Effect & Northbound Flow (April 30)**:
    - *What*: Risk-aversion sentiment on the final trading day before the long break.
    - *Why it matters*: Analyze "Cash vs. Stock" polls in Eastmoney's Guba and the actual sentiment behavior.

---

## 2. Key Analytical Questions
1.  **Sentiment Resilience at Earnings End**: During the dense disclosure period, has retail tolerance for poor performance improved compared to previous years? Which sectors saw a "bad news priced in" consensus?
2.  **Reach of DeepSeek V4**: What is the cognitive gap regarding this technical launch between general retail investors (Eastmoney) and professional individual investors (Xueqiu)?
3.  **Macro Data vs. Micro Perception**: Is there a significant decoupling between the prosperity reflected in PMI data and the "perceived temperature" discussed on social media?
4.  **Labor Day Sentiment Front-running**: Have the high-frequency tourism discussions on social media already been fully priced into stock valuations?
