# Healthcare Weekly Research Plan

**Coverage window: 2026-05-04 (Mon) → 2026-05-08 (Fri)**

## 1. Scope
The healthcare sector this week was driven by geopolitical news, policy updates, and major clinical milestones. Below are the 6 key developments:

*   **2026-05-04: Biosecure Act Decoupling Progress Hearing**
    *   **Event:** The US House held a hearing on the implementation progress of the Biosecure Act, assessing the decoupling pace of US-based pharma companies.
    *   **Impact:** WuXi AppTec (603259.SH) showed resilience in overseas order retention, helping repair extremely pessimistic market sentiment regarding the 2032 deadline.
*   **2026-05-05: NMPA Releases AI Drug R&D Guidelines 2.0**
    *   **Event:** China's NMPA published the "Guidelines for AI-Assisted Drug Development (AI-AID) 2.0."
    *   **Impact:** It clarifies standards for data compliance and clinical validation for AI-designed molecules, expected to shorten IND approval timelines by 15-20%.
*   **2026-05-05: Cross-border M&A Transaction**
    *   **Event:** A top 5 global MNC announced a $2 billion upfront payment to acquire a leading domestic ADC startup.
    *   **Impact:** Further validates the global competitiveness of Chinese ADC assets and drove a valuation rebound across the HKEX 18A sector.
*   **2026-05-06: 12th VBP Batch Consultation**
    *   **Event:** The National Healthcare Security Administration (NHSA) internally solicited opinions on the 12th batch of National VBP, involving several biosimilars.
    *   **Impact:** Signals that biological drug procurement is entering deep waters, prompting a re-valuation of profit margins for traditional blockbuster biosimilars.
*   **2026-05-07: Triple Agonist GLP-1 Clinical Data Release**
    *   **Event:** Innovent (01801.HK) released pivotal Phase III data for its GLP-1/GIP/GCG triple agonist.
    *   **Impact:** Superior weight loss results compared to existing dual-target drugs, solidifying its lead in the domestic metabolic disease market.
*   **2026-05-08: Phase II of Medical Equipment Renewal Launched**
    *   **Event:** The NDRC allocated special interest-subsidized funds for the second phase of "Medical Equipment Renewal."
    *   **Impact:** Funding is prioritized for imaging equipment (MRI/CT) upgrades in county-level hospitals, with major domestic leaders like Mindray (300760.SZ) securing large intent orders.

## 2. Questions
The weekly report must answer these 5 core questions:
1.  **Decoupling Pace:** As the 2032 deadline nears, have mid-sized CDMOs gained significant overseas market share under the "China-plus-one" strategy?
2.  **Procurement Pressure:** What is the marginal impact on gross margins for companies affected by the large-scale inclusion of biologicals in the 12th VBP?
3.  **R&D Efficiency:** Are AI-designed ADC drugs achieving a substantial 30% acceleration in Phase I clinical cycles?
4.  **Earnings Support:** What is the expected earnings contribution of the county-level equipment renewal policy to domestic imaging leaders in their 2026 annual reports?
5.  **Valuation Levels:** Does the valuation of the HKEX 18A sector fully reflect the recent frequency of cross-border M&A dividends at current price levels?
