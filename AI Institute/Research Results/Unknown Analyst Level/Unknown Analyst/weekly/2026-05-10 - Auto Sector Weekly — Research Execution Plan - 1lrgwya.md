## 2. Analytical questions — 4–6

1. **Q1 — Demand**: How did April NEV penetration shift vs March MoM? Is YoY growth vs Apr-2025 still double-digit? Did end-market discount intensity ease or re-escalate?
2. **Q2 — Single-name alpha**: Among April deliveries, who beat/missed YoY and MoM, and how does the mix (high vs low-ASP models) explain the dispersion given product-cycle stage?
3. **Q3 — Autonomy**: With L3 pilot expansion in play, how does visibility shift for compute (Orin / Thor / Horizon J6) and LiDAR (Hesai / RoboSense / Seyond) order books? Is Tesla FSD's China push a headwind or a market-education tailwind for domestic NOA names?
4. **Q4 — Overseas**: How do May-window China auto export numbers + EU / Mexico / SE Asia tariff developments adjust the marginal 2026E overseas-profit contribution for BYD, Chery, SAIC, Geely — upgrade or downgrade?
5. **Q5 — Upstream costs**: GPM sensitivity for battery makers (CATL, EVE, Sunwoda) and vertically integrated OEMs (BYD) to weekly lithium moves; permanent-magnet / copper pass-through to motor makers (Inovance, Juyi)?
6. **Q6 — Flows & valuation**: Where does the sector sit on weekly flows and on PE / PB-TTM percentile vs the 2020-onwards range? What style signal (OEM vs parts vs AD) does rotation send?
