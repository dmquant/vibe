# T Research Plan: TMT Sector Weekly

**Coverage window:** 2026-04-27 (Monday) → 2026-05-01 (Friday)

## 1. Scope of Research

The following 5–8 material developments in the TMT sector occurred within this window:

1.  **US Hyperscalers Earnings Season (MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN):** Cloud revenue (Azure, GCP, AWS) growth exceeded expectations, with a significant increase in AI contribution (April 28-30). *Significance: Validating whether AI infrastructure investment has entered a virtuous ROI cycle.*
2.  **NVIDIA Blackwell Capacity Updates:** Supply chain reports indicate improved yields for B200 chips and an upward revision of the 2026-end order outlook (April 27). *Significance: Confirming the sustainability of computing demand and semiconductor sector momentum.*
3.  **Apple (AAPL) Pre-Earnings & AI Smartphone Shipments:** Market expectations for localized Apple Intelligence adaptation in Asian markets hit a breakthrough (April 29). *Significance: Testing if the consumer electronics replacement cycle can be driven by on-device AI.*
4.  **DeepSeek V4 Launch Expectations:** Rumors circulate regarding a next-gen model achieving a generational leap in logical reasoning and cost efficiency (April 30). *Significance: The marginal impact of domestic Chinese LLMs on the global AI ecosystem and compute price-performance reconstruction.*
5.  **Labor Day Market Holidays:** China A-shares, Hong Kong, and several European markets were closed on May 1. *Significance: Adjusting weekly metrics for reduced liquidity and trading days.*
6.  **Post-Intel Earnings Ripple Effects:** Intel Foundry losses narrowed slower than expected (April 27). *Significance: Rebalancing the global foundry landscape and validating TSMC’s competitive moat.*

## 2. Key Analytical Questions

1.  **Has the AI ROI inflection point arrived?** Analyze the relationship between Hyperscaler Capex and AI revenue share to determine the profitability of compute investments.
2.  **Does Edge AI have a "killer app"?** Review channel feedback for AI-native smartphones and PCs to gauge consumer willingness to pay.
3.  **Is the semiconductor inventory cycle fully cleared?** Combine earnings data to determine if traditional chip demand is recovering enough to support valuations.
4.  **What is the impact of declining LLM cost curves on commercialization?** Assess how drastic reductions in inference costs are driving penetration in enterprise SaaS.
5.  **How much pressure is the Fed’s macro stance putting on tech valuations?** Analyze the marginal impact of the discount rate on the TMT sector given the week's macro data.
