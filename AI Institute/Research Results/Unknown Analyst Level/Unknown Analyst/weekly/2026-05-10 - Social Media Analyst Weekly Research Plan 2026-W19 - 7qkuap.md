# Social Media Analyst: Weekly Research Plan (2026-W19)

**Coverage window: 2026-05-04 (Monday) → 2026-05-08 (Friday)**

*Note: May 4 and May 5 were public holidays in China (Labor Day). The trading week consists of only 3 sessions (Wednesday to Friday).*

## 1. Scope of Research

The market this week experienced an "extreme release of sentiment" following the long holiday. Turnover volume stabilized around the 3 trillion RMB level, and social media sentiment is currently in a phase of divergence after reaching peak euphoria.

*   **May 6 (Wed) - "Opening Red" & The 3-Trillion New Normal**: The first trading day after the Labor Day holiday saw market turnover exceed 3.2 trillion RMB, with the STAR 50 index surging 5%. Discussions on Xueqiu and Eastmoney focused on whether the "second phase of the bull market" has officially begun.
*   **May 7 (Thu) - DeepSeek's 50 Billion RMB Financing Shock**: Domestic AI giant DeepSeek announced a 50 billion RMB financing round, with founder Liang Wenfeng personally contributing 200 billion. This event triggered deep debates on social platforms regarding AI model commercialization, domestic computing power, and "sovereign pricing power."
*   **May 8 (Fri) - The "Psychiatric Hospital" Viral Story & Storage Chip Retreat**:
    *   **Social Media Viral Point**: A viral post on Xueqiu noted that the "Fifteen Ling Mountain Psychiatric Hospital in Guangfeng District, Shangrao" appeared as the 9th largest shareholder in the Q1 report of Shengtong (002599), ranking above Goldman Sachs. This led to a carnival-like discussion about "retail-style institutions" and "irrational investment."
    *   **Stock Warning**: The storage chip stock "Dapu Micro," which soared 10x in 14 days, showed signs of exhaustion with high volume but stalled price growth. Market discussion shifted from "blind bullishness" to "regulatory expectations."
*   **Sector Rotation**: Capital began attempting to rotate from high-flying AI/Storage sectors to lower-valuation sectors like Humanoid Robotics, Commercial Space, and Satellite Internet.

## 2. Key Analytical Questions

1.  **Turnover Logic**: Is a daily turnover of over 3 trillion RMB sustainable? On social media, is this viewed as "new sideline capital entering" or "extreme leverage of existing capital"?
2.  **DeepSeek Effect**: How does this massive financing round change the valuation models for the domestic AI sector among sophisticated investors on Xueqiu?
3.  **Irrational Sentiment**: How do bizarre viral stories like "Psychiatric Hospital as Top Shareholder" affect retail risk appetite this week? Does it signal a local peak in sentiment?
4.  **Style Switch**: Has the "High-to-Low" rotation from Storage/Computing to Robotics/Space achieved a consensus among financial influencers on Weibo?

*   **Trading Data**: Daily total turnover from May 6 to May 8, and net inflows/outflows of Northbound and Main funds.
*   **Sentiment Indicators**: Top 10 trending stocks on Xueqiu and their weekly changes, interaction volume for the Weibo hashtag #A股开门红#, and Main fund flow rankings on Eastmoney.
