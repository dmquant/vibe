## 2. Analytical Questions

1. **Q1:** Did credit-bond net financing flip positive in the post-holiday week? Which sectors/ratings/regions led or lagged? Was there a cluster of cancelled issues?
2. **Q2:** How did credit spreads, rating spreads, and the LGFV-vs-industrial spread move vs. the last pre-holiday session (2026-04-30) — compression or widening?
3. **Q3:** Were there new defaults / extensions / downgrades this week? What is the sector, region and ownership distribution? Any contagion signals?
4. **Q4:** Entering year three of the LGFV debt-resolution package (clock starting July 2023 Politburo), what is this week's progress on special refinancing bonds and hidden-debt swaps in priority provinces? Is the market re-pricing "platform exit" idiosyncratically?
5. **Q5:** Can property maintain its "benchmark issuer primary + distressed-issuer restructuring" twin-track pattern? Are private-developer spreads tightening further?
6. **Q6:** Under the seasonal-easing assumption for May funding, what is the lead-lag relationship between NCD/DR007 and credit spreads this week? For next week, lean short or long duration; up-in-quality or down-in-quality?
