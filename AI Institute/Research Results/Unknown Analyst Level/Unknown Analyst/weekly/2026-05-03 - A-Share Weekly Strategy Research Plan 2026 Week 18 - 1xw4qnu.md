# A-Share Weekly Strategy Research Plan (2026 Week 18)

**Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)**
*Note: May 1, 2026 (Friday) is the Labor Day holiday; A-share markets are closed.*

## 1. Core Scope
Material developments during the trading window (April 27 - April 30, 2026):

1. **Apr 30: Q1 Earnings Peak** — Over 1,500 companies filed results in the final two sessions. This is critical for sentiment and valuation recalibration across sectors.
2. **Apr 30: April Official PMI Release (50.3)** — Data landed slightly above the expansion threshold, showing manufacturing resilience and easing fears of a Q2 slowdown.
3. **Apr 29: Pre-holiday Margin Deleveraging** — Margin balances saw a seasonal dip as investors lowered leverage to avoid uncertainty over the long break.
4. **Apr 28: Growth Stock Volatility** — Semiconductor stocks led a STAR 50 surge following earnings beats from heavyweights, driving volume in the tech sector.
5. **Apr 27: Lockup Expiry Pressure Test** — Approximately RMB 60bn worth of shares were released this week, concentrated in Healthcare and New Materials, weighing on liquidity.

## 2. Key Analytical Questions
1. **Earnings Inflection:** Following the Q1 finale, is the profit growth inflection point for non-financial A-shares confirmed?
2. **Leverage Sentiment:** Was the pre-holiday margin outflow purely seasonal, or does it signal a shift in the medium-term outlook?
3. **Style Rotation:** Post-earnings, will market style shift from "Earnings-Driven" back to "Theme/Growth-Driven"?
4. **Post-holiday Expectations:** Is the mild PMI recovery sufficient to support a sustained rebound in May?

*   **Lockup Details:** List of companies where upcoming single-day expiries exceed 5% of total share capital.
