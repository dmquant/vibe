# Chief Risk Officer Weekly Research Plan

**Coverage window:** 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)

## 1. Core Scope
The focus this week is on inflation stickiness and the impact of credit tightening on portfolio tail risks:
- **Core PCE Release (May 1):** Data indicates stalled disinflation, driving Treasury yields higher and increasing valuation risks for duration-heavy portfolios.
- **Regional Bank CRE Exposure:** Earnings reports from several regional banks showed increased provisions for Commercial Real Estate loans, triggering movement in credit spreads.
- **Tech Earnings Volatility & Valuation Retracement:** High volatility in Mega-cap Tech (Mag 7) post-earnings has heightened liquidity risks due to extreme positioning crowding.
- **BoJ Policy Signals:** Rising concerns over JPY volatility and the risk of Carry Trade Unwind impacting global liquidity.
- **International Labor Day (May 1):** Closures in Asian and several European markets led to thinner liquidity during US hours, exacerbating end-of-week volatility.

## 2. Key Analytical Questions
- **VaR Volatility Spillover:** Has the volatility in the Tech sector spilled over to the broader market? Is the current portfolio 95% VaR hitting internal warning thresholds?
- **Duration Exposure Stress Test:** What would be the expected drawdown for a balanced portfolio if the 10Y Treasury yield breaks above 4.8%?
- **Liquidity Exhaustion Mapping:** Did bid-ask spreads in credit markets widen abnormally given the Asian market holiday closures?
- **Crowding Risk:** Has the concentration in AI-themed holdings reached a structural reversal tipping point?

## 3. Data Requirements
- **Volatility Indicators:** VIX, VVIX, SKEW curves, and implied volatility surfaces for major indices.
- **Yield Curve:** 10Y-2Y spread movements and 5Y/10Y Breakeven Inflation rates.
- **Credit Spreads:** Weekly spread trends for CDX.NA.IG and CDX.NA.HY.
- **Liquidity Metrics:** Tech concentration index, market depth indicators, and cross-asset correlation matrices.

## 4. Proposed Visualizations
- **Chart 1 (30D Rolling VaR):** Displaying the Value at Risk fluctuations for a 60/40 portfolio over the last 30 days, highlighting this week's extremes.
- **Chart 2 (Yield Curve Shift):** Overlapping the yield curves from last Friday vs. this Friday to visualize duration pressure.
- **Chart 3 (Sector Tail Risk Heatmap):** Weekly Max Drawdown and tail correlation by sector.
- **Chart 4 (CDS Spread Spikes):** Credit Default Swap trends for key sectors (Banking, Tech).

## 5. Report Outline
- **Section 1: Risk Dashboard:** Weekly summary of core risk metrics (VaR, CVaR).
- **Section 2: Macro & Volatility Analysis:** Impact of inflation expectations and interest rate volatility on asset pricing.
- **Section 3: Credit & Liquidity Monitoring:** Focus on banking credit risk and shifts in market liquidity depth.
