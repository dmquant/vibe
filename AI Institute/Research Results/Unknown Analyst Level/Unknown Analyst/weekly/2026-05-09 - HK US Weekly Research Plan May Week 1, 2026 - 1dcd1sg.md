# HK/US Weekly Research Plan: May Week 1, 2026

**Coverage Window: 2026-05-04 (Mon) → 2026-05-08 (Fri)**

## 1. Research Scope
The market witnessed a dramatic shift from "war panic" to "economic resilience." Key developments include:

- **May 4: Geopolitical Escalation (Project Freedom)** — The US launched "Operation Project Freedom" to escort merchant ships through the Strait of Hormuz, leading to naval skirmishes with Iran. Brent crude spiked to $114/barrel.
- **May 5: Hong Kong Q1 Economic Resilience** — The HK government released advance Q1 GDP estimates showing 3.1% YoY growth, a near 5-year high, driven by strong domestic demand and tourism recovery.
- **May 6: HKEX TECH Channel Launch** — The SFC and HKEX officially launched the "Technology Enterprises Channel," featuring confidential filing options to attract global cutting-edge tech firms.
- **May 7: Big Tech Earnings & Ceasefire Hopes** — Amazon (AMZN) reported blockbuster earnings; meanwhile, rumors of Pakistan-mediated ceasefire talks cooled oil prices and restored market sentiment.
- **May 8: US Labor Market "Goldilocks" Surprise** — April Non-Farm Payrolls (NFP) added 115k jobs (vs. 65k expected), while unemployment held steady at 4.3%. The S&P 500 and Nasdaq reached all-time highs on Friday.

## 2. Analytical Questions
- **Q1:** How deeply did the geopolitical energy spike impact HK’s logistics and manufacturing PMI, despite the strong GDP headline?
- **Q2:** Can the new HKEX "TECH Channel" reverse the sluggish IPO market of H1 and serve as the primary growth engine for H2 2026?
- **Q3:** How does the massive NFP beat recalibrate the Fed's rate path for the June meeting? Is the hawkish minority gaining influence?
- **Q4:** What is the underlying logic behind US mega-cap tech (e.g., Amazon, AMD) acting as "safe havens" during this week's volatility?

## 3. Data Requirements
- **Price Series:** Daily Brent/WTI crude prices; intra-week performance comparison of Hang Seng Tech vs. HSI.
- **Macro Indicators:** Detailed breakdown of US April NFP (Manufacturing vs. Services); HK April Manufacturing PMI final reading.
- **Flow Data:** Weekly net inflows of Southbound HK Connect funds; fund flows into US Tech ETFs (QQQ, XLK).
- **Policy Specs:** Detailed listing rules for the HKEX TECH Channel and the current pipeline of confidential filings.

## 4. Proposed Visualizations
- **Chart 1 (Dual-axis Line):** "War & Peace" — The evolving negative correlation between oil prices and US/HK indices.
- **Chart 2 (Bar Chart):** Sector Returns — Performance of various sectors in HK/US markets (Energy & Tech leading; Aviation & Retail lagging).
- **Chart 3 (Trend Chart):** US NFP Actual vs. Forecast (6-month historical comparison).
- **Chart 4 (Flowchart):** The streamlined HKEX "TECH Channel" listing process and its advantages over the Main Board.

## 5. Report Outline
1. **Executive Summary:** The "Whiplash" Week — Recovering from Strait of Hormuz skirmishes to labor market strength.
2. **Geopolitics & Energy:** Risk pricing at $114 oil; why did markets successfully "de-risk" by Friday?
3. **Macro Depth:** Structural concerns behind HK's record GDP growth vs. the dual nature of the US "Goldilocks" jobs data.
4. **Sector Spotlight:** HKEX TECH Channel — A new era for tech financing under institutional innovation.
5. **Strategy & Outlook:** Navigating the intersection of hawkish Fed expectations and geopolitical easing; positioning high-beta tech assets.
