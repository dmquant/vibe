Macro is a headwind for long-duration TMT valuation. The yield curve moved higher across the week, with the 10-year reaching 4.39%, raising the DCF discount-rate burden for expensive AI and software assets. Because AI revenue growth is still strong, the market did not de-rate the whole sector; it favored stocks whose earnings can cover the higher discount-rate pressure.
