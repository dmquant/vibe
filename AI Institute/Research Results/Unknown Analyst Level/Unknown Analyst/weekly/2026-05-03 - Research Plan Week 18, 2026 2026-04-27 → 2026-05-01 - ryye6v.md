# Research Plan: Week 18, 2026 (2026-04-27 → 2026-05-01)

本周覆盖范围 / Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)

## 1. Research Scope

This week focuses on the intersection of peak earnings season and pivotal macroeconomic data releases:

1. **Big Tech Earnings (2026-04-28/29):** Microsoft and Alphabet report Q1 2026 results. The focus is on whether AI Capital Expenditure (CapEx) is translating into accelerated cloud revenue growth.
2. **Core PCE Price Index (2026-05-01):** Release of March Core PCE data. As the Fed's preferred inflation metric, whether YoY growth falls below 2.5% is critical for June rate cut expectations.
3. **Apple Event Fallout & Earnings Preview (2026-04-30):** Market positioning ahead of Apple’s Q1 earnings and updates on its "Edge AI" strategy.
4. **BoJ Rate Decision Impact (2026-04-27):** Currency market reactions to Yen depreciation and potential intervention impacts on global liquidity.
5. **Energy Price Volatility (2026-04-29):** Retreat in Brent Crude prices amid easing Middle East tensions and its marginal impact on inflation expectations.
6. **Sector Rotation (2026-05-01):** Pre-weekend capital migration from the semiconductor sector to defensive plays like Healthcare and Consumer Staples.

## 2. Analytical Questions

1. **AI ROI Inflection Point:** Is the revenue growth for software giants sufficient to justify the massive investments in GPU infrastructure?
2. **Inflation Persistence:** Is the slowdown in the PCE Shelter component offsetting the upward pressure from service-sector wage growth as expected?
3. **Treasury Yield Curve:** How is the 10-year Treasury yield's performance at the 4.5% resistance level squeezing small-cap stocks (IWM)?
4. **Global Semiconductor Value Chain:** Can the leadership in AI-specific chips sustain the premium for the Philadelphia Semiconductor Index (SOX) amid overcapacity in mature nodes?

## 3. Data Requirements

- **Market Data:** Weekly returns and intraday volatility (VIX) for SPX, NDX, SOX, and IWM.
- **Macro Indicators:** March Core PCE (YoY/MoM), Initial Jobless Claims.
- **Corporate Financials:** Revenue growth, AI-related business share, and CapEx guidance for MSFT, GOOGL, and AMZN.
- **Fund Flows:** Weekly flows into global equity and money market funds (via EPFR).
