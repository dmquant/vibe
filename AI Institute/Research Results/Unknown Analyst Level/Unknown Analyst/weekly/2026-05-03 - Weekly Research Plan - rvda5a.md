# Weekly Research Plan
**Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)**

## 1. Core Scope
The week of April 27 to May 1, 2026, represents the most critical intersection of macro policy and corporate earnings for the first half of the year, featuring the FOMC decision, Non-farm Payrolls, and earnings from four of the "Magnificent 7."

- **FOMC Interest Rate Decision (Apr 29)**: Powell's latest stance on inflation and guidance for Q3 rate cut expectations.
- **"Magnificent 7" Earnings (Apr 28-30)**: Microsoft (MSFT), Alphabet (GOOGL), Amazon (AMZN), and Apple (AAPL). Key focus: AI Capital Expenditure (CapEx) and Return on Investment (ROI).
- **US April Non-farm Payrolls (May 1)**: Determining if the labor market is cooling significantly and whether the unemployment rate remains below 4.0%.
- **AI Hardware Chain Reaction (DeepSeek V4)**: Re-evaluating Nvidia (NVDA) and data center supply chain valuations following the enhanced efficiency of DeepSeek V4.
- **China Manufacturing PMI (Apr 30)**: Assessing domestic recovery momentum ahead of the Labor Day holiday.
- **Yen Volatility & Carry Trade Rebalancing**: Potential impacts on global liquidity as the JPY fluctuates in the 155-160 range.

## 2. Key Analytical Questions
1. **Fed Policy Shift**: Did the Fed provide a clear "Higher for Longer" signal in response to sticky inflation data?
2. **AI ROI Inflection Point**: Is cloud growth at Microsoft and Google sufficient to justify the surge in AI infrastructure spending?
3. **Labor Market Resilience**: How did the NFP surprise (or miss) shift interest rate probabilities in the CME FedWatch tool?
4. **Sector Rotation**: Did defensive sectors (Utilities, Staples) outperform Growth, signaling rising risk aversion?

## 3. Data Requirements
- **Macro Indicators**: US April NFP, Unemployment Rate, Average Hourly Earnings, FOMC Policy Statement, China Official PMI.
- **Corporate Financials**: Revenue, EPS, Cloud revenue, and AI CapEx guidance for MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, and AAPL.
- **Market Pricing**: S&P 500 (SPX), Nasdaq 100 (NDX), US 10Y Yield (TNX), USD/JPY Exchange Rate, NVDA weekly performance.
- **Sentiment/Valuation**: CNN Fear & Greed Index, CME FedWatch Probabilities.

- **Chart 3 (Clustered Bar)**: Tech Giants' AI CapEx vs. Free Cash Flow (Trend over the last 3 quarters).
- **Chart 4 (Scatter Plot)**: Market Volatility (VIX) reaction relative to NFP data surprises.
