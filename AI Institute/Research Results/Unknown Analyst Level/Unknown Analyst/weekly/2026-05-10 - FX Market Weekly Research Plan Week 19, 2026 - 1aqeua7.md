# FX Market Weekly Research Plan (Week 19, 2026)

**Coverage window: 2026-05-04 (Mon) → 2026-05-08 (Fri)**

## 1. Key Market Developments (Scope)
During the week of May 4 to May 8, global FX markets were dominated by labor market data and diverging monetary policy narratives:

1.  **US Non-Farm Payrolls (May 8):** The market focus is on the May NFP print. Expectations lean towards a cooling labor market; a weak reading (below 100k) could propel EUR/USD above the 1.1800 resistance level.
2.  **China Labor Day Holiday (May 1–5):** Chinese markets were closed on Monday and Tuesday, reopening on Wednesday (May 6). Saturday (May 9) is an adjusted working day. The onshore/offshore (CNY/CNH) spread post-liquidity return is a key metric.
3.  **RBA Interest Rate Decision (May 5):** The Reserve Bank of Australia is expected to hike rates by 25bps to 4.35% in response to persistent inflation. This has significant implications for AUD crosses and broader APAC sentiment.
4.  **US ISM Services PMI (May 5):** The service sector expansion is expected to slow. The "Prices Paid" sub-index will be scrutinized for its impact on inflation expectations.
5.  **Geopolitical Risks (Strait of Hormuz):** Tensions kept Brent oil prices volatile above $90/bbl, pressuring the JPY (import costs) while providing support for the safe-haven USD.
6.  **BoJ Minutes (May 7):** Disclosure of details from the March meeting. Markets are hunting for hawkish clues regarding the timing of future rate hikes.
7.  **Central Bank Speeches (May 8):** Lagarde, Bailey, and several FOMC members are scheduled to speak, potentially offering the latest guidance on policy paths.

## 2. Analytical Questions (Questions)
1.  **Does NFP support a Fed pivot?** Will the "higher for longer" consensus be challenged by cooling labor demand?
2.  **Where are the pressure points for CNY post-holiday?** How did the USD performance during the holiday translate into the PBoC's fixing on Wednesday?
3.  **Can the JPY find relief in the BoJ Minutes?** Can hawkish signals offset the depreciation pressure stemming from elevated oil prices?
4.  **Is the Carry Trade at a turning point?** Could the combination of an RBA hike and hawkish BoJ signals lead to a deleveraging of yen-funded carry trades?
5.  **What is the "Risk Premium" of the Hormuz situation?** Quantify the contribution of geopolitical factors to the current DXY and USD/CNY premiums.
