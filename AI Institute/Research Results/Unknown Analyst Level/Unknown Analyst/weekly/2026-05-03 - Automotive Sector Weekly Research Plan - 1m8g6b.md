# Automotive Sector Weekly Research Plan
## Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Monday) → 2026-05-01 (Friday)

**Market Notice**: May 1st (Friday) is a Labor Day holiday in China, Hong Kong, and many global markets. A-shares and HK-shares are closed. US markets remain open.

### 1. Scope: Key Developments
This week focuses on the 2026 Beijing Auto Show (Auto China) and April delivery data:

- **Opening of Auto China 2026 (Apr 27)**: Massive debut of all-solid-state battery prototypes, marking a critical generational shift; several OEMs announced mass-production plans for Level 3 autonomous driving.
- **BYD (002594.SZ) Q1 2026 Financials (Apr 28)**: Analyzing the hedge between vehicle margins and overseas profit contributions amidst the ongoing price war.
- **Tesla (TSLA) FSD V13 Testing in China (Apr 29)**: Rumors of key regulatory approvals for end-to-end models, acting as a valuation catalyst for domestic smart-driving players.
- **NEV April Delivery Results (May 1)**: Xiaomi, Li Auto, and AITO compete for the top spot in premium EV/EREV segments; specific focus on conversion rates for Xiaomi SU7 Ultra and 2026 Li Mega.
- **EU Anti-Subsidy Investigation Update (Apr 30)**: Geopolitical uncertainty accelerating localization plans in Eastern Europe (e.g., Hungary) and Southeast Asia.
- **Nvidia (NVDA) Thor Chip Production Timeline (Apr 27)**: Accelerating the evolution of domestic smart-driving platforms toward 1000 TOPS computing power.

### 2. Analytical Questions
1. **What are the "Standard Configuration" trends emerging from Auto China 2026?** (e.g., 800V platforms, semi-solid batteries, end-to-end autonomous driving).
2. **Xiaomi vs. Huawei (AITO/Luxeed): Who won the battle in the 200k-300k RMB segment in April?**
3. **Can BYD maintain its 18% gross margin red line in Q1?** Has the scale effect reached a point of diminishing marginal returns?
4. **Solid-State Batteries: From Prototype to Pilot Production.** Which supply chain companies (electrolytes, anodes) are actually entering Tier 1 systems?
5. **"De-risking" Export Strategies:** Can growth in Southeast Asia and the Middle East offset potential gaps in the European market?

### 3. Data Needs
- **Delivery Data**: April official figures for Li Auto (LI), Nio (NIO), Xpeng (XPEV), Xiaomi (1810.HK), and Seres (601127.SH).
- **Financial Metrics**: BYD Q1 revenue, net profit, gross margin, and overseas sales ratio.
- **Industry Indicators**: China NEV penetration forecast for April, weekly lithium carbonate futures trends.
- **Valuation Comparison**: Historical P/S and P/E percentiles for key OEMs (as of April 30 close).
