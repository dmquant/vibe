## 2. Key analytical questions (4–6) / Questions

1. **Q1 — Did global manufacturing return to expansion in April?** Are ISM, Caixin, HCOB, and au Jibun PMIs directionally aligned, are price sub-indices re-inflating, and what does that mean for industrial pricing power?
2. **Q2 — Is the commercial-aerospace ramp delivering?** Has Boeing 737 MAX run-rate recovered (38? 42?), is 787 back to 7/month, has Airbus held A320 at the 75/month target, and where is the binding supply-chain bottleneck?
3. **Q3 — Was the "FY27 budget + FMS acceleration" thesis for defense validated or weakened this week?** Congressional budget friction, FMS approval cadence, European rearmament orders (Rheinmetall, BAE) vs US primes' relative performance.
4. **Q4 — Are large-cap machinery Q1 guides being raised or cut?** Focus on CAT, DE, PCAR, CMI four scorecards: orders, backlog, price/cost, dealer inventory — split N. America vs international, resource vs construction.
5. **Q5 — Is the "AI electrification" theme still seeing accelerating fund flows?** Compare ETN, VRT, TT, CMI, GNRC, GE Vernova, SMR weekly flows and multiples vs hyperscaler capex prints — measure the expectations gap.
6. **Q6 — What is the marginal direction of US-China tariffs / export controls?** Whether May's new tariff or Entity-List actions hit machine tools, robotics, aerospace, or commercial-vehicle components; how A/H-share industrials reacted on the relative.
