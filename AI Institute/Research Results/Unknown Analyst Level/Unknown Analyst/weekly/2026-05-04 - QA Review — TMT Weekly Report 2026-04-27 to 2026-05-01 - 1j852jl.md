Concrete edits for `03_weekly_report.md` / `03_weekly_report.html`:

US BLS typically publishes April CPI mid-May; verify the actual BLS calendar and unify the report. If the research date is canonical, fix all three locations in the report.

Splitting the table into "Risks (red/yellow)" and "Upside catalysts (green)" would prevent misreading.

Table 3 should add: "Note: percentile is computed from 3 annual snapshots (FY23/24/25) vs current TTM PE; not a daily 3-year distribution. Sample size small — relative position only."

Cloud-margin resilience under heavy AI capex was already partly visible in 2025; soften to "the first quarter where the three companies show this alignment in margin terms" or just "synchronously visible this quarter" to avoid an unnecessary historical dispute.
