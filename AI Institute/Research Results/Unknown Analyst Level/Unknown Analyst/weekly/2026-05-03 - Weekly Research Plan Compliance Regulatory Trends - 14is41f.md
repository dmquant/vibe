# Weekly Research Plan: Compliance & Regulatory Trends
## Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Monday) → 2026-05-01 (Friday)

### 1. Scope
Five material developments impacting market compliance and regulation during this window:

1.  **2026-04-27: SEC Final Rule Proposal on Predictive Data Analytics (PDA)**
    *   **Event**: The SEC advanced rules targeting broker-dealers’ use of AI in investor interactions, mandating the elimination of algorithmic bias.
    *   **Significance**: Forces quant funds and retail trading platforms into unprecedented compliance reviews of black-box models.
2.  **2026-04-28: Landmark "Shadow Trading" Settlement**
    *   **Event**: Regulators settled a case involving trading in a competitor's stock based on Material Non-Public Information (MNPI) about a different company.
    *   **Significance**: Redefines the boundaries of insider trading; multi-strategy funds must urgently re-evaluate their information barrier (Chinese Wall) protocols.
3.  **2026-04-29: ESMA ESG Fund Naming Guidelines Enforcement**
    *   **Event**: The European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA) technical standards for ESG fund naming officially entered the enforcement phase.
    *   **Significance**: Triggers a wave of renamings or asset divestments for European funds to combat "greenwashing" regulatory scrutiny.
4.  **2026-04-30: HK SFC Virtual Asset Trading Platform (VATP) Licensing Deadline**
    *   **Event**: Non-licensed exchanges must cease Hong Kong operations by this date.
    *   **Significance**: Major compliance-driven migration of crypto liquidity in Asia, impacting OTC desk operations.
5.  **2026-05-01: US DOJ Whistleblower Pilot Program Update**
    *   **Event**: The Department of Justice released specific incentives for corporate self-disclosure and financial rewards for whistleblowers (Note: US markets were open while mainland China/HK were closed for Labor Day).
    *   **Significance**: Increases the urgency of internal corporate investigations and reinforces the independence of the Chief Compliance Officer.

### 2. Analytical Questions
1.  How will the latest SEC PDA rules alter the compliance baseline for automated trading systems?
2.  What specific constraints does the "Shadow Trading" judicial interpretation place on information flow management in multi-strategy funds?
3.  What percentage of existing ESG funds face compliance downgrades or capital outflows due to the new ESMA naming standards?
4.  How does the mandatory enforcement of the Hong Kong crypto licensing regime impact regional fintech arbitrage activities?
5.  Will the new DOJ whistleblower incentives trigger a significant wave of corporate self-reporting?
