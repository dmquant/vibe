# Weekly Research Plan: Frontier Tech & Thematic Investing (2026-W18)

**Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)**
*Note: May 1st is International Workers' Day. Major markets in China and Europe are closed, while US markets remain open.*

## 1. Scope
The following 6 material developments occurred in the frontier technology sectors this week:

1. **AI: Official Launch of DeepSeek-V4 (Apr 27)**
   - Event: Released the next-generation reasoning model DeepSeek-V4, achieving SOTA performance in logical reasoning and code generation.
   - Impact: Its extreme inference efficiency (costing 1/10th of GPT-5) will force Silicon Valley giants to re-evaluate their compute-to-value ratios.

2. **Quantum Computing: IBM's Breakthrough in Qubit Error Correction (Apr 28)**
   - Event: IBM announced the successful operation of a new error-correction code on a 2000-qubit processor.
   - Impact: Addressing the core bottleneck of quantum scalability, this marks the countdown to "Quantum Utility."

3. **Biotech: AlphaFold 4-Designed Vaccine Enters Clinical Trials (Apr 29)**
   - Event: The first personalized cancer vaccine designed entirely by Google DeepMind's AlphaFold 4 protein prediction technology entered Phase I trials.
   - Impact: Validates AI's dominance throughout the entire drug discovery and design pipeline.

4. **Space Economy: SpaceX Starship 10th Orbital Test Success (Apr 30)**
   - Event: SpaceX successfully demonstrated stable second-stage recovery.
   - Impact: Drastically reduces the cost of heavy-payload delivery to LEO, paving the way for the late-2026 Mars window.

5. **Web3: Approval of RWA Tokenization Funds in Hong Kong (May 1)**
   - Event: The Hong Kong SFC approved the first batch of Real-World Asset (RWA) tokenized funds for institutional investors.
   - Impact: A critical regulatory step toward merging traditional financial assets (debt/real estate) with on-chain liquidity.

6. **Macro Impact: US Core PCE Data and AI Premia (May 1)**
   - Event: March Core PCE price index rose 2.8% YoY, exceeding expectations.
   - Impact: Rate cut expectations were pushed back again, putting discount-rate pressure on high-valuation tech sectors, particularly AI mid-stream.
