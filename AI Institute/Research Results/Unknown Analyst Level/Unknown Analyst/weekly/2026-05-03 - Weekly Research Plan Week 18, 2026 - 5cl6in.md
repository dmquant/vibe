# Weekly Research Plan: Week 18, 2026
## Coverage Window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)

### 1. Scope
This week was marked by the convergence of the "Triple Threat": Federal Reserve policy meetings, earnings from five Big Tech giants, and critical inflation data. Key developments include:

*   **FOMC Policy Decision (Apr 29):** The Federal Reserve maintained the benchmark rate at 3.50%–3.75%. Chair Jerome Powell delivered a "hawkish pause" message, emphasizing the bumpy path toward the 2% inflation target.
*   **"Super Wednesday" Tech Earnings (Apr 29):** Microsoft (MSFT), Amazon (AMZN), Alphabet (GOOGL), and Meta Platforms (META) reported results simultaneously post-market. Investors shifted focus from AI potential to actual Return on Investment (ROI), sparking significant volatility.
*   **US Q1 GDP & Core PCE Data (Apr 30):** Q1 GDP growth came in at 2.0%, meeting expectations. However, the Core PCE Price Index indicated persistent inflationary pressure, dampening hopes for H2 2026 rate cuts.
*   **International Labor Day Market Closures (May 1):** Major markets in China, Europe, and Hong Kong were closed. US, UK, and Japanese markets remained open, though liquidity dropped significantly in early sessions due to absent international desks.
*   **Geopolitics & Energy Volatility:** Concerns over an Iranian blockade of the Strait of Hormuz pushed oil prices to four-year highs. Chevron (CVX) and ExxonMobil (XOM) Friday earnings reflected strong profits driven by higher energy prices.
*   **Apple (AAPL) Earnings (Apr 30):** Apple’s results showed resilient high-end iPhone demand and record Services revenue, offsetting concerns about slowing hardware growth.
*   **Bank of Japan (BoJ) Policy Stance (Apr 28):** The Yen fluctuated near intervention levels. The BoJ held rates steady but hinted at future tightening, causing a slight unwinding of the Yen carry trade.

### 2. Analytical Questions
*   **Has the AI ROI Inflection Point Arrived?** Analyze the relationship between CapEx and revenue growth for MSFT, META, and GOOGL to determine if AI infrastructure is delivering tangible returns.
*   **What is the Threshold for "Higher for Longer"?** Will rising Core PCE and Employment Cost Index (ECI) figures force the Fed to consider a rate hike in late 2026?
*   **Assessing Second-Round Inflation Risks from Geopolitics:** Evaluate the long-term impact of rising energy costs (due to the Iranian blockade) on Manufacturing PMIs and consumer spending.
*   **The Breadth Disconnect:** Is S&P 500 market breadth deteriorating amid the heavy focus and volatility in Big Tech?
*   **BoJ Policy Shift & Global Liquidity:** Does the risk of Yen repatriation pose a threat to the stability of the US Treasury market?

### 3. Data Requirements
*   **Indices & Equities:** S&P 500, Nasdaq 100, Russell 2000, MSFT, META, GOOGL, AMZN, AAPL, NVDA, XOM, CVX.
*   **Macro Indicators:** US 10Y Yield, DXY Index, Brent Oil Price, US Q1 GDP (Advance), Core PCE YoY/MoM, ISM Manufacturing PMI.
*   **Sentiment & Liquidity:** VIX Index, MOVE Index, S&P 500 Advance-Decline Line, US Treasury Auction Bid-to-Cover Ratios.
*   **Earnings Metrics:** Capital Expenditures (CapEx) for Big Tech, Cloud Revenue Growth, Forward Guidance (Revenue/EPS).
