# FX Strategy Weekly Research Plan

**本周覆盖范围 / Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)**
*Note: 2026-05-01 (Friday) is International Labour Day. Markets in mainland China, Hong Kong, major European countries, and Singapore are closed. US markets remain open.*

## 1. Scope

During the week of April 27 to May 1, 2026, global FX markets are driven by a dense central bank policy calendar and critical data releases:

1.  **FOMC Meeting (April 28-29)**: Focus on the Fed's "higher-for-longer" stance and any shifts in the dot plot. Monitoring DXY's dominance over G10 peers.
2.  **Bank of Japan (BoJ) Policy Decision (April 30)**: Amidst persistent JPY weakness, observing Kazuo Ueda’s rhetoric on policy normalization and potential FX intervention triggers.
3.  **US April Non-Farm Payrolls (May 1)**: Despite global market closures, the NFP report will be the primary driver for Treasury yields and USD direction heading into May.
4.  **Eurozone Q1 GDP & April CPI (April 30)**: This data will dictate the probability of an ECB rate cut in June, directly impacting EUR/USD support levels.
5.  **China Official Manufacturing PMI (April 30)**: Released ahead of the Golden Week holiday, reflecting fundamental support for the CNH.
6.  **Carry Trade Volatility**: Assessing the risk of carry trade unwinding as JPY volatility increases and its spillover effects on emerging market currencies.

## 2. Analytical Questions

1.  **Has the Fed turned Hawkish?** How will the FOMC's characterization of "lack of progress" on inflation affect market pricing for H2 2026 rate cuts?
2.  **Where is the BoJ's Line in the Sand?** Following the breach of key psychological levels in USD/JPY, how will verbal vs. physical intervention impact price action?
3.  **CNY Stability and the "Holiday Effect"**: How will the PBoC use the daily fixing to guide expectations during the thin liquidity of the Labour Day holiday?
4.  **Is the US Labor Market Overheating?** Will wage growth and headline employment figures reverse the USD's mid-April consolidation?

## 3. Data Needs

*   **FX Pairs**: DXY, USD/CNY (Fixing/Spot), USD/CNH, USD/JPY, EUR/USD, CFETS RMB Index.
*   **Interest Rates**: 10Y & 2Y US Treasury Yields, 10Y JGB Yield, US-German Yield Spreads.
*   **Macro Indicators**: US NFP & Unemployment Rate, Eurozone CPI (YoY/MoM), China Official PMIs.
*   **Sentiment**: CFTC Positioning (specifically JPY net shorts), VIX Index.
