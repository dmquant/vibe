# ESG Weekly Research Plan

**Coverage window: 2026-04-27 (Mon) → 2026-05-01 (Fri)**

## 1. Core Research Scope
This week’s market was shaped by the tail end of the Q1 earnings season and significant policy updates. Key developments include:

*   **MEE Releases "2025 Greenhouse Gas Emission Bulletin" (04-27):** Official data confirms China effectively reached a carbon peaking plateau in 2025. *Significance:* Validates the success of energy structure transitions over the past two years and sets the baseline for 2026 quota allocations.
*   **CATL (300750.SZ) Q1 ESG Disclosure (04-28):** For the first time, detailed "Zero-Carbon Factory" coverage and real-time carbon footprint tracking across the entire value chain were released. *Significance:* Establishes a new benchmark for environmental information disclosure in the power battery industry.
*   **HKEX Releases FY2025 ESG Disclosure Compliance Report (04-29):** Notes that over 95% of blue-chip companies have adopted ISSB standards for disclosure. *Significance:* Marks the full alignment of the HK stock market with international ESG disclosure standards.
*   **EU CBAM (Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism) Q2 2026 Rule Update (04-30):** Announced expansion of controlled sectors, potentially including hydrogen and certain downstream metal products. *Significance:* Directly increases compliance costs for Chinese green energy exports to Europe.
*   **China Green Bond Market Q1 Issuance Statistics (04-29):** Total quarterly issuance reached a record high, with a significant increase in Blue Bonds (Ocean ESG). *Significance:* Reflects the diversification trend in green financing demand.
*   **International Labor Day (05-01):** Markets in Mainland China and Hong Kong closed. Non-trading day.

## 2. Key Analytical Questions
1.  **Policy:** Will the MEE’s new bulletin trigger a further tightening of National Carbon Market (CEA) quotas?
2.  **Earnings:** Has the correlation between ESG performance and secondary market valuation premiums for energy transition leaders (e.g., CATL, LONGi) strengthened in 2026?
3.  **International:** What is the estimated impact on the actual profit margins of Chinese hydrogen exporters due to the expansion of EU CBAM?
4.  **Market:** Has there been an inflection point in the inflow of foreign ESG funds following the full shift to ISSB standards in the HK market?
5.  **Innovation:** Does the rising share of Blue Bonds imply that ESG investment is shifting from traditional decarbonization to biodiversity protection?

## 3. Data Requirements
*   **Policy Texts:** Full version of the "2025 Greenhouse Gas Emission Bulletin" and expert interpretations.
*   **Corporate Data:** 2026 Q1 ESG disclosure summaries for CATL, BYD, and LONGi; industry-average carbon intensity metrics.
*   **Market Indicators:** Weekly closing prices and volumes for the National Carbon Market (CEA); weekly returns of CSI ESG indices.
*   **Cross-border Data:** Official European Commission consultation documents on CBAM sector expansion; carbon content estimates for major export products.
*   **Financing Data:** Wind/Bloomberg 2026 Q1 green/blue bond issuance list and coupon rate comparisons.
