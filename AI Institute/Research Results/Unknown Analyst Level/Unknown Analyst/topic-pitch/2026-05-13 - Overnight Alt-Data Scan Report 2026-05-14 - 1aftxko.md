# Overnight Alt-Data Scan Report (2026-05-14)

## 1. Summary
Overnight alt-data scans reveal significant divergences in AI infrastructure construction pace and cross-border e-commerce momentum. Satellite imagery indicates a slowdown in core North American data center nodes, while mobile app data shows the first weekly decline in penetration for top cross-border platforms in US and European markets.

## 2. Divergence Analysis

### A. AI Infrastructure: Power and Transformer Bottlenecks Hitting Physical Progress
- **Signal Sources:** Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) satellite imagery, local utility (Dominion Energy) interconnection queues.
- **Observations:** 
    - Out of 14 hyperscale data centers under construction in Northern Virginia (Loudoun County) and Texas (Dallas), 6 sites have shown stalled progress in exterior HVAC and cooling tower installations over the last 21 days.
    - Median time for local grid interconnection approval has extended from 14 months to 19 months.
- **Divergence from Consensus:** Sell-side consensus assumes "unconstrained growth" in H2 2026 following H200/B200 capacity release. Alt-data suggests the constraint is shifting from silicon to physical power infrastructure, potentially delaying delivery by 1-2 quarters.
- **Key Tickers:** NVDA, VRT, MSFT.

### B. Cross-Border E-commerce: User Penetration Hitting a Local Ceiling
- **Signal Sources:** Mobile App Activity (WAU/MAU), Port of Long Beach AIS vessel tracking.
- **Observations:**
    - Weekly Active Users (WAU) for Temu and SHEIN in the US and German markets declined by 1.8% WoW, the first negative turn since the post-Lunar New Year recovery.
    - Docking frequency of specialized small-parcel carriers at the port has dropped 12%, suggesting a slowdown in the marginal growth of "air-to-sea" strategies.
- **Divergence from Consensus:** Current market pricing reflects a seasonal acceleration into the summer months. Leading indicators (App downloads and activity) suggest Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) has hit a critical peak.
- **Key Tickers:** PDD, BABA.
