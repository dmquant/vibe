## 2. Viral Sentiment & Narrative Spikes
### 🚀 Rocket Lab (RKLB): The "Mini SpaceX" Consensus Leap
*   **Phenomenon:** After dropping 7% intraday, RKLB rallied +4.6% after-hours on strong earnings. WSB is flooded with long-form posts titled "This is our generation's SpaceX."
*   **Core Logic:** Retail attention has moved beyond small-sat Electron launches toward the **Neutron rocket** commercialization and US Space Force contracts.
*   **Divergence:** While institutions are still refining valuation models, social media has begun pricing in a "2030 Space Supremacy" premium.

### 🛒 Bubugao (002251): The "Social Deification" of the Pang Dong Lai Model
*   **Phenomenon:** Ranked #1 on Xueqiu's trending list. Discussions on "how traditional supermarkets can be reborn through Pang Dong Lai-style management" have overshadowed concerns about the broader consumption slowdown.
*   **Narrative Shift:** Markets are ignoring macro data and searching for localized earnings elasticity driven by "extreme service."

## 3. Cross-Platform Divergence
*   **Reddit (RDDT):** Shares dipped 1.6% overnight, but within the Reddit community, users are convinced of the platform's "rent-seeking" status as a primary training corpus for LLMs. User conviction is diverging from short-term price action.
*   **China-Concept AI Compute:** Inspur Information is trending high on Eastmoney, but X (Twitter) discussions focus on supply chain vulnerabilities. This "information cocoon" suggests amplified short-term volatility.
