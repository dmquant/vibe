# Morning Thematic-Rotation Scan
**Date:** May 12, 2026
**Analyst:** Thematic Researcher

## 1. Thematic Basket Relative-Strength Shifts: From "Digital AI" to "Physical AI"
Overnight US markets (May 11) exhibited a clear structural rotation. While the S&P 500 (~7,418) and Nasdaq (~26,291) hit fresh highs, capital is accelerating out of pure-play AI chips/software into the **physical infrastructure** required to sustain the boom.

| Basket/Sector | Overnight Performance | Thematic Logic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **GRID ETF (Smart Grid)** | +2.25% | Grid interconnection delays are now the #1 bottleneck for compute deployment; grid optimization demand is surging. |
| **Liquid Cooling (VRT, MOD)** | Strong Outperformance | NVIDIA Vera Rubin platform power consumption is crossing the 1kW threshold, moving liquid cooling from "optional" to "mandatory." |
| **On-Site Power (Bloom Energy)** | Continued Breakout | Bypassing grid delays via fuel cells for data centers has become the highest-premium logic in the market. |
| **Semiconductors (SOXX)** | +0.15% | While AMD (+18.6%) shone, overall momentum is broadening out toward hardware facilities. |

## 2. Cross-Sector Convergence: The AI-Power-Thermal Nexus
A new correlation logic is forming: **AI Capex ↔ Utilities ↔ Thermal Management**.
*   **Valuation Resonance:** Historically "defensive" Utilities are being re-rated as "growth" assets due to projected data center power demand (166GW by 2030).
*   **Vertiv (VRT) as the Anchor:** With an 11.3% global market share and deep NVIDIA integration, Vertiv is the core health metric for AI's physical base. Its $15B backlog points to a delivery peak in 2H2026.

## 3. Regime-Shift Signal: Geopolitical Stagflation vs. Compute Boom
*   **The Hormuz Crisis:** Brent crude spiked near $103/bbl. Combined with today's expected CPI (est. 3.7%), the market is entering a "Geopolitical Stagflation" risk regime.
*   **Defensive Growth:** Inflows into utilities and the energy grid are not just for AI growth, but act as a natural hedge against energy-driven inflation. This was manifest overnight: as oil rose, grid-related tickers strengthened in tandem.

## 4. Next-Week Positioning & Follow-ups
*   **Overweight:** Physical AI Infrastructure (Liquid Cooling, On-Site Power, Grid Upgrades). Focus on names bypassing the "Interconnection Queue."
*   **Neutral:** Pure AI Application Software. Under inflationary pressure, markets prefer AI chains backed by tangible assets.
*   **Risk Warning:** If today's CPI exceeds 3.8%, the market may shift from "Growth Rotation" to "Valuation Compression," risking pullbacks in high-multiple semiconductor names.
