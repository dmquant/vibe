# Thematic Rotation Report: May 2026 Morning Scan

## 1. Core Observations & Signals: Shift from "Training" to "Physical Layer"
As of early May 2026, overnight US market activity indicates a clear structural shift: capital is moving from a pure-play GPU compute race toward the "physical skeleton" required to sustain the AI ecosystem.

*   **Energy & Grid (Regime Shift)**: Tech giants (Amazon, Microsoft) are increasingly bypassing traditional utilities to sign long-term direct-supply agreements with Small Modular Reactor (SMR) providers. This marks a fundamental re-rating of utilities from "defensive sectors" to "high-growth AI infrastructure."
*   **Commercial Space (Relative Strength)**: Following the successful debut of Rocket Lab's Neutron rocket and the normalization of Intuitive Machines' lunar navigation nodes, the space economy has matured from a speculative theme into a definitive "Space Infrastructure" sector.
*   **Edge AI & Biotech (Convergence)**: Markets are beginning to reward biotech firms with "AI-native" R&D pipelines. The compute bottleneck is shifting from cloud-based training to edge inference (NPUs) and lab automation.

## 3. Cross-Sector Convergence: AI-Capex & Utilities 2.0
We observe a powerful "spillover effect" from AI capital expenditure (Capex):
*   **Thermal & Grid**: Beyond chips, liquid cooling systems and transformers have emerged as the new supply chain bottlenecks.
*   **Nuclear Renaissance**: The demand for 24/7 zero-carbon baseload power for data centers has positioned the nuclear sector as the "hidden AI beneficiary."
*   **Conclusion**: Capital is no longer just betting on model capabilities; it is betting on who controls the "admission ticket" to the power grid.

## 4. Strategic Positioning & Actionables
*   **Short-term Advice**: Over the next week, focus on utility leaders and transformer manufacturers currently in the early stages of re-rating.
*   **Medium-term Positioning**: Monitor lunar infrastructure providers. Following the stabilization of the IM-2 mission, space-based communications will become the next major commercial narrative after Starlink.
*   **Warning Signals**: Exercise caution regarding legacy SaaS platforms lacking an "Agentic AI" transition; their moats are being eroded by low-cost, highly automated AI-native applications.
