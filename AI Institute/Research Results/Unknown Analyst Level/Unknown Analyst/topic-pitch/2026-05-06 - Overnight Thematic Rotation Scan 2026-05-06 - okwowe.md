# Overnight Thematic Rotation Scan (2026-05-06)

## 1. Theme Rotation Evidence: Structural Shift from "Pure Software" to "Pipes & Power"
Overnight market activity signals a significant shift in capital allocation: investors are rotating out of high-valuation pure-play AI software platforms (highlighted by Palantir's post-earnings drawdown) and into the physical backbone of AI—**Energy and Power Infrastructure**.
- **The Rise of "Atomic Dividends"**: Hyperscalers are evolving beyond cloud service providers into "virtual utilities."
- **Capital Inflows**: Funds are flowing into nuclear operators (CEG, VST) and industrial sectors linked to grid modernization.
- **Conclusion**: Phase 2 of AI investment is firmly established as the "Infrastructure Anchoring Phase."

## 2. Cross-Sector Convergence: AI Capex & The Nuclear Renaissance
The boundaries between AI and Energy are blurring. To secure 24/7 carbon-free power, Big Tech has moved from buying credits to directly funding reactor restarts and Small Modular Reactor (SMR) development.
- **Key Milestones**: Microsoft’s deal with Constellation for the Three Mile Island restart, and Amazon’s "behind-the-meter" deployment at the Susquehanna nuclear plant.
- **Strategic Shift**: Google’s fleet agreement with Kairos Power signifies a pivot from short-term PPAs to long-term equity and cornerstone investments in energy assets.

## 3. Thematic Basket Divergences: Hardware Premiums & Quantum-AI Fusion
- **Foundry 2.0**: The semiconductor sector (TSMC, Intel) is benefiting from advanced packaging demand, outperforming general internet platforms.
- **Quantum-AI Fusion**: Quantum pure-plays (IONQ, QBTS) have rebounded following a Q1 risk-off phase, driven by deeper commercial integration with major cloud platforms (AWS, Azure).
- **Space Economy**: The "Space" theme is increasingly converging with national security, bolstered by progress on the Gemini Space Station and Boeing’s defense contracts.

## 4. Regime-Shift Signals: From "Implicit Subsidies" to "Corporate Responsibility"
A major turning point in May 2026 is the signing of the **Voluntary Taxpayer Protection Pledge**.
- **Impact**: Tech giants must now underwrite the cost of new grid capacity required for data centers, ending the long-standing practice of shifting infrastructure costs to residential consumers.
- **Pricing Shift**: This move will compress hyperscaler margins, forcing AI service pricing into an "energy-sensitive" regime.
