# Overnight Thematic Rotation Scan: Structural Migration from "AI Pure-Plays" to the "AI Physical Layer" (2026-05-08)

## 1. Thematic Landscape: The "Second Wave" of Physical Infrastructure
As of May 2026, global markets are undergoing a profound "rotation within a rotation." The momentum driven by AI software and semiconductors in 2024-2025 is now migrating toward the **physical infrastructure** and **energy systems** required to sustain AI operations.
- **Capital Flows:** Funds are exiting AI growth pure-plays and rotating into utilities, electrical equipment, and grid modernization.
- **Cross-Sector Convergence:** AI compute demand and energy security (constrained by grid capacity and geopolitics) have become deeply coupled, driving the "Compute is Power" trade.

## 2. Structural Drivers: Grid Constraints and Storage Premiums
- **"Bring Your Own Capacity" (BYOC) Strategy:** Data center operators are no longer waiting for utility grid connections (which often face 3-5 year backlogs) and are instead moving to "behind-the-meter" power solutions. Nuclear (specifically SMRs) and natural gas baseload power are the preferred choices.
- **Surge in Storage Demand:** As AI shifts from training to large-scale inference, the need for high-velocity data read/write has made storage hardware (HDD/SSD) the dark horse of 2026.
- **Sovereign Security:** National governments are treating AI-energy clusters as strategic assets, driving capital repatriation and localized development.

- **Catalysts:** Late May Big Tech earnings will confirm the sustainability of infrastructure capex; SpaceX's rumored $2T valuation IPO is a landmark event for the space sector.
- **Core Risk:** Any slowdown in AI capex guidance could lead to significant volatility in the "Power & Infra" sectors, which are currently trading at a premium.
