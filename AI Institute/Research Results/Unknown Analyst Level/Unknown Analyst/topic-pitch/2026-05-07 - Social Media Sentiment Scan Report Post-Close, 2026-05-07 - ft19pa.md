# Social Media Sentiment Scan Report (Post-Close, 2026-05-07)

## 1. Retail Sentiment & Psychological State
- **Euphoria:** Market turnover remained at a high of 3.17 trillion RMB. Cambricon (寒武纪) surged to an all-time high of 1,966 RMB during the session, hailed by Eastmoney retail investors as the "New Stock King," officially replacing traditional blue chips as the primary market sentiment leader.
- **Micro-Capitulation:** A "flash crash" in "Xin Duoduo Concept Stocks" triggered localized panic on Weibo and Xueqiu this afternoon. Influencer Liu Xin (ID: Xin Duoduo) was accused of breaching his "six-month no-sell" promise by offloading shares of Guosheng Tech, causing his known holdings to collapse, with several hitting the 10% limit-down. This marks a significant breakdown in retail trust towards "KOL-led" portfolio models.
- **Disbelief & Debate:** Legendary investor Duan Yongping’s disclosure of swapping his entire China Shenhua position for Pop Mart sparked intense debate on Xueqiu. There is a sharp divide regarding the valuation pivot from "high-dividend value stocks" to "high-growth consumer stocks."

## 2. Narrative Reversals & Post-Close Buzz
- **Morning Narrative:** "Hold and wait for the rise; the main wave has no ceiling."
- **Post-Close Narrative:** "Beware of 'Pig-Butchering' scams; embrace hard-core computing power." Retail investors are shifting from blindly following influencers to seeking stocks with tangible earnings and infrastructure support in the computing power sector.
- **Assetization of Computing Power:** BlackRock CEO Larry Fink’s statement that "computing power will become a new asset class" went viral after hours. Domestic investors are debating whether China will launch an official "Computing Power Trading Platform."
- **AI Media Explosion:** An AI-generated short film, *Huo Qubing*, garnered 500 million views on Weibo with a production cost of only 3,000 RMB, reshaping market expectations for the commercialization logic of "AI + Media."

## 3. Viral Threads & Key Events
- **#XinDuoduoConceptStocksFlashCrash#:** Top 1 on Weibo’s Finance Hot Search. Liu Xin’s broken promise has become a laughingstock, triggering large-scale discussions on regulatory compliance.
- **Duan Yongping’s Xueqiu Update:** Deep-dive into the logic behind his Pop Mart position.
- **Tesla Optimus V3 & State Grid Procurement:** The humanoid robot narrative is shifting from pure AI hype to "real order delivery" logic, following State Grid’s 6.8 billion RMB procurement plan.

## 4. Positioning for Tomorrow’s Open
- **Bearish Momentum:** Small-caps and concept stocks associated with the "Xin Duoduo" incident face massive selling pressure at the open.
- **Bullish Momentum:** 
  - **Computing Power / CPO:** Stimulated by the Nvidia-Corning partnership and Fink’s remarks, capital is highly likely to chase leaders in this sector at the open.
  - **AI + Media:** The media sector has catch-up potential due to the phenomenal reach of *Huo Qubing*.
- **Key Observation:** Pop Mart’s opening performance will determine whether the "Duan Yongping Effect" can drive a valuation recovery for the broader consumer sector.
