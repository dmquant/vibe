Midday sentiment has staged a rare and violent shift from geopolitical panic to policy-pivot positioning. ## 2. Key Decouplings: Neutralizing Geopolitics, Anchoring on the "Fed Put"
*   **Geopolitical Resilience:** Despite the escalation in the Middle East, the U.S.-UK trade breakthrough (hinting at a $10B Boeing order) has provided a tangible economic anchor. Sentiment has pivoted from "energy supply shocks" to "trade-driven growth dividends."
*   **The NFP "Rate Cut Bait":** The 65K payroll addition fell far short of the 178K consensus. Midday flow data indicates hedge funds are liquidating long oil positions and rotating heavily into rate-sensitive Nasdaq-100 names.
*   **The AI Infra "Safe Haven" Effect:** The Nvidia-Corning strategic partnership is being framed as the "Second AI Supercycle." We are seeing a significant "Regime Break" in the sector; AI infrastructure names are no longer tracking broad indices but exhibiting independent, high-conviction resilience.

## 3. Narrative Breakdown: The Ultimate K-Shaped Reckoning
A brutal consensus is hardening midday: **"AI Infrastructure thrives, while the Consumer starves."**
*   **High-End Tech/AI:** Capital is not just staying put; it is accelerating into AI power and connectivity segments (OKLO, NNE profit-taking notwithstanding) as the ultimate hedge against macro instability.
*   **Mass Consumption:** The 29% collapse in Shake Shack (SHAK) and Airbnb’s cautious outlook signal a near-total breakdown in the consumer discretionary narrative. Midday flow tapes show a violent style rotation from cyclical consumption to AI structural growth.

## 4. Strategic Outlook & Pitches
**Core Thesis:** The market is currently walking a tightrope, hoping that Fed-induced liquidity will sufficiently buffer the inflationary shocks of a potential regional war.
