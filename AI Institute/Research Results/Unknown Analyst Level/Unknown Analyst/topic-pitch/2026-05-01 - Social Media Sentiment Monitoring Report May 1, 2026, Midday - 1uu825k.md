While domestic markets are closed, social media activity on Xueqiu, Weibo, and Eastmoney has surged regarding global macro shifts and domestic consumption trends. ## 2. In-depth Topic Analysis

*   **Contradiction Point:** While official media emphasizes an "orderly exit," social media is rife with speculation about a "collective centrifugal move" among oil producers.
*   **Sentiment Metrics:** Strong risk-aversion (Search volume for "Physical Gold" and "Crude Oil Futures" up 400% WoW).

### B. Low-Altitude Economy: From "Concept Showroom" to "Production Floor"

*   **Contradiction Point:** Retail optimism regarding the pace of "Airworthiness Certification" contrasts sharply with industry experts' caution on the actual implementation of "Airspace Management."

### C. The "May Day" Consumption Paradox: "Defensive Tourism" Amid Crowds
