# Pre-Market Thematic Strategy: The Shift from "Compute Idolatry" to "Agentic Realization" (May 5, 2026)

## 1. Overnight Divergence: Infrastructure Fatigue vs. Agentic Resurgence
Overnight tape in US infrastructure (NVDA, AVGO, MU) showed high-volume corrections on "capex peak" anxieties. However, Asian pre-market "Agentic Middleware" and "Edge Intelligence" names (Lenovo, Kingsoft Office, and vertical Agent service providers) are showing unusual resilience.
- **Key Divergence**: The market is pivoting from "Paying for Training" to "Profit from Inference." The ultra-low inference cost benchmarks released by DeepSeek V4 have shattered the consensus that "Compute is the only Moat," pushing capital up the value chain toward applications.

## 2. Thematic Basket Mismatch: Inference Efficiency vs. Training Scale
The "Inference Efficiency" basket is currently priced as a "niche experiment," while the "Training Infrastructure" basket is priced for "perpetual growth."

## 3. Structural Narrative: From "Token Generation" to "Task Completion"
The significance of DeepSeek V4 lies not in parameter count, but in its compression of Chain-of-Thought (CoT) inference costs.
- **The Missed Signal**: The "iPhone 4 moment" for Autonomous Agents has arrived. With inference costs reduced by 90%, Agents can perform multiple self-corrections without financial collapse, meaning "Zero-Touch" Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) has officially entered a viable commercial loop.
