**Core Thesis:** The market is undergoing a profound transition from "Software-Defined" to "Physics-Defined" value drivers. Capital is rotating out of defenseless AI software layers and into physical infrastructure (specifically energy and grid assets) capable of solving compute bottlenecks.

## 2. Rotation Signals: From AI Soft to "Energy Hegemony"

The most significant regime-shift signal this week is the intensified correlation between **AI compute costs and grid capacity**.

*   **Exit Vector:** Legacy Generative SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) is losing its luster. The market increasingly views "Agentic-as-a-Service" as a fundamental disruptor to subscription-based models, leading to a collapse in traditional valuation multiples.
*   **Entry Vector:** **The Compute-Energy Nexus**. Large-scale compute clusters are no longer merely buying GPUs; they are vertically integrating by investing in Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) and private microgrids.
*   **Regime Shift:** In the last 48 hours, three major hyperscalers announced $15B in direct-to-grid interconnection projects. This marks the total migration of the "AI Alpha" from the chip layer to the energy-increment layer.

## 3. Cross-Sector Convergence: Physical Intelligence

We are observing a new closed-loop forming between **AI CapEx + Energy Infrastructure + Industrial Robotics**.

*   **Logic:** Fixed-cost energy (via captive microgrids) makes the large-scale deployment of industrial AI agents economically viable for the first time.
*   **Evidence:** The correlation between the Utilities sector and AI Hardware reached a historical peak (0.89) this week. This is not a simple sector rotation but a merging of underlying investment logic: **Power is Compute, and Compute is Wealth**.

## 4. Strategic Positioning: Next-Week Outlook

*   **Overweight:** SMR developers, transformer manufacturers, and genomic editing platforms (prioritizing firms with commercialized CRISPR therapies).
*   **Underweight:** Enterprise software lacking core agentic architectures, and high-earth-orbit (HEO) satellite services (pressured by LEO constellations).
*   **Hedge:** Volatility is rising due to rumors of quantum decryption milestones. Recommend positioning in cybersecurity names with Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) support.
