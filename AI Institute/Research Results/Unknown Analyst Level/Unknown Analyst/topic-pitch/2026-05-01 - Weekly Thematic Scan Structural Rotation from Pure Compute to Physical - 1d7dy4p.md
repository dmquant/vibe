# Weekly Thematic Scan: Structural Rotation from "Pure Compute" to "Physical AI" & Energy Infrastructure

## 1. Evidence of Rotation: Paradigm Shift in Capital Flows
This week, we observed a significant outflow from "Pure AI Software (SaaS)" and "LLM Wrappers" into "AI Physical Infrastructure."
- **Outflows:** Valuation compression of ~15% in pure software AI names within the Nasdaq 100, reflecting market concerns over "monetization lag" at the application layer.
- **Inflows:** Energy infrastructure (Utilities), Small Modular Reactors (SMRs), and grid upgrade-related names recorded their highest net inflows since 2024. Capital is shifting from "algorithm anxiety" to "power anxiety."

## 2. Cross-sector Convergences: AI Capex + Energy Resilience
AI capital expenditure is merging deeply with energy sovereignty.
- **Key Convergence:** Hyperscalers are evolving from mere "tenants" to "micro-grid operators."
- **Energy Hedging:** 20-year Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) signed between tech giants (Microsoft, Google, Oracle) and nuclear/utility providers (e.g., Constellation Energy, Vistra) have become the core variable determining AI competitiveness.
- **Impact:** Electricity is no longer an OPEX item; it is a strategic asset.

## 4. Regime-Shift Signals
We are entering the **"Scarcity Migration"** phase:
- **Old Regime:** Compute chips (GPUs) were the primary bottleneck.
- **New Regime:** Power capacity and liquid cooling infrastructure have become the hard constraints for AI scaling.
- **Conclusion:** Recommend rotating positions from "compute chip beneficiaries" toward "low-base energy infrastructure" and "high-efficiency edge computing" for the coming week.
