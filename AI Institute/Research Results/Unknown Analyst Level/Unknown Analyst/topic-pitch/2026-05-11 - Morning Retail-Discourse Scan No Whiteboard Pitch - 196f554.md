**Anchored date: 2026-05-11.** Every reference to “today,” “morning,” “last hour,” or “last 2-3 hours” is resolved against the shell date and the data snapshot around 11:30 +08.

## 1. Priority Conclusion

**No whiteboard pitch.** The morning tape had clear search heat and price confirmation, but no single name met the full bar: retail discussion materially ahead of fundamental coverage, cross-platform diffusion, and enough substance for a multi-analyst debate.

The closest watch item is **Helitai (002217.SZ)**: Baidu Finance’s 11:00 hourly list ranked it No. 3, with 762,000 composite heat, and the stock was +4.66% by midday. But Xueqiu ranked it only No. 1323 by hot posts and No. 1278 by follows, so the signal looks more like localized search/retail-blog circulation than true cross-platform amplification. Public information has already addressed repeated restructuring and compute-capacity questions, and one recent item says there is no official-level major asset restructuring plan; this looks like a recurring low-priced restructuring rumor trade, not a whiteboard topic.

**No pitch on the chip packaging / AI hardware line.** JCET (600584.SH) hit limit-up, ranked No. 6 on the Baidu hourly list, and ranked No. 138 on Xueqiu deal heat, but this theme is already heavily covered by brokers and financial media. Retail is not leading the institution here.

**No pitch on Shanghai Electric (601727.SH).** The stock was +8.24% by midday, ranked No. 4 on the Baidu hourly list, and ranked No. 138 on Xueqiu follows. But the company’s May 9 public earnings-briefing material already discussed industrial software and embodied-intelligence robots, so the narrative is not an information vacuum.
