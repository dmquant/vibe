# Overnight Thematic Scan: Cross-Sector Regime Shifts & Structural Rotations (May 1, 2026)

## 1. Executive Summary: From "Algorithm-Driven" to "Physical Constraints"
Overnight US markets signaled a significant regime shift. While the Nasdaq remained range-bound, internal leadership rotated violently: capital is exiting pure-play "LLM Model Layers" and "SaaS" en masse, rotating into the **AI Physical Infrastructure Trinity** (Grid, Thermal Management, and Nuclear).

A powerful consensus is hardening: the primary bottleneck for AI is no longer just Compute (GPUs), but **Power** and **Thermal Efficiency**.

## 2. Core Thematic Rotations

### A. AI Infrastructure: The "Hard Regime" of Grid, Nuclear, and Thermal
*   **Signal:** Outsized gains in Constellation Energy (CEG) and Vistra (VST) overnight, coupled with a low-volume breakout for Vertiv (VRT) at all-time highs.
*   **Thesis:** "Behind-the-Meter" power generation has become the standard for Hyperscale Data Centers. Accelerated commercialization of SMRs (Small Modular Reactors) is driving a fundamental re-rating of Utilities from defensive assets to "Growth Multiples."
*   **Conclusion:** This is a multi-week structural shift. Recommend overweighting Grid Assets and Liquid Cooling leaders for the upcoming week.

### B. Space Economy: The Rise of Orbital Edge Computing
*   **Signal:** SpaceX Starship V3 successfully achieved high-frequency launch cadence, driving cost-per-kg below a critical threshold. Space-AI names (e.g., BlackSky, Rocket Lab) saw anomalous volume spikes.
*   **Thesis:** Lower launch costs are enabling the deployment of large models directly to LEO (Low Earth Orbit) satellite edges. Data is no longer "downlinked for processing"; insights are generated in orbit. This marks the formal commercial monetization phase of "Orbital Compute."
*   **Conclusion:** The space economy is shifting from "Launch-Driven" to "Data-Driven."

### C. Biotech: The Empirical Phase of Quantum-AI Convergence
*   **Signal:** A major pharma player announced Phase II success for a candidate discovered via Quantum-Enhanced AI.
*   **Thesis:** The AIDD (AI Drug Discovery) hype of the past two years is hitting "Physical Reality" validation. Quantum simulation's edge in protein dynamics is finally translating into actual pipeline success.
