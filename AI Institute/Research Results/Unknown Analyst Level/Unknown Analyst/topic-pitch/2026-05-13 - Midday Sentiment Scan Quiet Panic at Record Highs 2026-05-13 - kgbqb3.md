# Midday Sentiment Scan: "Quiet Panic" at Record Highs (2026-05-13)

## 1. Core Thesis: Regime Break Signal
The market midday session presents a stark "bifurcation." While the S&P 500 and Nasdaq-100 remain near historical highs, internal sentiment has decoupled aggressively from price action. We are witnessing a "quiet build in fear"—the index is buoyed by the "AI 11" heavyweights, but market breadth and options positioning have pivoted sharply toward defensive and bearish regimes.
