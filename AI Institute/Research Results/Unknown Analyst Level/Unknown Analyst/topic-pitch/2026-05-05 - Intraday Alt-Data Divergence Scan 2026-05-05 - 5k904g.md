# Intraday Alt-Data Divergence Scan (2026-05-05)

## Executive Summary
The S&P 500 remains at all-time highs (7,230) today, buoyed by AI-driven earnings optimism. However, alternative data reveals a sharp "K-shaped" deconstruction of consumer behavior beneath the surface, alongside significant intraday divergences in energy and tech infrastructure.

---

## 1. Retail Divergence: Michigan Sentiment vs. Real-Time Foot Traffic (The Trading Down Narrative)
*   **Signal Identification (Divergence):** The University of Michigan Consumer Sentiment Index released intraday dropped to 49.8 (near record lows), yet Placer.ai's real-time mall foot traffic data shows a 1.8% YoY increase, with visit durations up 3.3%.
*   **Alt-Data Evidence:** 
    *   Credit card transaction data shows average ticket sizes at discount retailers (Walmart, Costco, TJX) rose by 12%, while traffic at mid-to-high-end department stores (Nordstrom, Macy's) fell by 7%.
    *   A "counter-selection" pattern is evident: consumers aren't stopping their spending; they are "trading down."
*   **Market Mapping (The Tape):** The market remains broadly cautious on retail, failing to fully price in the traffic resilience of value-oriented retail giants.
*   **Conclusion & Recommendation:** High risk of earnings misses for Discretionary retailers; overweight value-retail blue chips.

## 2. Tech Divergence: AI Stock Record Highs vs. "East-West" Traffic Bottleneck (The Networking Wall)
*   **Signal Identification (Divergence):** The Nasdaq reached new highs led by the Mag-7, but backbone real-time traffic monitoring shows "East-West" traffic (intra-datacenter communication) hitting physical switch limits.
*   **Alt-Data Evidence:** 
    *   Autonomous AI agent traffic now accounts for 20% of total web traffic, growing 8x faster than human-generated traffic.
    *   Satellite monitoring shows power interconnection delays for major North American compute hubs (Ashburn, Northern Virginia) have stretched to 18 months.
*   **Market Mapping (The Tape):** Investors continue to chase LLM algorithm developers while ignoring the physical hard constraints of underlying network connectivity and power capacity.
*   **Conclusion & Recommendation:** Focus on network upgrade providers and distributed compute nodes.

## 3. Energy Divergence: WTI Price Spike vs. Satellite "Shadow Inventory" (The Shadow Buffer)
*   **Signal Identification (Divergence):** WTI crude is holding firm at $91/bbl due to Strait of Hormuz tensions. However, SAR satellite imagery reveals that the global "Shadow Fleet" floating storage has surged to 300 million barrels.
*   **Alt-Data Evidence:** 
    *   Satellite SAR confirms floating tanker counts near Iran and sanctioned regions are at their highest levels since 2024.
    *   While US commercial stocks showed a 6.2M barrel weekly draw, total global inventory (including floating) is actually in surplus.
*   **Market Mapping (The Tape):** Intraday traders are pricing in geopolitical supply disruption risks but overlooking this massive "invisible buffer."
*   **Conclusion & Recommendation:** Any slight de-escalation in geopolitics could trigger a supply dump, crashing oil prices.
