Bottom line: the two topics worth taking into a whiteboard are whether the U.S.-Iran peace trade is already backfiring into an oil/inflation repricing, and whether the crowded AI hardware trade has entered a profit-taking phase. I do not see capitulation, and I do not see enough after-hours evidence for broad euphoria; the cleaner read is rising distrust at record levels.
