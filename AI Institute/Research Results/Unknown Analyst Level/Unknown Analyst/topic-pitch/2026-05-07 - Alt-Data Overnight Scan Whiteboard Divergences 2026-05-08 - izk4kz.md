# Alt-Data Overnight Scan: Whiteboard Divergences (2026-05-08)

## 1. DeepSeek-V4 Adoption: The "Democratization Shock" of Frontier Reasoning
**Data Source:** Global App Download Trends (Business of Apps), API Traffic Cost Metrics, Developer Retention Rates.
**Divergence:** While sell-side analysts maintain high-margin valuation models for US closed-source labs (OpenAI/Anthropic) at $15-20/1M tokens, alt-data shows DeepSeek V4 Pro costs have collapsed to $0.28/1M tokens (a 54x reduction). Meanwhile, its SWE-bench score (85%) has surpassed Claude 4.6.
**Key Takeaways:**
- **Cliff-Edge Drop in Inference Costs:** Global MAUs surpassed 150M in early May 2026, with retention rates >40%, signaling that DeepSeek is not just a "budget alternative" but is capturing the high-value developer market.
- **Valuation Paradigm Shift:** The myth of "pricing power" for closed-source labs is fracturing. Markets have not fully priced in the systemic compression of inference API margins.
**Strategic Focus:** Underweight/hedge high-valuation closed AI exposure; overweight hardware vendors with "edge deployment" capabilities (surging demand for DeepSeek V4 Flash local instances).

## 2. The "Johor Power Cliff": Satellite Thermal Verification
**Data Source:** High-resolution Satellite Thermal Infrared (TIR) Monitoring, Tenaga Nasional Berhad (TNB) Grid Load Indices, Land Use Verification.
**Divergence:** While the secondary market continues to hype the infinite expansion of SE Asia AI compute clusters, satellite data reveals that actual power utilization in core Johor parks (Sedenak/Kulai) is only ~47%, and grid load hit an operational ceiling in early May.
**Key Takeaways:**
- **Regulatory Hard Landing:** The Malaysian government's early-May "AI-First" mandate forces DC power utilization to 85% and halts non-AI data center approvals.
- **Satellite Verification:** Heat signatures from newly built DC clusters are significantly higher than traditional DCs, confirming a shift to GPU-intensive architectures, but this is accelerating grid fragility.
**Strategic Focus:** Bullish only on operators with direct Renewable Energy access (CRESS) protocols (e.g., YTL); avoid speculative DC developers unable to meet the 85% efficiency mandate.
