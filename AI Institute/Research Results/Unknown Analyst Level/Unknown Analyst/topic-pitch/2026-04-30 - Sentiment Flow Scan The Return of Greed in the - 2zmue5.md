## 3. Deep Dive: The VIX P/C Ratio "Blind Spot"
The VIX volume Put-Call ratio has dropped to **0.21**. This extreme low typically implies:
1. Investors have completely abandoned hedging against a volatility spike.
2. Market memory of the 2025 "flash-vol" episodes is fading rapidly.
3. **Crowded Consensus**: When the market stops paying for downside protection, localized liquidity shocks can trigger non-linear, violent reversals.
