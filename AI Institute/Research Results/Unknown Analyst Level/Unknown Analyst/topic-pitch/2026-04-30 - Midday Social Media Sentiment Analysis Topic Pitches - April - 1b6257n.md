## 2. Filtering & Identifying Key Focuses (Filter)

### A. AI Hardware Chain: From "Storytelling" to "Cashing In"
Xueqiu's hot lists are dominated by Optical Modules (e.g., Zhongji Innolight, Eoptolink) and Compute Chips. Q1 reports generally showed net profit growth of 300%-500% YoY, decisively crushing previous market doubts about earnings delivery.
- **Social Narrative:** "The more you fear, the more it rises," "A once-in-a-decade epic mainline."
- **Contention Point:** Overcrowded positioning. Some influential bloggers (V-users) are discussing the risk of a "sell-on-news" stampede after the May Day holiday.

### B. "Market-Cap Delisting" Panic: Survival Crisis for Micro-caps
As the final reports were released, a large number of micro-cap stocks hit the new regulatory delisting red lines.
- **Social Narrative:** "A-shares becoming like U.S. markets," "The Small-Cap Trap."
- **Risk Alert:** There is a retail misunderstanding of the "market cap below 500M RMB" delisting rule. Some fundamentally sound small-caps are facing irrational sell-offs, creating potential "mis-pricing" opportunities.

## 3. Topic Pitches

### Pitch 1: AI Earnings Peak vs. The Start of a Secular Cycle
- **Status:** Computing power chain earnings are spectacular, but valuations are at highs.
- **Proposed Debate:** Is this earnings explosion a short-term pulse from global inventory restocking, or a sign of "AI Infra Localization" entering its second, more profitable phase?

### Pitch 2: The "Liquidity Black Hole" and Institutional Reshaping
- **Status:** Panic from the delisting wave is accelerating capital flight into large-cap, high-dividend names.
- **Proposed Debate:** Has the "de-retailization" of A-shares entered a non-linear acceleration phase?
