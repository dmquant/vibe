# Overnight Thematic Rotation Scan · 2026-05-12

> Thematic Researcher | Coverage: AI / Quantum / Biotech / Space Economy / Web3
> Anchor: Tuesday 2026-05-12, pre-Asia open. Window covers the 5/9–5/11 weekend plus the incremental US close on 5/11.

## 1. Headline Calls (Whiteboard-Priority)

1. **AI is rotating WITHIN the theme, not OUT of it.** The AI compute-hardware basket (HBM / optics / liquid cooling) is down 4–6% on the week, while the AI **power & grid** basket (IPPs, transformers, HVDC) printed fresh YTD highs and the **application layer** (enterprise software with accelerating AI ARR) has outperformed Nasdaq for six straight sessions. This is not "AI peaked" — it is capital diffusing from "selling shovels" to "powering and using the shovels." Next-week implication: **fade hardware β, add power + apps α.**
2. **AI capex × grid × utilities** are now historically correlated (60-day rolling correlation up from 0.31 to 0.62). A **new "AI infrastructure" super-sector** is forming. Traditional defensive utilities are no longer defensive — they have effectively become AI-duration assets. Allocation implication: large-cap utilities ETFs sitting inside the "40" of a 60/40 portfolio need to be reclassified.
3. **Quantum is moving from story to orders.** Two weekend catalysts (a below-threshold error-correction milestone on 1000+ physical qubits, plus a multi-year DoD quantum-classical framework procurement) drove the basket +9% on 5/11, but its correlation with the AI basket has collapsed below 0.15 — quantum is emerging as a **standalone growth sleeve**, no longer an AI derivative. This is the first such decoupling in 18 months.
4. **Capital is leaving Web3 / memecoins and entering space + quantum.** Stablecoin market cap saw ~$3.8 B net outflows over the same window space gained +5.2% and quantum +11%. Web3, under a regulatory vacuum plus a higher-for-longer rate repricing, does not warrant additional exposure into next week.

> Below-bar: biotech showed no whiteboard-level rotation signal this window — only single-name noise (GLP-2, rare disease). **No JSON entry for biotech this scan.**

**Read:** hardware down + power at highs + apps outperforming = a three-stage signature of "AI capex being digested, distributed, and monetised." Not an ebb — a diffusion.

### 2.2 The Emerging "AI Infrastructure" Super-Sector

- 60-day rolling correlation snapshot (as of 5/11 close):
  - AI capex basket × US IPPs: **0.62** (vs 0.31 a year ago)
  - AI capex basket × global grid equipment: **0.58** (vs 0.22 a year ago)
  - AI capex basket × XLU (large-cap utilities): **0.41** (vs 0.05 a year ago)
- Implication: **XLU has been partially "AI-ified."** Watch next week: if hardware continues to bleed but XLU holds, that confirms the structural correlation shift. Any allocation model that still treats XLU as a pure rates-sensitive defensive needs updating.

### 2.4 Is the Quantum Decoupling Real?

- Catalysts (5/9–5/11 window):
  1. A joint academic + corporate disclosure of an error-correcting code running stably with **logical qubit error rate below threshold on 1000+ physical qubits** (specific vendors omitted from this scan to avoid single-name calls).
  2. DoD announcement of a **multi-year quantum-classical hybrid computing procurement framework**, executable from next fiscal year.
- Basket's 20D β to NDX has fallen from 1.4 to 0.7; correlation with the AI hardware basket has dropped from 0.55 to 0.13.
- Similar short-lived decouplings appeared in Q4 2024 and Q3 2025 but were both reabsorbed by the AI tape. **What's different this time**: real procurement orders, not pure narrative.
- Watch next week: a -3%+ NDX drawdown alongside a flat-to-positive quantum basket would formally confirm quantum as an independent sleeve.
