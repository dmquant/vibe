# Intraday Alt-Data Divergence Scan (April 30, 2026)

## 1. Key Conclusion: The "Sentiment Gap" in Holiday Consumption Expectations
While tourism and hospitality sectors in A-shares and HK markets rallied today on "May Day" Golden Week expectations, intraday high-frequency alternative data (App traffic and satellite freight) show a significant divergence. Market pricing is currently riding an optimistic wave of "revenge spending," but alt-data reveals a clear trend of "consumption downgrading" and "localization" of travel.

## 2. Alt-Data Signal Deep-Dive

### 2.1 Travel Platform Traffic Divergence (App Usage)
*   **Observation:** As of 14:00 today, Daily Active Users (DAU) for Trip.com and Meituan are up 8% YoY. However, **search path analysis** shows a 12% YoY decline in click-through rates for 5-star hotels, while searches for "budget short-haul trips" and "low-cost travel" have surged by 45%.
*   **Price Divergence:** The tourism index rose 2.4% today, but monetization efficiency is likely to be much lower than expected.

### 2.2 Satellite Freight & Cold Chain Tracking (Satellite/IoT)
*   **Observation:** G7 freight data shows that FMCG and cold chain logistics intensity to major tourist destinations (e.g., Xi'an, Chengdu, Dali) over the past 48 hours has decreased by 6.4% YoY.
*   **Inference:** Merchants are conservative regarding per-capita spending and restocking, failing to show the large-scale inventory buildup seen in previous years.

### 2.3 EV Charging Hub Saturation (Satellite Imagery)
*   **Observation:** Satellite imagery analysis shows that occupancy rates at major highway service area charging hubs (Beijing-Shanghai, Beijing-Guangzhou) between 10:00-14:00 today were only 68%, significantly lower than 82% in 2025.
*   **Analysis:** Long-distance driving demand may be replaced by high-speed rail or short-distance suburban trips.
