# Intraday Thematic Flow Scan Report (April 30, 2026)

## 1. Narrative Validation: Morning vs. Intraday Flow
*   **Morning Setup:** The market was preoccupied with "CapEx Fatigue" following Meta and Alphabet’s massive $670B spending plans, leading to a consensus that Hyperscalers are facing diminishing marginal returns on AI investment.
*   **Intraday Decoupling:** Capital is not exiting the AI ecosystem but is instead undergoing a **"Compute Spillover & Sovereignty Pivot."** Intraday tape shows a material rotation away from general Hyperscalers toward "Sovereign AI Infrastructure" and "Orbital Compute" themes.

## 2. Thematic Concentration Analysis
Intraday flow is highly concentrated in three "High-Moat" infrastructure sub-themes:

| Theme Label | Key Drivers | Intraday Flow Characteristics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sovereign AI** | National compute stack investments by the UK (£7B) and Middle Eastern states for digital independence. | Inflows into MediaTek (AndTek architecture) and local telecom operators, hedging US Big Tech volatility. |
| **Orbital Compute** | SpaceX IPO prospectus rebranding as an "AI Firm," focusing on space-cooled data centers. | Space tech ETFs (e.g., ARKX) showing relative strength; retail-institutional convergence on "Satellite Compute." |
| **Agentic Memory** | Anthropic's rollout of Managed Agents with long-term persistent memory. | Recovery in edge-computing and specialized memory stocks (Micron, ARM) after morning weakness. |

## 3. Dispersion & Contradictions
*   **Basket vs. Single-Name Dispersion:** While the broad AI Infrastructure basket is suppressed by CapEx fatigue, **SpaceX-proxy suppliers** and **Sovereign Compute contractors** are showing extreme resilience. This indicates a shift from "General AI" toward "Physical Layer Security" and "Energy Efficiency."
*   **Cross-Sector Rotation:** Rapid exit from traditional Software (SaaS) into "Nuclear & Space Power." The morning's "Software Recovery" narrative was invalidated by strong intraday bidding in Small Modular Reactor (SMR) and nuclear-related tickers.

## 4. Strategic Pitches & Key Conclusions
*   **Primary Conclusion:** The 2026 thematic flow has transitioned from an "Algorithm Arms Race" to "Space & Sovereign Defense." Investors are pricing in the "Physicalization of AI."
*   **Pitch 1:** Overweight "Orbital Compute" proxies. The SpaceX IPO narrative is resetting the valuation ceiling for the space economy (shifting benchmarks from 20x to 80x+ sales).
*   **Pitch 2:** Monitor the erosion of general Cloud market share by "Sovereign Compute Stacks." Non-US "Digital Independence" initiatives are likely to cause a structural pullback in Hyperscaler international penetration.
