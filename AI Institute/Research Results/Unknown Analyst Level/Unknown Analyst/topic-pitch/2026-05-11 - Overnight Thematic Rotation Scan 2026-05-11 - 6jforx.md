# Overnight Thematic Rotation Scan (2026-05-11)

## 1. Structural Shift Summary: The Great Migration from "Digital AI" to "Physical AI"
Overnight market action confirms a decisive capital rotation. As the marginal returns from LLM training begin to normalize, global capital is aggressively shifting from **AI Software/Algorithm layers** to **Physical Infrastructure layers**.

- **Power as the Hard Constraint**: Global data center electricity demand is projected to exceed 545 TWh in 2026 (approx. 2% of global load). The market focus has shifted from "parameter counts" to "grid reliability."
- **Emergence of "Reliability Anchors"**: Due to grid interconnection delays, hyperscalers are increasingly bypassing traditional utilities via natural gas peaker plants and SMR (Small Modular Reactor) pre-orders to establish "private energy perimeters."
- **Capex Polarization**: The "Big Five" tech giants' 2026 Capex is expected to surge by 75%, with total spending now eclipsing global investment in oil and gas production.

## 2. Thematic Rotation Matrix

## 3. Cross-Sector Convergence: AI + Natural Gas Reliability Anchors
A critical signal: The approval of 7.7 GW of dedicated natural gas generation in Texas in early 2026 as a "reliability anchor" for data centers. This marks a deep binding between **AI Capex and fossil fuel infrastructure**, diverging from earlier "pure green" expectations. This "Pragmatic Transition" favors mixed-energy utility providers.

## 4. Actionable Thematic Baskets
- **Overweight**: Grid Modernization (Transformers, HVDC), Natural Gas Midstream, Fault-Tolerant Quantum Tech.
- **Neutral**: AI SaaS (monitoring inference cost reduction), SMR Concepts.
- **Underweight**: Legacy Biotech (non-AI integrated), Pure Consumer Hardware.
