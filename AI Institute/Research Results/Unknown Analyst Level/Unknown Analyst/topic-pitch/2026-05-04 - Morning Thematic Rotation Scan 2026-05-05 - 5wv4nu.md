## 2. Regime Shift Signals & Critical Convergences

*   **Pivot from "AI Compute" to "Physical Constraints":** The market is completing a paradigm shift from pure-play chips (e.g., NVIDIA) to AI physical bottlenecks. The correlation between grid utilities and thermal management systems has reached historical highs, reflecting the diffusion of AI Capex into power capacity and cooling efficiency.
*   **Deepening of "The Great Rotation of 2026":** Despite the overnight pullback triggered by geopolitical risk, the relative strength of small-caps (Russell 2000) vs. the S&P 500 remains in an uptrend. Institutional signals suggest that extreme index concentration (38.5%) is forcing active capital into "left-behind" value/growth names.
*   **Return of Geopolitical Volatility:** Reports of conflict in the Strait of Hormuz sent oil prices to $105. This marks a shift in the volatility regime, where risk-off sentiment is beginning to challenge pure growth premiums.

## 3. Cross-Asset Context & Rotation Evidence

*   **Earnings Gap:** Projected 12-month earnings growth for the Russell 2000 stands at 43%, compared to just 11% for the S&P 500. This massive divergence is the core engine driving the current regime shift.
*   **Capital Flows:** Overnight data shows significant active profit-taking in mega-cap tech, while industrials, utilities, and small-cap value showed resilience during the dip (aggressive "buy the dip" activity).
*   **Bond Market Signals:** Despite the flight-to-safety, long-term inflation expectations rose due to the oil spike, limiting the "safety bid" for Treasuries and placing secondary valuation pressure on long-duration tech.

## 4. Thematic Strategy & Positioning

*   **Core Strategy:** Maintain overweight in "AI Phase 2" (Infrastructure & Energy solutions). Incrementally add to small-cap growth on dips, utilizing geopolitical pullbacks to optimize entries.
*   **Key Watchlist:**
    *   **AMD/PLTR:** Earnings this week will serve as a litmus test for AI thematic resilience and valuation support.
    *   **Circle/Stablecoins:** Monitor legislative progress for financial infrastructure re-rating opportunities.
*   **Risk Hedge:** Increase exposure to the Energy basket as a hard hedge against escalating geopolitical conflict.
