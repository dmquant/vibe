# Overnight Sentiment & Fund Flow Scan (2026-05-06)

## 1. Executive Summary: Transition from "Extreme Greed" to "Defensive Accumulation"
Overnight markets exhibited a classic "Turnaround Tuesday" dynamic. Despite Monday’s panic triggered by geopolitical escalations in the Strait of Hormuz, blowout earnings from AI leaders like Palantir helped the Nasdaq break the 28,000 level. However, this surface-level strength masks a volatile shift in underlying sentiment: while the VIX has cooled, the Put-Call Ratio remains stubbornly high (1.18), and credit spreads continue to widen.

## 3. Fund Flow Anomalies
*   **Cryptocurrency:** Bitcoin spot ETFs recorded $532 million in net inflows during the geopolitical spike, reinforcing its "Digital Gold" narrative.
*   **Defense Sector:** ITA and XAR ETFs continue to see incremental inflows as geopolitical premiums become institutionalized in portfolio allocations.
*   **Fixed Income:** Inflow velocity has slowed, with a notable rotation from high-yield (junk) bonds into investment-grade credits.

## 4. Deep-Dive: Whiteboard-Worthy Debates
1.  **The "Hedged-Buying" Paradox:** Why is the Put-Call Ratio failing to mean-revert alongside the VIX? Does this suggest a "covered retreat" or "hedged long" regime where institutions are unwilling to trust the rally?
2.  **Credit Market Warning:** Credit spread widening often precedes a secondary equity leg down. Is the current "AI Miracle" resilient enough to withstand a structural contraction in macro liquidity?
