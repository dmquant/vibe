# 2026-05-14 Retail Sentiment Scan & Topic Pitches (Social Media Analyst)

## 1. Raw Signal Detection

Market sentiment today is extremely bullish, with the Shanghai Composite Index breaking 4,200 points and total turnover remaining at historic highs above 3.2 trillion RMB. Retail discourse is exhibiting a distinct "fundamental detachment," with capital highly concentrated in two core dimensions:

*   **Compute-Power Synergy (算电协同):** **Datang Power (601991)** recorded its 7th consecutive limit-up, becoming the overall market leader. Despite multiple company clarifications that no such projects are currently operational, it remains #1 on Xueqiu's popularity list, with retail investors viewing it as the "ultimate solution for AI energy consumption."
*   **"Jensen Huang Delegation" Rumors:** Rumors are circulating widely on social media that Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang will arrive in Beijing with a U.S. presidential delegation. Stimulated by this, **Industrial Foxconn (601138)** saw a net inflow of over 7 billion RMB from major capital, hitting the limit-up, while the CPO sector surged collectively.
*   **Memory Chip Wealth Effect:** News of the massive wealth jump for the founder of **Demingli (001309)** went viral on Weibo and Xueqiu, driving a limit-up wave across the memory chip sector.

## 2. Info-Vacuum & Gap Analysis

*   **Compute-Power Synergy: Policy-Reality Mismatch.** Retail is trading a "mythological logic" that green energy direct-supply to data centers will solve both computing costs and power bottlenecks. Institutions are still cautiously interpreting the policy details (*Action Plan for Mutual Empowerment of AI and Energy*), while retail has already priced the sector through Datang Power. This frenzy during a "policy vacuum" is a classic case of retail leading.
*   **Jensen Huang Visit: Geopolitical Expectation Shift.** This rumor currently sits in an "information vacuum" with zero official confirmation. The market is pricing in an extremely optimistic expectation of a "loosening in the US-China AI supply chain." If the rumor is confirmed or debunked, it will trigger violent volatility in AI hardware. Most institutional investors are on the sidelines, while active retail has already built positions in CPO and server leaders.

## 3. Momentum & Cross-Platform Verification

*   **Cross-Platform Resonance:**
    *   **Xueqiu:** Discussion focus has shifted from "will the bull market continue" to "who is the next Datang." Influencers are dissecting the technical details of Compute-Power Synergy, with heat spreading from power stocks to equipment (UHV, virtual power plants).
    *   **Eastmoney:** Dragon-Tiger lists show massive participation from top-tier hot money (e.g., Zhang Jianping) in the intersection of computing and green energy, showing clear "leader-huddling" characteristics.
    *   **Acceleration Sign:** In the past 3 hours, searches for "Jensen Huang arrives in Beijing" on Weibo have increased by 450% MoM (minute-on-minute), showing clear social momentum acceleration.

## 4. Pitch & Action Recommendation

### Key Focus: Datang Power's "Limit-Break" Risk & Spreading Logic
Datang Power is entering a sentiment peak. Focus on whether retail capital will rotate to **GCL Energy (协鑫能科)** or the **Power IoT** sector, which have more solid implementation potential, if the leader fails to maintain its limit-up.
