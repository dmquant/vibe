## 2. In-Depth Filtering

### A. DeepSeek $45B Valuation Rumor: Narrative Emergence
- **Social Narrative**: Viral posts claim the "National Big Fund" is leading DeepSeek's first funding round with a valuation hitting $45 billion. Retail sentiment treats this as the ultimate endorsement for domestic compute and LLMs.
- **Info Vacuum**: No official confirmation yet. However, due to price spikes in stocks like Cambricon and SMIC, the market has accepted this as truth. This "believe first, verify later" sentiment creates a risk of a "sell-the-fact" reversal this afternoon.

### B. Zhongjin Lingnan (000060) Retail Rebellion: Info-Vacuum Gap
- **Social Narrative**: On Eastmoney and Xueqiu, minority shareholders are organizing to vote "No" against the majority shareholder's proposal to extend a low-priced private placement.
- **Analysis**: While fundamental analysts focus on mineral valuations, social media is hyper-focused on "governance risk" and "retail rights." This could lead to a rejection of the proposal, causing unexpected price volatility.

### C. Oriental Securities x Shanghai Securities: Thematic Shift
- **Social Narrative**: Oriental Securities surged upon resumption. Social media is now "detective-hunting" for the next mega-merger candidate (e.g., Zheshang, Caitong) based on minor corporate clues.

## 3. Conclusions & Priorities

- **Core Conclusion**: The market is currently "narrative-driven," with social media feedback loops preceding fundamental confirmation. The DeepSeek rumor is the temporary anchor for the AI sector, but valuation risks are being entirely ignored by retail traders.
- **Watchlist Items**:
    1. Potential "flash crashes" or wide swings in AI supply chain stocks if DeepSeek rumors are debunked or tempered this afternoon.
    2. Social media pulse-checks regarding the upcoming shareholder vote at Zhongjin Lingnan.
