## 5. Key Risks and Tracking Signals

1. **Persistence of breadth divergence** — if decliners stay >3,000 for the next 3–5 sessions while the index holds up, probability of a distribution top rises materially.
2. **Northbound streak break** — 9 consecutive net-inflow days is already long; statistically the break probability rises into days 10–12, and the break day often comes with a 1–3% intraday drawdown.
3. **¥3.24 trn turnover durability** — historically, sustained ¥3 trn+ daily turnover is hard to maintain beyond five sessions. Pullbacks on shrinking volume are healthy; breakdowns on shrinking volume are dangerous.
4. **Trump-Xi summit** — once the event lands, "sell-the-news" odds are non-trivial; re-rate after the readout.
5. **Repeat ETF heavy redemption** — if broad-based ETFs see another single-day net redemption of ¥50 bn+, immediately mark the fund-flow sub-score down ≥10 points.
