# Financial Influencer & Institutional Analyst Sentiment Report (May 2026)

## 1. Executive Summary
The market is currently navigating a critical phase of verifying macro policy effectiveness amid diverging sector fundamentals. Institutions generally recommend a "Dividend Core + Growth Elasticity" allocation strategy while maintaining a neutral-to-positive stance.

*   **Dividend Assets:** The most reliable safe haven, particularly in Power, Banking, and Telecom Operators.
*   **AI Compute:** The primary engine for tech growth, focusing on domestic substitutions and global AI infrastructure.
*   **Resource Commodities:** Base metals (Copper, Gold) are at the intersection of global manufacturing recovery and supply disruptions.
*   **Real Estate Policy:** Following the "5.17" policy package, market sentiment has shifted from policy anticipation to verifying actual transaction data.

## 4. Key Investment Themes & Risk Outlook

1.  **"5.17" Real Estate Policy Validation:** The key metric is the sustainability of transaction volume recovery in Tier-1 cities.
2.  **Trade-in Policy:** Auto and Home Appliance sectors are boosted by subsidy details; Q2 retail data is expected to surprise on the upside.
3.  **Global Manufacturing Recovery:** Commodities like Copper and Gold may enter a "Super Cycle" driven by supply constraints.
4.  **AI Compute Iteration:** With NVDA/GOOG capex exceeding expectations, the domestic 800G/1.6T optical module supply chain has the highest certainty.

**Risk Notice:**
*   Persistent overseas inflation leading to a delay in Fed rate cuts.
*   Recovery slope of domestic real estate being shallower than expected, weighing on supply chain rebound.
