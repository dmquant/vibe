## 1. Executive Summary
The TMT sector's institutional positioning has reached a historic high of **32%**, a level consistent with major market peaks in 2015 and 2023. While the "Quality Bull" narrative (Card 01) and earnings inflection (Card 02) remain intact, sentiment and liquidity indicators reveal a growing structural fragility. The convergence of multi-strategy "pod shops" and style-drift from consumer/growth funds has created "invisible crowding." While leverage ratios (margin/turnover) are healthier than in 2015, the extreme concentration in AI hardware and accelerators suggests that any catalyst—be it AI monetization disappointment or geopolitical shocks—could trigger a high-velocity, liquidity-driven unwind.

### 2.1 The "Invisible Crowding" Phenomenon
Our analysis identifies a critical risk: **Invisible Crowding**. Unlike the visible crowding of TMT-themed funds, we see a massive "style drift" from non-tech funds (Consumer, Healthcare) into AI infrastructure (Optical Modules, PCBs, Accelerators). These "tourist" funds lack long-term conviction and represent the first wave of selling in a downturn. Furthermore, multi-strategy platforms ("pod shops") are converging on identical "Domestic AI Substitution" trades (e.g., Cambricon, SMIC), creating a correlated stop-loss trigger.

## 3. Leverage Dynamics: 2026 vs. 2015
While the absolute margin balance has surpassed 2.8 trillion yuan, the **quality** of leverage differs significantly from 2015:
*   **Turnover Coverage:** Margin trading accounts for ~11% of turnover today (vs. 22% in 2015).
*   **Collateral Quality:** Modern leverage is concentrated in "Core AI Assets" with institutional backing, rather than the "Small-cap Garbage" of 2015.
*   **The Risk:** The risk is not a systemic collapse of the lending system, but a **volatility spike**. High-frequency and algorithmic traders are highly sensitive to these concentrated margin positions.

## 4. Fundamental vs. Sentiment Divergence (Stress-Test)
We tested the "DeepSeek V4 + Huawei Ascend" catalyst mentioned by the TMT Analyst. While foreign capital (Northbound) is buying into the "Revenue Verification" phase of these companies, the **retail sentiment index** has hit a 3-year high.
*   **Edge AI:** Sentiment remains healthy; earnings are catching up to expectations.
*   **AI Accelerators:** Trading at 95th percentile of historical valuation. Even with earnings upshoots, the "forward-looking" premium is fully priced.
*   **Stampede Trigger:** A failure of the 15th Five-Year Plan's early AI deployment targets or a 10% miss in AI server gross margins (due to domestic chip transition costs) could trigger a 15-20% sector-wide correction.

## 5. Conclusion & Strategic Stance: "Stress-Test"
We **stress-test** the bullish conclusion of Card 01 and 02. While we agree that the "Quality Bull" has legs, the **TMT sector is now a "Crowded Positive" trade.**
*   **Near-term:** Risk of a "Sell in May" style liquidity squeeze is elevated.
*   **Key Watch:** If TMT trading share hits **45%**, we recommend an immediate tactical deleveraging.
*   **Stance:** Cautious. Avoid "concept-heavy" AI hardware; prefer "Edge AI" with proven cash flow.
