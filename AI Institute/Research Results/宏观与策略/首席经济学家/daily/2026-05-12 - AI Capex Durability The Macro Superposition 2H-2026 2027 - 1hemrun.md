# AI Capex Durability: The Macro Superposition (2H-2026/2027)
**Date:** 2026-05-12
**Analyst:** Chief Economist

## Executive Summary
As of May 2026, the global macro environment is entering a "High Plateau" phase. While the core AI capex cycle led by GOOG/AWS remains robust and largely self-funded via Operating Cash Flow (OCF), the downstream infrastructure chains in A-shares and HK-shares (Optical Modules, PCB, Liquid Cooling) are facing a valuation "pincer movement." This is driven by a Federal Reserve cautious about cutting rates due to AI-led productivity gains raising the neutral rate (R*) and a persistent USD strength that complicates CNY liquidity.

## 1. Fed Trajectory: The "Productivity-Adjusted" Landing
The Federal Reserve has successfully navigated the 2025-26 inflation cycle, but the emergence of AI as a broad-based productivity driver has structurally altered the policy path for 2H-2026 and 2027.

*   **Rate Path:** We expect the Fed to continue its "Measured Normalization." Following the 25bp cut in March 2026, the current Fed Funds Rate stands at 4.75%. Our baseline for 2H-2026 includes two additional 25bp cuts (September, December), reaching 4.25% by year-end. For 2027, we target a terminal rate of **3.75%**, significantly higher than the pre-AI "neutral" of 2.5%.
*   **The R* Factor:** Structural investment in AI data centers (Capex) acts as a persistent demand floor for capital. This "High Investment, High Productivity" regime justifies a higher-for-longer policy stance, which limits the potential for a massive liquidity "re-rating" in tech multiples.

## 2. USD/CNY and Global Liquidity Plumbing
The interest rate differential between the US and China remains the primary friction point for A-share/HK-share AI names.

*   **USD Strength:** With the Fed terminal rate anchored near 4%, the DXY is likely to hold the 102–106 range through 2H-2026. This "Strong Dollar" environment acts as a gravity well for global growth capital, keeping it concentrated in US-domiciled assets (GOOG/AMZN).
*   **USD/CNY Outlook:** We forecast USD/CNY to fluctuate in the **7.25–7.40** corridor. While China's export strength in AI hardware (as detailed in Card 02) provides a fundamental support for the currency, the capital account remains sensitive to the US yield curve steepening.
*   **Liquidity Sensitivity:** The A-share AI infrastructure chain (e.g., Optical Modules, PCB) has high operational leverage but also high "Beta" to global liquidity. A sudden USD spike or a delay in Fed cuts (due to sticky services inflation) would trigger a "Multiple Compression" event, even if the underlying orders from GOOG/AWS remain intact.

## 3. Durability of the AI Infrastructure Trade
The trade identified in Card 01-03—shifting from broad AI beta to GOOG/AWS bottleneck names—is durable but requires a shift in expectation from "Multiple Expansion" to "Earnings Realization."

*   **The Bottleneck Hedge:** Names in the 1.6T optical and M8 CCL space (Card 02) possess unique "Bottleneck Rent." Because GOOG/AWS cannot build their 2026/27 clusters without these components, these firms have higher pricing power than traditional hardware. This makes them more resilient to a macro downturn than generic consumer electronics or legacy server names.
*   **The Valuation Ceiling:** In a 4% Fed Funds environment, A-share leaders (PE 25x-30x) are near their fair value "ceiling." Upside must now be driven by EPS growth (upward revisions) rather than P/E expansion.

## 4. Failure Scenarios and Hedging Strategies
Based on the risks identified in Card 03 (GOOG capex softening or AWS power constraints), we propose the following macro-overlay hedges:

| Risk Scenario | Macro Indicator | Recommended Hedge |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **GOOG 2027 Capex Cut** | Flattening 2s10s US Curve | Long USD/JPY or Long US 2Y Treasury |
| **Global Liquidity Freeze** | USD/CNY > 7.45 | Short HK-listed Tech Beta (Hang Seng Tech Index) |
| **Power/Shell Constraint** | Industrial Metals (Cu) Price Drop | Long Utilities (Clean Energy) / Long VRT (Vertiv) |

**Conclusion:** The AI capex cycle is no longer a "free lunch" liquidity trade. It is a "Cycle of Resilience." Investors should remain overweight the GOOG/AWS supply chain but must utilize the USD/CNY range as a tactical entry/exit signal. A break above 7.40 in USD/CNY should trigger a reduction in high-beta A-share positions, regardless of the order book.
