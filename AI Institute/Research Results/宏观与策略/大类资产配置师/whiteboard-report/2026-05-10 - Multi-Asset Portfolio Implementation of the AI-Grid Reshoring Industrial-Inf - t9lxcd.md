## 1. Thesis

The seven prior cards collapse into one regime statement: **contractualized AI load + 3–5 year transformer/GOES lead times + reshoring tariff floors are a structurally stagflationary mix for the long end of the US curve.** The portfolio response is *not* a reflation trade and *not* a deflation trade — it is a **scarcity + term-premium trade**: long the bottlenecks (equipment OEMs, GOES/copper, contractual-fortress utilities), neutral-to-short duration with curve steepeners, and explicit hedges against the deficit/term-premium leg that the chain itself generates.

Net equity active risk: ~3–4% tracking error; sector concentration is intentional and is the trade.

### 3.2 Rates sleeve — duration & curve

- **Duration:** modified duration of bond sleeve = **0.6× benchmark** (i.e., ~30–40% UW duration).
- **Curve:** **2s10s steepener** sized to ~25 bps DV01 of total portfolio NAV. The chain produces both anchored front-end (Fed cutting toward neutral but neutral is higher) and rising long-end (term premium + supply). 
- **Breakevens:** **long 5y TIPS breakevens** vs. nominal, ~5% NAV exposure. Core-goods PPI feed-through from Card 7 (+70–110 bps annualized) is the cleanest direct hedge.
- **Avoid:** long-duration 30y nominal Treasuries as a "safe-haven" sleeve — they are the *short* leg of this regime, not the hedge.

### 3.3 Commodity sleeve — physical bottlenecks

- **Copper:** **+300 bps** via a blend of FCX equity and LME copper futures (60/40). Card 5 cites a 600 kt deficit in 2026 — the cleanest scarcity expression.
- **Electrical steel (GOES) proxy:** CLF equity is the only liquid public proxy; sized inside the equity sleeve (Section 3.1) to avoid double-counting.
- **Gold:** **+150 bps** as a deficit/term-premium tail hedge. Not a thesis bet; a debasement insurance line.
- **Avoid:** broad BCOM exposure — too much energy and ag weight dilutes the scarcity signal.

### 3.4 Explicit deficit / term-premium hedges

- **Long-end payer swaption strip** (10y10y, 12-month tenor), ~75 bps premium budget. Pays off precisely in the bear-steepening + term-premium-blowout scenario the chain self-generates.
- **USD curve cap** via SOFR options: convex hedge against the Fed being forced to *re-tighten* on sticky core goods.
- **No USD short** — the same forces (higher r*, scarcity premia, capital repatriation to fund reshoring capex) keep USD bid against G10. FX-hedge international equity exposures back to USD.

## 4. What this portfolio is *not* doing

- Not buying broad utilities ETF (XLU) — half the index is affordability-capped (Card 6).
- Not buying broad semis or AI mega-caps — the bottleneck has moved *upstream* of compute (Cards 3, 4, 5); hyperscalers are now the *payers*, not the price-makers.
- Not running a flat-curve / long-duration "growth scare" hedge — the chain explicitly produces the *opposite* curve shape.
- Not shorting USD against EUR/JPY — same regime is USD-supportive.

## 6. Why this is the right *final* synthesis card

The thread has walked the chain end-to-end: macro framework (Card 1) → utility selection (Cards 2, 6) → hyperscaler stress-test (Card 3) → equipment OEM super-cycle (Card 4) → upstream materials (Card 5) → reshoring inflation feedback (Card 7). The asset-allocator card closes the loop by translating *every* prior conclusion into a position with explicit sizing, an explicit funding leg, and explicit invalidation triggers. The portfolio is internally consistent: each leg's payoff scenario is produced by the same underlying chain, and the deficit / term-premium hedge in Section 3.4 specifically insures the one place the chain folds back on itself (utility WACC).

## 7. Confidence

**0.72.** The directional call (scarcity + steepener + selective utilities) rests on physical bottlenecks (transformer/GOES lead times) that are slow-moving and well-evidenced upstream of this card. The main residual uncertainty is the AI-efficiency air-pocket risk (Card 4) — a genuine fat tail that this portfolio cannot fully hedge without dismantling the thesis. Sizing is calibrated to survive a 20–25% drawdown in the OEM sleeve without forced de-grossing.
