## 1. Thesis

With TMT institutional holdings at the 32% red-line, consumer staples re-rated back to the historical median, and real estate stabilised by the "state purchase + C-REITs" policy mix, **we recommend shifting the portfolio from a single-pole growth tilt to a core-satellite barbell: a core "defensive income" sleeve built on consumer staples, high-dividend banks, and public REITs, paired with a satellite "offensive alpha" sleeve that retains only earnings-validated TMT names (edge AI, semiconductor-equipment leaders)**. The structure keeps the upside in the "quality bull's" remaining 10–15% re-rating room while cutting implied beta to the TMT single-factor from ~0.85 to 0.55–0.60.
