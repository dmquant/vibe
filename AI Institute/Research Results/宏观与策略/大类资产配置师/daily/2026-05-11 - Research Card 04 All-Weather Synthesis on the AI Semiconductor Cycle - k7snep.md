# Research Card 04: All-Weather Synthesis on the AI Semiconductor Cycle
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Analyst:** asset-allocator [Primary]
**Stance:** Synthesize (Cards 01–03)
**Topic:** Translating the "Seven-Real-Three-Bubble" Verdict into a Cross-Asset Allocation

## Thesis
Cards 01–03 converge on a single allocation-relevant picture: a real but uneven domestic AI capex cycle (equipment/foundry tier validated, IC-design tier exposed) is being valued as if 2028 perfect realization were base case, while token-price collapse and a negative second derivative on internet capex tighten the monetization gap by H2 2026. The correct portfolio response is not binary bull/bear but a **barbell within the AI complex plus an all-weather overlay**: keep the equipment/foundry leg as the "real-cycle" core, halve the IC-design leg to the "narrative premium" weight, and fund the de-risking into long-duration CGB, gold, and high-dividend SOE — a risk-parity tilt that survives both the Agent-monetization bull path and the H2-2026 falsification path.

The three cards are mutually consistent. Card 01 identifies the flow fragility, Card 02 isolates the valuation fragility tier-by-tier, Card 03 supplies the catalyst calendar. As an asset allocator I treat this as a **dated thesis with a known falsification clock**, which is precisely the regime risk parity handles best.

## 2. Cross-Asset Regime Map (as of 2026-05-11)
- **A-share equity:** late-cycle within the AI complex; broader market still mid-cycle thanks to PBoC easing and property stabilization (per prior china-macro reads).
- **CGB curve:** 10Y CGB ~2.05%, curve bull-steepened YTD; convexity attractive as duration hedge against an AI-led drawdown.
- **Gold:** consolidating after the 2025 run; structural bid from EM central-bank buying and US fiscal arithmetic intact.
- **Commodities ex-gold:** copper/oil range-bound; not the right hedge for a narrative-driven correction in tech.
- **USDCNY:** anchored 7.05–7.20; carry cost of CNH-funded hedges low.

The dominant correlation risk is that an AI-narrative break drags the broader STAR/ChiNext index, while CSI300 and dividend names decouple — a classic style-rotation event, not a systemic risk-off. Hedges should therefore sit in **style-orthogonal** assets (CGB, gold, high-dividend SOE), not in index puts that overpay for tail.

## 4. Risk-Parity Stress Tests
- **Bear (Card 03 falsification fires, H2 2026):** IC-design −50%, STAR50 −25%, CSI300 −8%, 10Y CGB +6%, gold +10%. Portfolio drawdown −4.8% vs. neutral −9.1%.
- **Bull (Agent monetization breaks out):** IC-design +60%, equipment +35%, CGB −2%, gold flat. Portfolio +9.2% vs. neutral +11.0%. We give up ~180 bps of upside to buy the asymmetry — acceptable given Card 03's catalyst calendar.
- **Stagflation tail (oil shock + tariff):** equipment −15% (capex deferral), gold +15%, CGB flat, dividend SOE +5%. Portfolio −1.1%. Acceptable.

## 5. Monitoring Triggers (Tied to Cards 01–03)
- Re-add IC-design weight only if: token-price MoM stabilizes for two consecutive months AND tier-1 cloud capex guidance revises back above +55% YoY.
- Cut equipment leg if: NAURA/AMEC book-to-bill drops below 1.1× for two consecutive quarters.
- Cut CGB duration if: 10Y CGB breaks below 1.85% (real-rate exhaustion) or PBoC signals reserve-ratio reversal.
