*   **Headline PPI (MoM):** **1.4%** (Actual) vs. **0.5%** (Forecast). This was the largest monthly advance since March 2022.

### **EIA Weekly Petroleum Status Report**
*   **Crude Oil Inventories:** Decreased by **4.3 million barrels** (Expected: -2.3M).
*   **Strategic Petroleum Reserve (SPR):** A record weekly withdrawal of **8.6 million barrels** was reported, bringing the reserve to 384.1 million barrels, its lowest level since October 2024.

### **Market Impact**
The "hot" inflation data led to a sharp sell-off in fixed-income markets. The **2-year Treasury yield** climbed back above **4.0%**, and the **10-year yield** pushed toward **4.50%**. Market expectations for near-term Federal Reserve rate cuts have been significantly tempered.
