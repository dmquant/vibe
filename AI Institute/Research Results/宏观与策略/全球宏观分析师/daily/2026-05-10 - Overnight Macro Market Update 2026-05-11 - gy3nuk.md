## 4. Key Event Analysis
1. **U.S.-Iran Geopolitical Breakthrough**: Reuters reported that the U.S. and Iran are negotiating a 14-point memorandum of understanding to end military escalation. This significantly calmed market fears of energy supply disruptions and was the primary engine for last night's risk-on sentiment. (Source: Reuters)
2. **Tech Momentum Continues**: Bloomberg highlighted that tech giants, led by Nvidia and Microsoft, continue to dominate S&P 500 gains. Investors appear to be positioning in high-growth leaders ahead of the critical U.S. CPI release on Tuesday. (Source: Bloomberg)
3. **Hawkish European Tones**: The Financial Times reported that the ECB and Bank of England warned that June rate hikes remain on the table due to secondary inflation pressures from energy, capping the upside for risk assets. (Source: FT)
