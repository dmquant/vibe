# 2026 H2 AI Infrastructure Strategy: Upgrading BYOP & Amorphous to Base Configuration

**Date:** 2026-05-14
**Analyst:** offshore-strategist (HK/US Strategist)
**Stance:** Stress-test (Supportive with structural upgrade)

## 1. Executive Summary: From "Trigger" to "Base"
Following the investigation of GE Vernova (GEV) and Siemens Energy (ENR) 2026 Q2 disclosures, alongside hyperscaler (Microsoft, Meta, Oracle) capital allocation shifts, we recommend upgrading the **9% hedge basket** (5% BYOP Gas + 4% Amorphous) from an "optional trigger" to a **Base Configuration** for the H2 2026 portfolio. 

The core thesis is that the "Grid Bottleneck" is no longer a tactical delay but a structural ceiling. The "Bring Your Own Power" (BYOP) strategy has matured into a multi-gigawatt industrial standard, and the "Amorphous Scramble" is the primary regulatory and efficiency escape hatch for the transformer shortage.

---

## 2. Evidence 1: The "Slot is the new GPU" Narrative (BYOP Gas)
The 2026 Q2 earnings cycle confirms that gas turbine manufacturing slots have become as scarce and strategically vital as AI training chips.

*   **GE Vernova (GEV) & Siemens Energy (ENR):** Both companies reported record-breaking Q2 orders. GEV's management disclosed that turbine slots are now reserved through **2028-2030**, with hyperscalers paying non-refundable deposits to lock in capacity. 
*   **Pricing Power:** New orders in H1 2026 are priced **10-20% higher** on a $/kW basis than Q4 2025. This reflects a shift from "competitive bidding" to "capacity-constrained allocation."
*   **Hyperscaler Commitments:** 
    *   **Microsoft:** Confirmed the **2.5 GW Pecos (Texas)** campus, anchored by a **$7 billion on-site gas plant** (7x GE 7HA turbines). 
    *   **Oracle:** Deploying **1.2 GW of Bloom Energy fuel cells** as an immediate "behind-the-meter" bridge.
*   **Conclusion:** With grid connection wait times reaching 4-7 years in PJM/ERCOT, "On-site Gas" (BYOP) is the only path to 2026/2027 speed-to-market. A 5% weighting is no longer a hedge; it is a core participation in the AI deployment timeline.

---

## 3. Evidence 2: The "Amorphous Scramble" & The 2027 Wall
The 4% Amorphous Alloy hedge addresses the "Transformer Cliff" (160-week lead times) and the "GOES Margin Peak" identified in Cards 03 and 04.

*   **Regulatory Catalyst:** The **2027 DOE Efficiency Standards** act as a "hard wall." For 2026 H2, all new data center specs are shifting to Amorphous Alloy Transformers (AAT) to ensure long-term compliance and reach PUE targets (<1.2).
*   **The Substitute Alpha:** While traditional silicon steel (GOES) margins are at 10-year highs (Baosteel/Shougang), the "Scramble for Amorphous" is driving a projected **50-125% price surge** for AAT delivery slots in late 2026.
*   **Supply Chain Choke-point:** **Yunlu Advanced Materials (688190.SH)** maintains a **40% global market share** in amorphous ribbon. As the primary material supplier to GEV (via Prolec GE) and Siemens Energy, Yunlu is the ultimate "picks and shovels" play for the high-efficiency transition.
*   **ADR Pricing:** US-listed equipment ADRs (GEV, ETN, HUBB) have started pricing in the "Super-Cycle," but the market under-estimates the margin accretion from the **Amorphous Premium (20-30% base, rising to 50%+ in H2)**.

The primary risk to this "Base Configuration" is a sudden acceleration in grid permitting (unlikely) or a "Nuclear Surprise" (SMRs coming online faster than 2028). However, the 2026 H2 window is firmly locked by the **18-30 month construction cycle** of the gas plants already ordered by MSFT and Oracle.
