## 1. Screening Rules

### 1.1 Bullish — Volume-confirmed breakout to new high
**All conditions required**:
- Close ≥ 60 / 120 / 250-day rolling high (graded by window);
- Today's volume ≥ 1.8× of trailing-20-day average;
- Close above MA20 AND MA60, with MA20 crossing or already above MA60;
- Solid bullish body, upper shadow ≤ ⅓ of body;
- Exclude limit-up "1-字板", ST names, and first day post-suspension to avoid false signals.

### 1.2 Bearish — Breakdown of support
**All conditions required**:
- Close breaks the lower edge of the 60-day consolidation range, OR breaks MA60 / MA120 / MA250;
- Today's volume ≥ 1.5× trailing-20-day average (real distribution, not low-volume noise);
- Close ≥ 1.5% below the broken level;
- Pattern confirms one of: H&S top, double-top, descending-triangle lower edge break.
