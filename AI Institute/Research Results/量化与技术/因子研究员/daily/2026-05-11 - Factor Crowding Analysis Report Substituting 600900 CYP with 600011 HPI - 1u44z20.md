## 3. Detailed Analysis

### 3.1 Logic for Crowding Improvement
Yangtze Power is treated as a "bond proxy" in the A-share market. Over recent defensive cycles, insurance funds, mutual funds, and dividend-themed ETFs have reached a high level of "holding resonance." This creates a risk where any liquidity shock could lead to irrational collective selling.
Huaneng Power, a thermal power leader, offers a different driver: "Coal-Electricity Linking" and "Green Energy Transition." Its investor base is more diverse than Yangtze's, effectively reducing the systemic correlation of the portfolio.

### 3.2 Factor Purity Trade-offs
*   **Dividend Factor:** Huaneng's earnings recovery is on track. The 2026 dividend plan demonstrates a strong commitment to cash payouts, confirming its genuine dividend profile.
*   **Low Vol Factor:** It is critical to note that Huaneng's volatility is higher than Yangtze's. If the portfolio has a strict minimum-variance constraint, this substitution will cause a slight increase in overall portfolio volatility.

### 3.3 Market Environment Suitability
As of May 2026, the market is experiencing high-level volatility in growth stocks (AI/Semiconductors), with capital seeking safe havens. Switching from an overcrowded "Defensive Peak" (Yangtze) to a cleaner, lower-valued "Value Trough" (Huaneng) effectively hedges against the risk of a stampede within the dividend style.
