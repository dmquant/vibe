The "service leg" (CXO) is more exposed to direct legislative bans (BIOSECURE), while the "product leg" (Biotech) is more sensitive to commercial hurdles and data compliance (EO 14117).

## 2. The "BD-NPV" Stress Test: Impact on ADC & Bispecific Assets

The "BD Option" is a core driver for Chinese Biotech valuations. Geopolitical friction affects the **Net Present Value (NPV)** of these deals through three primary channels:

### A. CMC Complexity & Switching Costs (The ADC Bottleneck)
Antibody-Drug Conjugates (ADCs) and Bispecifics require highly specialized CDMO capabilities. 
- **The "WuXi Discount":** Assets currently in Phase II/III utilizing WuXi Biologics for CMC are facing a "BIOSECURE Discount." We estimate a **12-18 month delay** for any asset forced to switch CDMOs mid-stream. 
- **Valuation Impact:** A 1-year delay in a US P3 trial for a potential blockbuster ADC (e.g., Trop2 or Her3 targets) reduces its NPV by **18-22%** (assuming a 12% WACC and 8-year remaining patent life).

### B. Data Compliance & EO 14117
- **Data Room Friction:** Since the DOJ's final rule on "Sensitive Personal Data" (Oct 2025), BD due diligence cycles have extended from 3-4 months to 6-9 months. 
- **De-risking Clauses:** 2026-era Term Sheets now routinely include "Geopolitical Exit Clauses," allowing MNCs to return rights with minimal penalty if legislative changes (like BIOSECURE or Section 232) significantly impair the asset's commercial path.
- **Upfront vs. Milestone:** The Upfront-to-Total-Deal-Value ratio has dropped from ~10% (2023) to **4-5% (2026)**. Investors must discount "Headline Values" more aggressively.

## 3. Addressing the 20-40% Valuation Compression Risk

We agree with the Policy Analyst's (Card 03) assessment of a 20-40% compression risk, but emphasize that it is **sector-specific and stack-specific**:

1.  **CXO (Direct Compression):** For entities like WuXi AppTec, the 40% compression has largely materialized (P/E dropped from 30x+ to ~12-15x). The risk now lies in **terminal value assumptions**—if US business is seen as "wind-down only" by 2032, DCF values collapse.
2.  **Biotech (Optionality Compression):** For Biotechs, the 20-40% risk applies to the **"Out-licensing Value Component"**. 
    - If a company's valuation is 50% domestic sales + 50% BD-NPV, the geopolitical friction applies a ~30% discount to the BD half, resulting in a **15% total company valuation hit**.
    - For "BD-only" pre-revenue biotechs, the full **30-40% hit** is real and justified.

## 4. Final Verdict: Selective Revaluation Criteria (2026-05-14)

We refine the "Selective Revaluation" thesis:

*   **Premium Assets (The "Survivors"):** 
    - Clinical MRCTs led by non-named CROs (e.g., IQVIA, PPD, or pure-play European CROs).
    - Manufacturing stacks established in SE Asia or Europe (e.g., Singapore/Ireland hubs).
    - Commercial assets with approved FDA labels and robust US royalty streams (e.g., BeiGene, Legend).
*   **Discounted Assets (The "Stranded"):**
    - Early-stage platforms relying heavily on Chinese genomic datasets (High EO 14117 risk).
    - ADCs with no CMC alternative to WuXi Bio.
    - Entities with significant US Federal funding exposure (HHS/DoD).
