## 1. Executive Summary: Compute Sky, Energy Ceiling

Following the multi-layered analysis of the AI semiconductor industry (TMT), funding structures (Real Estate/Macro), and credit risks (CRO), the energy stress test provides the final and most rigid constraint on the "Agent Era True Capex Cycle" hypothesis.

**Stress Test Judgment:** I support the CRO's downward revision of fundamental confidence (Card 07). I recommend further compressing the "Fundamental Support Level" of the AI semiconductor basket from **50-55%** to **45-50%**. The primary rationale: **Starting in H2 2026, energy quotas and regulatory utilization redlines will act as "physical stop-loss lines" that trigger earlier than funding constraints.**

## 2. Energy Quotas and Utilization: The Ignored Hard Constraints

Latest policy shifts in May 2026 (preliminary research for the 15th Five-Year Plan) have established:
- **Utilization Redline (60%):** Local government-backed AIDCs must achieve a minimum 60% actual compute utilization rate; otherwise, new project approvals are banned. This directly punctures the expansion bubble of "Digital LGFVs" discussed in Card 07.
- **PUE Hard Ceiling (1.25):** In Eastern and Central clusters (Hefei, Wuhan, Wuxi), existing projects with a PUE above 1.25 face punitive electricity pricing or mandatory decommissioning.

| Cluster | Energy Redundancy (2026E) | Power Cost Sensitivity | Stress Test Risk Point |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hefei / Wuxi** | **Critically Low.** Manufacturing peaks overlap with AIDC demand. | **High.** Lack of cheap local green power; dependent on inter-provincial imports. | **Priority Conflicts:** When semiconductor fabs (SMIC) and AIDCs compete for power, the state will inevitably prioritize manufacturing over compute. |
| **Wuhan / Central** | **Moderate Shortage.** Seasonal hydro fluctuations. | **Medium.** | **VRE Mismatch:** 24/7 AIDC loads mismatch with renewable volatility, requiring an additional 15-25% storage amortization cost. |
| **Western Hubs** | **Abundant.** | **Very Low (30-40% discount).** | **"East-West" Drainage:** As UHV corridors complete, training loads are shifting West, making the 60% utilization target even harder for Eastern/Central AIDCs to hit. |

## 3. The "Inefficiency Subsidy" Trap: A Double-Edged Sword for Domestic Chips

Domestic AI chips mentioned in Card 02 (Cambricon, Haiguang) face an awkward physical reality: **At equivalent compute levels, their performance-per-watt is approximately 35-50% lower than the NVIDIA B200 series.**

- **Subsidy Offset:** 2026 policies provide a 50% electricity subsidy for AIDCs using domestic chips. While this supports short-term adoption, it means the competitiveness of these chips is built on **fiscal subsidization of energy waste.**
- **Bubble Risk:** If local fiscal capacity (Cards 05/06) fails due to land revenue declines, the cost disadvantage of domestic chips will immediately surface. This is the most fragile component of the 45% "Narrative Premium."

## 4. Physical "Asset Scarcity": The End of Energy-as-Credit

In the H2 2026 stress scenario:
1. **Carbon Tariffs & ESG Constraints:** Internet giants, chasing 2030 Carbon Neutrality goals, will reduce leasing from high-consumption, low-efficiency IDCs, shifting instead to advanced nodes (SMIC/Equipment) with higher energy efficiency.
2. **"Zombie Compute" Cleanup:** We expect the first wave of "Digital LGFV" projects to have their energy supply cut in 2026Q4 due to utilization rates dropping below 40%.

## 5. Final Investment Guidance: From "Compute Expansion" to "Energy Efficiency"

- **Downgrade:** Pure compute leasing and high-consumption chip design. These areas are most vulnerable to utilization redlines and subsidy withdrawals.
- **Maintain:** Advanced nodes (Foundries) and Equipment. Energy efficiency gains ultimately rely on advanced process nodes (e.g., 3nm/2nm), which actually strengthens the thesis for SMIC and Naura (Northern Huachuang).
- **New Allocation Suggestions:** Focus on **Power Electronics (SiC/GaN), AIDC Liquid Cooling, and Green Power Providers**. They are the true "shovels" in this compute cycle.
