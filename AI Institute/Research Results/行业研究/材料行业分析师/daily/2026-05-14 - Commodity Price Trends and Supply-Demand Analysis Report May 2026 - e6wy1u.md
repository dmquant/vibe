# Commodity Price Trends and Supply-Demand Analysis Report (May 2026)

**Report Date:** 2026-05-14
**Analyst:** Materials Analyst

## Executive Summary
In May 2026, global commodity markets exhibit a stark divergence. **Copper** and **Aluminum** are surging toward historic highs, driven by geopolitical shocks and structural deficits. **Lithium** prices have rebounded strongly, reclaiming the 200,000 CNY mark as energy storage demand explodes. **Steel**, meanwhile, is oscillating at low levels as cost support battles seasonal construction headwinds.

---

## 1. Copper: AI Demand & Supply Crisis
*   **Current Price:** LME Copper ~ $14,200/t; SHFE Copper ~ 105,000 CNY/t.
*   **Price Trend:** 📈 Strong Upward. Broken the $14,000 threshold, approaching all-time highs.
*   **Supply-Demand Dynamics:**
    *   **Supply:** Extremely tight. Global Treatment Charges (TC) for copper concentrates have dropped into negative territory. A global **sulfuric acid shortage** (exacerbated by Middle East conflicts and Chinese export curbs) is restricting ~15-20% of global refining capacity.
    *   **Demand:** AI Data Centers and power grid upgrades are the primary drivers. JPMorgan estimates AI-related demand will add nearly 500k tons of incremental demand in 2026.
*   **Outlook:** Supply bottlenecks are unlikely to ease in the short term; prices are expected to continue their ascent.

## 2. Aluminum: Geopolitical "Black Swan"
*   **Current Price:** LME Aluminum ~ $3,650/t; Domestic ~ 24,000 CNY/t.
*   **Price Trend:** 📈 Surging. 4-year highs.
*   **Supply-Demand Dynamics:**
    *   **Supply:** The escalation in the Middle East has led to the **blockade of the Strait of Hormuz**. Smelters in the UAE and Bahrain (representing ~3% of global supply) have faced production cuts due to attacks or energy disruptions. Recovery is estimated to take 6-12 months.
    *   **Demand:** Robust demand for EV chassis and solar frames. However, high domestic inventories in China (>1.4M tons) have partially dampened the domestic price surge compared to LME.
*   **Outlook:** Monitor Middle East developments; the supply gap provides strong fundamental support for high prices.

## 3. Lithium: Storage Demand Reshaping the Market
*   **Current Price:** Battery-grade Lithium Carbonate average > 200,000 CNY/t.
*   **Price Trend:** 📈 Rebounding. Up over 250% from the 2025 lows.
*   **Supply-Demand Dynamics:**
    *   **Supply:** Mines in Australia and Jiangxi (China) have not fully recovered from previous shutdowns. Zimbabwe's export ban has further delayed arrivals, causing a short-term supply vacuum.
    *   **Demand:** **Stationary storage for AI power grids** has emerged as the new growth engine, outstripping EV battery growth. May production schedules are up 6% MoM.
*   **Outlook:** The destocking cycle is nearing its end; the price floor has shifted up to the 200k-220k CNY range.

## 4. Steel: Cost Support vs. Seasonal Pressure
*   **Current Price:** Rebar ~ 3,200-3,300 CNY/t; HRC ~ 3,400 CNY/t.
*   **Price Trend:** 📉 Neutral/Soft. Oscillating at a bottom.
*   **Supply-Demand Dynamics:**
    *   **Supply:** A rebound in iron ore prices provides cost support, but mills are maintaining low utilization rates due to thin margins.
    *   **Demand:** Supported by manufacturing and the start of the "15th Five-Year Plan" infrastructure projects. However, the **Wet Season** in Southern China is slowing construction, increasing seasonal inventory pressure.
*   **Outlook:** Focus on demand for high-end "New Productive Forces" related steel products.
