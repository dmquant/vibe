# Lithium Ore / Li2CO3 / LiOH — Supply-Demand Balance & Price Outlook

> Report date: 2026-05-11 (Mon) | Analyst role: Materials Analyst
> Price benchmarks: SMM spot, Fastmarkets, Asian Metal, GFEX LC2607 contract
> Upstream benchmarks: SC6 spodumene CIF China, SC6 contract reference, brine cost curve
> Note: forward-looking judgments are subjective; see "Risks & Downside Scenarios"

---

## 1. TL;DR

1. **Price call**: Battery-grade Li2CO3 (SMM spot) is currently **RMB 74,000–76,000/t**, up ~10% from the January 2026 trough of RMB 68k. We maintain a **2026 full-year midpoint of RMB 70k–90k/t**; an H2 spike toward **RMB 95k–100k/t** is possible if the ESS + EV peak season coincides with Australian mine cuts, but **a sustained break above 100k is unlikely** — that level reactivates Chinese lepidolite tolling and North American DLE phase-2 capacity.
2. **Supply-demand**: 2026 global LCE supply is **~1.65–1.72 Mt**, demand **~1.58–1.64 Mt**, surplus **60–100 kt LCE** — meaningfully narrower than the 180 kt surplus in 2025. **The real inflection is H1 2027**: if Greenbushes CGP3 and brine DLE projects come online on schedule, the surplus widens again to >150 kt.
3. **LiOH premium returns**: The LiOH-Li2CO3 spread has recovered from -RMB 8,000/t in 2024 to **+RMB 3,000–5,000/t** today, driven by overseas high-nickel NCM9 demand (LG / Samsung SDI Q1 2026 build plans up 18% QoQ). We expect H2 2026 spreads to widen further to **+RMB 8,000–10,000/t**.
4. **Structural positioning**: Overweight **integrated names with resource self-sufficiency >70%** (Tianqi, Ganfeng-overseas, Pilbara); avoid **high-cost tollers (lepidolite buyers, processing-fee models)**. Lithium equities have **already front-run Li2CO3 futures by ~1 quarter**; the beta trade window suggests waiting for a **June–July pullback** before the peak-season buy.

### 4.2 ESS — The biggest 2026 variable
- US 2026 new ESS revised up from **70 GWh to 95 GWh** (data-center PPAs land; Microsoft/Meta/AWS signed 32 GWh for 2025-2027).
- China 2026 new ESS **180 GWh** (vs 130 last year); mandated independent-storage ratio raised from 10% to 15-20% (NDRC 2026-03-12 notice).
- **Each GWh of ESS consumes ~600 t LCE** (LFP); global 350 GWh → 210 kt LCE, 14% of total demand.
- Risk: ESS project IRR is sensitive to lithium price; if Li2CO3 returns above RMB 90k, some US merchant storage projects defer.

### 4.3 EV — High-nickel ternary revival drives LiOH
- LFP global share rose from 42% to 55% over 2024-2025; in 2026 ternary regains share via 800V high-nickel (NCM9) penetration in RMB 300k+ segments.
- LG / Samsung SDI Q1 2026 LiOH purchases up +25% YoY, structurally widening the LiOH-Li2CO3 spread.

## 8. Risks & Downside Scenarios

1. **ESS demand falsification**: if China merchant-storage IRR sits below 5%, H2 2026 installs miss by 30 GWh → demand cut by 18 kt LCE, midpoint reverts to RMB 65k.
2. **Transient LiOH oversupply**: Yahua + multiple processors are bringing LiOH capacity online concurrently; if NA high-nickel penetration disappoints, LiOH could re-collapse back to parity with Li2CO3.
3. **Sodium-ion substitution**: CATL's gen-2 sodium pack (175 Wh/kg) reaches mass production in Q3 2026, hitting sub-RMB 20k entry EVs and the 30% long-duration tail of ESS. **2026 actual displacement <20 kt LCE**, but the 2027 risk grows.
4. **Policy**: A tighter US FEOC reading on "Chinese controlling capital" would close Ganfeng's Argentina-to-US route.
5. **Macro / FX**: RMB beyond 7.5/USD raises SC6 import cost, paradoxically helping domestic integrated names; but broader commodity strength (iron ore + copper) would erode lithium's relative attractiveness.

## 10. Conclusion & Recommendation

- **Positioning**: Buy the integrated leaders (Tianqi / Ganfeng / Pilbara) on a June–July pullback; target the Q3 ESS-peak + EV-build window for **25-35% upside**.
- **Absolute price**: Do not chase spot above RMB 100k; instead, run a LC2607 call + LC2701 put-spread calendar to capture deepening H2 backwardation.
- **2027 risk**: With CGP3 and Chilean brine DLE commercialization landing, price midpoint reverts to RMB 70k. **Trim positions in Q4 2026**, when P/B is likely to be back above the 3.5x historical mid.
