## Executive Summary
The expected divergence in the US quantum market (Realizers vs. Narrators) during the May 2026 earnings window provides a critical template for A-share and ADR positioning. While US names are judged by "auditable dollars" (Novera ARR, DARPA-QBI), the A-share logic centers on the "National Quantum Science and Technology Project" (量子科技专项) order flow. This report identifies "Realizers" in the China-linked universe and constructs a tradable two-stage basket to capture the localized alpha.

## I. Divergence Mapping: Realizers vs. Narrators

### 1. A-Share Realizers (The Implementation Core)
*   **QuantumCTek (688027.SH):** The primary beneficiary of the National Project's 2026-2028 roadmap. Unlike 2023, when it was purely a QKD narrative, 2026 data shows clear revenue from superconducting quantum computing room-temperature control systems and dilution refrigerators. Classification: **Strong Realizer.**
*   **Accelink (002281.SZ):** Key supplier for the optical backbone of the China Quantum-Safe Network. Recent tender wins for 800G modules in "quantum-enhanced" data centers confirm its role in the "picks and shovels" layer. Classification: **Realizer.**
*   **Sanwei Security (688489.SH):** Leading the PQC (Post-Quantum Cryptography) migration within the People's Bank of China (PBOC) pilot projects. As standards finalize in 2026, HSM (Hardware Security Module) replacement orders are providing high-margin growth. Classification: **Realizer.**

### 2. A-Share Narrators (The Strategic Beta)
*   **Sugon (603019.SH) & Inspur (000977.SZ):** While these names provide the classic HPC infrastructure that hosts quantum simulations, the direct "quantum" revenue remains <5%. They will follow the sector's beta but lack the pure-play "divergence" alpha. Classification: **Narrators.**

### 3. ADR / Big Tech Positioning
*   **Baidu (BIDU):** Despite being a pioneer with the "Qian Shi" hardware, the 2025 lab donation shifted its focus to the "Liang Xi" software stack. Without direct hardware sales, it trades more as an AI-proxy. Classification: **Narrator.**
*   **Alibaba (BABA):** DAMO Academy's quantum initiatives remain strategic. Revenue impact is currently negligible compared to the Cloud/AI core. Classification: **Narrator.**

## II. The Two-Stage Tradable Basket

To navigate the divergence, we propose a two-stage basket structure based on the National Project order flow:

### Basket A: The Hardware Backbone (Picks & Shovels)
*   **Target:** Capture the immediate CAPEX of the National Quantum Project.

*   **Thesis:** Direct play on the "Order Realization" theme seen in the US (RGTI/QBTS proxy).

Investors should remain neutral on the broad AI-ETF beta and overweight **Basket A** in the immediate 48-hour window following the US earnings reports.
