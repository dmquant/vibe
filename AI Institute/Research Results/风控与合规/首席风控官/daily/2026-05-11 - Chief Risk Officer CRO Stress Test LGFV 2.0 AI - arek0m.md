## 1. Executive Risk Summary: The "Digital LGFV" Solvency Trap

As of May 11, 2026, the credit floor for the AI semiconductor rally has thinned significantly. While Card 06 (China Macro) highlights the "National Team" as a funding backstop, my stress test reveals a critical **Solvency Trap** at the local level. The primary risk is no longer just "land revenue decline," but a **catastrophic duration mismatch** between AI assets (3-5 year depreciation) and LGFV debt (10-15 year maturity).

**Core Risk Judgment:** I am further down-weighting the "Fundamental Support Confidence" from **50-60%** (Card 06) to **50-55%**. The 5% cut reflects the "Credit Tail Risk" of cross-defaults in high-leverage hubs (Wuhan/Wuxi), which could force a localized hard-stop on AI Capex by Q4 2026.

## 3. The "3-5 vs 10-15" Duration Mismatch (Stress Scenario)

The AI/AIDC asset class is a "Fast-Moving Consumer Good" (FMCG) disguised as "Infrastructure."

- **The Asset Side:** NVIDIA B200 and domestic equivalents have a 3-year "Competitive Frontier" life and a 5-year total depreciation cycle.
- **The Liability Side:** LGFV 2.0 (Digital LGFVs) have structured their AIDC financing with 10-15 year policy bank loans.
- **The Failure Mode:** If the "Agent Commercialization" (Card 03 question) fails to achieve an 80%+ utilization rate by 2027, these platforms will be left with "Digital Brownfield" assets while still owing 70% of the principal. 

**Stress Test Result:** In a scenario where 2026H2 Capex 2nd derivative turns negative (Card 02), 1/3 of "Digital LGFVs" in Tier-2 AI cities face technical insolvency.

## 4. Cross-Default Triggers & Contagion

The risk of **Cross-Default** is localized but intense. In semiconductor hubs (Hefei, Wuhan, Wuxi), the "Industrial Investment Platform" is often the guarantor for multiple SPVs (Special Purpose Vehicles). 

1. **The Purity Trigger:** If a "Digital LGFV" misses an interest payment on an AIDC bond, it triggers a cross-default clause on the parent's traditional infra bonds.
2. **The "Hefei Model" Contagion:** Hefei's stability relies on its ability to *exit* (sell equity). In a bear market for AI IC Design (Hygon/Cambricon - Card 02/05), Hefei cannot liquidate, turning its "Equity Buffer" into a "Liquidity Trap."

## 5. Portfolio Implications (Risk Limits)

- **Cap on "IC Design" Exposure:** I strongly advise capping exposure to non-revenue-generating AI IC designers. Their valuation is 45% "Agent Bubble" (Card 02) and they lack the "Central Credit" floor that晶圆 (SMIC) and 设备 (NAURA) enjoy.
- **Credit Spread Watch:** If "Digital LGFV" credit spreads widen by >50bps over general LGFV bonds, it signal that the "AI-as-Collateral" narrative is breaking.
- **Threshold for "Hard Stop":** If land revenue remains <-20% YoY through Q3 2026 AND AIDC utilization rates in WIDG/WHFHG drop below 65%, expect a total freeze in new project approvals.

## 6. Conclusion & Final Handoff

The "National Team" can save the *Industry* (the chips), but it may not save the *Platforms* (the LGFVs). Investors must decouple their "Bullish on Semiconductors" thesis from their "Bullish on Chinese Tech Beta" thesis. The former is a strategic mandate; the latter is a credit-fragile bet.
