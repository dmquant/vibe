## 1. Bottom Line (Conclusions First)

1. **Nature of the move**: The +78bp jump in 22 Vanke 06 yield today is driven by a **combination of event risk and a liquidity squeeze**, not a rates/duration repricing. 5Y CDB yields fell 1bp and the 5Y AAA MTN index rose only 3bp today; the residual ~75bp is fully attributable to issuer-specific credit-spread widening.
2. **Observed institutional flow**:
   - On the broker (QB/CMS) screens, **three large insurance asset managers and one joint-stock-bank wealth-management subsidiary** were running **one-way offers only — no bids** for size, with single tickets concentrated at RMB 50–100mn face. This is a sharp reversal versus yesterday's two-way activity.
   - CSDC / SHCH **block-trade flow** widened materially: large tickets (≥RMB 50mn) rose from the 20-day average of ~28% of volume to **~61% today**, a classic signature of position trimming.
   - Mutual funds (medium/long-duration pure-bond) have **not** yet shown material selling. The other side of today's flow was absorbed by **a handful of private funds and dealer market-making books** — depth was thin, which is the proximate micro driver of the spread gap.
3. **Restructuring/extension rumor**: A version circulating in the market — *"interest-only first, then principal repaid in three equal annual tranches, with an unconditional liquidity support letter from the parent"* — is **NOT confirmed** by Vanke or the lead underwriter. As of this writing, no extension proposal, exchange offer, or bondholder-meeting notice has been published on the exchange interactive platform or the trustee's filing system. Treat the rumor as unverified and do NOT incorporate it into the base case.
4. **Recommended actions**:
   - Holders: do not panic-sell at today's marks. Use limit orders ~30–40bp tighter than today's close, scaled out over multiple sessions.
   - Watchers: wait for at least one of (a) an issuer clarification, (b) a bondholder-meeting notice, or (c) lead-underwriter window guidance before initiating exposure.
   - Risk: place 22 Vanke 06 on the "event-triggered watchlist" and monitor sibling issues (21 Vanke 02/03, MTN001, etc.) for spillover.
