# Middle East 2026 Procurement of Rubin-Class Chips and Impact on Global Compute Distribution

> Data cut-off: 2026-05-11
> Recipient: TMT Analyst · AI Industry Tracker (run b7ec2fbd-d9d0-4958-8183-0b11c38fede7)

## 1. Top-line conclusions (read these five first)

1. **In 2026, the two sovereign anchor buyers in the Middle East — UAE's G42 / Stargate UAE and Saudi Arabia's HUMAIN — will absorb a combined ~800K–1.3M Rubin-class GPU equivalents** (GB200/GB300/R100 mix). Against a total NVIDIA+AMD high-end data-center GPU pool of ~7M units in 2026E, this represents **~12–18%**, up from <1% in 2024 and 4–6% in 2025. **The Middle East has graduated from a marginal customer to the #2 demand cluster after North American hyperscalers.**
2. **Actual 2026 Rubin (R100) deliveries to the Middle East are constrained by a triple bottleneck**: (a) TSMC CoWoS-L 2026 capacity of ~1.1M wafer-equivalents; (b) a soft per-end-user annual ceiling implied by US Commerce/BIS licensing practice (industry estimate ~500K high-end GPUs per entity per year); and (c) local power availability — Stargate UAE Phase 1 (1GW) energises only late-2026, and HUMAIN's 1.5GW Dammam campus reaches steady state in 2027. **As a result, 2026 will see signed >> shipped, with order back-loading into 2027–28.**
3. **After the Trump administration rescinded Biden's AI Diffusion Rule (the three-tier country framework) in May 2025**, the US shifted to a bilateral-MOU-plus-security-commitment model. UAE and Saudi Arabia have been de-facto upgraded to near-Tier-1 treatment, but with binding conditions: (i) G42 must divest Huawei legacy hardware; (ii) US third-party audit rights; (iii) no resale or transshipment to mainland China, Iran, or Russia. **Any breach signal triggers immediate export suspension — this is the largest non-market risk for 2026.**
4. **First-order impact on global compute distribution**: North American hyperscalers (Microsoft / Google / Meta / Amazon / xAI) drop from ~78% share of NVIDIA high-end GPUs in 2024 to **~62–65% in 2026**; Middle East rises to **12–18%**; Europe (including EU AI Gigafactories) ~6–8%; mainland China (incl. de-rated H20/B30A SKUs) ~8–10%; rest of APAC ~5–7%. **This is the largest geographic redistribution of AI compute since cloud computing went global in 2017.**
5. **Second-order implications — for markets and supply chains:**
   - NVIDIA FY2026 data-center revenue: revision risk is slightly positive (Middle East contracts are locked), but blended ASP slips ~5–8% on volume discounts.
   - "Middle East premium" on power, HVAC, liquid cooling, and HBM — particularly SK Hynix HBM4, Vertiv liquid-cooling CDUs, and Schneider MV switchgear gain order visibility through 2028 from H2 2026.
   - Tier-2 GPU clouds in North America (CoreWeave, Nebius, Crusoe) face allocation crowding-out from Middle East sovereigns; valuation premia compress.
   - Chinese domestic AI silicon (Huawei Ascend 920, Cambricon 690) gets an **asymmetric opening** in 2026 because resale conduits via the Middle East are sealed — the third-tier Gulf states (Egypt, Jordan, Bahrain) accelerate toward Chinese alternatives.

## 一、核心结论（先看这五条）

1. **2026 年中东两大主权买家（UAE G42 / Stargate UAE、沙特 HUMAIN）合计承接的 Rubin 级 GPU 出货量，量级落在 80 万–130 万颗 GB200/GB300/R100 等效**（按 NVIDIA 数据中心总出货 ~700 万颗 Blackwell/Rubin 等效估算，占比 **~12–18%**）。这一份额在 2024 年还几乎为零，2025 年仅约 4–6%。**中东已从"边缘买家"升级为继北美超大规模厂商之后的第二大需求极**。
2. **Rubin（R100）2026 年向中东的实际交付量受三重瓶颈钳制**：(a) TSMC CoWoS-L 产能 2026 年仅 ~110 万片晶圆等效；(b) 美国商务部对单一终端用户单年出口许可的"软上限"（业内引述约 50 万颗高端 GPU/家/年）；(c) 当地电力——UAE Stargate 一期 1GW 2026 年底才并网，沙特 HUMAIN 的 1.5GW Dammam 园区 2027 年才达产。**因此 2026 年中东"签约量 >> 实际交付量"，订单结构性后置至 2027–2028**。
3. **特朗普政府 2025 年 5 月废止 Biden 的 AI Diffusion Rule（"三层国别框架"）后，改采"逐国双边协议 + 安全承诺"模式**。UAE/沙特已被实质性"升级"至接近 Tier 1 待遇，但保留 (i) G42 必须剥离华为存量设备；(ii) 美方第三方审计权；(iii) 禁止转售/转运至中国大陆、伊朗、俄罗斯。**任何违约迹象将立即触发出口暂停，是 2026 年最大的非市场风险**。
4. **对全球算力分布的一阶影响**：北美超大规模厂商（Microsoft/Google/Meta/Amazon/xAI）在 NVIDIA 高端 GPU 中的份额将从 2024 年的 ~78% 降至 2026 年的 **~62–65%**；中东升至 **12–18%**；欧洲（含 EU AI Gigafactories 计划）约 6–8%；中国大陆（含 H20/B30A 等降配版）约 8–10%；其他亚太约 5–7%。**这是 2017 年云计算时代以来最大的一次地缘算力再分配**。
5. **二阶影响——对资本市场与产业链的具体启示**：
   - NVIDIA 2026 财年数据中心营收上调风险偏正向（中东合同已锁定，但 ASP 因体量折扣下滑 ~5–8%）；
   - 电力/HVAC/液冷/HBM 链条出现"中东溢价"——尤其 SK Hynix HBM4、Vertiv 液冷 CDU、施耐德 MV 配电 2026 H2 起订单能见度延伸至 2028；
   - 北美二线云厂（CoreWeave、Nebius、Crusoe）面临"中东主权云"挤占可分配 GPU 配额的风险，估值溢价收敛；
