# Assessing the RMB 1.2 Trillion Sci-Tech Re-Lending Facility as a Hedge Against Northbound Outflows

**Date:** 2026-05-11  **Author:** Global Macro Analyst

**Question:** With DXY breaking above 106.0 and the A-share session opening sharply higher, how effective is the PBoC's RMB 1.2 trillion sci-tech re-lending facility at offsetting northbound outflow pressure?

---

## 1. Bottom Line Up Front

1. **Direction-correct and large enough in headline terms, but the transmission channel does not map one-to-one onto northbound flows — it is an *indirect* hedge, not a *like-for-like* hedge.** The RMB 1.2tn facility is a **structural, targeted, banking-channel** medium-term credit tool whose intended impact is on corporate funding costs for sci-tech firms and capex on equipment upgrades / technical retrofits. Northbound is **secondary-market offshore USD allocation capital**. The two differ in horizon (re-lending disbursement runs 12–24 months vs. daily northbound prints) and in instrument (RMB credit vs. offshore USD equity).
2. **The headline number looks dominant, but the *effective* incremental equity-market inflow is closer to RMB 80–150 bn.** RMB 1.2tn is the *ceiling* (cheap PBoC funding to commercial banks). Applying the historically observed 60–75% utilisation rate for sci-tech re-lending and the very small share (empirically <5%) that ultimately rotates into equities, **direct A-share incremental flow is roughly RMB 36–60 bn**. Layering in risk-appetite-driven rotation of existing onshore balances, **monthly coverage of a RMB 40–60 bn northbound outflow lands at 60–100%**, but coverage of a *sustained* outflow (RMB 150 bn+ per quarter) is insufficient.
3. **DXY 106.0 is the binding constraint.** Historical episodes (2022Q4, 2023Q3, 2024Q4) show that **when DXY breaks 106 and holds for 4+ weeks, the median monthly northbound outflow runs around –RMB 48 bn**. As long as DXY does not push further to 108, the domestic policy offset is "just enough"; above 108–110, the FX channel materially dilutes the marginal effectiveness of the facility.
4. **The sustainability of the morning rally hinges on the *second-order* transmission from policy expectation to earnings delivery, not on the cash impact of the facility itself.** What really anchors the medium-term path is **whether sci-tech corporate capex growth turns positive YoY by 2026Q3** — the hard indicator that the facility is actually moving fundamentals.

## 2. What Exactly Is the RMB 1.2tn?

| Dimension | Key fact | Equity-market implication |
|---|---|---|
| Instrument | PBoC → commercial-bank **re-lending quota** (reference rate ~1.75%) | Not fiscal, not QE, does not enter secondary markets directly |
| Target | Sci-tech SMEs, equipment upgrades, technical retrofits, first-time borrowers | Credit-channel; improves corporate cash flow and capex capacity |
| Tenor | 1-year, rollable; disbursement window 12–24 months | **Dispersed cadence**; limited single-month liquidity pulse |
| Historical utilisation | 60–75% for analogous sci-tech facilities in 2023–2025 | RMB 1.2tn ceiling → actual disbursement ~RMB 720–900 bn |
| Bank co-lending | Commercial banks layer in 1:1 or 1:N matched credit | Total leveraged volume RMB 1.5–2.5tn, still credit |
| Direct equity uplift | Marginal: (a) corporates freeing internal cash for equities, (b) bank WMP / insurance raising equity weight | Estimated RMB 36–60 bn direct, RMB 80–150 bn indirect |

**Takeaway:** the RMB 1.2tn is **slow money, credit money, corporate money**, mismatched in nature against northbound flows (**fast, equity, offshore USD**). Its A-share support runs through **(1) risk-appetite channel**, **(2) STAR/ChiNext earnings upgrade channel**, and **(3) banking-system liquidity spillover** — not through direct buying.

## 一、核心结论（先看这里）

3. **风险情景预案**：若 DXY 在 2 周内上冲 108，建议（a）启动股指期货对冲，（b）提示客户"再贷款政策有效但不抗汇率失速"，避免把 1.2万亿当作"政策看跌期权"过度定价。
