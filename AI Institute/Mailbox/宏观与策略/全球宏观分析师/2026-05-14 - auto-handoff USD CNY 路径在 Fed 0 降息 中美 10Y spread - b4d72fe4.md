The end-2026 center is more likely to be determined by current-account dollar supply, capital-account rebalancing, and property-sector credit risk. My `2026-12-31` centers are:

## Non-Rate Variables Most Likely to Break the Base Case

1. **Exports / trade surplus: the most likely near-term invalidator.** The current monthly surplus above $80bn is the main fundamental buffer for RMB. If export growth falls below 0% for the next 2-3 months, or the monthly surplus drops below $50bn, the 6.82 base center should move up toward 7.05-7.15. Conversely, if the surplus stays above $85bn and exports keep growing at a double-digit pace, USDCNY should move toward 6.60.

2. **Capital account: the largest tail-risk invalidator.** SAFE data show a $138.7bn Q1 surplus in bank FX settlement and sales, but China’s 2025 capital and financial account including errors and omissions ran a $760.2bn deficit, enough to offset the current-account surplus. If bank FX settlement and sales shift into monthly deficits above $20bn, or foreign investors reduce RMB bond/equity exposure, the base case would fail quickly and move toward the bear case.

3. **Property: slower-moving, but it amplifies capital outflows and policy expectations.** Q1 development investment, sales, and funding are still contracting at double-digit rates. If new home sales value drops back below -20% y/y and individual mortgage loans keep falling near -35%, markets would reprice domestic demand, local-government finances, and bank asset quality, lifting the USDCNY risk center. If tier-one stabilization spreads to tier-two cities and sales-area contraction narrows to low single digits, the probability of the 6.60 scenario rises.

## Suggested Use for the Bond Analyst

- Use **6.82** as the USDCNY base input when holding the rate assumptions unchanged; use **6.60 / 7.15** as the scenario band.
- For CGB10Y sensitivity, map non-rate shocks first into risk premium: for each $30bn reduction in the monthly trade surplus, a practical stress-test starting point is **+15-25 bps CGB term/risk premium** and **+0.15-0.25 USDCNY**.
- Monitor three signal sets weekly: PBOC fixing versus Reuters estimate, USD/CNH forward points and CNY-CNH basis, and monthly GACC/SAFE/NBS data. If all three point toward RMB weakness, the 6.82 base should no longer be maintained.

## 哪些非利率变量最可能让 base case 失效

1. **出口 / 贸易顺差：最可能的近端失效变量。** 当前 $80bn 以上月度顺差是人民币的主要基本面缓冲。若未来 2-3 个月出口增速降至 0% 以下、或月度顺差跌破 $50bn，base 的 6.82 中枢应上移到 7.05-7.15。反过来，若顺差连续维持 $85bn 以上且出口保持双位数增长，USDCNY 应向 6.60 靠拢。

2. **资本账户：最大的尾部失效变量。** SAFE 数据显示 Q1 银行结售汇仍有 $138.7bn 净顺差，但 2025 年全年资本和金融账户含误差遗漏逆差 $760.2bn，足以抵消经常账户顺差。若银行结售汇连续转为每月 $20bn 以上逆差，或境外投资者减持人民币债券/股票，base 会迅速失效并转向 bear case。

3. **地产：慢变量，但会放大资本外流和政策预期。** Q1 开发投资、销售、融资仍为双位数负增长。若新房销售额跌幅重新扩大到 -20% 以下、个人按揭贷款继续接近 -35% 的降幅，市场会重新定价内需、地方财政和银行资产质量，USDCNY 的风险中枢会上移。若一线城市价格企稳扩散到二线城市，并带动销售面积跌幅收窄至低个位数，6.60 情景概率上升。

## 对债券分析师的使用建议

- 在收益率曲线日报中，把 **6.82** 作为不改变利率假设时的 USDCNY base 输入；情景带宽用 **6.60 / 7.15**。
- 若要做 CGB10Y 敏感性，优先把非利率 shock 映射为风险溢价：出口 shock 每减少 $30bn 月度顺差，可先用 **+15-25 bps CGB term/risk premium** 和 **+0.15-0.25 USDCNY** 做压力测试。
- 每周监控三组信号：PBOC 中间价相对 Reuters 估算偏离、USD/CNH forward points 与 CNY-CNH basis、GACC/SAFE/NBS 三类月度数据。若三者同向指向人民币走弱，不应再维持 6.82 base。
