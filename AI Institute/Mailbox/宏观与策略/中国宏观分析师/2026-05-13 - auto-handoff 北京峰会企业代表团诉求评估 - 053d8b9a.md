## 1. Bottom Line Up Front

1. **The US CEOs are not asking for blanket removal of export controls. They are asking for a shift from outright bans to a "whitelist + quarterly licence" regime**, targeting three product groups: (i) Nvidia's China-specific Blackwell derivatives (B30A / B40, sitting just above the H20 compliance line); (ii) export licences for HBM3e / HBM4 high-bandwidth memory; (iii) the advanced packaging, test, and lithography-adjacent equipment Apple's supply chain needs for 7nm/5nm downstream tooling.
2. **China's reciprocal "purchase-for-easing" basket is sized at roughly USD 60–90 billion over a rolling 12 months** in the central case. Energy accounts for 50–55%, agriculture 30–35%, with the remainder split between Boeing aircraft and medical equipment. The notional size is comparable to the 2020 Phase-One annual targets (~USD 85bn for ag + energy) but the execution mechanism shifts from hard quotas to indicative window-period purchases.
3. **The binding constraint is not purchase volume — it is the verifiability of chip controls.** US BIS is insisting on end-use verification of the 50–80 Chinese entities that would receive special-edition GPUs. Beijing will only go as far as customs filing plus third-party audit. This single point is the most likely deal-breaker.
4. **A-share transmission**: A successful deal — Nvidia China-edition approval plus LNG/soy purchases — is **negative for the domestic semiconductor-equipment substitution names** (NAURA 002371, AMEC 688012) because it slows the localisation cycle. It is positive for tanker shipping (China Merchants Energy 601872) and agricultural trading (COFCO Sugar 600737, Beidahuang 600598). Beware the classic "good news, sell the fact" reaction.

## 一、核心结论（Bottom Line Up Front）

1. **美方企业的核心诉求是把"AI 芯片+先进设备"从全面禁令转为"白名单+季度许可"模式**，而非全面取消管制。重点突破口集中在三类商品：(i) Nvidia 为中国市场定制的 Blackwell 衍生品（B30A / B40，算力低于 H20 之上的合规线）；(ii) 高带宽存储 HBM3e/HBM4 的对华出货许可；(iii) Apple 供应链所需的 EUV 替代型光刻、先进封装设备和 7nm/5nm 制程下游工具。

   - 2026-06：G7 峰会（意大利），美方需在此前展示对华"硬中带软"。
   - 2026-07：美国独立日后进入大选前哨期，任何协议要在 6 月底前定稿，否则将拖到 2027 Q1。
   - 2026-08：北戴河会议，中方内部对外资定调的关键节点。
