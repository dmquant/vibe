# US-China Mid-May Summit Scenario Analysis: Quantitative Impact on Q3 Export Growth

**Date:** May 11, 2026  
**Analyst:** China Macro Analyst  
**Intended Recipient:** FX Strategist (Source: 75ba6c08-04ef-4329-9950-858553548bc7)

## Executive View

Regarding the Beijing Summit on May 14-15, we project China’s Q3 export growth to fluctuate between **7.5% and 14.5%** depending on the diplomatic outcome. April’s surprise surge (+14.1%) was largely driven by "front-loading" due to Middle East tensions and tariff fears. Without a meaningful breakthrough at the summit, Q3 exports face the dual headwinds of "demand exhaustion" and "high base effects."

---

## Scenario Quantification

| Scenario | Probability | Q3 Export Growth Forecast (YoY) | Key Drivers & Logic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Tariff Maintenance** | 50% | **11.0% - 12.5%** | "Busan Truce" holds; July 24 Section 301 decision postponed. "New Three" (EVs, batteries, solar) maintain >50% growth, offsetting traditional trade shrinkage. |
| **2. Partial Removal** | 20% | **13.5% - 14.5%** | Removal of tariffs on List 3/4 consumer goods; Boeing mega-deal (500 aircraft) finalized. Boosts headline exports by ~1.5-2.0 percentage points via sentiment and volume. |
| **3. Failed Agreement** | 30% | **7.5% - 9.0%** | Summit fails; US raises base tariff rate to 50% on July 24. Targeted sanctions on the "New Three." Significant order cancellations and sentiment shock. |

---

## Detailed Logic

### 1. Baseline: The Front-Loading Risk
*   **April Outperformance:** Exports hit a record $359.44B (+14.1%) as firms pulled forward Q3 orders to avoid the July 24 tariff deadline and hedge against Iranian war disruptions.
*   **Overdraft Effect:** High Q2 growth has "borrowed" demand from Q3. Even under a status quo scenario, growth will naturally decelerate in Q3 as this front-loading fades.

### 2. Structural Support from the "New Three"
*   **Export Share:** As of early 2026, the "New Three" (EVs, Lithium batteries, Solar) account for **7%** of total exports. Their robust global demand acts as a structural floor for growth, likely preventing a drop below 7%.
*   **Anti-Involution Policy:** Beijing’s campaign to curb overcapacity and price wars may reduce export volumes but increase unit prices, providing a value-based buffer for total export value in USD terms.

### 3. Quantitative Impact of Tariff Changes
*   **Elasticity:** The current effective tariff rate is ~**23.7%**. Our model suggests every 5% change in the effective rate impacts US-bound exports by 3-4 percentage points.
*   **Escalation Risk:** A jump to a 50% base rate would likely cause a >20% contraction in US-bound trade (which is 15% of the total). This would directly shave **3.0 percentage points** off headline growth, with additional sentiment-driven drags pushing growth below 10%.

---

## Strategic Implications for FX
*   **Scenario 1 (Base):** RMB volatility subsides, trading within the 7.25-7.35 range.
*   **Scenario 2:** Upside export surprises provide conversion support; bullish for CNY with a target of 7.15.
*   **Scenario 3:** Heavy depreciation pressure; markets may test the 7.45-7.50 handle. Watch for PBoC counter-cyclical adjustments.

# 5月中旬中美峰会情景分析：对Q3出口增速的量化影响

**日期：** 2026年5月11日  
**分析师：** 中国宏观分析师  
**目标读者：** 外汇策略师 (Source: 75ba6c08-04ef-4329-9950-858553548bc7)

## 核心结论 (Summary)

针对5月14-15日的北京中美峰会，我们预计Q3出口增速将根据谈判结果在 **7.5% - 14.5%** 之间波动。当前4月出口超预期（+14.1%）主要受伊朗局势导致的前置订单（Front-loading）驱动，若峰会未能提供实质性关税缓和，Q3将面临“前置透支”与“基数效应”的双重压力。

---

## 情景量化预测 (Scenario Quantification)

| 情景 (Scenario) | 概率 | Q3出口增速预测 (YoY) | 核心驱动因素与逻辑 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. 关税维持 (Status Quo)** | 50% | **11.0% - 12.5%** | 维持“釜山休战”现状；7月24日Section 301新关税决定推迟；“新三样”出口维持50%以上增长，对冲传统贸易萎缩。 |
| **2. 部分取消 (Partial Removal)** | 20% | **13.5% - 14.5%** | 取消部分List 3/4消费品关税；波音大单落地（500架）；美国市场信心回升。直接拉动出口总量约1.5-2.0个百分点。 |
| **3. 协议落空 (Escalation)** | 30% | **7.5% - 9.0%** | 峰会无果，美方于7月24日将基础关税率升至50%；对“新三样”实施专项制裁；出口面临显著的订单流失和情绪冲击。 |

---

## 详细分析 (Detailed Logic)

### 1. 基准背景：前置订单的透支风险
*   **4月异常增长：** 4月出口额创下3594亿美元历史新高（+14.1%），主因是企业担忧中东局势升级及美方7月关税截止日期，提前锁定三季度订单。
*   **透支效应：** 无论峰会结果如何，Q2的高增长都预支了Q3的部分需求。若峰会仅维持现状，Q3增速将由于“前置效应”消退而自然回落。

### 2. “新三样”的结构性支撑
*   **贡献度：** 2026年初“新三样”（电车、锂电、光伏）占总出口比重已升至**7%**。其极高的行业景气度是支撑出口底线（约8%）的关键。
*   **反内卷政策：** 国内“反内卷”运动（削减过剩产能出口退税）在短期内可能提升出口单价，虽然出口量可能受限，但出口总值（美元计价）在Q3仍有支撑。

### 3. 关税变动的量化计算
*   **弹性系数：** 目前对美出口有效税率约**23.7%**。根据模型，有效税率每变动5%，影响对美出口额约3-4个百分点。
*   **协议落空风险：** 若关税升至50%，对美出口（占总额15%）可能在Q3下滑20%以上，直接拖累整体出口增速**3个百分点**，叠加情绪负面影响，总增速将跌破10%。

## 外汇策略建议 (Follow-up for FX)
*   **情景1（基准）：** 人民币汇率波动率将下降，维持在 7.25-7.35 区间。
*   **情景2：** 出口超预期增长将提供结汇支撑，短期利好人民币，目标 7.15。
*   **情景3：** 强烈的贬值压力，市场可能测试 7.45-7.50 关口，需关注中间价窗口指导。
