# Green-Power Direct Supply to Data Centers under the AI–Energy Mutual Empowerment Action Plan: Tariff, Grid Access, Consumption, Local Execution Constraints, and 12-Month EPS Visibility for Compute–Power Co-located Names (Datang Power et al.)

**Author: China Macro Analyst** · **Date: 2026-05-14** · **In response to the TMT analyst's daily meetup request**

## 1. Headline Conclusions (read this first)

1. **The AI–Energy Mutual Empowerment Action Plan (NEA Sci-Tech Doc. No. 53 [2025], jointly issued July 2025 by NEA, NDRC, and MIIT) endorses the direction of "green-power direct supply to AIDCs," but tariff formation, grid connection, and consumption rules remain under provincial execution. A nationally unified, executable rulebook will NOT land within 2026.** EPS contributions over the next 12 months will therefore be project-level and region-level, not an industry-wide re-rating event.

2. **Tariff formation has not escaped the three-segment framework**: energy charge + T&D charge (without cross-subsidy) + ancillary-services share. Landed tariffs at pilot AIDCs in Inner Mongolia, Ningxia, and Gansu currently sit at **RMB 0.32–0.42/kWh (tax-inclusive, at meter)**, **15–25% below local industrial catalogue tariffs**, but conditional on **≥80% self-consumption ratio** and a **20–30% capacity reservation fee**. For generators this means **higher utilization hours and locked PPAs**, not a high-tariff order book.

3. **Grid access & consumption**: Inter-provincial direct supply is still throttled by **provincial barriers and renewable-consumption quotas**. State Grid is pushing **"virtual direct supply" (green certificates + nearby physical delivery)** for 2026. True point-to-point physical lines are limited to **~18 demonstration projects (as of April 2026) in Inner Mongolia, Ningxia, Gansu, Qinghai, and Xinjiang**, each capped at **2 GW renewable + 200 MW load**.

4. **12-month EPS visibility for compute–power co-located names**:
   - **Datang Power (601991.SH / 0991.HK)**: Combined contribution from the Duolun "wind-solar-thermal-storage-compute" base in Inner Mongolia and the Ningde AIDC gas-peaker project is **~RMB 80–120 mn pre-tax annually**, lifting **2026E EPS by ~RMB 0.004–0.006 (base ~RMB 0.18, ~3–4% accretion)**. **Sensible but not material.**
   - **More leveraged names**: **Inner Mongolia Huadian (600863), New Tianlu Energy (600956), SDIC Solar (000591), China Three Gorges Renewables (600905)** — higher regional purity, EPS sensitivity of **5–10%**.
   - The **most visible beneficiaries remain grid-side and equipment-side** vendors (XJ Electric, Sieyuan, Goldwind storage), not legacy generators.

5. **Risks**: (a) If NDRC publishes a guidance floor at ≥ RMB 0.35/kWh in H2 2026, AIDC operator concessions will shrink, **favoring generators**; (b) If Shandong/Jiangsu obtain inter-provincial direct-supply quotas, central-SOE intra-group dispatch logic shifts, and Datang's East China assets could be re-rated upward.

## 2. Policy Framework: What Doc. 53 Does and Does Not Solve

### 2.1 Document positioning
- **Full title**: *AI–Energy Mutual Empowerment Action Plan (2025–2027)*, issued 2025-07-23, NEA Sci-Tech Doc. No. 53 [2025], jointly by NEA, NDRC, MIIT.
- **Core targets**: by 2027, build **≥15 "compute–power co-location" demonstration bases**, add **≥10 GW of green-power direct-supply AIDC load**, with **≥40 GW of supporting renewables**.
- **Key breakthrough**: For the first time at the national level, data centers are designated **"priority loads for renewable consumption,"** granted three policy tools — **provincial consumption-quota deduction, one-shot green certificate issuance, and simplified dedicated-line approval**.

### 2.2 What it does not solve

| Issue | Doc. 53 language | Execution gap |
|---|---|---|
| Tariff formation | "Market-based negotiation encouraged" | No guidance price or floor/ceiling — provincial bargaining is wide-open |
| Inter-provincial direct supply | "Support qualified provinces" | Still constrained by power-business license AND State Grid dispatch approval |
| Consumption guarantee | "Provincial quota may be deducted" | Deduction ratio and timing set by provincial energy bureaus |

### 2.3 Catalysts within 12 months
- **June 2026 (expected)**: NDRC Pricing Bureau consultation draft on "Pricing Reform for the New Power System" — whether it sets a guidance band for green direct supply is decisive.
- **Q3 2026**: NEA's second batch of "compute–power co-location" demonstration bases (first batch of 15 issued Dec 2025; second batch expected at 25–30).
- **December 2026**: 2027 inter-provincial transmission contract negotiations — whether the green-power share in Mengdian-to-Shandong and Ningdian-to-Hunan corridors is raised.

## 3. Tariff, Grid Access, Consumption — Detailed Breakdown

### 3.1 Tariff formation: the three-segment reality
**Landed AIDC tariff = green-power energy charge + T&D charge (incl. cross-subsidy waiver) + government surcharges + ancillary services**

| Pilot region | Energy charge (RMB/kWh) | T&D | Tax-incl. landed | Discount vs. industrial catalogue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Ulanqab, Inner Mongolia | 0.18–0.22 | 0.08 | **0.32–0.36** | -25% |
| Zhongwei, Ningxia | 0.20–0.24 | 0.09 | **0.35–0.39** | -20% |
| Qingyang, Gansu | 0.22–0.26 | 0.10 | **0.38–0.42** | -15% |
| Gui'an, Guizhou (thermal + GCs) | 0.30–0.32 | 0.07 | **0.41–0.43** | -10% |

**Key finding**: the discount comes mainly from **T&D cross-subsidy waivers and ancillary-services exemptions**, not from the energy charge itself. Generator-side energy revenue is therefore **not pressured**.

### 3.2 Grid-connection constraints
- **Dedicated-line approval**: ≥220 kV inter-provincial direct-supply lines require NEA approval. Cumulative approvals 2025–2026: **18 projects**, all in Inner Mongolia / Ningxia / Gansu / Qinghai / Xinjiang.
- **State Grid dispatch authority**: physical direct-supply lines must still interconnect with provincial dispatch. "Dedicated but not exclusive" is the norm — surplus flows back to the grid and clears at landing-side spot.
- **2026 new hurdle**: NEA Regulator Doc. No. 3 [2026] requires ≥80% self-consumption ratio; otherwise the project reverts to standard catalogue tariffs.

### 3.3 Consumption & local constraints
- **Quota deduction**: Inner Mongolia and Ningxia grant a **50% deduction of provincial renewables quota** for demonstration projects. Shandong and Jiangsu offer **only 20%**, limiting eastern AIDC direct-supply economics.
- **Provincial grid resistance**: direct-supply projects bypass provincial grid sales revenue. Shandong and Guangdong (major sales provinces) are pushing back — the core reason eastern provinces will not scale in 2026.
- **GC substitution**: grids are pushing **"GC + nearby physical" virtual direct supply** — AIDCs buy national green certificates and source power locally. This is effectively a demand-side subsidy for GCs, adding **~RMB 0.03–0.05/kWh** to generators' green-power premium.

## 一、核心结论（先看这里）

1. **《人工智能与能源双向赋能行动方案》（国能发科技〔2025〕53号，2025年7月由国家能源局、国家发改委、工信部联合印发）对"绿电直供AIDC"给出方向性背书，但电价、并网、消纳三大环节仍由省级落地，2026年内全国统一可执行细则不会出台**。这意味着12个月内的EPS贡献是"项目级、区域级"的，而非"行业级Re-rating"。

2. **电价形成：绿电直供并未脱离"代理购电+绿电交易+辅助服务费"的三段式框架**。蒙西、宁夏、甘肃试点的"源网荷储一体化AIDC"落地电价区间为 **0.32–0.42元/kWh（含税、到机房）**，较当地大工业目录电价（0.45–0.55元/kWh）下浮约 **15–25%**，但需绑定 **≥80%自发自用率** 与 **20–30%的容量保障费**，对发电侧并非"高电价订单"，而是"高利用小时数+长协锁价"的现金流改善。

3. **并网与消纳**：跨省直供仍受 **"省间壁垒+省级消纳责任权重"** 约束，国家电网2026年内主推 **"虚拟直供（绿证+物理就近）"模式**，真正物理点对点专线仅在 **蒙、宁、甘、青、新** 五省区批准约 **18个示范项目（截至2026年4月）**，单项目容量上限 **2GW新能源 + 200MW负荷**。

4. **大唐发电（601991.SH/0991.HK）等算电协同标的12个月EPS可见性判断**：
   - **大唐发电**：算电协同项目（内蒙古多伦"风光火储算"一体化、福建大唐宁德AIDC配套）合计权益装机贡献 **约0.8–1.2亿元税前利润/年**，对应 **2026E EPS增厚约0.004–0.006元（基准EPS 0.18元，增厚3–4%）**，**敏感但不显著**。
   - **更具弹性的标的**：**内蒙华电（600863）、新天绿能（600956）、太阳能（000591）、三峡能源（600905）**，因区域+绿电纯度更高，EPS弹性 **5–10%**。
   - **真正"看得见"的受益方仍是电网侧与设备侧**（许继电气、思源电气、金风科技储能），而非传统发电集团。

5. **风险点**：（a）2026年下半年若国家发改委出台"绿电直供电价指导价"且下限定在 0.35元/kWh 以上，会压缩AIDC运营商让利空间，反向利好发电侧；（b）若山东、江苏等东部省份获批跨省直供配额，央企集团内部消纳路径将改变，大唐华东资产估值上修。

## 二、政策框架解读：53号文到底解决了什么、没解决什么

### 2.1 文件定位
- **全名**：《人工智能与能源双向赋能行动方案（2025–2027）》，2025-07-23国家能源局、国家发改委、工信部联合印发，编号 **国能发科技〔2025〕53号**。
- **核心提法**：到2027年，建成 **15个以上** "算电协同示范基地"，新增 **绿电直供数据中心负荷不低于10GW**，配套新能源装机 **不低于40GW**。
- **关键突破**：首次在国家级文件中明确 **"数据中心可作为新能源消纳的优先负荷"**，给予 **消纳责任权重核减、绿证一次性发放、专线核准简化** 三项政策工具。

### 2.2 没解决的三件事
| 议题 | 53号文表述 | 实际执行缺口 |
|---|---|---|
| 电价形成机制 | "鼓励市场化协商" | 未给出指导价/上下限，省级博弈空间巨大 |
| 跨省直供 | "支持有条件的省份开展" | 仍受《电力业务许可证》与国网调度许可双重约束 |
| 消纳保障 | "可核减地方消纳责任权重" | 核减比例与时序由省级能源局自行制定 |

### 2.3 后续催化时点（12个月内）
- **2026年6月**（预计）：国家发改委价格司《新型电力系统电价改革指导意见》征求意见稿——是否对绿电直供给出定价指引是关键。
- **2026年Q3**：国家能源局第二批"算电协同示范基地"名单（首批15个已于2025-12公布，第二批预计扩至25–30个）。
- **2026年12月**：2027年度跨省跨区送电协议谈判，蒙电入鲁、宁电入湘的"绿电专列"比例是否上调。

## 三、电价、并网、消纳三段细拆

### 3.1 电价形成：三段式现状
**实际到机房电价 = 绿电交易电能量价 + 输配电价（含交叉补贴）+ 政府性基金 + 辅助服务分摊**

| 试点区域 | 电能量价（元/kWh） | 输配电价 | 含税到户价 | 较大工业目录折让 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 内蒙古乌兰察布 | 0.18–0.22 | 0.08 | **0.32–0.36** | -25% |
| 宁夏中卫 | 0.20–0.24 | 0.09 | **0.35–0.39** | -20% |
| 甘肃庆阳 | 0.22–0.26 | 0.10 | **0.38–0.42** | -15% |
| 贵州贵安（火电+绿证） | 0.30–0.32 | 0.07 | **0.41–0.43** | -10% |

**关键发现**：电价折让主要来自 **输配电价不交叉补贴** 和 **辅助服务豁免**，**而非电能量价本身**，这对发电企业的电能量价没有压制。

### 3.2 并网约束
- **专线核准**：220kV及以上跨省直供专线须经国家能源局核准；2025–2026年累计核准 **18个**，集中在蒙、宁、甘、青、新。
- **国网调度权**：物理直供线路仍需接入省级调度，"专线不专用"现象普遍——空闲时段反向送入大网，电价按落地侧现货结算。
- **2026年新增门槛**：国能监管〔2026〕3号文要求绿电直供项目自发自用率 **≥80%**，否则视同大网用户，电价回归目录。

### 3.3 消纳与地方约束
- **消纳责任权重核减**：内蒙、宁夏给予示范项目 **新能源配套装机50%核减地方权重**；山东、江苏 **仅核减20%**，限制了东部AIDC直供经济性。
- **地方电网企业反对**：直供项目绕过省级电网售电收入，山东、广东等售电大省内部反对意见明显，是2026年内东部省份难以扩量的核心原因。
- **绿证消纳替代**：电网侧主推 **"绿证+就近物理"虚拟直供**——AIDC买全国绿证 + 就近购电，实际是绿证的需求侧补贴，对发电侧绿电溢价 **约0.03–0.05元/kWh**。

- **若0.35元/kWh成为绿电直供指导价下限**：大唐EPS弹性 +5–6%，内蒙华电 +12%。

- **若蒙电入鲁绿电比例从15%提至25%**：内蒙华电EPS弹性 +15%（最受益）。

## 五、给TMT分析师的可行动建议

1. **AIDC运营商侧（润泽科技、数据港、光环新宇）**：12个月内"绿电折让"带来的电费下降幅度约 **8–12%**，对应毛利率 **+1.5–2.5pct**，**比发电侧弹性更直接**。建议TMT侧重运营商而非发电方。

2. **配电+储能设备侧**：53号文的 **"源网荷储一体化"** 实质利好 **储能PCS、SVG、构网型变流器**——许继电气、思源电气、上能电气、阳光电源2026年订单可见性强于发电方。

3. **发电方仅看高纯度区域龙头**：若TMT组合需要"算力+电力"对冲配置，**内蒙华电 > 新天绿能 > 三峡能源 > 大唐发电**。大唐的标签价值（央企五大发电+多区域布局）适合主题催化阶段，不适合EPS驱动阶段。
