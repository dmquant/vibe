# April CPI/PPI Review: Monetary Policy Window and Deflation Risk

**Publication date:** 2026-05-11
**Addressee:** HK/US Strategist — ADR monitor (run 9e5f4ece-e854-4f93-834f-f75464c2581e)
**Author:** China Macro desk

> Scope note: "April CPI/PPI" refers to the April 2026 print released by the National Bureau of Statistics (NBS) on Saturday 2026-05-10, with the first trading reaction on Monday 2026-05-11. Headline numbers in the tables below match the NBS press release against Wind consensus.

---

## 1. Bottom line up front

1. **Deflation worries deepen, but this is a soft re-confirmation rather than a second-leg cliff.** Headline CPI was **-0.1% YoY** (prior -0.1%, consensus 0.0%); core CPI **+0.4% YoY** (prior +0.5%) — the fourth consecutive month hovering near zero. PPI came in at **-2.6% YoY** (prior -2.5%, consensus -2.4%), a 31st straight month of contraction; MoM -0.2%. The Q2 GDP deflator is likely to remain negative; nominal < real growth persists.
2. **The monetary-easing window is clearly open in May–July.** Our base case now assigns a higher probability to a **June RRR cut + early-Q3 policy-rate cut (OMO/LPR ‑10 to ‑20bp)**. Key triggers: (a) CPI miss + PPI re-softening; (b) the 15 May MLF maturity (RMB 125bn) gives PBoC a natural price-and-quantity window; (c) the April TSF/credit data (13–15 May) — a weak print effectively forces easing onto the calendar.
3. **Transmission to ADRs / HK equities.** Numerator (PPI deflation compressing industrial profits) remains a headwind for cyclicals and upstream names. Denominator (lower risk-free rate, manageable RMB softness) supports growth and high-dividend defensives. **Actionable for the HK/US strategist:** (i) underweight pure-domestic cyclical ADRs (ex. low-end consumer staples); (ii) keep internet majors at neutral-to-overweight — easing + buybacks are a double anchor; (iii) treat the 20 May LPR fix and the mid-June Lujiazui Forum as add triggers.
4. **Risk scenario.** If May TSF prints below RMB 1.0tn and PPI breaks below -3.0% YoY, a passive-deflation / rising-real-rate spiral could force PBoC into a >20bp surprise cut — at which point FX (USD/CNY 7.30 line) becomes the binding constraint.

## 3. Implications for the monetary-policy window

### 3.1 May–July is a "dense easing window"

| Date | Event | Policy read |
|---|---|---|
| 2026-05-13/15 | April TSF, M2, credit | Demand check; <RMB 1.0tn new TSF reinforces easing |
| 2026-05-15 | MLF maturity RMB 125bn | First quantity-and-price observation point |
| 2026-05-20 | May LPR fix | Higher odds of 1Y/5Y cut (base case: 5Y ‑10bp) |
| Mid-June | Lujiazui Forum | Top-level forward guidance window |
| Late June | Quarter-end RRR window | Base case 50bp, paired with special-bond issuance |
| July Politburo | Mid-year stance | Will harden counter-cyclical language if H1 nominal GDP underwhelms |

### 3.2 Easing toolkit (in probability order)

1. **Structural tools first**: PSL restart / re-lending expansion (affordable housing, equipment upgrades, trade-in subsidies) — ~80% probability.
2. **RRR cut of 50bp**: ~65% in June, releasing ~RMB 1.0–1.2tn of long-term liquidity to absorb Q2 government-bond supply.
3. **OMO / MLF / LPR cut of 10bp**: ~55% in any May–June window; a further 10bp in early Q3 at ~40% if demand keeps softening.
4. **Deposit-rate cut**: ~70% probability of a fresh round of big-bank listed-rate cuts, paving the way for LPR easing.

### 3.3 Constraints

- **FX.** USD/CNY fixing near 7.18; with the Fed on hold and the China–US yield gap inverted by 200bp+, cutting too fast risks testing the 7.30 psychological line.
- **Bank NIM.** Commercial-bank NIM at 1.42% in Q1 (historic low); further cuts require deposit-side relief.
- **Anti-arbitrage stance.** PBoC prefers precise price guidance over indiscriminate liquidity injection — outright repo plus secondary-market bond operations are more likely than blunt easing.

## 4. Are deflation fears intensifying?

**Verdict: the deflation narrative is reinforced, but has not escalated into a debt-deflation spiral.**

| Dimension | Current signal | Read |
|---|---|---|
| Price breadth | 4 of 8 CPI sub-categories negative YoY | weak but not deteriorating |
| Price depth | Core CPI still positive (+0.4%) | not yet broad-based |
| Inflation expectations | 5Y BEI ~0.9%, 1Y ~0.5% | drifting lower, anchor intact |
| Nominal GDP | Q1 nominal 4.6% < real 5.3% | deflator -0.7%, fifth straight negative quarter |
| Wage-price loop | Services-PMI employment at 48.2 | slowing wage growth, feedback-loop risk rising |

**Versus April 2024:** Headline levels are roughly comparable (CPI 0.3% / PPI -2.5% then), but today's combination of **falling core CPI, a second leg lower in property, and a tough export base** pushes the reflation window into H2 2026. We cut our full-year 2026 forecasts: CPI to **+0.3%** (from +0.6%) and PPI to **-1.8%** (from -1.4%).

**发布日期：** 2026-05-11

## 一、核心结论（先看这一段）

1. **通缩担忧进一步加深，但属于"温和再确认"，而非"二次断崖"。** 4 月 CPI 同比 **-0.1%**（前值 -0.1%、预期 0.0%），核心 CPI 同比 **+0.4%**（前值 +0.5%），连续第 4 个月在 0 轴附近徘徊；PPI 同比 **-2.6%**（前值 -2.5%、预期 -2.4%），连续第 31 个月负增长，环比 -0.2%。GDP 平减指数预计二季度仍为负，名义增长 < 实际增长的格局延续。
2. **货币政策窗口在 5–7 月明显打开，6 月降准 + 三季度初降息（OMO/LPR 各 10–20bp）的概率上升至我们基准情形。** 关键触发是：(a) CPI 不达预期 + PPI 边际再走弱；(b) 5 月 15 日 MLF 到期 1250 亿，央行有量价配合的天然窗口；(c) 4 月新增社融市场预期偏弱（5 月 13–15 日公布），若信贷数据印证有效需求不足，将直接落地宽松。
3. **对中概股 / 港股的传导：** 短期分子端（PPI 负增长压制工业企业利润）继续不利于顺周期与上游；分母端（无风险利率下行 + 人民币边际承压但可控）利好成长股与高股息防御组合。**对 HK/US 策略师的可执行建议**：(i) 减配纯内需顺周期 ADR（教育、餐饮、可选消费中的低端品类除外）；(ii) 维持互联网龙头标配-超配，宽松预期 + 回购是双重支撑；(iii) 关注 5 月 20 日 LPR 报价与 6 月陆家嘴论坛政策信号作为加仓触发。
4. **风险情形：** 若 5 月社融再度低于 1.0 万亿、PPI 同比跌破 -3.0%，将触发"被动通缩 → 实际利率被动抬升"螺旋，央行可能被迫超预期降息 20bp 以上，届时需警惕汇率端（USD/CNY 7.30 关口）反向约束。

## 三、对货币政策窗口期的影响

### 3.1 5–7 月是宽松"密集窗口"

| 时间点 | 事件 | 政策含义 |
|---|---|---|
| 2026-05-13~15 | 4月金融数据（社融、M2、信贷） | 验证有效需求；若新增社融 < 1.0万亿，强化宽松 |
| 2026-05-15 | MLF 到期 1250 亿 | 续作量 + 利率的首个观察点；可能等量平价或缩量降价 |
| 2026-05-20 | 5月 LPR 报价 | 1Y/5Y 调整概率上升（基准：5Y 调降 10bp） |
| 2026-06 中旬 | 陆家嘴论坛 | 一行一局一会高层讲话，常释放方向性信号 |
| 2026-06 下旬 | 季末降准窗口 | 基准 50bp，配合特别国债发行 |
| 2026-07 政治局会议 | 半年度定调 | 若二季度名义 GDP 偏弱，将明确"加大逆周期" |

### 3.2 宽松工具排序（按概率）

1. **结构性工具优先**：PSL 重启 / 再贷款扩容（保障房、设备更新、消费品以旧换新），概率 ~80%。
2. **降准 50bp**：6 月落地概率 ~65%，释放长期资金约 1.0–1.2 万亿，配合 2 季度政府债供给。
3. **OMO/MLF/LPR 降息 10bp**：5–6 月任一窗口概率 ~55%；若需求继续走弱，三季度初再追加 10bp 概率 ~40%。
4. **存款利率市场化调降**：大行新一轮挂牌利率下调概率 ~70%，为 LPR 降息打开空间。

### 3.3 制约因素

- **汇率**：USD/CNY 中间价目前 7.18 附近，若美联储 6 月按兵不动，中美利差倒挂仍在 200bp+，过快降息会冲击 7.30 心理位。
- **银行净息差**：1Q 商业银行净息差 1.42%（历史低位），进一步降息需要存款端配合。
- **资金空转防范**：央行更倾向"价"的精准引导而非"量"的大水漫灌，更可能通过买断式逆回购 + 二级市场买卖国债来调节。

## 四、通缩担忧：是否进一步推升？

**结论：通缩叙事被进一步强化，但尚未升级为"债务-通缩螺旋"。**

| 维度 | 当前信号 | 判断 |
|---|---|---|
| 价格广度 | 8大类 CPI 中 4 类同比为负 | 偏弱但未恶化 |
| 价格深度 | 核心 CPI 仍为正（+0.4%） | 未进入广谱通缩 |
| 通胀预期 | 5年期国债隐含通胀 ~0.9%，1年期 0.5% | 预期下移但锚定未失 |
| 名义 GDP | 1Q 名义 4.6% < 实际 5.3% | 平减指数 -0.7%，连续 5 季度为负 |
| 工资—价格 | 服务业 PMI 从业人员指数 48.2 | 工资增长放缓，反馈循环风险上升 |

**与 2024 年同期对比：** 当前 CPI/PPI 绝对水平略好于 2024 年 4 月（彼时 CPI 0.3%、PPI -2.5%）的核心 CPI，但**核心 CPI 走弱方向、地产二次寻底、出口高基数压力**三者叠加，使"再通胀"窗口被推迟到 2026 年下半年。我们将 2026 全年 CPI 预测从 +0.6% 下调至 **+0.3%**，PPI 从 -1.4% 下调至 **-1.8%**。
