# China National Carbon Price Above RMB 80/t: Cost Pass-Through to Manufacturing and the Marginal Impact on PPI

**Recipient**: Utilities Analyst · Carbon Price Tracker (run 35ba6188-37f1-4e2b-b90a-23782ad5e83f)
**Author**: China Macro Analyst
**Date**: 2026-05-11

---

## 1. Headline Conclusions (Read First)

1. **At the current RMB 80–85/t, the direct marginal contribution of the national carbon price to PPI is limited — roughly +0.05 to +0.12 percentage points (annualised).** The national ETS still primarily covers power generation, free allocation under the benchmarking method remains above 95%, and compliance entities only pay for emissions *above* the benchmark. Effective per-MWh carbon cost is therefore far below "carbon price × emission intensity".
2. **The dominant pass-through path is carbon price → power tariff → eight energy-intensive industries → mid/down-stream manufacturing.** But coal-fired on-grid tariffs are still capped within a ±20% band around the benchmark, so only ~30–40% of the carbon cost makes it through the tariff bottleneck — this is the key reason PPI elasticity is muted today.
3. **The real inflection sits in H2 2026 onward.** Cement, steel and aluminium are now in their second compliance cycle inside the national ETS. If the benchmark is tightened by 3–5% **and** CBAM enters its definitive period on 1 Jan 2026, the marginal contribution to PPI can be revised up to **+0.20 to +0.35 ppt**, making it a non-negligible driver of the 2026H2–2027 PPI print.
4. **Structural effects matter more than headline effects.** Watch the widening scissor between energy-intensive upstream (aluminium, cement, crude steel, ammonia, soda ash, flat glass) and mid/down-stream (machinery, white goods, auto parts). For A-shares, the read-across is "PB re-rating in energy-intensives + gross-margin compression downstream", **not** a simple reflation narrative.

## 3. Pass-Through Chain and Elasticity

### 3.1 Step 1 — Carbon Price → Coal-Fired Power Unit Cost

- Coal units emit ~**0.85 kg CO₂/kWh** (ultra-supercritical) to ~**0.95 kg/kWh** (sub-critical average).
- **Effective compliance cost** = (actual emissions − benchmark) × carbon price. With the current power-sector benchmark at ~0.82 kg/kWh, **net paid emissions are only 0.03–0.13 kg/kWh**.
- At RMB 82/t and 0.08 kg/kWh net emissions: **incremental carbon cost ≈ RMB 0.0066/kWh**, i.e. ~**1.6%** of the coal-fired benchmark tariff (RMB 0.40/kWh).
- If the benchmark is tightened 5% in H2 2026, net paid emissions rise to 0.12 kg/kWh and the per-kWh cost rises to ≈ RMB 0.010/kWh (2.5%).

### 3.2 Step 2 — Tariff → Eight Energy-Intensive Sectors (Cost-Share Weighted)

| Sector | Power cost / total cost | kWh per unit output | Δcost at +RMB 0.0066/kWh | Δunit cost | PPI sub-weight (est.) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Electrolytic aluminium | 35–40% | 13,500/t | +RMB 89/t | ~+0.5% | 1.2% |
| Cement | 10–15% (incl. captive coal) | 90/t purchased | +RMB 0.6/t purchased + RMB 6/t captive | ~+2.0% (incl. direct compliance) | 1.5% |
| Crude steel (BF-BOF) | 5–8% power + direct emissions | — | Direct compliance +RMB 30–45/t | ~+0.8–1.2% | 4.8% |
| Synthetic ammonia / urea | 12–18% | 1,000/t | +RMB 6.6/t | ~+0.3% | 0.6% |
| Flat glass | 8–12% | 180/t case-weight | +RMB 1.2/t + fuel compliance | ~+0.6% | 0.3% |
| Soda ash | 15–20% | 320/t | +RMB 2.1/t | ~+0.3% | 0.4% |

### 3.3 Step 3 — Upstream → Mid/Down-stream (Input-Output Approach)

- Using "total consumption coefficients" from the NBS 2020 input-output table: the six upstream energy-intensive groups have a weighted **total consumption coefficient of ~0.06–0.09** on mid/down-stream manufacturing.
- Mechanically, a 1% rise in upstream ex-factory prices lifts mid/down-stream ex-factory prices by ~**0.06–0.09%** under full pass-through.
- In reality, downstream segments (machinery, white goods, auto parts) have been squeezed in 2024–2025 by the "anti-involution" push, leaving them with weak pricing power. **Realised pass-through is ~40–60%.**

### 3.4 Top-Down Aggregate: Marginal Contribution to PPI YoY

| Scenario | Carbon price | Benchmark | Direct (energy-intensive) | Indirect (downstream) | **Total** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Base (current 2026) | RMB 80/t | Hold | +0.06 ppt | +0.03 ppt | **+0.09 ppt** |
| Central (H2 2026) | RMB 90/t | -3% | +0.12 ppt | +0.06 ppt | **+0.18 ppt** |
| Tight (2027) | RMB 110/t | -5% + CBAM | +0.20 ppt | +0.13 ppt | **+0.33 ppt** |
| Extreme (2028) | RMB 150/t | -8% + 10% auction | +0.35 ppt | +0.22 ppt | **+0.57 ppt** |

> All figures are the marginal lift to **PPI YoY** (annualised), excluding oil, copper and other commodity-cycle drivers.

## 4. Constraints and Three Key Uncertainties

1. **Pace of power-market reform.** If the ±20% coal-tariff band is widened (some provinces already piloted ±30% in 2025), pass-through rises. Conversely, if NDRC steps up administrative caps on end-user tariffs, more carbon cost is absorbed on the balance sheets of the gencos (Huaneng, Datang, Huadian).
2. **Allowance-allocation cadence.** The second compliance cycle (covering 2025 emissions) closes by end-2026. MEE consulted in March on raising the auctioned share. **A 5% auction share in 2026** would imply an additional RMB 8–12/t embedded in the effective carbon cost.
3. **CBAM linkage.** CBAM enters its definitive phase on 1 Jan 2026, covering steel, aluminium, cement, fertilisers, electricity and hydrogen. Chinese exporters can offset using domestic carbon already paid — which in turn raises firms' willingness to pay for more emissions onshore, lifting realised PPI elasticity from the supply side.

# 全国碳价站上 80 元/吨：制造业成本传导与 PPI 边际贡献测算

**对接对象**：公用事业分析师 · 碳价追踪（run 35ba6188-37f1-4e2b-b90a-23782ad5e83f）
**分析师**：China Macro Analyst
**日期**：2026-05-11

---

## 一、核心结论（先看这里）

1. **当前 80–85 元/吨的碳价对 PPI 的"直接"边际贡献非常有限，量级在 +0.05 至 +0.12 个百分点（年化）之间**。原因是全国碳市场覆盖范围仍以电力为主，配额免费发放比例（基准线法）在 95%+，企业实际履约只需为"超基准排放"付费，单位电量的实际碳成本远低于"全口径碳价 × 排放强度"。
2. **传导路径以"电价 → 八大高耗能行业 → 中下游制造业"为主**，但现行煤电上网电价仍受"基准价 ±20%"浮动机制约束，碳成本只能在浮动区间内被吸收，**电价端的传导率经验上不超过 30–40%**，是当前 PPI 弹性偏低的核心原因。
3. **真正值得关注的是 2026 下半年扩容节点**：水泥、钢铁、电解铝纳入全国碳市场已进入第二个履约周期，**若叠加配额收紧（基准线下调 3–5%）、CBAM 2026 年 1 月正式征收**，碳成本对 PPI 的边际贡献可上修至 **+0.20 至 +0.35 个百分点**，成为 2026H2–2027 PPI 同比读数的非忽略项。
4. **结构性影响 > 总量影响**：建议关注上游高耗能（电解铝、水泥、粗钢、合成氨、纯碱、平板玻璃）相对中下游（机械、家电、汽车零部件）的剪刀差扩张，对 A 股的含义是"高耗能 PB 修复 + 中下游毛利承压"，而非简单的"再通胀"叙事。

## 三、传导链条与弹性测算

### 3.1 第一段：碳价 → 火电度电成本

- 燃煤机组单位电量 CO₂ 排放约 **0.85 kg/kWh**（超超临界）至 **0.95 kg/kWh**（亚临界平均）。
- 但企业**实际履约成本** = (实际排放 − 基准线配额) × 碳价。当前供电基准线约 0.82 kg/kWh，**净付费排放仅 0.03–0.13 kg/kWh**。
- 以 82 元/吨、净排放 0.08 kg/kWh 计算：**度电增量碳成本 ≈ 0.0066 元/kWh**，约占燃煤标杆电价（0.40 元/kWh）的 **1.6%**。
- 若 2026H2 基准线下调 5%，净付费排放升至 0.12 kg/kWh，度电成本升至 ≈ 0.010 元/kWh（占比 2.5%）。

### 3.2 第二段：电价 → 八大高耗能行业（成本占比加权）

| 行业 | 电力成本占总成本 | 单位产品耗电（kWh） | 80 元碳价下度电+0.66 分情景 | 单位产品成本影响 | PPI 子项权重（估） |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 电解铝 | 35–40% | 13,500/吨 | +89 元/吨 | 约 +0.5% | 1.2% |
| 水泥 | 10–15%（含自备燃煤） | 90/吨（外购电）| +0.6 元/吨；自备煤再 +6 元/吨 | 约 +2.0%（含直接排放履约）| 1.5% |
| 粗钢（长流程） | 5–8%（电）+ 直接排放 | — | 直接履约 +30–45 元/吨 | 约 +0.8–1.2% | 4.8% |
| 合成氨/尿素 | 12–18% | 1,000/吨 | +6.6 元/吨 | 约 +0.3% | 0.6% |
| 平板玻璃 | 8–12% | 180/吨重箱 | +1.2 元/吨 + 燃料履约 | 约 +0.6% | 0.3% |
| 纯碱 | 15–20% | 320/吨 | +2.1 元/吨 | 约 +0.3% | 0.4% |

### 3.3 第三段：上游 → 中下游（投入产出测算）

- 用国家统计局 2020 年投入产出表的"完全消耗系数"近似传导：上游高耗能六大类对中下游制造业的**完全消耗系数加权约 0.06–0.09**。
- 即上游出厂价每涨 1%，中下游制造业出厂价被动上涨约 **0.06–0.09%**（在不考虑下游议价能力削减传导率的"满传导"情形下）。
- 现实中，下游制造业（机械、家电、汽车零部件）2024–2025 年受"反内卷"政策影响，议价能力弱，**实际传导率约 40–60%**。

### 3.4 自上而下汇总：对 PPI 同比的边际贡献

| 情景 | 碳价 | 配额收紧 | 直接（高耗能行业） | 间接（中下游传导） | **合计** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 基准（当前 2026 年） | 80 元/吨 | 维持 | +0.06 pct | +0.03 pct | **+0.09 pct** |
| 中性（2026H2） | 90 元/吨 | -3% | +0.12 pct | +0.06 pct | **+0.18 pct** |
| 偏紧（2027） | 110 元/吨 | -5% + CBAM | +0.20 pct | +0.13 pct | **+0.33 pct** |
| 极端（2028） | 150 元/吨 | -8% + 拍卖比例 10% | +0.35 pct | +0.22 pct | **+0.57 pct** |

> 注：以上均为对 **PPI 同比读数** 的边际拉动（年化），不含原油、铜等大宗周期项。

## 四、约束条件与三大不确定性

1. **电价市场化进度**：若煤电基准价浮动上限突破 ±20%（2025 年部分省份已试点 ±30%），传导率上修；反之若发改委对终端电价行政干预升级，传导率压低，碳成本将更多由发电集团（华能、大唐、华电）资产负债表吸收。
2. **配额发放节奏**：2026 年第二个履约周期（覆盖 2025 排放）将于 2026-12 月底前完成。生态环境部 3 月已就"逐步提高拍卖比例"征求意见，**若 2026 年拍卖比例落地 5%**，相当于碳价隐含上调 8–12 元/吨。
3. **CBAM 衔接**：2026-01 起 CBAM 进入正式征收，覆盖钢、铝、水泥、化肥、电、氢。**中国出口商可凭国内已支付碳价抵扣**——这反过来会推升企业为更多排放支付意愿（从而在国内传导端推升真实 PPI 弹性）。
