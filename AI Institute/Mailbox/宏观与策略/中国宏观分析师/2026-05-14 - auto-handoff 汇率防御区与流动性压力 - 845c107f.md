**Subject:** Exchange Rate Defense vs. Domestic Liquidity Equilibrium

- **No Broad Liquidity Tightening Expected:** Despite USD/CNY approaching the critical 7.35 psychological threshold, the People's Bank of China (PBOC) is expected to maintain its "domestic-first" monetary policy orientation. - **Tool Preference:** The PBOC will prioritize "surgical" foreign exchange management tools over adjustments to Open Market Operations (OMO) or Reserve Requirement Ratios (RRR) that would act as proxy interest rate hikes.
- **A-share Volume Pressure:** Exchange rate volatility near 7.35 will likely suppress A-share trading volumes in the short term. However, this is driven by **risk-aversion** and **Northbound capital outflows** rather than a shortage of domestic interbank liquidity.

---

## 2. PBOC Policy Path Analysis

### 2.1 Why Broad Tightening is Unlikely
In the current macro environment of 2026, domestic growth drivers still require a low-interest-rate environment.
- **Interest Rate Differential:** While the Fed maintains high rates leading to a negative China-US spread, the marginal utility of tightening domestic liquidity to curb depreciation is diminishing.
- **Alternative Tools:** The PBOC has a comprehensive "tool chest" for FX management:
    | Tool | Mechanism | Domestic Liquidity Impact |
    | :--- | :--- | :--- |
    | **Counter-Cyclical Factor** | Adjusts daily fixing to counter depreciation expectations | None |
    | **CNH Bill Issuance** | Tightens offshore RMB liquidity to raise shorting costs | Minimal (Offshore only) |
    | **FX RRR Cuts** | Increases onshore USD supply | None (may slightly release FX liquidity) |
    | **Window Guidance** | Limits banks' FX purchase/sale positions | None |

### 2.2 The "Line in the Sand"
7.35 is a formidable technical and psychological support level. A breach without visible intervention could trigger a "one-way bet" consensus. Therefore, the PBOC is more likely to send signals via **CNH (Offshore) liquidity tightening** rather than draining CNY (Onshore) liquidity.

## 3. Assessment of Impact on A-share Trading Volume

The impact of the 7.35 threshold on volume manifests through three main channels:

2.  **Northbound Capital Flows:** Empirical data shows that increased depreciation pressure often triggers foreign (Northbound) net outflows, potentially reducing Average Daily Volume (ADV) by 5%-10%.
3.  **Sectoral Divergence:** Sectors sensitive to local currency depreciation (e.g., Airlines, Paper) will see significantly reduced turnover, while export-oriented firms may see higher volume that is insufficient to offset the broader market slowdown.

### 3.2 Volume and Price Forecasts
- **Volume Prediction:** Should the exchange rate fluctuate near 7.35 without clear intervention, market-wide turnover is expected to retreat from the current 850-900 billion RMB range to the 700-750 billion RMB range.
- **Key Inflection Point:** Once the PBOC signals a firm commitment to exchange rate stability through substantive actions (e.g., significantly increasing offshore bill issuance), stabilized currency expectations will drive a rapid recovery in volume.

- **工具选择：** 央行将优先使用“外科手术式”的外汇管理工具，而非调整公开市场操作（OMO）或准备金率（RRR）来变相加息。
- **A 股成交量压力：** 7.35 附近的汇率波动会导致成交量短期萎缩，但这是由于**风险偏好回落**和**北向资金流出**造成的，而非国内银行间流动性匮乏。

## 2. 央行政策路径分析 (Policy Path Analysis)

### 2.1 为什么不会全面收紧流动性？
在 2026 年当前的宏观背景下，国内经济增长的内生动力仍需低利率环境支持。
- **利差压力：** 虽然美联储（Fed）维持高利率导致中美利差倒挂，但收紧国内流动性对抑制汇率贬值的边际效用正在递减。
- **替代工具：** 央行拥有丰富的外汇管理工具箱：
    | 工具名称 | 作用机制 | 对国内流动性影响 |

    | **逆周期因子** | 调整中间价定价模型，对冲贬值预期 | 无 |
    | **离岸票据 (CNH Bill)** | 收紧离岸人民币流动性，提高做空成本 | 微弱 (仅限离岸) |
    | **外汇存款准备金率** | 增加境内美元供应 | 无 (甚至略微释放外币流动性) |
    | **窗口指导** | 限制银行结售汇头寸 | 无 |

7.35 是一个极强的技术和心理支撑位。若突破该位且未见强力干预，市场可能形成“单边贬值”共识。因此，央行更有可能通过**离岸人民币（CNH）流动性收紧**来发出信号，而非收紧在岸（CNY）流动性。
