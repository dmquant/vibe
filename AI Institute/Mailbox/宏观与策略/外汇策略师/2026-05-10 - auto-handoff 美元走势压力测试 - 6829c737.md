# Chief Economist Memo: Assessment of FX Spillover Risks Following DXY Breakout Above 108

**Analyst:** FX Strategist
**Date:** May 11, 2026
**Context:** Formal inauguration of Kevin Warsh (Fed Chair/Treasury) and anticipated May 12 CPI jump.

---

## Executive Summary

1.  **The "Warsh Discipline" Paradigm Shift:** DXY breaking above 108 is more than a technical breach; it signals market validation of the "Warsh Paradigm." Unlike the "Flexible Average Inflation Targeting" of the Powell era, Warsh prioritizes the long-term purchasing power of the dollar and aggressive Quantitative Tightening (QT). A May 12 CPI overshoot will likely cement the "death of the Fed Put" in the eyes of the market.
2.  **Emerging Market (EM) Liquidity Squeeze:** Warsh’s intent to aggressively "right-size" the Fed’s $6.8 trillion balance sheet will directly drain offshore USD liquidity. The 108 level on DXY serves as a "pain threshold" for EM sovereign debt servicing and carry-trade stability.
3.  **Step-wise Depreciation Pressure on CNY:** A DXY surge above 108 will force USD/CNY to test the 7.38 - 7.42 range. Under Warsh’s "Purchasing Power First" doctrine, the US may show less tolerance for currency interventions, forcing EM currencies to bear the full brunt of the adjustment.

---

## Transmission Channels of Spillover Risk

### 1. Monetary Policy Divergence & Spread Compression
*   **US Side:** A May 12 CPI jump (assuming Headline > 3.4% YoY) will force Warsh to temporarily pivot from his "AI-driven disinflation" thesis toward a more hawkish stance, potentially pushing the 10Y Treasury yield above 4.85%.
*   **EM Side:** Many EM central banks (e.g., Brazil, Mexico) have already commenced rate-cutting cycles. Rising US yields will cause a rapid narrowing of interest rate differentials, triggering capital flight from EM fixed-income markets.

### 2. Exhaustion of Offshore USD "Shadow Liquidity"
*   Warsh’s aggressive stance on QT will reduce excess reserves and tighten the Eurodollar market. At DXY 108, the value of USD as collateral becomes prohibitively high, leading to a non-linear increase in financing costs for EM corporates.

## Strategic Recommendations

*   **For Corporate Clients:** Immediate hedging for any exposure above DXY 108, particularly for EM corporates with significant USD-denominated liabilities.
*   **For Institutional Investors:** Reduce exposure to "High-Beta" EM currencies. Pivot to defensive safe havens or direct long USD positions against vulnerable peers (BRL, TRY).

**参考日期：** 2026年5月11日
**背景：** Kevin Warsh 正式就职（美联储主席/财政部关键职位）及 5月12日 CPI 预期跳升

---

## 核心结论 (Key Conclusions)

1. **“沃什纪律”引发的范式转移：** DXY 突破 108 不仅仅是技术性破位，更标志着市场对“沃什范式”（Warsh Paradigm）的确认。不同于鲍威尔时代的“灵活均值目标”，沃什更强调货币的购买力保护及激进的缩表（QT）。一旦 5月12日 CPI 超预期，市场将彻底定价“美联储看跌期权”的消失。
2. **新兴市场（EM）流动性挤兑风险：** 沃什计划将美联储资产负债表从 6.8 万亿美元快速压缩至更精简水平，这将直接收紧离岸美元流动性。DXY 108 是新兴市场主权债务偿还和利差交易（Carry Trade）的“痛点阈值”。
3. **人民币（CNY）面临阶梯式贬值压力：** DXY 突破 108 将推升 USD/CNY 考验 7.38 - 7.42 区间。在沃什推崇的“购买力优先”政策下，美国可能减少对强美元的干预，迫使新兴市场本币承担全部调整压力。

---

## 溢出风险传导路径 (Transmission Channels)

### 1. 货币政策脱钩与利差收窄
*   **美国端：** 5月12日 CPI 跳升（假设核心 CPI 同比 > 3.4%）将迫使沃什暂时搁置“AI 驱动的去通胀”假设，转向鹰派立场，推升 10年期美债收益率至 4.85% 以上。
*   **新兴市场端：** 许多 EM 国家（如巴西、墨西哥）已进入降息周期。美债收益率上行将导致利差快速收窄，引发资本撤出 EM 债市。

### 2. 离岸美元“影子流动性”枯竭
*   沃什对缩表（QT）的激进态度将减少超额准备金，收紧欧元美元（Eurodollar）市场。DXY 108 意味着美元作为抵押品的价值过高，导致 EM 企业融资成本呈非线性上升。
