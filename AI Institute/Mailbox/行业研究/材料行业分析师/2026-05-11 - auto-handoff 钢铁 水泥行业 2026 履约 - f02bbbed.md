# 2026 Steel and Cement Carbon-Allowance Cost Scenario

**Date anchor:** This analysis uses the shell date `2026-05-11` as “today.” I did not find a public official copy of the requested `2026-05-06` draft notice by that date. The analysis below is therefore a scenario based on the ESG Analyst’s assumptions, not a statement that the draft has been officially published.

## Conclusions First

1. **The 3% paid-auction share creates a cost floor for high-carbon products, but benchmark deviation drives the spread.** Using the latest trading-day CEA close before `2026-05-11`, `RMB 80.06/tCO2` on `2026-05-08`, a company exactly at benchmark would face a cash-cost floor of about `RMB 4.4/t` for BF-BOF steel and `RMB 1.3/t cement` or `RMB 2.0/t clinker` for new dry-process cement. If a company is 3-7 percentage points worse than benchmark, profit pressure can rise to `RMB 6-12/t steel`; for cement it can enter the `RMB 3-7/t cement` range.[S3]
2. **EAF steel is not the main direct-compliance pressure point.** The current expansion design focuses on direct emissions, and independent EAF producers were not included in the 2024/2025 allocation scope. Even if the 2026 scenario includes them, EAF direct-emission cost is usually only `RMB 0.2-0.4/t steel`. The key swing factor is power-price pass-through from the additional `1.5%` tightening of thermal-power benchmarks plus the 3% auction share; full pass-through would add about `RMB 1.0-1.3/t steel`.[S1][S5]
3. **At the stock level, companies able to offset more than 50% of incremental cost are mainly cement leaders; steel companies are more line-level hedges than company-wide hedges in 2026.** On a company-wide basis, the priority ranking is `Huaxin Cement > Conch Cement`. `Baoshan Iron & Steel` leads in Zhanjiang’s million-tonne near-zero-carbon line and HyCROF trials, but coverage of total group steel volume remains small in 2026; treat it as >50% offset for low-carbon product lines, but below 50% at company level.[S7][S8][S9]
4. **Names facing `RMB 3-7/t product` profit pressure:** On the steel side, prioritize `Shandong Iron and Steel, Xinyu Iron & Steel, Chongqing Iron & Steel, Nanjing Iron & Steel, Shougang, Valin Steel`. On the cement side, prioritize `Shangfeng Cement, Wannianqing, Tapai Group, Tianshan Materials, Jidong Cement`. The ranking reflects BF-BOF/clinker exposure, disclosed low-carbon-process maturity, carbon-asset reserves, earnings buffer, and regional price pass-through.

## Method

| Item | Value and Formula | Comment |
|---|---:|---|
| CEA price | `RMB 80.06/tCO2` | National carbon-market close on `2026-05-08`, the latest trading day before `2026-05-11`.[S3] |
| CCER price | `RMB 84.77/tCO2` | Average transaction price on `2026-05-07`; currently not cheaper than CEA, so it is more a compliance-flexibility and shortage-hedging tool.[S4] |
| Allowance gap ratio | `max(0, E - 0.97B) / E` | `E` is actual emissions intensity and `B` is the 2026 benchmark. If `E=B`, the gap is 3%; if the company is clearly better than benchmark, free allowances can cover part of the auction pressure. |
| Carbon cost per tonne product | `gap ratio × emissions intensity × 80.06` | Excludes management cost, trading slippage, and taxes. |
| Thermal-power pass-through | `0.82 tCO2/MWh × (3%+1.5%) × 80.06 = RMB 2.95/MWh` | Equivalent to about `RMB 1.0-1.3/t steel` for power-intensive EAF steel; the impact on cement grinding is smaller. |
| CCER cap | Historical rule: up to `5%` of compliance obligation | If carried into 2026, CCER can cover the 3% auction component and part of any benchmark shortfall. Since current CCER prices exceed CEA, it should not be treated automatically as cost reduction.[S14] |

## Data Needed from the ESG Team

1. **2026 benchmark `B`:** For steel, obtain the ironmaking + sintering `tCO2/t hot metal` benchmark; for cement, obtain the clinker-line `tCO2/t clinker` benchmark. Without `B`, only ranges are possible.
2. **Verified line-level emissions:** Especially cement clinker lines and steel blast-furnace/sintering lines. Group averages hide tradable allowance surpluses and shortfalls.
3. **Eligible CCER volume and qualification:** Separate self-owned reserves, registered CCER, local carbon credits, green certificates, and internal reductions. Only CCER eligible under national-market offset rules can be used for compliance.
4. **Product-price pass-through:** Auto sheet, silicon steel, high-end pipeline steel, and coordinated core cement regions can pass through part of the cost; common construction materials and commodity steel are more likely to absorb it in profit.

# 2026 年钢铁/水泥碳配额成本情景测算

**日期锚定：**本次分析按 shell 日期 `2026-05-11` 作为“今天”。我未在公开渠道检索到题述 `2026-05-06`《2026 年度全国碳市场配额管理通知（征求意见稿）》正式文本；以下为按 ESG Analyst 给定假设做的情景测算，不把该假设视作已公告事实。

## 结论先行

1. **3% 有偿拍卖把高碳产品的成本底线抬高，但真正拉开差距的是基准线偏离。**按 2026-05-08 全国碳市场 CEA 收盘价 `80.06 元/tCO2` 测算，若企业排放强度等于基准线，长流程钢的吨钢现金成本底线约 `4.4 元/t`，新型干法水泥约 `1.3 元/t水泥` 或 `2.0 元/t熟料`；若企业较基准线落后 3-7 个百分点，长流程钢净利压力会进入 `6-12 元/t钢`，水泥会进入 `3-7 元/t水泥` 区间。[S3]
2. **短流程钢不是本轮直接履约压力的主战场。**现行扩围方案聚焦直接排放，且 2024/2025 年独立电炉短流程钢暂不纳入配额管理；即使 2026 情景纳入，电炉直接排放对应成本通常只有 `0.2-0.4 元/t钢`。主要变量是火电基准再降 `1.5%` 叠加 3% 拍卖后的电价传导，若全额传导，约增加 `1.0-1.3 元/t钢`。[S1][S5]
3. **标的层面，真正能对冲超过 50% 增量成本的公司集中在水泥龙头；钢铁公司更多是“产品线对冲”，不是 2026 年公司口径对冲。**公司口径优先排序：`华新水泥 > 海螺水泥`。`宝钢股份`在湛江百万吨级近零碳产线和 HyCROF 试验上领先，但 2026 年对集团总钢量的覆盖仍小，应按低碳产品线对冲超过 50%、公司口径尚不足 50% 处理。[S7][S8][S9]
4. **3-7 元/吨产品净利压力名单：**钢铁侧优先关注 `山东钢铁、新钢股份、重庆钢铁、南钢股份、首钢股份、华菱钢铁`；水泥侧优先关注 `上峰水泥、万年青、塔牌集团、天山股份、冀东水泥`。排序依据是长流程/熟料敞口、披露的低碳工艺成熟度、碳资产储备、盈利缓冲和区域传导能力。

## 测算框架

| 项目 | 取值与公式 | 说明 |
|---|---:|---|
| CEA 价格 | `80.06 元/tCO2` | 2026-05-08 全国碳市场收盘价；这是 `2026-05-11` 前最近交易日价格。[S3] |
| CCER 价格 | `84.77 元/tCO2` | 2026-05-07 成交均价；短期不比 CEA 便宜，更多是履约灵活性和缺口保险。[S4] |
| 缺口比例 | `max(0, E - 0.97B) / E` | `E` 为实际排放强度，`B` 为 2026 基准线。若 `E=B`，缺口为 3%；若企业明显优于基准，可用免费配额覆盖部分拍卖压力。 |
| 吨产品碳成本 | `缺口比例 × 吨产品排放强度 × 80.06` | 不含管理费用、交易滑点和税费。 |
| 火电传导 | `0.82 tCO2/MWh × (3%+1.5%) × 80.06 = 2.95 元/MWh` | 对高用电电炉钢折合约 `1.0-1.3 元/t钢`，对水泥粉磨影响较小。 |
| CCER 上限 | 历史规则为不超过应清缴配额 `5%` | 若 2026 沿用，CCER 可覆盖 3% 拍卖和部分落后缺口，但当前 CCER 价格高于 CEA，不能简单视作降本。[S14] |

## 需要 ESG 侧补充的数据

1. **2026 基准线 `B`：**钢铁要拿到炼铁+烧结工序 `tCO2/t生铁` 基准；水泥要拿到熟料线 `tCO2/t熟料` 基准。没有 `B`，只能做区间。
2. **企业逐线核查排放：**特别是水泥熟料线、钢铁高炉/烧结线。集团平均值会掩盖可交易配额盈余与缺口。
3. **CCER 可用量和资格：**需区分自有储备、已登记 CCER、地方碳普惠、绿证和内部减排量。只有符合全国市场抵销条件的 CCER 才能用于履约。
4. **产品价格传导：**汽车板、硅钢、高端管线钢、水泥核心区域协同价格可传导一部分；普通建材和普钢更可能直接压净利。
