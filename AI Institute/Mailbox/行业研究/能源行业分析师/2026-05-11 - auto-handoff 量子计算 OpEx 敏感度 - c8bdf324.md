## Priority Conclusions

1. **Using the company’s disclosed “fuel and power” cost as the auditable proxy, a +10% electricity-price shock would reduce 2025 EBIT by about RMB 115.7k.** QuantumCTek disclosed RMB 1.157m of fuel and power cost inside 2025 R&D spending. 2. **The impact is about -2.10% of 2025 EBIT.** EBIT in this note is defined as profit before tax plus interest expense: RMB 5.226m + RMB 0.273m = RMB 5.500m. The RMB 115.7k cost shock equals 2.10% of that EBIT. On an operating-profit cross-check using RMB 4.948m, the impact is 2.34%.
3. **The practical conclusion is low cost sensitivity, but a small profit base makes the percentage look non-trivial.** The absolute hit is only about RMB 0.12m, not enough to change the main drivers of the equity case: quantum communication and quantum computing orders, R&D spending, government grants, and investment income. 4. **Key limitation: the company does not disclose full-scope electricity expense.** The annual report only discloses fuel and power cost within R&D expenses/R&D spending; it does not break out electricity embedded in cost of goods sold, management expenses, or building operations. Therefore this is a disclosed-cost proxy, not an invoice-level company-wide electricity bill. The true sensitivity formula is: `Change in EBIT = - actual annual electricity bill × 10%`.

## 投资含义

对资产配置讨论，建议把国盾量子归为**电价成本敏感度低、利润基数敏感度高**的科技制造/研发型标的，而不是传统高耗能制造标的。公司 2025 年收入 3.10 亿元、研发投入 1.23 亿元，燃料动力费只占收入约 0.37%；电价 +10% 的直接 EBIT 冲击约 11.57 万元，显著低于政府补助、投资收益、量子计算收入确认节奏、应收账款回款和研发投入变化对利润的影响。

但需要注意，2026Q1 公司仍为亏损，利润总额 -1,888.67 万元；若季度订单验收不足，任何固定/半固定成本扰动都会放大亏损率。因此电价不是主变量，但在低 EBIT 年份会影响利润边际。

## 后续需确认

1. 向公司或渠道确认 2025 年实际总电费、用电量、合同电压等级、是否执行两部制、最大需量或容量计费方式。
2. 获取峰/平/谷用电曲线；若量子计算低温设备、实验室空调和洁净/机房负荷偏连续运行，峰谷结构会影响平均电价，但“全电费 +10%”敏感度仍等于实际电费的 10%。
3. 拆分合肥总部自用电与对外出租楼层用电，避免把可转嫁或代收代付电费计入公司 EBIT 敏感度。
