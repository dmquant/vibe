# Temu/Shein Cannibalization Risk to Dollar General Q2

Date anchor: 2026-05-14.

## Conclusions First

1. **Direct cannibalization risk to DG Q2 is low**: In the base case, Temu/Shein should drag Dollar General's Q2 2026 total sales by only **0 to 10 bps**, or roughly **$0-$10 million**; the drag on the substitutable discretionary pool, namely seasonal, home, and apparel, is about **0%-0.5%**. That is not large enough by itself to determine whether DG meets its annual same-store sales guide.
2. **The latest public North America signal is not continued acceleration; it is U.S. cooling**: Sensor Tower data showed Temu U.S. MAU down **30%** in May 2025 versus March 2025, while Shein was down **12%**; Temu U.S. DAU fell **52%** and Shein fell **25%** over the same period. A Similarweb/Investing compilation put Temu U.S. app MAU at **133.6 million** in October 2025, down **28%** YoY; Shein U.S. app MAU was about **25.7 million** in May 2025. Unless the alt-data set shows a clear North America MAU rebound in April-May 2026, Temu/Shein should not be treated as a new major Q2 pressure point for DG. [^cnbc-sensor][^temu-stats][^shein-stats]
3. **DG's exposed category pool is small**: In fiscal 2025, consumables were **82.04%** of DG sales, while seasonal was **10.13%**, home products **5.18%**, and apparel **2.65%**. Temu/Shein compete most directly with apparel, home, and parts of seasonal, which together are only **17.96%** of sales. DG's core food, cleaning, health, and everyday consumables basket depends much more on immediacy and store convenience, making cross-border online substitution low. [^dg-10k]
4. **Even if North America MAU rebounds, the risk ceiling is limited and category-specific**: Using DG Q2 2025 sales of $10.7277 billion as the base, seasonal, home, and apparel totaled **$1.9078 billion**, or **17.8%** of sales. Applying the midpoint of DG's FY2026 sales growth guide, **3.95%**, implies Q2 2026 sales of about **$11.15 billion** and a substitutable category pool of about **$1.98 billion**. Even if Temu/Shein capture **1%-2%** of that pool, the total DG sales drag is only **18-36 bps**, or about **$20-$40 million**, with a rough EPS effect of **$0.02-$0.04**. [^dg-10q-q2][^dg-pr]

目前公开数据支持的结论是：**Temu/Shein 在北美对 DG Q2 的新增蚕食较小，基准影响应低于 10 bps；真正需要监控的是 DG 的 home/seasonal/apparel 是否在 Q2 出现相对 consumables 的异常走弱。** 如果 altdata-analyst 的北美 MAU 序列显示 2026 年 4-5 月 Temu/Shein 已重新加速，则建议把模型敏感性放在 **DG 非食品销售池约 $2.0 billion** 上，而不是全公司销售上。

## 后续跟踪项

- 用 2026 年 4-7 月 Temu/Shein 北美 MAU、DAU、下载、广告投放恢复幅度，验证是否偏离公开“美国降温”基准。
- 跟踪 DG Q2 期间 home products、seasonal、apparel 的同店销售相对 consumables 的 spread；若非食品低于 consumables 超过 **300 bps**，再提高 Temu/Shein 蚕食权重。
- 交叉验证 Consumer Edge/Bloomberg Second Measure 卡交易：关注 former Temu/Shein shoppers 是否流向 dollar stores，还是更多流向 Old Navy、Kohl's、Nordstrom Rack、Savers Value Village。
- 监控关税和 de minimis 规则变化；若 Temu/Shein 价格优势恢复，DG home/seasonal 的压力会先于 consumables 体现。
