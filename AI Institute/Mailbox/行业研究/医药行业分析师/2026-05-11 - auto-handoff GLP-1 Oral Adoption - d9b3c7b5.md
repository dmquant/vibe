# Market-Share Erosion for Injectable GLP-1s After the Oral Semaglutide Launch

## Bottom Line

Using the shell date **2026-05-11** as the anchor, the Q1 2026 launch of oral semaglutide (Wegovy pill) points to **low-single-digit near-term erosion for injectable GLP-1s, not a structural collapse**.

- **Base case for 2026: true cannibalization pressure on injectable GLP-1 prescription share is about 2-4 percentage points**. The basis is that, as of 2026-04-17, Wegovy pill had reached about **207k** weekly TRx, **>2m** cumulative prescriptions and **>1m** patients, but early data indicate roughly **90%** of users were new to the GLP-1 class, implying only about **10%** of oral use came from injectable switching. [Novo Nordisk Q1 2026 investor presentation](https://www.novonordisk.com/content/dam/nncorp/global/en/investors/pdfs/financial-results/2026/Q1-2026-investor-presentation.pdf); [Axios, 2026-02-04](https://www.axios.com/2026/02/04/ozempic-zepbound-wegovy-eli-lilly-glp1)
- **Reported oral share growth will look much larger than actual cannibalization**. Counting every oral prescription as a lost injectable prescription would overstate the hit; the early evidence supports market expansion first and switching second. CVS Caremark expects weight-management GLP-1 use to grow about **25%** in 2026, with oral GLP-1s helping drive interest and utilization. [CVS Caremark 2026 GLP-1 outlook](https://business.caremark.com/content/dam/enterprise/business-caremark/insights/pdfs/2026/2026_glp1_insights_report.pdf)
- **By 2030, oral GLP-1s could erode injectable route-of-administration share by 25-35 percentage points**, but that should not be read as the same percentage of incumbent injectable patients switching off therapy. Novo management expects oral weight-loss drugs to represent **one-third or more** of the overall GLP-1 market by 2030; Structure Therapeutics has framed a more aggressive **25-50%** range. [Reuters via archive, 2026-01-12](https://archive.vn/2026.01.12-211842/https%3A/www.reuters.com/business/healthcare-pharmaceuticals/novo-nordisk-sees-oral-pills-capturing-over-third-glp-1-market-by-2030-2026-01-12/); [Reuters via Yahoo, 2026-01-14](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/structure-expects-pills-capture-25-195957608.html)
- **Investment implication: in 2026, oral semaglutide should not be modeled as a direct replacement shock to Wegovy/Ozempic or Zepbound/Mounjaro injectables.** The more important variables are price, reimbursement access, Lilly’s oral orforglipron ramp, and whether Novo can use the oral entry point to regain new-prescription share.

## Healthcare Analyst Read-Through

Oral semaglutide is not clinically positioned as a full substitute for injectables. In OASIS 4, Wegovy pill 25 mg delivered **16.6%** average weight loss under the trial-product estimand and **13.6%** under the treatment-policy estimand; Novo’s disclosed data for high-dose injectable Wegovy 7.2 mg show about **20.7%** average weight loss. [FDA approval PR](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/fda-approves-novo-nordisks-wegovy-pill-the-first-and-only-oral-glp-1-for-weight-loss-in-adults-302648344.html); [Novo Q1 2026 investor presentation](https://www.novonordisk.com/content/dam/nncorp/global/en/investors/pdfs/financial-results/2026/Q1-2026-investor-presentation.pdf)

The first patients oral semaglutide is likely to capture are not the efficacy-sensitive, stable injectable users. They are more likely to be three marginal groups: GLP-1-naive obesity patients who dislike injections; cash-pay and DTC users sensitive to affordable monthly pricing; and mild-to-moderate patients for whom physicians want a lower-friction entry point into chronic weight management. For the incumbent injectable market, that means **low 2026 share erosion, with pricing competition and channel redesign mattering more than patient switching**.

## Suggested Wording for the Thematic Radar

> After the Q1 2026 launch of oral semaglutide, near-term share erosion for injectable GLP-1s should be modeled as low single digits, with a base case of roughly **2-4 percentage points**. Wegovy pill weekly TRx reached **~207k** by 2026-04-17, but early data suggest about **90%** of users were GLP-1 naive, indicating that oral therapy is expanding the market more than replacing injectables. Medium term, if oral GLP-1s reach **25-35%** route share by 2030, injectable route share will compress meaningfully, but true cannibalization will be lower than the apparent route-share migration.

- Net pricing: Watch whether PBMs keep oral Wegovy at net-price parity with injectables; CVS has already disclosed better-than-expected oral Wegovy net cost and parity with injectables.
- Efficacy segmentation: Higher-dose injectables, CagriSema, retatrutide and other high-efficacy injectable assets could limit oral erosion among severe-obesity patients.

- **2026 年基准情景：注射型 GLP-1 的真实 cannibalization 约为 2-4 个百分点的处方量份额压力**。测算依据是：截至 2026-04-17，Wegovy pill 周度 TRx 约 **207k**、累计处方 **>2m**、患者 **>1m**；但早期数据称约 **90%** 使用者为 GLP-1 新用户，即可归因于注射剂转换的量约 **10%**。[Novo Nordisk Q1 2026 investor presentation](https://www.novonordisk.com/content/dam/nncorp/global/en/investors/pdfs/financial-results/2026/Q1-2026-investor-presentation.pdf)；[Axios, 2026-02-04](https://www.axios.com/2026/02/04/ozempic-zepbound-wegovy-eli-lilly-glp1)

## 医药行业判断

口服 semaglutide 的临床定位并非全面替代注射剂。OASIS 4 中，Wegovy pill 25 mg 在依从治疗估计下平均减重 **16.6%**，治疗政策估计下 **13.6%**；同期高剂量 Wegovy 7.2 mg 注射剂在公司披露中达到约 **20.7%** 平均减重。[FDA approval PR](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/fda-approves-novo-nordisks-wegovy-pill-the-first-and-only-oral-glp-1-for-weight-loss-in-adults-302648344.html)；[Novo Q1 2026 investor presentation](https://www.novonordisk.com/content/dam/nncorp/global/en/investors/pdfs/financial-results/2026/Q1-2026-investor-presentation.pdf)

因此，口服剂型最先侵蚀的不是疗效敏感、已稳定使用注射剂的核心患者，而是三类边际人群：一是恐针或不愿启动注射治疗的新增肥胖患者；二是现金支付和 DTC 渠道中对月度可负担价格敏感的人群；三是医生希望先用低门槛方案启动长期管理的轻中度患者。对既有注射剂市场而言，这意味着 **2026 年份额侵蚀低、价格竞争和渠道重构更重要**。
