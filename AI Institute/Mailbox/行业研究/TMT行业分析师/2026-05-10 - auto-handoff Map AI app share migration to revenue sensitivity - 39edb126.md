# TMT Handoff: AI App Share Migration and Revenue Sensitivity Across Cloud, Search, Ads, and Enterprise Software

Date anchor: `2026-05-10`. All “current / last quarter / this weekend” references are resolved against this date.

## Prioritized Conclusions

1. **Separate “total AI demand” from “platform share migration.”** Total AI demand should be measured through aggregate prompts, search-enabled prompts, API tokens, AI seats/agents, and AI infrastructure backlog. Platform migration should be measured through traffic/share shifts among ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot, and Claude, plus referral share and backend cloud/model-provider mapping. Current evidence supports “expanding total AI demand plus AI-app-layer share redistribution,” not “AI has already broadly impaired Google/Meta ad revenue.”

2. **AI app traffic is large, but it has not converted one-for-one into ad inventory migration.** Similarweb estimates March 2026 monthly visits of roughly 5.7B for `chatgpt.com`, up 28.52% YoY, and roughly 2.6B for `gemini.google.com`, up 283.80% YoY; Google.com still had roughly 86.9B visits, about 15.2x ChatGPT.[Similarweb](https://www.similarweb.com/blog/research/market-research/most-visited-websites/) Meanwhile, StatCounter’s April 2026 global search-engine data shows Google at 90.02%, while its “Google vs ChatGPT” view shows ChatGPT at only 0.41%.[StatCounter Search](https://gs.statcounter.com/search-engine-market-share/all) [StatCounter Google vs ChatGPT](https://gs.statcounter.com/google-vs-chatgpt-market-share)

3. **Share migration inside AI apps is real, but it is primarily a platform/cloud-share issue.** StatCounter’s April 2026 AI chatbot share: ChatGPT 76.85%, Google Gemini 9.00%, Perplexity 7.73%, Microsoft Copilot 3.76%, Claude 2.66%.[StatCounter AI Chatbot](https://gs.statcounter.com/ai-chatbot-market-share) If weekend alt-data shows Gemini or Perplexity accelerating, the first mapping should be to GCP, Google subscriptions, Search retention, and cloud backlog, not a mechanical deduction from Google Search revenue.

4. **Search-ad revenue sensitivity is high, but “AI visits” need a steep discount before being treated as search revenue leakage.** Alphabet Q1 2026: Google Search & other revenue was $60.399B, up 19% YoY; Google advertising was $77.253B; Google Cloud was $20.028B, up 63% YoY, with backlog above $460B.[Alphabet Q1 2026](https://s206.q4cdn.com/479360582/files/doc_financials/2026/q1/2026q1-alphabet-earnings-release.pdf) Therefore, a 100bp move in Google Search revenue equals about $604M per quarter, or $2.42B annualized. But Semrush U.S. clickstream data shows only 34.5% of ChatGPT queries enabled search as of February 2026, and more than 20% of ChatGPT referral traffic went to Google.[Semrush](https://www.semrush.com/blog/chatgpt-search-insights/) Raw AI sessions should not be treated as one-for-one search ad leakage.

5. **Enterprise software AI sensitivity is more investable as an add-on/consumption layer than as near-term seat replacement.** Microsoft FY26 Q3 disclosed AI business ARR above $37B, up 123% YoY, and more than 20M M365 Copilot paid seats; at a rough $30/seat/month list-price assumption, each additional 1M Copilot seats represents $360M of list ARR.[Microsoft FY26 Q3](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/investor/earnings/fy-2026-q3/press-release-webcast) [Microsoft call](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/investor/events/fy-2026/earnings-fy-2026-q3) Salesforce FY26 Q4 disclosed Agentforce ARR of $800M, up 169% YoY, and Agentforce + Data 360 ARR above $2.9B, up more than 200% YoY.[Salesforce FY26 Q4](https://s205.q4cdn.com/626266368/files/doc_financials/2026/q4/CRM-Q4-FY26-Earnings-Press-Release.pdf)

## How to Translate Alt-Data Into Revenue Calls

**Numerator 1: total AI demand.** Track aggregate AI visits, app DAU/WAU, prompts per session, search-enabled prompt share, API tokens, enterprise AI seats, agent work units, and cloud backlog. If these rise together, the first-order implication is positive for compute, cloud, and AI software attach, not necessarily negative for search ads.

**Numerator 2: platform share migration.** Track share across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot, and Claude. ChatGPT still leads, but Gemini is growing faster in web traffic; for Alphabet, that is a mix of AI app share gain, Search defense, and GCP upside. For Microsoft/OpenAI, it is consumer app share pressure, but Azure/OpenAI enterprise/API can still be offset by total demand expansion.

**Denominator: monetizable inventory.** Search revenue risk should only be applied to queries that meet three conditions: web search is enabled, the query has commercial/transactional intent, and the user would otherwise have clicked or converted. Semrush’s 34.5% search-enabled figure is the first discount factor; the fact that more than 20% of referrals go to Google shows some AI use flows back into search/navigation.

## Investment Implications

- **Alphabet:** raw AI traffic migration is not a direct bear case. Search & other was still up 19% in Q1 2026, Google Cloud grew 63%, and Gemini paid MAU grew 40% QoQ. This is currently “internal AI-ification + GCP acceleration + search format migration.” The true risk signal would be Search query/revenue deceleration while Gemini/AI Mode engagement fails to restore ad load or CPC.

- **Microsoft:** the key issue is not whether ChatGPT consumer share loses 1-2pp in a month; it is Microsoft AI ARR, Azure AI capacity, M365 Copilot paid seats, and ARPU. More than 20M Copilot paid seats makes enterprise software attach visible. If AI app share moves toward Gemini/Claude, watch whether Azure AI backlog and OpenAI workloads are diverted toward GCP/AWS.

- **Amazon:** AWS was up 28%, chips run-rate exceeded $20B, and Advertising was up 24%. If AI shopping/referral grows while Amazon Ads stays above 20% growth, AI agents are expanding the product-discovery funnel. If third-party AI agents move product discovery upstream from Amazon’s search box, the first warning should appear in Sponsored Ads query volume / CPC, not total GMV.

- **Meta:** AI is currently an ad-efficiency lever. Q1 2026 ad revenue grew 33%, impressions grew 19%, and average price per ad rose 12%; more than 8M advertisers used genAI creative tools, and the value optimization suite reached a run-rate above $20B. For Meta, near-term AI app share migration risk is lower than the risk that AI capex/infrastructure cost growth pressures margins.

## Follow-Up Checklist for Alt Data Analyst

1. Build an `AI demand index`: ChatGPT + Gemini + Perplexity + Copilot + Claude visits, mobile WAU, prompts/session, search-enabled share, API/token metrics.
2. Build a `platform migration index`: four-week share changes by AI app, with separate flags for Gemini share, Perplexity share, and ChatGPT paid conversion.
3. Build a `monetizable search leakage index`: AI referrals to the web, AI referrals to Google, commercial/transactional prompt mix, and Google Search CPC/paid-click proxies.
4. Build a `cloud endpoint map`: OpenAI/Azure/AWS Trainium, Gemini/GCP, Claude/AWS/Google, and enterprise API volumes, so app-share migration maps into cloud-share migration.
5. Build an `enterprise attach index`: M365 Copilot paid seats, Salesforce Agentforce ARR/deals, ServiceNow Now Assist ACV, and Adobe AI-first ARR; prioritize paid seats/ARR over broad MAU.

## Bottom Line

As of `2026-05-10`, the cleanest interpretation is: AI app usage represents incremental total demand, while AI app share changes represent platform and cloud share migration. Only proven migration of monetizable search/query/ad inventory should flow into downward revisions for Google, Amazon, or Meta ad revenue.

## 如何把另类数据转成收入判断

**分子 1：AI 总需求。** 观察总 AI visits、app DAU/WAU、prompts/session、search-enabled prompt 占比、API tokens、enterprise AI seats、agent work units、cloud backlog。若这些指标同步上升，优先利好 compute、cloud、AI software attach，而不是必然利空搜索广告。

**分子 2：平台份额迁移。** 观察 ChatGPT vs Gemini vs Perplexity vs Copilot vs Claude 的 share。当前 ChatGPT 仍领先，但 Gemini 在 web traffic 上增长更快；这对 Alphabet 是“AI app share gain + Search defensive + GCP positive”的组合，而对 Microsoft/OpenAI 是“consumer app share pressure，但 Azure/OpenAI enterprise/API 仍可由总需求抵消”。

**分母：可货币化库存。** Search revenue 风险应只套到满足三项条件的 query：启用 web search、商业/交易意图、用户原本会点击或转化。Semrush 的 34.5% search-enabled 口径可作为第一道折扣；referrals 中 >20% 流向 Google 说明部分 AI 使用反而回流搜索/导航。

## 投资含义

- **Alphabet：** raw AI traffic migration 不是直接熊点。Search & other Q1 2026 仍 +19%，Google Cloud +63%，Gemini paid MAU +40% QoQ，说明目前是“内部 AI 化 + GCP 加速 + 搜索格式迁移”。真正风险信号是 Search query/revenue 增速掉头，同时 Gemini/AI Mode engagement 增长不能补回广告 load 或 CPC。

- **Microsoft：** 关键不是 ChatGPT consumer share 是否每月丢 1-2pp，而是 Microsoft AI ARR、Azure AI capacity、M365 Copilot paid seats 和 ARPU。Copilot >20M paid seats 对应企业软件 attach 的可见化；但若 AI app share 流向 Gemini/Claude，需观察 Azure AI backlog 与 OpenAI workload 是否被 GCP/AWS 分流。

- **Amazon：** AWS +28%、chips run-rate >$20B、Advertising +24%。如果 AI shopping/referral 增加但 Amazon Ads 同步保持 >20% 增长，说明 AI agents 在扩大商品发现漏斗；若第三方 AI agents 把 product discovery 从 Amazon 搜索框前移，风险会先体现在 Sponsored Ads query volume / CPC，而不是总 GMV。

- **Meta：** 当前 AI 是广告效率杠杆。Q1 2026 ad revenue +33%，impressions +19%，price per ad +12%；AI creative tools 被超 8M advertisers 使用，value optimization suite run-rate 超 $20B。对 Meta 来说，AI app share migration 的短期风险低于“AI capex/infra cost 增速是否侵蚀 margin”。

## 给 Alt Data Analyst 的跟踪清单

1. 建立 `AI demand index`：ChatGPT + Gemini + Perplexity + Copilot + Claude visits、mobile WAU、prompts/session、search-enabled share、API/token 指标。
2. 建立 `platform migration index`：各 AI app share 的 4 周变化，单独标注 Gemini share、Perplexity share、ChatGPT paid conversion。
3. 建立 `monetizable search leakage index`：AI referrals to web、AI referrals to Google、commercial/transactional prompt mix、Google Search CPC/paid-click proxy。
4. 建立 `cloud endpoint map`：OpenAI/Azure/AWS Trainium、Gemini/GCP、Claude/AWS/Google、enterprise API 调用量，把 app share 迁移映射为 cloud share 迁移。
5. 建立 `enterprise attach index`：M365 Copilot paid seats、Salesforce Agentforce ARR/deals、ServiceNow Now Assist ACV、Adobe AI-first ARR；优先看 paid seats/ARR，不看泛 MAU。

## 结论句

截至 `2026-05-10`，最稳妥的判断是：AI 应用使用量代表新增总需求，AI 应用份额变化代表平台和云端份额迁移；只有被证明迁出的、可商业化的 search/query/ad inventory，才应进入 Google/Amazon/Meta 广告收入下修模型。
