# PE Stress Test for NVDA and the Huawei Compute Chain at a 2.30% Real Rate

Anchor date: 2026-05-14. This note treats the “2.30% real rate” as a stress assumption. The U.S. Treasury’s official TIPS curve for 2026-05-13 shows 1.99% for 10Y, 2.48% for 20Y, and 2.74% for 30Y, so 2.30% is not the spot 10Y real yield; it is a higher-risk valuation anchor between the 10Y and long end.[2]

## Priority Conclusions

1. **NVDA: a 2.30% real rate is not a valuation-break event, but it caps the FY2027 PE range around 24-27x.** NVDA closed at $225.83 on 2026-05-13, with a 45.06x TTM PE and 26.37x forward PE; consensus FY2027 EPS is about $8.45 and FY2028 EPS is about $11.45.[3] At a 2.30% real rate, if FY2027 EPS is not revised lower, the reasonable range is roughly $203-228. If the stock de-rates to 21-23x FY2027 PE, the implied price range is $177-194, or about -14% to -22% from the latest close.
2. **NVDA’s defense is earnings delivery, not rates.** NVIDIA FY2026 revenue was $215.9 billion, up 65%; full-year data-center revenue was $193.7 billion, up 68%; Q1 FY2027 guidance calls for $78.0 billion of revenue and assumes no data-center compute revenue from China.[4] If the 2026-05-20 earnings release sustains order visibility, Blackwell/Rubin supply confidence, and margins, a 2.30% real rate mainly limits multiple expansion rather than breaks the AI thesis.
3. **Huawei compute chain: at a 2.30% real rate, move from theme PE back to order-backed PE.** Digital China trades near 21x TTM PE,[7] and a public Hwa Create note implies about 37x/25x 2026E/2027E PE,[8] both of which can still be digested through orders and profit delivery. By contrast, iSoftStone is around 133x 2026E PE,[9] Chengdu Hi-Tech Development is around 212x PE-TTM,[10] Talkweb has a TTM PE above 700x,[11] and Changshan Beiming is close to 900x on a rough 2025 EPS calculation of RMB0.02.[12] These names are much more exposed to long-end real rates and risk appetite.
4. **Allocation conclusion: NVDA screens better than high-PE Huawei-theme equities; within the Huawei chain, keep only names with orders, delivery, and profit all verifiable.** At a 2.30% real rate, acceptable 2026E PE ceilings should be compressed to 25-35x for server/integrator names, 35-45x for high-certainty hardware, connector, cooling, and PCB suppliers, and below 80x for software or operating-platform stories unless cash flow is proven.

# NVDA 与华为算力链在 2.30% 真实利率下的 PE 压力测试

## 优先结论

| 叙事高弹性型 | 软通动力 2026E PE 约 132.9x；AI 相关业务收入占比提升但利润率仍需验证[9] | 承压。真实利率上行时，市场会先要求算力租赁利用率和净利率证明 |
| 高 PE/低 EPS 型 | 高新发展 PE-TTM 约 211.75x；拓维信息 TTM PE 超 700x；常山北明 2025 年 EPS 约 0.02 元[10][11][12] | 高风险。2.30% 真实利率下不宜用主题估值外推，除非出现重大订单与利润跃迁 |

## 给总编的可用表述

热 PPI 后，2.30% 真实利率对 AI 算力股的影响不是线性杀估值，而是把“远期叙事 PE”切回“可验证利润 PE”。NVDA 当前 26.4x forward PE 基本落在压力区间上沿，若 2026-05-20 财报维持 FY2027 EPS 8.45 美元以上的可见度，合理支撑在 203-228 美元；若 PE 回落到 21-23x，风险区间为 177-194 美元。华为算力链更分化，神州数码、华胜天成这类能用订单和利润解释的标的仍可看 25-35x/37x 向 25x 过渡；但软通动力、高新发展、拓维信息、常山北明这类高 PE 或低 EPS 标的，在 2.30% 真实利率下应下调容忍度，等待采购、交付和现金流确认。
