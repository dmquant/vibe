## View on China’s AI Agent Ecosystem

**1. Open source should accelerate the formation of domestic Agent building blocks.** openJiuwen/AgentArts breaks out modules such as Agent Studio, Agent Core, Agent Runtime, DeepSearch, MCP/A2A protocols, Skills, and Team Skills. Combined with Ascend, Huawei Cloud MaaS, Pangu, and third-party models, this can become a domestic controllable Agent reference stack. For customers, the practical value is that each project no longer needs to rebuild permissions, memory, tool access, logs, evaluation, and security sandboxing from scratch.

**2. Huawei Cloud is more likely to monetize through cloud resources, MaaS, industry solutions, and partner revenue sharing.** The free AgentArts basic edition lowers developer barriers, but real monetization will occur in model calls, inference compute, enterprise runtime, private deployment, MCP/plugin/Skill marketplaces, and industry implementation. China’s cloud infrastructure and AI cloud demand were already being pulled by generative AI in 2025. Canalys reported that Mainland China cloud infrastructure spending reached US$11.6 billion in 1Q25, up 16% year over year, with Huawei Cloud at 18% share. IDC’s 2H25 China public-cloud IaaS tracker showed a US$15.97 billion market, up 20.9% year over year, with competition shifting from resource scale to full-stack AI capability. [Canalys](https://canalys.com/newsroom/china-cloud-market-q1-2025), [IDC blog](https://www.idc.com/resource-center/blog/%E5%85%AC%E6%9C%89%E4%BA%91iaas%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA%E5%A2%9E%E9%80%9F%E9%87%8D%E5%9B%9E20%E7%9A%84%E8%83%8C%E5%90%8E%EF%BC%9A-ai-%E7%BA%A2%E5%88%A9%E8%8A%B1%E8%90%BD%E8%B0%81%E5%AE%B6%EF%BC%9F/)

**3. The application layer will diverge: open source compresses pricing for generic builders, while vertical workflows and data assets appreciate.** If AgentArts/openJiuwen becomes a de facto standard, vendors selling only low-code Agent builders will face valuation pressure. Vendors controlling financial trading, ERP, collaborative office, construction cost estimation, insurance core systems, or education/healthcare knowledge bases can embed Agents directly into high-frequency workflows such as approvals, reporting, research, customer service, claims, and project management, preserving stronger pricing power.

## Quantitative Elasticity Framework

- **Bull case:** openJiuwen developer activity rises from May to August, AgentArts commercial pricing is launched, and Huawei Cloud plus partners release office, code, marketing, and customer-service “Claw” agents. Vertical software vendors’ Agent-related revenue reaches 3%-5% of 2025 revenue, with 30%-45% contribution margin for highly reusable software. Kingdee, Jiangsu Hoperun, iSoftStone, and Yonyou show the most visible profit or loss-reduction leverage.
- **Base case:** 2026 is mainly POC, migration, and showcase projects, with Agent-related revenue at 1%-3% of 2025 revenue. Profit elasticity diverges: stronger for pure software/subscription vendors, lower for systems integration and hardware-heavy vendors.
- **Bear case:** open-source community activity disappoints, AgentArts extends the free period, and customers remain in trial/internal-tool mode. Revenue mainly shows up as cloud Tokens, consulting, and small projects; 2026 listed-company profit impact stays below 5%.

## 对国内 AI Agent 生态的判断

**1. 开源将加速“国产 Agent 标准件”形成。** openJiuwen/AgentArts 把 Agent Studio、Agent Core、Agent Runtime、DeepSearch、MCP/A2A 协议、Skill/Team Skill 这类模块拆出来，配合昇腾、华为云 MaaS、盘古和第三方模型，能形成国产化可控的 Agent 参考栈。对客户的实际意义是：不必每个项目从权限、记忆、工具接入、日志、评测、安全沙箱重新造轮子。

**2. 华为云更可能通过云资源、MaaS、行业解决方案和伙伴分成变现。** AgentArts 基础版免费降低开发者门槛，但真正商业化会发生在模型调用、推理算力、企业运行时、私有化部署、MCP/插件/Skill 市场和行业实施中。2025 年中国云基础设施和 AI 云需求已经由生成式 AI 拉动，Canalys 数据显示 2025Q1 中国大陆云基础设施支出 116 亿美元、同比增长 16%，华为云份额 18%；IDC 对 2025H2 中国公有云 IaaS 口径显示规模 159.7 亿美元、同比增长 20.9%，竞争焦点从资源规模转向全栈 AI 能力。[Canalys](https://canalys.com/newsroom/china-cloud-market-q1-2025)、[IDC 博客](https://www.idc.com/resource-center/blog/%E5%85%AC%E6%9C%89%E4%BA%91iaas%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA%E5%A2%9E%E9%80%9F%E9%87%8D%E5%9B%9E20%E7%9A%84%E8%83%8C%E5%90%8E%EF%BC%9A-ai-%E7%BA%A2%E5%88%A9%E8%8A%B1%E8%90%BD%E8%B0%81%E5%AE%B6%EF%BC%9F/)

**3. 应用层会分化：通用搭建平台被开源压价，垂直工作流和数据资产升值。** 如果 AgentArts/openJiuwen 变成事实标准，单纯卖“低代码 Agent builder”的厂商会被压估值；但掌握金融交易、ERP、协同办公、工程造价、保险核心系统、教育/医疗知识库的厂商，能把 Agent 直接嵌入审批、报表、投研、客服、理赔、项目管理等高频流程，议价能力更强。

## 量化弹性框架

- **乐观情形：** openJiuwen 5-8 月开发者活跃提升，AgentArts 商业定价落地，华为云联合伙伴发布办公、代码、营销、客服等“龙虾”智能体；垂直软件商 Agent 相关收入达到 2025 收入的 3%-5%，高复用软件贡献利润率 30%-45%。金蝶、润和、软通、用友的利润/减亏弹性最明显。
- **基准情形：** 2026 年主要是 POC、迁移和示范项目，Agent 相关收入达到 2025 收入的 1%-3%。利润弹性按公司不同分化：纯软件/订阅强的高，系统集成和硬件占比高的低。
- **谨慎情形：** 开源社区活跃不及预期，AgentArts 免费期延长，客户停留在试用和内部工具；收入主要表现为云 Tokens、咨询和小规模项目，对上市公司 2026 年利润影响低于 5%。

## 垂直行业落地优先级

1. **办公/协同：** OfficeClaw、WPS、致远互联、用友/金蝶的流程入口最直接。华为云 OfficeClaw 已覆盖内容生成、文件处理、知识搜索、邮件整理、纪要生成、PPT 制作等场景，并强调 Windows 本地部署、安全护栏、日志脱敏加密。[华为云 OfficeClaw](https://www.huaweicloud.com/news/2026/20260416163440660.html)
2. **金融/保险：** 恒生电子、中科软、金蝶信用 AI、讯飞听见/会议等受益于私有化、审计和合规要求；但上线周期慢，商业化要看头部银行、券商、保险合同。
3. **工业/能源/港口/矿山：** 华为已披露青岛港、晋云煤矿、万华化学案例，验证调度、SOP 审核、生产诊断价值；软通、润和、汉得更容易参与交付。
4. **教育/医疗：** 科大讯飞已具备教育 89.67 亿元、医疗 8.58 亿元收入基座；AgentArts 生态会推高行业接受度，但讯飞自身模型和渠道更关键。
