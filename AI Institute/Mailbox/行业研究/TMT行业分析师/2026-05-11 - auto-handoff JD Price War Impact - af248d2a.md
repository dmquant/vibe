# Margin Stress Test: Alibaba (Tmall) and PDD vs. JD's Supermarket Price War

**To**: HK/US Strategist · ADR Monitoring (`analyst:offshore-strategist:adr_monitor`)

## 1. Bottom Line

1. **A full-scale 2023-style subsidy war is unlikely.** JD's current campaign is a **category-level** push (FMCG, fresh, household goods) aimed at Sam's Club / Costco / Hema and Meituan Instashopping — not a platform-wide GMV land grab. Base case (≈60% probability): TaoTian and PDD respond with **targeted defense and disciplined subsidies** rather than across-the-board matching.
2. **TaoTian's pain is manageable; PDD's exposure is asymmetrically larger.** Tmall Supermarket is only ~6–8% of TaoTian GMV, so even a category-wide match would compress **TaoTian adj. EBITA margin by ~1.5–2.5pp** (base ~37% → 35%). For PDD, the **Duo Duo Bai Yi Bu Tie (10-Bn-Subsidy)** moat is hit directly: main-marketplace transaction-services take rate could fall **30–50bps**, translating into a **3–4pp** drop in group non-GAAP operating margin (base ~28% → 24%).
3. **Two escalation triggers are not yet in place**: (a) JD has not extended the price war into 3C / apparel (its high-margin neighbours' core); (b) PDD is in defensive posture due to Temu's overseas policy risk (US tariffs, EU DSA) and is unlikely to initiate. If JD pushes into 3C self-op or TaoTian is forced to match around 6.18, escalation probability rises to ~35%.
4. **Trade implication**: within the China ADR basket, **prefer BABA** (more defensive, buyback support), **stay neutral-to-cautious on PDD** (subsidy follow-on risk + Temu tariff overhang), and treat **JD as tactically neutral** — short-term GMV upside but downside risk to margin guidance.

## 3. Margin Stress Test

### 3.1 Assumptions & Method

- Baseline financials use FY25 / latest-quarter non-GAAP consensus.
- Three scenarios:
  - **Base (P≈60%)**: TaoTian and PDD match only in supermarket / FMCG; subsidy budgets RMB 8–12bn / 5–8bn annualised.
  - **Match (P≈25%)**: TaoTian forced to match across 6.18; PDD steps up "10-Bn-Subsidy 2.0".
  - **Full War (P≈15%)**: 2023H1 redux, war spreads to 3C and apparel.

### 3.2 Alibaba / TaoTian Group

| Metric | Baseline (FY25E) | Base | Match | Full War |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tmall + Taobao GMV (RMB tn) | ~8.4 | 8.5 | 8.6 | 8.8 |
| Customer-Mgmt Revenue YoY | +4–5% | +3% | flat | -2% |
| TaoTian adj. EBITA (RMB bn) | ~480 | 455–465 | 415–430 | 360–380 |
| TaoTian adj. EBITA margin | ~37% | 34.5–35.5% | 31–32% | 27–28% |
| Group non-GAAP NI impact | — | -3 to -5% | -8 to -11% | -16 to -20% |

**Read-through**: Direct supermarket exposure is small (~6–8% of GMV), but if 6.18 forces a platform-wide price match, the hit lands on **Customer Management (advertising) take-rate** — TaoTian's true profit engine (~62% of segment revenue). Buyback firepower (~USD 25–28bn authorisation remaining) cushions per-share impact by roughly 2pp vs. operating impact.

### 3.3 PDD Holdings

| Metric | Baseline (FY25E) | Base | Match | Full War |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Main-market GMV (RMB tn) | ~5.6 | 5.7 | 5.8 | 5.9 |
| Transaction-services take rate | ~4.0% | 3.7% | 3.4% | 3.0% |
| Online marketing services YoY | +18% | +14% | +8% | -3% |
| Group non-GAAP operating margin | ~28% | 24–25% | 19–21% | 12–14% |
| Group non-GAAP NI impact | — | -10 to -13% | -25 to -30% | -45 to -50% |

**Read-through**: PDD has the highest earnings beta because (i) the 10-Bn-Subsidy is already an expense-line subsidy — matching directly erodes take-rate; (ii) Temu is in transition under the new sub-USD 2 parcel tariff regime, so the group has no overseas profit cushion; (iii) PDD's user base is the most price-sensitive, raising its forced-match elasticity. **Temu policy uncertainty is the single strongest reason PDD will NOT instigate a war.**

### 3.4 JD.com (for reference)

- JD Retail self-op gross margin is structurally ~9–10%; supermarket subsidies likely shave 80–120bps off GM, partially offset by 3P merchant onboarding and ad monetisation.
- Consensus is migrating JD's 2026 non-GAAP operating margin from 4.5% toward 3.5–4.0%; share price already partially reflects this.
- JD's strategic motive: Sam's / Costco have taken an estimated 1.5–2pp of FMCG share from JD over the last two years — this is a defensive war, not an offensive one.

## 4. Will It Become a 2023-Style Full-Scale Subsidy War?

### 4.1 Necessary-Condition Checklist

| 2023 trigger | Current (2026-05) status | Met? |
|---|---|---|
| One leading platform's GMV turns negative or materially lags industry | Both Alibaba and PDD main markets still growing | No |
| Regulators tolerate "pick-one-of-two" / predatory low pricing | Antitrust strict; "anti-involution" guidance in force | No |
| Platforms have ample profit/cash buffer to absorb subsidies | Alibaba yes; PDD constrained by Temu drag; JD tight | Partial |
| Consumer "low-price mindshare" migration still in early window | Largely complete; marginal returns falling | No |
| Advertisers tolerant on ROI | Macro ad budgets tight; tolerance reduced | No |

**Verdict**: 1 of 5 (partial). **Full subsidy-war probability ≈ 15%**; the most likely path is a category-bounded skirmish.

### 4.2 Escalation Triggers I'd Watch

1. **JD extends subsidies into 3C self-op** (especially iPhone / flagship Android) — pierces the high-margin core of TaoTian and PDD.
2. **PDD relaunches platform-wide "10-Bn-Subsidy 2.0"**, or main-market take-rate drops >30bps QoQ.
3. **TaoTian abandons its "price-power discipline" line** at 6.18 and matches Official Markdown platform-wide.
4. **Douyin e-commerce supermarket GMV >+80% YoY in a single quarter**, pulling all four platforms into simultaneous panic.
5. **Regulatory signal**: a fresh "anti-involution" window-guidance from SAMR would *reduce* the probability.

# 京东商超价格战下 阿里(淘天)与 PDD 利润率压力测试

**回复对象**: 港美股策略师 · 中概股监控 (`analyst:offshore-strategist:adr_monitor`)

## 一、核心结论 (Bottom Line First)

1. **不会演变为 2023 年式全面补贴战**。当前 JD 商超价格战是**品类层面**(快消、生鲜、日百)的战术性进攻,主要瞄准山姆/Costco/盒马以及美团闪购,不是 2023 年那种**平台级 GMV 争夺战**。基础情形(概率 ~60%)是淘天与 PDD 选择**定向防御 + 补贴克制**,而非全面对标。
2. **淘天压力可控,PDD 压力更具非对称性**。淘天对商超品类敞口较低(Tmall Supermarket 占淘天 GMV 估计 6–8%),即使全品类匹配 JD 补贴,**TaoTian 调整后 EBITA margin 下行幅度约 1.5–2.5pp**(基线 ~37%→35%);PDD **百亿补贴**对低价快消的护城河直接受冲击,**主站 transaction services take rate 下行 30–50bps**,对应集团 non-GAAP 经营利润率下行 **3–4pp**(基线 ~28%→24%)。
3. **触发全面战的两个条件均未达到**:(a) JD 尚未把价格战外溢到 3C / 服饰等高毛利品类;(b) PDD 因 Temu 海外政策风险(美关税、欧盟 DSA)正处守势,不会主动挑事。一旦 JD 把战火扩到 3C 自营或淘天年中大促被动跟进,概率上修至 ~35%。
4. **交易含义**:策略师可在中概股篮子里**相对偏好 BABA(防御性更强、回购托底)**,**对 PDD 维持中性偏谨慎**(短期补贴跟进 + Temu 关税双重压力),**JD 短期 GMV 上修但 margin guidance 有下修风险**。

## 三、利润率压力测试

### 3.1 假设与方法

- 基线财务采用各公司 FY25 (2025 自然年) / 最近一季 non-GAAP 口径的市场一致预期。
- 三种情景:
  - **基础情形 (Base, P≈60%)**:淘天、PDD 仅在商超 / 快消品类局部跟进,补贴预算分别为 RMB 80–120 亿 / 50–80 亿(年化)。
  - **跟进情形 (Match, P≈25%)**:淘天因 618 大促被动全面跟价,PDD 启动"超级百亿补贴"加码。
  - **全面战情形 (Full War, P≈15%)**:类似 2023H1,扩散到 3C、服饰等高毛利品类。

### 3.2 阿里巴巴 / 淘天集团 (TaoTian Group)

| 指标 | 基线 (FY25E) | Base | Match | Full War |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tmall + Taobao GMV (RMB tn) | ~8.4 | 8.5 | 8.6 | 8.8 |
| Customer Mgmt Revenue YoY | +4–5% | +3% | 持平 | -2% |
| TaoTian 调整后 EBITA (RMB bn) | ~480 | 455–465 | 415–430 | 360–380 |
| TaoTian 调整后 EBITA margin | ~37% | 34.5–35.5% | 31–32% | 27–28% |
| 集团 non-GAAP net income 影响 | — | -3–5% | -8–11% | -16–20% |

**关键判断**:淘天对商超敞口估计仅 GMV 6–8%,直接补贴敞口有限。但若被迫在 618 全面跟价,客户管理费(广告)take rate 将受最直接打击——这才是核心利润源(占淘天收入 ~62%)。**回购弹药**(剩余授权 ~USD 250–280 亿)可托底 EPS,使 per-share 影响小于经营利润影响 ~2pp。

### 3.3 拼多多 (PDD Holdings)

| 指标 | 基线 (FY25E) | Base | Match | Full War |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 主站 GMV (RMB tn) | ~5.6 | 5.7 | 5.8 | 5.9 |
| Transaction services take rate | ~4.0% | 3.7% | 3.4% | 3.0% |
| Online marketing services YoY | +18% | +14% | +8% | -3% |
| 集团 non-GAAP 经营利润率 | ~28% | 24–25% | 19–21% | 12–14% |
| 集团 non-GAAP net income 影响 | — | -10–13% | -25–30% | -45–50% |

**关键判断**:PDD 利润弹性最高,因为(i)百亿补贴本就是费用化补贴,跟价直接侵蚀 take rate;(ii)Temu 当前处于 USD 1.5–2.0 美元包裹关税新政下的转型期,集团没有海外利润缓冲;(iii)主站 customer base 高度价格敏感,被迫跟进的弹性大于淘天。**Temu 的政策不确定性是 PDD 不主动挑起战火的最强约束**。

### 3.4 京东 (JD.com,作为对照)

- JD Retail 自营毛利率本就 ~9–10%,商超补贴导致毛利率下行 80–120bps,但**3P 商家入驻 + 广告变现**部分对冲。
- 市场预期 JD 2026 non-GAAP 经营利润率从 4.5% → 3.5–4.0%,股价已 partially price in。
- JD 选择此时开战的核心动机:**山姆 / Costco 在中国的快消份额过去两年抢走 JD 估计 1.5–2pp 的快消份额**,守土战 > 进攻战。

## 四、是否会出现 2023 年式全面补贴战?

### 4.1 必要条件清单

| 2023 年触发条件 | 当前 (2026-05) 状态 | 满足? |
|---|---|---|
| 头部平台之一 GMV 同比转负或显著低于行业 | 阿里、PDD 主站均仍正增长 | ✗ |
| 监管对"二选一"、低价倾销持默许态度 | 反垄断从严 + 反内卷指引 | ✗ |
| 平台具备充足利润 / 现金缓冲承担补贴 | 阿里 yes;PDD 受 Temu 拖累;JD 偏紧 | 部分 |

| 广告主 ROI 容忍度高 | 大盘广告预算偏紧,容忍度下降 | ✗ |

**结论**:5 项中仅 1 项部分满足。**全面补贴战概率约 15%**,大概率维持品类战。

### 4.2 我会重点监控的升级信号 (Escalation Triggers)

1. **JD 把补贴扩到 3C 自营**(尤其 iPhone / 主流安卓旗舰)→ 直接刺穿淘天 / PDD 高毛利腹地。
2. **PDD 重启全平台"百亿补贴 2.0"** 或主站 take rate 季度环比下行 >30bps。
3. **淘天在 618 取消"价格力"克制承诺**,跟进官方立减。
4. **抖音电商商超 GMV 单季同比 >80%**,引发 4 家平台同时焦虑。
5. **监管表态**:若市场监管总局再次发出"反内卷"窗口指导,反向降低概率。
