# Incremental Copper Demand Model for AI Data Center Evolution (2026-2027)

**Report Date:** 2026-05-14
**Analyst:** TMT Analyst

## 1. Executive Summary
As power consumption for AI chips (e.g., NVIDIA Blackwell and the upcoming Rubin architecture) skyrockets, data center physical architectures are undergoing a structural shift from "Air Cooling" to "Liquid Cooling" and from "Cabled Distribution" to "Busbar Distribution."
*   **Peak Demand:** 2026-2027 will represent the peak deployment phase for large-scale global AI compute clusters.
*   **Intensity per Unit:** The copper consumption intensity for AI Data Centers is approximately **58 tonnes/MW**, a **132% increase** over the 25 tonnes/MW typical of traditional data centers.
*   **Total Impact:** Projected AI-driven copper demand is **0.52 million tonnes (Mt)** in 2026, rising to **0.81 Mt** in 2027.

---

## 2. Key Technology Drivers

### 2.1 Cooling Architecture: The "Copper-Intensive" Liquid Cooling Era
As rack power densities exceed 100kW (e.g., GB200 NVL72 systems), Cold Plate Liquid Cooling has become the industry standard.
*   **Cold Plates:** Manufactured using high-purity oxygen-free copper, directly attached to GPU/CPU surfaces for maximum thermal conductivity.
*   **Manifolds & CDUs:** Coolant Distribution Units and manifolds contain extensive copper piping and heat exchanger components.
*   **Intensity Contribution:** Liquid cooling systems add approximately **12 tonnes** of copper demand per MW of compute capacity.

### 2.2 Power & Interconnects: Transition from Cables to Busbars
AI server clusters require extreme current densities, where traditional cabling faces space and efficiency constraints.
*   **Busbars:** Heavy-duty copper busbars are increasingly replacing cables in rack backplanes and row-level distribution. In NVL72 architectures, busbar copper usage has doubled compared to H100 predecessors.
*   **High-Speed Copper Cables (DAC/ACC):** In the 224G era, copper remains the preferred medium for short-range interconnects (e.g., NVLink switch-to-rack), with single clusters utilizing miles of copper cabling.

---

## 3. Incremental Demand Forecast Model (2026-2027)

| :--- | :---: | :---: | :--- |
| **New AI Compute Capacity (GW)** | 9.0 | 14.0 | Global hyperscale & cloud additions |
| **Traditional DC Copper Intensity (t/MW)** | 25.0 | 25.0 | Baseline |
| **AI Architecture Copper Delta (t/MW)** | 33.0 | 33.0 | Power opt. + Liquid cooling + High-density interconnects |

---

## 4. Key Data Breakdowns

### Copper Consumption per MW of AI Capacity:
1.  **External Distribution (Grid to Row):** 28 tonnes (Includes transformer windings, high-voltage cabling).
2.  **Rack-Level Distribution (Rack Power):** 18 tonnes (Primarily high-density vertical busbars and blind-mate connectors).
3.  **Cooling Systems (Thermal Management):** 12 tonnes (Cold plates, manifolds, heat exchangers).

### Critical Supply Chain Observations:
*   **NVIDIA Rubin R100 (2026/2027):** With estimated Thermal Design Power (TDP) approaching 1.5kW-2kW per chip, Immersion Cooling pilots are expected to scale, further increasing demand for specialized copper heat exchangers.
*   **Copper Foil & PCBs:** Increasing PCB layer counts (20+) in compute nodes drive higher consumption of high-frequency, high-speed electrodeposited copper foil.

---

## 5. Risks & Follow-up Items
*   **Aluminum Substitution:** The pressure to switch to aluminum busbars for long-range distribution may increase if copper prices exceed $12,000/t.
*   **Optical Interconnect Adoption:** Faster-than-expected adoption of CPO (Co-Packaged Optics) could dampen demand for long-distance copper interconnects.
*   **Grid Connection Delays:** Power availability and grid-tie speeds remain the primary bottleneck for realizing projected capacity.

## 1. 核心结论 (Executive Summary)
随着AI芯片（如NVIDIA Blackwell及即将推出的Rubin架构）功耗的剧增，数据中心物理架构正经历从“空气冷却”到“液冷散热”、从“电缆配电”到“铜排配电”的结构性转变。
*   **需求爆发点：** 2026-2027年将是全球AI算力集群大规模交付的高峰。
*   **单位强度：** AI数据中心的铜消耗强度约为 **58 吨/MW**，较传统数据中心的 25 吨/MW 提升了 **132%**。
*   **总增量：** 预计2026年AI驱动的铜增量需求为 **52.2万吨**，2027年将达到 **81.2万吨**。

---

## 2. 核心技术驱动因素

### 2.1 散热架构：液冷时代的“铜密集”
随着机架功率突破 100kW（如 GB200 NVL72 系统），冷板式液冷（Cold Plate Liquid Cooling）已成为标配。
*   **冷板 (Cold Plates)：** 采用高纯度无氧铜，直接贴合GPU/CPU表面。
*   **集分水器 (Manifolds) 与 CDUs：** 内部包含大量铜管、热交换器组件。
*   **强度贡献：** 液冷系统为每MW算力贡献了约 **12吨** 的额外铜需求。

### 2.2 配电与互连：从电缆到铜排 (Busbars)
AI服务器集群需要极高的电流密度，传统布线方案空间受限且效率不足。
*   **铜排 (Busbars)：** 在机架背板及排级配电中大量替代电缆。以 NVL72 为例，单机架铜排用量较传统H100架构翻倍。
*   **高速铜缆 (DAC/ACC)：** 224G 时代，短距离互连仍首选铜缆（如 NVLink 交换机到机架），单集群铜缆长度以英里计。
