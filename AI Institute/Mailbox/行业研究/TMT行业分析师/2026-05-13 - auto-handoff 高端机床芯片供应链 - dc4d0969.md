# Assessment of Domestic Substitution and Supply-Chain Stability for “5nm/7nm-Class Motion-Control Chips” at Shanghai Top and Other High-End CNC Machine-Tool Companies

Anchor date: 2026-05-13, from local `date +%Y-%m-%d`. Bottom line: public evidence does not support the claim that Shanghai Top and comparable high-end CNC machine-tool companies are pursuing domestic substitution of 5nm/7nm semiconductor-node motion-control chips. The better framing is domestic substitution across the high-end CNC system, servo, encoder, linear scale, spindle, rotary-axis, and broader motion-control chain. On that basis, substitution has advanced materially at the machine-tool and CNC-system level, but reliability, precision consistency, and substitution depth in key components remain the bottlenecks.

## Prioritised Conclusions

1. **If “5nm/7nm” means semiconductor process node, the premise is likely wrong.** Shanghai Top’s Hong Kong prospectus dated May 12, 2026 does not disclose “chips” as a core product or procurement category, and it contains no 5nm/7nm references. It also states that the company does not design, manufacture, or package integrated circuits, but purchases parts that contain integrated circuits. Therefore, Shanghai Top’s localization progress should not be read as advanced-node chip localization progress. Source: [HKEX prospectus, 2026-05-12](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf).

2. **If “5nm/7nm” means motion-control resolution or position feedback, public metrics still do not prove 5-7nm-class whole-machine performance.** Shanghai Top’s disclosed comparable performance metrics are machine-level indicators: maximum linear-axis feed speed of 120 m/min, maximum rotary-axis positioning accuracy of 3 arc seconds, five-axis RTCP accuracy of 0.02 mm, and maximum travel range of 300-35,000 mm. 0.02 mm equals 20 microns, which is not in the same order of magnitude as 5-7 nanometers. Source: [HKEX prospectus, competitive analysis](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf).

3. **Domestic substitution is progressing quickly at the machine-tool and CNC-system level, but key components are not fully autonomous.** Shanghai Top ranked first in China’s aerospace five-axis CNC machine-tool market in 2025 with a 10.0% share; across all China five-axis CNC machine-tool suppliers, it ranked fifth overall and second among domestic suppliers with a 3.9% share. At the industry level, domestic suppliers’ revenue share in China’s five-axis CNC machine-tool market rose from 18.0% in 2020 to 59.5% in 2025, and is expected to exceed 78.0% in 2030. The same prospectus, however, states that domestic products still lag overseas peers in precision and reliability in CNC systems, encoders, linear scales, spindles, and rotary axes, and that most of these parts still rely on imports. Source: [HKEX prospectus, industry overview](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf).

4. **Supply-chain stability: Shanghai Top’s own concentration has declined and its quality processes look reasonably complete, but external controls and high-end key components remain the main risk.** Its top-five supplier procurement share fell from 35.6% in 2023 to 18.4% in 2025; the largest supplier share fell from 14.6% in 2023 to 5.1% in 2025. Its 2025 top suppliers included Shanghai Jinjia, Shanghai Anyu, Shanghai Shide, Gate Precision, and Guangzhou Haozhi, covering mechanical components, structural parts, and CNC systems. The company uses an approved supplier list, annual supplier reviews, incoming inspections, in-process testing, and customer pre-acceptance/final-acceptance processes. Still, industry reliance on imported key components and export controls remains a resilience risk. Source: [HKEX prospectus, suppliers and quality control](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf).

## Technical Framing of “5nm/7nm-Class Motion-Control Chips”

- **Advanced process nodes are not the core constraint in high-end CNC motion control.** Industrial real-time control depends more on determinism, noise immunity, long lifecycle supply, functional safety, fieldbus support, servo-loop control, and reliability validation. Many motion controllers, servo drives, industrial MCUs, and FPGAs can be implemented on mature nodes; 5/7nm is more typical of smartphone SoCs, AI accelerators, and data-center CPUs/GPUs.
- **Nanometer-level resolution is not the same as nanometer-level machining accuracy.** Controller interpolation units, scale resolution, servo feedback, and final machining accuracy are different layers. Five-axis machine accuracy is constrained by mechanical rigidity, thermal drift, spindle quality, tooling, rotary tables, assembly error, compensation models, and process parameters.
- **What can be verified for Shanghai Top is machine capability plus software/system integration capability.** Its disclosed software copyrights include open CNC system software, multi-axis motion-controller software, and upper-computer software for five-axis mill-turn CNC systems. That supports software and system-integration capability, but it does not prove that the underlying motion-control chip is a domestic 5/7nm device.

## Supply-Chain Stability View

**Near to medium term: delivery capability looks relatively stable, but key-component substitution still needs verification.** Shanghai Top’s 2025 revenue was RMB578.0 million, including RMB512.4 million from aerospace intelligent manufacturing equipment, or 88.7% of revenue. Its products are mainly customized and made to order. The company usually arranges procurement after customer contracts and product specifications are confirmed, so it says material inventory-overstock risk is limited; however, customization also makes production cycles and acceptance processes highly variable. Source: [HKEX prospectus, financial information and inventory management](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf).

**Long term: the localization direction is clear, but the bottleneck has moved from “whether domestic solutions exist” to “whether they work reliably for long periods in high-end applications.”** Domestic CNC-system suppliers such as Huazhong CNC and Guangzhou CNC already have productized capabilities in five-axis linkage, high-speed/high-precision machining, and servo systems; Guangzhou CNC disclosed annual capacity of 200,000 CNC systems and 400,000 servo motors. Source: [China Machine Tool & Tool Builders’ Association: Guangzhou CNC feature](https://www.cmtba.org.cn/web/13/202012/1843.html). But Shanghai Top’s prospectus language on industry shortcomings shows that aerospace-grade five-axis applications still require close monitoring of batch consistency in domestic CNC systems, encoders, linear scales, spindles, and rotary axes.

## Suggested Next Steps for the Industrials Analyst

1. **Reframe the question as BOM verification:** for each Shanghai Top model, identify the supplier of the CNC system, servo drive, servo motor, encoder, linear scale, rotary table, and spindle, and split domestic versus imported content.
2. **Do not build a “5/7nm chip localization rate” assumption.** More useful metrics are high-end five-axis CNC-system installations, customer acceptance cycles, MTBF/repair rate, key-component localization rate, repeat orders, and aerospace customer qualification.
3. **Track three company groups:** machine-tool OEMs such as Shanghai Top, Kede CNC, and Beijing Jingdiao; CNC-system vendors such as Huazhong CNC and Guangzhou CNC; and core-component vendors in encoders/linear scales, spindles, rotary tables, and servo drives.
4. **Due-diligence questions:** What are the underlying brands for CNC-system purchases from Shanghai Top’s 2025 top-five suppliers; does each key component have at least two qualified alternative suppliers; are any U.S./European/Japanese-origin components above customer or export-control thresholds; and what are continuous operating hours, failure rates, and first-pass acceptance rates for domestic systems at aerospace customers?

# 上海拓璞等高端数控机床企业在“5nm/7nm级运动控制芯片”国产化替代及供应链稳定性的判断

锚定日期：2026-05-13（本机 `date +%Y-%m-%d`）。结论先行：公开资料不支持“上海拓璞等高端数控机床企业正在围绕5nm/7nm半导体制程运动控制芯片进行国产替代”的说法；更合理的口径是“高档数控系统、伺服/编码器/光栅尺/主轴/转台等关键运动控制链条的国产化替代”。在这个口径下，国产替代已经从整机和数控系统层面明显推进，但关键部件的可靠性、精度一致性和进口替代深度仍是短板。

## 优先结论

1. **“5nm/7nm”若指芯片制程节点，基本是口径错误。** 上海拓璞2026年5月12日港交所招股书全文未披露“芯片”作为核心采购或产品，也未出现5nm/7nm相关表述；其还说明公司不设计、制造或封装集成电路，而是采购包含集成电路的零部件。因此，不能把拓璞的国产化进度理解为先进制程芯片国产化进度。来源：[HKEX招股书，2026-05-12](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf)。

2. **若“5nm/7nm”指运动控制分辨率或定位反馈，公开指标也没有证明拓璞整机达到5-7nm级。** 拓璞披露的可比性能指标是五轴机床整机层面的：最大直线轴进给速度120米/分钟、最大旋转轴定位精度3角秒、五轴RTCP精度0.02毫米、最大加工行程300-35,000毫米。0.02毫米等于20微米，和5-7纳米不是同一量级。来源：[HKEX招股书，行业竞争分析](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf)。

3. **国产替代在整机和数控系统层面进展快，但核心部件仍未完全自主。** 拓璞在中国航空航天五轴数控机床市场2025年排名第一、份额10.0%；在中国五轴数控机床全部供应商中排名第五、国内供应商中排名第二、份额3.9%。行业层面，国内五轴数控机床供应商收入份额从2020年的18.0%提升至2025年的59.5%，预计2030年超过78.0%。但同一招股书也明确指出，数控系统、编码器、光栅尺、主轴、旋转轴等领域国产产品在精度和可靠性上仍落后，相关部件“大部分仍依赖进口”。来源：[HKEX招股书，行业概览](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf)。

4. **供应链稳定性：拓璞自身集中度下降、质量流程较完整，但外部封锁和高端关键部件仍是主要风险。** 拓璞五大供应商采购占比从2023年的35.6%降至2025年的18.4%；最大供应商占比从2023年的14.6%降至2025年的5.1%。2025年五大供应商包括上海津甲、上海安驭、上海誓德、盖特精工、广州昊志等，采购品类覆盖机械组件、结构件和数控系统。公司建立认可供应商名单、年度审核、来料检查、过程测试和客户预验收/最终验收流程。但行业关键部件进口依赖和出口管制仍会影响供应韧性。来源：[HKEX招股书，供应商与质量控制](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf)。

## 对“5nm/7nm级运动控制芯片”的技术拆解

- **先进制程芯片不是高端CNC运动控制的核心约束。** 工业实时控制更看确定性、抗干扰、长生命周期供货、功能安全、现场总线、伺服闭环和可靠性验证。大量运动控制器、伺服驱动和工业MCU/FPGA可用成熟制程实现；5/7nm通常用于手机SoC、AI加速器、数据中心CPU/GPU等高算力场景。
- **纳米级分辨率不等于整机纳米级加工精度。** 控制器插补单位、光栅尺分辨率、伺服反馈和最终加工精度是不同层级。五轴机床最终受机械刚性、热漂移、主轴、刀具、转台、装配误差、补偿模型和工艺参数共同限制。
- **拓璞可验证的是整机能力和软件/系统集成能力。** 其披露的软件著作权包括开放式数控系统、多轴运动控制器软件、五轴车铣复合数控系统上位机软件等；但这证明的是软件和系统集成能力，不证明底层运动控制芯片为国产5/7nm。

## 供应链稳定性判断

**中短期：可交付性相对稳，但关键部件替代仍需验证。** 拓璞2025年收入人民币578.0百万元，其中航空航天智能制造装备512.4百万元、占88.7%；产品以定制化、按订单生产为主。公司通常在客户合同和产品规格确认后安排采购，因此公开披露认为重大存货过剩风险较低；但定制化项目导致交付周期和验收流程高度差异化。来源：[HKEX招股书，财务资料与存货管理](https://www1.hkexnews.hk/listedco/listconews/sehk/2026/0512/2026051200018_c.pdf)。

**长期：国产化替代方向明确，但瓶颈从“有没有国产方案”转向“高端场景能否长期稳定好用”。** 华中数控和广州数控等国产数控系统厂商已经具备五轴联动、高速高精、伺服系统等产品化能力；广州数控披露形成20万套数控系统、40万台伺服电机年产能。来源：[中国机床工具工业协会：广州数控专题](https://www.cmtba.org.cn/web/13/202012/1843.html)。但拓璞招股书对行业短板的表述说明，高端航空航天五轴场景仍需关注国产系统、编码器、光栅尺、主轴和旋转轴的批量一致性。

## 建议给 Industrials Analyst 的下一步

1. **把问题改写为BOM验证问题：** 拓璞具体机型中CNC系统、伺服驱动、伺服电机、编码器、光栅尺、转台、主轴分别来自哪家供应商，国产/进口占比是多少。
2. **不要建立“5/7nm芯片国产化率”假设。** 更有用的指标是高端五轴数控系统装机数、客户验收周期、MTBF/返修率、关键部件国产替代率、客户复购和航空航天认证。
3. **重点跟踪三类公司：** 整机厂（拓璞、科德、北京精雕等）、数控系统厂（华中数控、广州数控等）、核心部件厂（编码器/光栅尺、主轴、转台、伺服驱动）。
4. **尽调问题清单：** 拓璞2025年前五大供应商中“数控系统”采购的底层品牌是什么；是否有至少两家合格替代供应商；是否存在欧美/日本原产比例超过客户或出口管制阈值的部件；国产系统在航空航天客户处的连续运行小时数、故障率和验收一次通过率是多少。
