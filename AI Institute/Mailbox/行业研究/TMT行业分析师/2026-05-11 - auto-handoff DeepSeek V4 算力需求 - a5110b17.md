# Actual Impact of DeepSeek V4 on A-Share Compute Leasing and Optical Module Export Orders

Date anchor: `2026-05-11`. The assessment uses public information available as of that date.

## Prioritized Conclusions

1. **A-share compute leasing: near-term impact is a trading catalyst, not yet a disclosed large-order catalyst.** DeepSeek V4 went live on `2026-04-24`, the API was available the same day, and `deepseek-chat` / `deepseek-reasoner` will migrate to V4-Flash. But as of `2026-05-11`, public evidence shows V4 platform launches, domestic chip adaptation and market reaction, not attributable large new compute-leasing contracts. The confirmed real impact should be treated as a “low-single-digit Q2 revenue uplift” at most, rather than a new sector-wide order wave.

2. **The real industrial driver is “AI token growth plus tight high-end GPU/domestic compute supply”; V4 reinforces that logic but does not create it.** V4’s low pricing and long context should expand use cases, but it also materially reduces compute required per token: at 1M context, V4-Pro uses `27%` of V3.2 single-token FLOPs and `10%` of its KV cache; V4-Flash uses `10%` and `7%`, respectively. The net effect for compute lessors depends on whether token growth exceeds the efficiency gain.

3. **Optical module export orders: there is no evidence that V4 has generated new export orders; the export order cycle is still driven by overseas hyperscaler AI capex, 800G/1.6T upgrades and the Google/Meta/Microsoft/Nvidia ecosystem, not DeepSeek V4.** The optical module leaders’ 2026Q1 growth occurred before V4 was released, and the order/capacity story is tied mainly to global AI data-center builds. If V4 accelerates Ascend-based domestic clusters, the more direct read-through is domestic AI-cluster optical interconnect demand, not export orders.

4. **Quantified view: V4-attributable incremental scale is currently smaller than the market narrative.** For A-share compute leasing, if V4 creates `1-10T tokens/week` of incremental usage through third-party A-share platforms, DeepSeek’s current non-cache and discounted prices imply annualized end-model usage revenue of roughly `RMB 0.10-4.16bn`. If A-share third-party platforms capture only `10%-20%` of that traffic and `30%-50%` turns into compute/leasing revenue, annualized compute-side revenue would be about `RMB 3mn-416mn`. This is a scenario range, not a disclosed order figure. For optical module export orders, the publicly confirmed V4-attributable increment is `0`.

## Usable Takeaway for the Chief Economist

**A-share compute leasing:** A suitable framing is: “V4 improves confidence in the domestic model/domestic compute ecosystem and supports valuation, but as of `2026-05-11`, the disclosed order-level impact is limited.” A prudent magnitude is: for a small group of platforms that already have MaaS/token billing, V4 may create an annualized `RMB 3mn-416mn` compute-side revenue scenario; for the broader A-share compute-leasing sector, it is unlikely to exceed the already-running supply-shortage and price-hike cycle in near-term fundamental impact.

**Optical module export orders:** A suitable framing is: “The direct V4 uplift to export orders is currently zero-confirmed; export-order strength should still be tracked through overseas AI capex, Google TPU/OCS, Nvidia Blackwell/Rubin, Meta/Microsoft data-center builds and 800G/1.6T upgrades.” If domestic Ascend supernodes ramp, the corresponding optical interconnect orders are more likely to appear first in domestic AI-cluster demand, not exports.

# DeepSeek V4 对 A 股算力租赁与光模块出口订单的实际提振评估

日期锚点：`2026-05-11`。结论基于截至该日可见的公开信息。

## 优先结论

1. **A 股算力租赁：短期是交易催化，不是已经落地的大额订单催化。** DeepSeek V4 于 `2026-04-24` 上线，API 当日可用，且 `deepseek-chat`、`deepseek-reasoner` 后续迁移到 V4-Flash；但截至 `2026-05-11`，公开资料只看到算力平台上线 V4、国产芯片适配和板块行情反应，未看到可归因于 V4 的新增大额算力租赁合同。可确认的真实提振规模应按“低个位数 Q2 收入增量”处理，而不是新一轮板块级订单爆发。

2. **实际产业驱动来自“AI token 总量增长 + 高端 GPU/国产算力供给偏紧”，V4 只是强化这个逻辑。** V4 的低价与长上下文会扩大调用场景，但它同时显著降低单位 token 算力需求：在 1M 上下文下，V4-Pro 单 token 推理 FLOPs 为 V3.2 的 27%、KV cache 为 10%；V4-Flash 分别为 10% 和 7%。因此，V4 对算力租赁的净影响取决于 token 增量能否超过单位算力效率提升。

3. **光模块出口订单：没有证据显示 V4 已带来新增出口订单；出口订单主线仍是海外 hyperscaler AI capex、800G/1.6T 升级和 Google/Meta/Microsoft/Nvidia 体系，而不是 DeepSeek V4。** 光模块龙头 2026Q1 高增长发生在 V4 发布前，且订单和扩产主要绑定全球 AI 数据中心建设。若 V4 促进国产昇腾集群建设，更多对应国内 AI 集群内部光互联需求，不应直接计入“出口订单”。

4. **可量化判断：V4 归因的新增规模目前应给“小于市场叙事”的区间。** 对 A 股算力租赁，若假设 V4 通过第三方 A 股平台带来 `1-10T tokens/周` 的新增调用，按 DeepSeek 当前非缓存价格与折扣价粗算，对应年化终端模型调用收入约 `1.0-41.6 亿元`；若 A 股第三方平台只捕获 `10%-20%` 流量、其中 `30%-50%` 转化为算力/租赁收入，则对应年化算力侧收入约 `0.03-4.16 亿元`。这是情景上限，不是已披露订单。对光模块出口订单，V4 归因的公开可确认增量为 `0`。
