# Impact of Jensen Huang's China Visit Rumor on China's Domestic GPU Substitution Thesis

Anchor date: 2026-05-14, based on local shell output from `date +%Y-%m-%d`. Bottom line: Jensen Huang's China visit is no longer just a rumor. Reuters reported on May 13 that President Trump arrived in Beijing with a CEO delegation that included Nvidia's Jensen Huang, who joined the trip at the last minute. The near-term trading risk is therefore not whether Huang physically went to China, but whether investors extrapolate the visit into a policy trade: large-scale H200 access, Chinese customer approvals, and a rotation back to Nvidia hardware. My view: the domestic GPU substitution trade faces a short-term sentiment and valuation hit, but the thesis is not broken. If the item that gets disproven is a “policy breakthrough / large order” rather than the physical visit, the TMT drawdown should be manageable: broad TMT down roughly -1.5% to -3.0%, while core domestic compute names may see -4% to -8% peak-to-trough pressure.

## Prioritised Conclusions

1. **Near-term impact: core domestic GPU names remain under pressure, but this is mostly a valuation and positioning shock, not a fundamental break.** The 2026-05-14 midday tape already reflected the concern: Cambricon-U (688256.SH) was at RMB 1267.98, down -3.71%; Hygon Information (688041.SH) at RMB 330.49, down -5.08%; Jingjia Micro (300474.SZ) at RMB 66.42, down -2.77%; ChinaAMC Chip ETF (159995.SZ) down -1.59%; STAR 50 down -1.55%. Core compute names underperformed the ETF by 1.2-3.5ppts, showing that investors were pricing in lower scarcity value for domestic substitution.
2. **The substitution thesis is not invalidated by the visit itself.** On January 13, BIS only moved H200, AMD MI325X, and similar chips into case-by-case China license review subject to security conditions, including no reduction of semiconductor capacity available to U.S. customers, buyer compliance screening, and independent U.S. third-party testing. Reuters also reported a condition that China could not receive more than 50% of the volume sold to U.S. customers, while Chinese tech companies had placed more than 2 million H200 orders at about $27,000 each versus Nvidia inventory of about 700,000 units at that time. Even if production restarts, supply is not an unconstrained return.
3. **Nvidia's own guidance argues against treating the visit as a near-term China data center revenue restart.** Nvidia reported FY2026 revenue of $215.9bn and full-year data center revenue of $193.7bn; its FY2027 Q1 revenue outlook of $78.0bn explicitly assumed no data center compute revenue from China. This matters more than the news flow: management has not modeled China recovery into the near-term guide.
4. **Domestic GPU fundamentals are still being delivered.** Cambricon reported 2026Q1 revenue of RMB 2.885bn, up +159.56% YoY, and attributable net profit of RMB 1.013bn, up +185.04% YoY. Hygon reported 2026Q1 revenue of RMB 4.034bn, up +68.06% YoY, and attributable net profit of RMB 687mn, up +35.82% YoY; it also disclosed DCU deployments across more than 20 key industries and 300+ application scenarios, with more than 6,000 ecosystem partners.
5. **Drawdown framework: if the “policy breakthrough” is disproven, TMT should not face a systemic selloff; only a clear H200 bilateral approval path would trigger a second valuation reset for domestic compute.** On the January 13 BIS H200 case-by-case review event day, Cambricon, Hygon, and Jingjia Micro fell -6.76%, -4.26%, and -4.01%, respectively; the Chip ETF fell -3.35% and STAR 50 fell -2.80%. That event is the most relevant stress test for this “Nvidia re-enters China” trade.

## 投资含义

- **不要把“黄仁勋到北京”直接等同于“国产GPU替代结束”。** 真正需要跟踪的是三件事：美国出口许可是否从逐案变成批量、北京是否允许大客户采购、云厂商是否重启以 H200 为核心的资本开支计划。
- **短期交易上，先降高估值纯弹性，保留有业绩和订单兑现的国产算力核心。** 寒武纪、海光这类标的估值敏感，但财报增速和订单数据能提供下沿；缺少确定订单、只靠主题弹性的边缘GPU/服务器链条回撤更深。
- **若传闻证伪，应买回“国产替代确定性”，而不是全面卖出TMT。** 证伪政策突破对国产GPU是利好，对TMT宽基只是风险偏好降温。
- **若传闻升级成政策文件，国产GPU的首轮防守位看 2026-01-13 事件冲击幅度。** 单日 -4% 至 -7% 是可比冲击；若叠加融资盘和高位获利盘，1-2周 -8% 至 -15% 才是熊情景。

## 跟踪清单

1. 白宫、BIS、商务部是否发布 H200/MI325X 对华许可新文件。
2. 中国外交部、商务部、网信办是否表态允许或限制 H200 采购与部署。
3. 阿里、腾讯、字节、百度、中国移动等是否出现 H200 服务器招标或取消国产GPU订单的证据。
4. 寒武纪、海光新增合同负债、预收款、存货与晶圆/先进封装产能预付款变化。
5. CUDA 生态迁移指标：国产框架适配 DeepSeek、Qwen、混元、智谱等模型的训练/推理稳定性。
