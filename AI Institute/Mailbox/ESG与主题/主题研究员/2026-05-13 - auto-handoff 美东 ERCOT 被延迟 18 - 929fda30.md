# US Data Center Delayed Projects Research Report (Data Center Watch 2024.05 - 2025.03)

According to monitoring data from **Data Center Watch** (published by **10a Labs**), approximately **$46 billion** in projects across the U.S. experienced significant delays between May 2024 and March 2025, with an additional **$18 billion** in projects being blocked entirely. The core bottleneck has shifted from early-stage "supply chain issues" to **"local community resistance (NIMBY)" and "grid interconnection saturation."**

This trend serves as a critical "counterparty indicator" for IDC deployment in China: **Even where nominal power supply is abundant, local regulatory certainty and environmental carrying capacity (water/noise) are becoming the new hard constraints.**

## 3. Strategic Transmission: Logic for Selecting Direct Green Power IDC Provinces in China
Based on the U.S. findings, we propose the following selection logic for "Direct Green Power Supply" provinces in China:

1.  **Shift from "Energy Quotas" to "Resource Carrying Capacity" Validation:** The North American experience shows that even with quotas, projects in water-stressed or densely populated areas (like the Loudoun periphery) face prohibitive social costs later. In China, priority should be given to **Ulanqab (Inner Mongolia) and Zhongwei (Ningxia)**, where land is vast and water planning is specifically tailored for industry.
2.  **Avoiding "Pseudo-Green Power" Hubs:** The lesson from Texas is that the "Interconnection Queue" is the ultimate gatekeeper. In China, the focus should be on the feasibility of **"Direct Transmission Lines" or "Microgrids."** Provinces like **Gansu and Xinjiang**, which face pressure to consume curtailed wind/solar power, offer the strongest policy resilience if they can provide physical direct supply rather than just certificate-based trading.
3.  **Proactive Noise and Community Management:** Even in remote areas, IDC noise interference with local livestock or small settlements has triggered lawsuits in North America. When selecting sites in **Inner Mongolia or Zhangjiakou (Hebei)**, acoustic buffer zones and distance from residents should be key screening factors to avoid repeating Northern Virginia's "NIMBY" pitfalls.

# 美国数据中心延迟项目调研报告 (Data Center Watch 2024.05 - 2025.03)

## 核心结论
根据 Data Center Watch (由 10a Labs 发布) 的监测数据，2024 年 5 月至 2025 年 3 月期间，全美范围内约有 **460 亿美元** 的项目遭遇显著延迟，另有约 **180 亿美元** 项目被直接阻断（Blocked）。延误的核心矛盾已从早期的“供应链瓶颈”彻底转向**“本地社会阻力（NIMBY）”与“电网互联饱和”**。

这种趋势对中国侧 IDC 布局具有强烈的“对手盘”指示意义：**即使电力供应名义充足，本地监管确定性和水资源/噪音等环境承载力正成为新的硬约束。**

---

## 1. 美东枢纽 (Loudoun / 北弗吉尼亚) 延迟项目清单
该区域作为全球数据中心心脏，目前正面临 PJM 电网升级缓慢与居民政治反弹的双重挤压。

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PW Digital Gateway** | Prince William, VA | ~5,000 MW | **全美最大单体延期项目。** 受阻于多起针对土地性质变更的诉讼、历史遗迹保护争议及居民对电费上涨的担忧。 |
| **Culpeper Acquisitions** | Culpeper, VA | ~800-1,200 MW | 重新分区（Rezoning）申请被搁置，主要由于本地水资源保护组织对冷却用水的强烈抵制。 |
| **Amazon King George** | King George, VA | 规模不详 | 亚马逊与县监事会就基础设施成本分摊及征地补偿产生持续纠纷。 |
| **Warrenton Amazon Campus** | Warrenton, VA | ~220 MW | **政治“翻盘”。** 2024 年 11 月选民投票罢免了所有支持该项目的镇议员，新议会已暂停审批。 |

## 3. 对手盘传导：中国 IDC 直供绿电省份选择逻辑
基于上述调研，我们对中国侧“直供绿电”省份的选择提出以下传导逻辑：

1.  **从“能耗指标”转向“资源承载力”验证：** 北美经验表明，即便有指标，若项目位于水资源紧缺或人口密集区（如 Loudoun 边缘化区域），后期社会成本极高。在中国，应优先选择**内蒙古乌兰察布、宁夏中卫**等土地极度开阔、水资源针对性规划较强的区域。
2.  **避免“伪绿电”枢纽：** 德州项目的教训是“电网互联队列（Queue）”决定生死。中国侧应重点考察**“直供线”或“微电网”**模式落地的可行性。**甘肃、新疆**等具备弃风弃光消纳压力的省份，若能提供物理上的绿电直供（非仅凭证交易），其抗政策波动风险能力最强。
3.  **噪音与社区治理前置：** 即使是偏远地区，IDC 噪音对周边畜牧业或小规模居民点的干扰已在北美引发多起诉讼。建议在**内蒙古、河北张家口**等选址时，将声学隔离带与居民距离作为关键筛选因子，避免重复 NoVa 的“邻避”老路。
