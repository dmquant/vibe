## Recommended Decomposition Framework

**1. Total-demand factor: AI Demand Index**

Standardize these inputs into a weekly index: total AI app visits/DAU, estimated paid subscriptions, direct API tokens, cloud AI backlog/RPO, AI capex constraint language, and enterprise AI ARR/usage credits. OpenAI’s scale remains an important total-demand anchor: AP reported that Sam Altman said ChatGPT has more than **900M** weekly active users and more than **50M** consumer subscribers. That user scale must still be separated from paid conversion and back-end cloud path.[AP](https://apnews.com/article/openai-amazon-nvidia-softbank-altman-microsoft-a0a915c32b85337d799fe2f9525a932a)

**2. Migration factor: Platform Migration Index**

Use four sub-indexes:

| Sub-index | Weekly inputs | Mapping target | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI assistant share | ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, Claude, Perplexity visits/DAU/referral share | OpenAI, GOOGL, MSFT, AMZN/Anthropic, independent AI apps | Measures front-end attention migration; does not equal revenue |
| Search displacement | Google/Bing share, AI search referral, commercial-query prompt share | GOOGL Search, MSFT Bing, ad agencies, SEO/AEO tools | Sensitive mainly to commercial queries and ad-capable answers |
| Cloud backend share | Model API, agent runtime, GPU/TPU/Trainium rentals, RPO | MSFT, AMZN, GOOGL, ORCL, NVDA | Fastest revenue transmission; requires identifying who hosts inference |
| Enterprise embed share | Paid seats, usage credits, AWU, cRPO, seat attach, workflow completion | MSFT, CRM, NOW, ADBE, TEAM, DDOG, etc. | Highest quality; filters out free consumer-usage noise |

**3. Revenue bridge formulas**

- Cloud revenue impact = total AI tokens or jobs × cloud backend share × blended price per unit × utilization × contract duration.
- Search revenue impact = commercial query volume × platform share × ad coverage × CPC/CPA × conversion quality.
- Ads revenue impact = AI-attributed discovery × reachable inventory × auction density × conversion lift × attribution capture.
- Enterprise software impact = eligible seats × paid penetration × AI ARPU + agentic work units × price per work unit + data/security attach.

## Misreads To Avoid

- **Treating AI app referrals as the full search replacement rate.** StatCounter’s AI chatbot share is an “AI chatbot referrals to websites” measure, not a measure of all queries, all app usage, or all ad clicks. It is useful for direction, not for directly multiplying against Google Search revenue.
- **Ignoring Google internalization.** Gemini, AI Overviews, and AI Mode can keep behavior inside the Google ecosystem even when the user is no longer on a classic search results page. Q1 2026 Google Search & other revenue was still +19% YoY, which shows negative migration is currently being offset by usage growth and ad-product improvements.
- **Ignoring the back-end cloud path.** ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, or enterprise-agent front-end share does not necessarily accrue to the same company’s revenue. The real cloud beneficiary depends on the mix across Azure, AWS, GCP, Oracle, or owned infrastructure.
- **Using free users to explain enterprise software.** Enterprise software revenue depends more on paid seats, usage-based credits, RPO/cRPO, and agent-completed work than on total consumer-chatbot visits.

## Actionable Suggestions For Downstream Analysis

1. **Run a two-factor regression, not a single-variable regression.** Estimate `AI_total_demand_beta` and `platform_migration_beta` for each company. GOOGL has Search risk, Gemini defense, and GCP upside; MSFT has Azure/OpenAI, M365 Copilot, and Bing/Copilot distribution; AMZN is more sensitive to back-end AI compute and ad conversion.

2. **Classify queries by intent.** Informational queries affect publisher traffic; commercial-research queries affect brand shortlists; transactional queries are closest to ad revenue. Similarweb’s 2026 data suggests AI referral volume is low but high quality: ChatGPT referrals to transactional sites convert at **7%**, versus **5%** for Google referrals; AI is used by **35%** of consumers at the discovery stage, versus **13.6%** for search. That means last-click search revenue can remain stable in the short term while upstream brand selection is already migrating.[Similarweb](https://www.similarweb.com/blog/marketing/geo/gen-ai-stats/)

3. **Build a winner matrix.**  
   - `AI demand up + backend share stable`: positive for hyperscalers, semis, data centers, and power.  
   - `AI demand up + Google internalizes share`: positive for GOOGL Cloud/Search defense; delays the search-bear case.  
   - `AI demand up + ChatGPT/Copilot share up`: positive for MSFT Azure/M365/GitHub; if AWS OpenAI/Anthropic expansion continues, AMZN also benefits.  
   - `AI app usage up + paid conversion weak`: risk for application-layer valuation; cloud can still see revenue, but margins depend on inference cost.

As of 2026-05-10, the most defensible conclusion is: **rising AI usage is already clear total-demand expansion; platform share migration is happening, but revenue direction depends on back-end cloud, commercial queries, and enterprise embedding rather than a single AI app’s visit share.** In the model, cloud should carry the highest immediate beta, enterprise software the highest-quality beta, and search/ads the largest but most nonlinear risk/opportunity pool.

3. **用于回归或事件研究时，先拆成两条变量：总需求 beta 与迁移 beta。** 总需求 beta 用 AI app 总访问、token、云消耗、AI ARR、capex 受限程度度量；迁移 beta 用 ChatGPT/Gemini/Copilot/Claude/Perplexity 相对份额、Google Search 份额、AI referral share、商业查询 share-of-voice 度量。不要把 “ChatGPT share up” 机械映射为 “OpenAI revenue up / Google revenue down”：它可能同时利好 Azure/AWS 算力、冲击传统搜索点击、并通过企业 Copilot 或 Agentforce 转化为软件 ARPU。

## 建议分解框架

**1. 总需求因子：AI Demand Index**

建议把以下变量标准化后合成周频指数：AI app 总访问/DAU、付费订阅估计、direct API token、云 AI backlog/RPO、AI capex 受限表述、企业 AI ARR/usage credits。OpenAI 的规模仍是总需求的重要锚点：AP 报道 Sam Altman 称 ChatGPT 每周活跃用户超过 **900M**、consumer subscribers 超过 **50M**；但这类用户规模需要与付费转化和后端云路径分开处理。[AP](https://apnews.com/article/openai-amazon-nvidia-softbank-altman-microsoft-a0a915c32b85337d799fe2f9525a932a)

**2. 迁移因子：Platform Migration Index**

建议拆四个子指数：

| 子指数 | 周频输入 | 映射对象 | 解释 |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI assistant share | ChatGPT、Gemini、Copilot、Claude、Perplexity 访问/DAU/referral share | OpenAI、GOOGL、MSFT、AMZN/Anthropic、独立 AI app | 衡量前端注意力迁移，不直接等于收入 |
| Search displacement | Google/Bing 份额、AI search referral、commercial query prompt share | GOOGL Search、MSFT Bing、广告代理商、SEO/AEO 工具 | 只对商业查询和可广告化回答高敏感 |
| Cloud backend share | 模型 API、agent runtime、GPU/TPU/Trainium 租用、RPO | MSFT、AMZN、GOOGL、ORCL、NVDA | 收入传导最快，需识别“谁托管推理” |
| Enterprise embed share | paid seats、usage credits、AWU、cRPO、seat attach、workflow completion | MSFT、CRM、NOW、ADBE、TEAM、DDOG 等 | 质量最高，能过滤免费 consumer usage 噪声 |

**3. 收入桥接公式**

- Cloud revenue impact = total AI tokens or jobs × cloud backend share × blended price per unit × utilization × contract duration。
- Search revenue impact = commercial query volume × platform share × ad coverage × CPC/CPA × conversion quality。
- Ads revenue impact = AI-attributed discovery × reachable inventory × auction density × conversion lift × attribution capture。
- Enterprise software impact = eligible seats × paid penetration × AI ARPU + agentic work units × price per work unit + data/security attach。

## 需要避免的误判

- **把 AI app referral 当作完整搜索替代率。** StatCounter 的 AI chatbot share 是“AI chatbot referral to websites”口径，不是所有查询、所有 app 使用或所有广告点击。它适合看方向，不适合直接乘 Google Search 收入。
- **忽略 Google 的内部化。** Gemini、AI Overviews、AI Mode 可能把“离开传统搜索页”的行为留在 Google 生态内。Q1 2026 Google Search & other 仍同比 +19%，说明目前负面迁移还被使用量和广告产品改善抵消。
- **忽略后端云路径。** ChatGPT、Claude、Perplexity 或企业 agent 的前端份额未必对应同名公司的收入；真正的云受益方取决于 Azure、AWS、GCP、Oracle 或自建基础设施的占比。
- **用免费用户解释企业软件。** 企业软件收入更取决于 paid seats、usage-based credits、RPO/cRPO、agent 完成的工作量，而不是 consumer chatbot 的总访问。

## 对下游分析的可执行建议

1. **做双回归而不是单变量回归。** 对每家公司拆出 `AI_total_demand_beta` 与 `platform_migration_beta`。例如 GOOGL 同时拥有 Search 风险、Gemini 防守、GCP 受益；MSFT 同时拥有 Azure/OpenAI、M365 Copilot、Bing/Copilot 入口；AMZN 对后端 AI compute 和广告转化更敏感。

2. **把 query 按意图分层。** 信息型 query 迁移影响 publisher traffic；商业调查 query 影响品牌 shortlist；交易型 query 才最接近广告收入。Similarweb 的 2026 年数据提示 AI referral 量低但质量高：ChatGPT referral 到交易网站转化率 **7%**，Google referral **5%**；AI 在 discovery 阶段使用率 **35%**，search **13.6%**。这意味着 last-click 搜索收入短期可能稳，但上游品牌选择权已经迁移。[Similarweb](https://www.similarweb.com/blog/marketing/geo/gen-ai-stats/)

3. **建“赢家矩阵”。**  
   - `AI demand up + backend share stable`: 看多 hyperscaler、半导体、数据中心电力。  
   - `AI demand up + Google internalizes share`: 看多 GOOGL Cloud/Search 防守，搜索熊案推迟。  
   - `AI demand up + ChatGPT/Copilot share up`: 看多 MSFT Azure/M365/GitHub；若 AWS OpenAI/Anthropic 扩张，AMZN 也受益。  
   - `AI app usage up + paid conversion weak`: 对应用层估值是风险，对云仍可能是收入但利润率要看推理成本。

## 底线

截至 2026-05-10，最稳健的结论是：**AI 使用量上升已经是明确的总需求扩张；平台份额迁移正在发生，但对收入的方向取决于后端云、商业查询和企业嵌入，而不是单一 AI app 访问份额。** 在模型里应把 cloud 设为最高即时 beta，把 enterprise software 设为最高质量 beta，把 search/ads 设为最大但最非线性的风险/机会池。
