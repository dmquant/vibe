## 1. Bottom Line Up Front

1. **"AI total demand" and "platform share migration" are two independent P&L drivers** and cannot be explained by the same proxy (capex / token volume). Total demand is read off token consumption growth, inference GPU-hours, and enterprise AI budget penetration. Share migration is read off relative shifts *within a fixed AI TAM* — IaaS GPU share, search query share, ad impression share, SaaS seat share.
2. **Search and traditional ads are the most sensitive segments in 2026.** Against a +35% YoY AI-TAM baseline, every 1pp of Google search query share lost to ChatGPT/Perplexity/Gemini drives roughly **-0.7 to -0.9pp** of Alphabet Search revenue (query-share × monetization-gap) and ~**-1.1% EPS**. Meta's 2026 EPS sensitivity to a 1pp ad-impression share loss is ~**-1.5%**, given operating leverage and AI-native feed substitution.
3. **Cloud (IaaS/PaaS) is currently a double-win** but with ongoing share migration: AWS AI-workload share has slipped from ~31% (2024) to ~29% (Q1 2026); Azure has risen from ~24% to ~28% on OpenAI exclusivity; GCP from ~12% to ~14%. 4. **Enterprise software is the most underestimated.** AI-native SaaS (Copilot/Glean/Harvey/Sierra) ARR ≈ $8B, less than 3% of the combined ~$280B license/seat base of MSFT 365 + CRM + NOW + WDAY. Share migration is a *secondary* factor in 2026; the dominant signal is **per-seat ARPU uplift** (e.g. Copilot's $30/seat add-on).
5. **Investable expression**: long "total-demand β" (NVDA, Azure, TSMC) paired short "share-loser" pockets (GOOGL Search segment, META Reels, CRM Service Cloud) is the cleanest 2026 AI thematic pair.

- 真实风险点：若 ChatGPT Search 在 2026H2 推出 transactional ads（已在测试），其变现单价从 $0.006 拉到 $0.015，则 query 份额迁移的 $-impact 翻倍。

来源：StatCounter 2026-04、SimilarWeb、Alphabet 10-Q FY26Q1、a16z 消费者 AI 报告 2025-11。

### 3.3 广告（社交+视频+原生）

| 平台 | 2026E 广告收入 | AI 内容/推荐占比 | AI 原生平台份额威胁 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Meta (FB+IG+Reels) | 175 bn | 已 65% AI 推荐 | 中—Character.ai、Replika 抢年轻用户时长 ~6% |
| TikTok | 35 bn (US) | 70% | 高—但 ByteDance 自研 Doubao，份额迁移内生化 |

| Snap | 5.5 bn | 30% | 高—被 Meta+AI feed 双向夹击 |

**Meta 敏感度**：DAU 时长每 -1pp 对应广告 impression -1pp，2026 收入敏感度 **-1.0pp**，EPS **-1.5%**（高经营杠杆）。但 Meta 本身是 Llama 3.5 / 4 的最大商业受益者之一—Advantage+ 自动化广告创意已贡献 +8% YoY ad price。**因此 Meta 是双因子净受益**，但市场仅 price-in 总需求项。

来源：Meta FY26Q1 10-Q、eMarketer 2026-03、Sensor Tower。

### 3.4 企业软件（SaaS）

| 类别 | 代表标的 | 2026E ARR | AI 净增量贡献 | 份额迁移净敞口 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Productivity | MSFT M365 + Copilot | 105 bn | +18 bn (Copilot $30 ARPU 升级) | +受益（替代 Google Workspace 部分） |
| CRM | Salesforce + Agentforce | 41 bn | +3.5 bn | -受益（被 HubSpot AI、Sierra、Decagon 蚕食低端） |
| ITSM/HCM | ServiceNow + Workday | 24 bn | +2.1 bn | 中性 |

**核心判断**：当前阶段（2026 中），AI 原生 SaaS 对存量 SaaS 巨头的份额冲击 < 3%，**ARPU 升级（即"在同一 seat 上加 AI 价格")才是主导**。Microsoft 365 Copilot seat 渗透率 2026Q1 已达 14%（vs. 2024Q4 的 5%），每 1pp 渗透率 ≈ MSFT M365 收入 +0.6%。Salesforce Agentforce 渗透 2026Q1 ~7%（落后预期）。

**Salesforce 敏感度**（份额迁移 + 总需求双向）：每 1pp Service Cloud 份额被 Sierra/Decagon 抢走 → 收入 -0.4%，EPS -0.7%；但 Agentforce ARPU 拉升每 +5% → 收入 +1.2%。**净敏感度方向取决于 Agentforce 落地速度，目前为弱负**。

来源：MSFT、CRM、NOW、WDAY 最新 10-Q；Sapphire / Sequoia AI ARR 跟踪报告 2026-04。
