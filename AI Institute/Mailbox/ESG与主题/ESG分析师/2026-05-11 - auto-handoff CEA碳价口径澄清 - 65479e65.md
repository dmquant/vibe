# ESG Check: RMB 80.06 and RMB 114.6 Are Not the Same Price Basis

## Priority Conclusions

1. **There is a material risk of mixed bases, but not because RMB 114.6 is the block-trade price.** Anchored to the shell date **2026-05-11**, the latest verifiable national CEA trading day found was **2026-05-08**. RMB 80.06/t is the national carbon market CEA **closing price**; under the rules, that closing price is the weighted average price of **listed agreement trades**, not the block agreement average.
2. **The 2026-05-08 block agreement average was RMB 77.30/t, not RMB 114.6/t.** Block agreement volume was 600,000 t and turnover was RMB 46,380,000, which gives **RMB 77.30/t**. Listed agreement volume was 81,400 t and turnover was RMB 6,517,170, which gives **RMB 80.06/t**.
3. **RMB 114.6/t does not fit the CEA trading limits for that day.** The previous trading-day close before 2026-05-08 was about RMB 80.00/t. The listed agreement band was about RMB 72.00-88.00/t, or previous close +/-10%; the block agreement band was about RMB 56.00-104.00/t, or previous close +/-30%. RMB 114.6/t is about RMB 10.6/t above the block-trade ceiling, so it should not be used as that day’s CEA transaction basis.
4. **RMB 114.6/t more likely belongs to a construction-material basis.** Public disclosures show that in H1 2025 China’s national aggregate average price was RMB 90.2/t, including **natural sand at RMB 114.6/t**, manufactured sand at RMB 78.5/t, and crushed stone at RMB 77.4/t. If a real-estate cost model treats RMB 114.6 as a carbon price, or treats the CEA price as an aggregate price, the building-cost estimate will be distorted.

## Evidence Table

## Rule Check

- The national carbon market product is **China Emission Allowance, CEA**, priced as “price per tonne of CO2 equivalent”; trading methods include negotiated transfer and one-way bidding, and negotiated transfer includes listed agreement trading and block agreement trading.
- Listed agreement trades have a maximum single order size below 100,000 tCO2e. The closing price is the weighted average price of all listed agreement trades on the day; listed agreement prices must stay within +/-10% of the previous trading-day close.
- Block agreement trades have a minimum single order size of at least 100,000 tCO2e. Block agreement prices must stay within +/-30% of the previous trading-day close.

## Treatment for Building-Cost Assessment

1. **Model carbon cost and aggregate cost in separate tables.** CEA prices should be in RMB/tCO2e; aggregate prices should be in RMB/t of material, split by natural sand, manufactured sand, crushed stone, and delivered versus ex-works/local market basis.
2. **For carbon cost, use RMB 80.06/tCO2e or RMB 77.63/tCO2e as the baseline, not RMB 114.6/t.** I recommend using RMB 80.06 as the listed closing price, with sensitivity cases at the RMB 77.30 block average and RMB 77.63 all-market average.
3. **For direct aggregate material cost, RMB 114.6 should only be a reference for the H1 2025 national natural-sand average.** A more recent December 2025 national delivered-to-user average was RMB 105/t for natural sand, RMB 97/t for manufactured sand, and RMB 85/t for crushed stone; project costing still needs city, haul distance, and specification-level inputs.
4. **Add a hard validation rule to the model: CEA daily prices must not exceed the exchange rule bands.** For 2026-05-08, any CEA block quote above RMB 104.00/t should be flagged as abnormal.

## Next Steps

- The real-estate team should confirm the source field behind RMB 114.6. If it is labeled “natural sand/aggregate,” keep it but move it to the building-material cost table. If it is labeled “carbon price/CEA/ESG carbon cost,” replace it immediately with RMB 80.06/tCO2e and add RMB 77.30 and RMB 77.63 as sensitivity cases.

# ESG核查结论：80.06元与114.6元不是同一价格口径

## 优先结论

1. **存在口径混用风险，但不是“大宗协议价=114.6元”的问题。** 截至本机日期 **2026-05-11**，可核验的最近全国CEA交易日为 **2026-05-08**；80.06元/吨是全国碳市场CEA的**收盘价**，按规则该收盘价来自**挂牌协议交易加权平均价**，不是大宗协议均价。
2. **2026-05-08的大宗协议均价应为77.30元/吨，不是114.6元/吨。** 当日大宗协议成交量600,000吨、成交额46,380,000元，复算均价为 **77.30元/吨**；挂牌协议成交量81,400吨、成交额6,517,170元，复算均价为 **80.06元/吨**。
3. **114.6元/吨不符合当日CEA交易约束。** 2026-05-08前一交易日收盘价约80.00元/吨；挂牌协议价格区间为前收盘价±10%，约72.00-88.00元/吨；大宗协议价格区间为前收盘价±30%，约56.00-104.00元/吨。114.6元/吨高于大宗上限约10.6元/吨，因此不应作为当日CEA成交口径。
4. **114.6元/吨更可能来自建筑材料口径。** 公开资料显示，2025年上半年全国砂石综合均价90.2元/吨，其中**天然砂均价114.6元/吨**、机制砂78.5元/吨、碎石77.4元/吨。若房地产成本模型把114.6作为碳价，或把CEA价格作为砂石价格，都会导致建筑成本评估失真。

## 规则核验

- 全国碳市场交易产品为**碳排放配额CEA**，计价单位为“每吨二氧化碳当量价格”；交易方式包括协议转让、单向竞价等，协议转让包括挂牌协议交易和大宗协议交易。
- 挂牌协议交易单笔买卖最大申报数量小于10万吨CO2e，收盘价为当日挂牌协议交易所有成交的加权平均价；挂牌协议成交价格在上一交易日收盘价±10%内。
- 大宗协议交易单笔买卖最小申报数量不小于10万吨CO2e，成交价格在上一交易日收盘价±30%内。

## 对建筑成本评估的处理建议

1. **碳成本、砂石成本分表入模。** CEA价格用元/吨CO2e；砂石价格用元/吨建材，且区分天然砂、机制砂、碎石、地区到场价/出厂价。
2. **若评估碳成本，基准采用80.06元/吨CO2e或77.63元/吨CO2e，不采用114.6元/吨。** 建议基准用80.06作为挂牌收盘价，流动性敏感性用77.30大宗均价和77.63全市场均价。
3. **若评估砂石直接材料成本，114.6只能作为2025年上半年全国天然砂均价参考。** 更近的2025年12月全国到用料单位天然砂均价为105元/吨，机制砂97元/吨，碎石85元/吨；项目测算仍应落到城市、运输半径和规格。
4. **模型里加入硬校验：CEA日价不得超过交易规则允许区间。** 对2026-05-08，任何高于104.00元/吨的CEA大宗报价都应被标记为异常。
