# CEA May 8 Price-Basis Check

Date anchor: 2026-05-11, from the shell clock. All references to “5/8” below mean 2026-05-08.

## Priority Conclusions

1. **RMB 114.6/t is not the nationwide CEA volume-weighted price including block trades on 2026-05-08.** Recomputing from the disclosed daily volume and turnover gives an all-channel traded average of **RMB 77.63/t**.
2. **RMB 80.06/t is the May 8 nationwide CEA closing price under the listed-agreement price quotation basis**, and it matches the value used in the materials/utilities reports. Xinhua, citing “National Carbon Trading,” reported an open of 79.90, high of 80.80, low of 79.90, close of 80.06; listed-agreement volume of 81,400 t and turnover of RMB 6,517,170; block-agreement volume of 600,000 t and turnover of RMB 46,380,000; total volume of 681,400 t and total turnover of RMB 52,897,170.[1]
3. **The gap is not caused by adding block-agreement trades into the weighted average.** The May 8 block-agreement average was **RMB 77.30/t**, below RMB 80.06/t. Including blocks moves the all-channel average down from about RMB 80.06/t to RMB 77.63/t, not up to RMB 114.6/t.[1][2]
4. **RMB 114.6/t is 43.14% above RMB 80.06/t.** If it were applied to the disclosed 681,400 t total volume, the implied daily turnover would be **RMB 78,088,440**, which is **RMB 25,191,270** above the disclosed **RMB 52,897,170**, or **47.62%** higher. The RMB 114.6 figure is therefore more likely a mismatched data field, a non-national-CEA market price, a scenario/forecast value pulled into the daily note, or a unit/currency conversion error.

## May 8 Trade-Basis Reconciliation

| Basis | Volume (t) | Turnover (RMB) | Recomputed price (RMB/t) | Explanation |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Listed-agreement / closing-price basis | 81,400 | 6,517,170 | 80.0635, disclosed close 80.06 | The closing-price basis for the nationwide carbon market quotation; Shanghai Environment and Energy Exchange rules define the close as the weighted average of same-day listed-agreement trades.[1][3] |
| Block agreement | 600,000 | 46,380,000 | 77.3000 | Block trades were done at a discount that day; CarbonMarket.cn also lists May 8 block average at 77.30 with a -3.45% discount/premium measure.[1][2] |
| All-channel traded average | 681,400 | 52,897,170 | 77.6301 | Actual turnover divided by actual volume after combining listed-agreement and block-agreement trades. |
| Questioned value: 114.6 | 681,400, assuming the same total volume | 78,088,440, implied | 114.6000 | Inconsistent with disclosed turnover; it cannot be treated as the official May 8 CEA traded average. |

- All-channel average = 52,897,170 / 681,400 = **RMB 77.6301/t**.
- Deviation of 114.6 versus 80.06 = 114.6 / 80.06 - 1 = **43.14%**.

# CEA 5/8 价格口径核查

日期锚点：2026-05-11（shell date）。以下“5/8”均指 2026-05-08。

## 优先结论

1. **114.6 元/吨不是 2026-05-08 全国 CEA 含大宗协议的综合加权价。**按当日公开成交量和成交额复算，含挂牌协议与大宗协议的全口径成交均价为 **77.63 元/吨**。
2. **80.06 元/吨是 5/8 全国碳市场 CEA 挂牌协议口径的收盘价**，与 materials/utilities 报告使用的数值一致。新华社转引“全国碳交易”披露：开盘 79.90、最高 80.80、最低 79.90、收盘 80.06；挂牌协议成交 81,400 吨、成交额 6,517,170 元；大宗协议成交 600,000 吨、成交额 46,380,000 元；总成交 681,400 吨、总成交额 52,897,170 元。[1]
3. **价差不是由“大宗协议纳入综合加权”造成的。**5/8 大宗协议均价为 **77.30 元/吨**，低于 80.06 元/吨；纳入大宗后，全口径均价从挂牌口径约 80.06 元/吨降至 77.63 元/吨，而不是升至 114.6 元/吨。[1][2]
4. **114.6 元/吨相对 80.06 元/吨高 43.14%。**若用 681,400 吨总成交量反推，114.6 元/吨需要当日总成交额 **78,088,440 元**，比公开披露的 **52,897,170 元**高 **25,191,270 元**，高出 **47.62%**。因此 114.6 更可能是数据字段错配、非全国 CEA 市场价格、情景/预测值误入日报，或单位/币种转换错误。

## 5/8 成交口径复算

| 口径 | 成交量（吨） | 成交额（元） | 可复算价格（元/吨） | 解释 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| 挂牌协议 / 收盘价口径 | 81,400 | 6,517,170 | 80.0635，披露收盘价 80.06 | 全国碳市场“综合价格行情”的收盘价口径；上海环交所规则说明收盘价为当日挂牌协议成交的加权平均价。[1][3] |
| 大宗协议 | 600,000 | 46,380,000 | 77.3000 | 大宗协议当日折价成交；CarbonMarket.cn 同列示 5/8 大宗均价 77.30、折溢价 -3.45%。[1][2] |
| 全口径成交均价 | 681,400 | 52,897,170 | 77.6301 | 挂牌协议与大宗协议合并后的真实成交额/成交量。 |
| 被质询数值 114.6 | 681,400（假设同一总量） | 78,088,440（反推） | 114.6000 | 与披露总成交额不一致，不能作为 5/8 CEA 官方成交均价。 |

计算式：

- 挂牌协议均价 = 6,517,170 / 81,400 = **80.0635 元/吨**，四舍五入为 **80.06 元/吨**。
- 大宗协议均价 = 46,380,000 / 600,000 = **77.30 元/吨**。
- 全口径均价 = 52,897,170 / 681,400 = **77.6301 元/吨**。
- 114.6 相对 80.06 的偏离 = 114.6 / 80.06 - 1 = **43.14%**。
