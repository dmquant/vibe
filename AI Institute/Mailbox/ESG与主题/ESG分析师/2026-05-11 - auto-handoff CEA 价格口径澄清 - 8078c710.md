## 2. Analysis of Key Terms (Based on CPIF Framework)

The two technical terms mentioned are specific components of the **Fudan Carbon Price Index (CPIF)** methodology:

*   **New Allowance Premium**:
    *   **Definition**: The price premium of newer vintage allowances (e.g., 2024-2025 cycle) over older ones.
    *   **Analysis**: While the 114.6 RMB price reflects local scarcity in Beijing, it is not a calculated CEA price derived by adding a premium to the 80.06 spot price. 114.6 is an independent market-clearing price for BEA.
*   **CCER Comprehensive Weight**:
    *   **Definition**: A weighting factor used by the Fudan Index to calculate the composite CCER price based on project types (forestry, wind, etc.) and regions.
    *   **Analysis**: This weight is used to measure the value of offset credits (CCERs) and is not a direct "adder" to the CEA spot price.

## 3. Why the 43% Spread Exists?
1.  **Scarcity Differentials**: Allocation benchmarks in the Beijing pilot market are significantly more stringent than those in the national carbon market, leading to a natural "scarcity premium" for BEA.
2.  **Sector Coverage**: The Beijing market covers a wider range of non-power sectors (petrochemicals, services, etc.) with generally higher marginal abatement costs than the power industry.
3.  **Market Maturity**: Beijing's pilot has operated for 12 years, resulting in a more mature market mechanism and stronger price discovery functions compared to the relatively newer national market.

## 4. Recommendations

针对您提到的两个专业术语，其在 Fudan Carbon Price Index (CPIF) 中的实际定义如下：

*   **新配额溢价 (New Allowance Premium)**：

    *   **分析**：虽然北京 114.6 元的价格体现了其本地配额的稀缺性，但它并不是全国 CEA 价格通过叠加“新配额溢价”得出的结果。114.6 元是独立的市场定价。

1.  **配额稀缺度差异**：北京试点市场的配额分配标准远严于全国碳市场，导致本地 BEA 价格具备天然的“稀缺溢价”。

3.  **履约周期不同**：全国碳市场目前处于第三个履约周期，而北京试点市场已运行 12 年，市场机制更为成熟，价格发现功能更强。
