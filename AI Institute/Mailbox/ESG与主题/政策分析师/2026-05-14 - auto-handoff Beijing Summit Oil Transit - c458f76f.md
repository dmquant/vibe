# U.S.-China Beijing Summit: Read on Hormuz Security Guarantees and Alternative Routes

Date anchor: 2026-05-14, from local `date +%Y-%m-%d`.

## Prioritised Conclusions

1. **There is no verifiable current signal that the United States and China will produce an executable Strait of Hormuz security guarantee, or jointly guarantee an alternative route, at the Beijing summit.** The summit is taking place in Beijing from 2026-05-13 to 2026-05-15; China’s foreign ministry confirmed Trump’s visit, AP says the 2026-05-14 meeting is expected to be heavy on ceremony and symbolism with limited prospects for major breakthroughs, and Reuters says Trump is expected to press Xi to push Tehran toward a deal while analysts doubt Xi will pressure Iran hard.[China MFA](https://www.mfa.gov.cn/mfa_eng/xw/wsrc/202605/t20260511_11908077.html), [AP](https://apnews.com/article/a1d63a711a037472f5c1c330c2120bd5), [Reuters/Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/trump-xi-set-for-beijing-talks-with-trade-truce-iran-war-at-stake-4686875)

2. **The most likely summit output is political language on no tolls, restored normal and safe passage, and diplomatic de-escalation, not a joint escort plan or an insurable transit guarantee.** The U.S. State Department told Reuters that Wang Yi and Marco Rubio agreed in an April call that no country should be allowed to impose shipping tolls in the Strait of Hormuz; China’s 2026-04-15 and 2026-05-06 statements stressed respect for Iran’s sovereignty and security while also saying freedom and safety of navigation in the international strait should be guaranteed and normal, safe passage should resume as soon as possible.[Reuters/Iran International](https://www.iranintl.com/en/202605120786), [China MFA 2026-04-15](https://www.mfa.gov.cn/eng/wjbzhd/202604/t20260416_11893024.html), [China MFA 2026-05-06](https://www.mfa.gov.cn/eng/xw/fyrbt/202605/t20260506_11905648.html)

3. **China joining a U.S.-led maritime security operation or co-guaranteeing shipping remains a low-probability case.** Reuters reported that Scott Bessent urged China to join an “international operation” to open Hormuz, but the same report notes Beijing would not act solely at Washington’s request; AP reports China has shown little appetite for deeper involvement and is reluctant to appear aligned with Washington.[Reuters/Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/commodities-news/factboxiran-war-raises-stakes-for-us-and-china-ahead-of-trumpxi-talks-4676274), [AP](https://apnews.com/article/48b0ca751712ce473ffcd207997928af)

4. **Alternative routes can cushion the disruption but cannot replace Hormuz.** IEA estimates that about 19.87 mb/d of oil products moved through Hormuz in 2025, including 14.95 mb/d of crude and condensates and 4.93 mb/d of oil products; available Saudi and UAE bypass capacity is only 3.5-5.5 mb/d. EIA’s May 2026 STEO shows Hormuz oil flows falling from about 20.2-20.7 mb/d across 2025 quarters to 14.6 mb/d in 1Q26, while also warning that 2026 AIS data are less reliable.[IEA Hormuz Factsheet](https://iea.blob.core.windows.net/assets/c8248eba-8689-46d9-ae4b-b858b59c0f1c/StraitofHormuz2026-Factsheet.pdf), [EIA STEO May 2026](https://www.eia.gov/outlooks/steo/report/energysecurity/article.php)

5. **For energy trading, do not treat summit diplomacy as a bankable shipping security backstop.** OFAC’s 2026-04-28 FAQ and 2026-05-01 Alert state that paying Iran’s government or the IRGC for safe passage, or soliciting safe-passage guarantees from Iran, creates major sanctions exposure for U.S. and non-U.S. persons; therefore any Iran-China side arrangement for “safe passage” would not be reliable for shipowners, banks, or P&I insurers without U.S. carveouts and insurance-market confirmation.[OFAC FAQ 1249](https://ofac.treasury.gov/faqs/1249), [OFAC Alert](https://ofac.treasury.gov/media/935556/download?inline=)

## 给能源分析师的下一步

1. 2026-05-14 至 2026-05-15 逐条比对 White House、State Department、中国外交部和 Xinhua readout：重点搜索 “Strait of Hormuz”、“safe passage”、“normal navigation”、“no tolls”、“ceasefire”、“regional security framework”。
2. 不把“China 支持恢复通行”计入等同于“China 担保通行”；只有出现护航安排、保险承保恢复、OFAC 豁免或 NAVCENT 指引变化，才可下调实货运输风险假设。
3. 每日跟踪 EIA/Vortexa/Kpler 对霍尔木兹实际流量的修正。EIA 已提示 2026 年 AIS 数据特别不可靠，单船 AIS 不能直接当作可运输量。
4. 对替代路线建模使用两组边界：保守值 EIA 约 4.7 mb/d 总绕行能力；弹性值 IEA 3.5-5.5 mb/d 可用能力。LNG 侧不应假设存在实质绕行。
5. 关注 China 2026-05 与 2026-06 原油和 LNG 进口、成品油出口配额、独立炼厂开工率；这些数据比峰会礼仪措辞更能验证 Beijing 的能源压力。

## 一句话判断

北京峰会的“可交易”结论是：美中在反对霍尔木兹收费和恢复通行上有有限交集，但尚无共同安全担保；替代路线容量远低于正常霍尔木兹流量，因此油价风险溢价只能因外交降温而边际回落，不能因“路线替代”而消失。
