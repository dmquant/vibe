# Paid Allocation of National Carbon Allowances: Potential Timetable and Liquidity Impact

**Anchor date: 2026-05-14. Bottom line: the regulatory question has moved from whether China will introduce paid allocation to when, by sector, and at what ratio. As of this date, regulators have not published a national paid-allocation ratio or auction calendar. The most likely path is: 2026 completes 2025 allowance verification, allocation, and compliance while preparing the 2027 entity list and data base; from 2027, small and sector-specific paid-allocation pilots begin in sectors with relatively stable emissions and stronger data quality; from 2028 to 2030, the ratio rises gradually, supporting a 2030 framework based on absolute caps plus a mix of free and paid allocation.**

## Priority Conclusions

1. **2026 is unlikely to be the year of broad national paid allocation.** The Ministry of Ecology and Environment’s 2026 work notice sets the timetable for 2025 emissions reporting, verification, pre-allocation, final allocation, and compliance: 2025 allowances for steel, cement, and aluminum smelting were to be pre-allocated by 2026-04-10, and power-sector allowances by 2026-06-30; 2025 allowances for all four sectors are to be allocated by 2026-09-30, with compliance due by 2026-12-31.[S3] The notice does not set a paid-allocation or auction schedule.

2. **2027 is the key institutional transition point, but it should not be read as a high-auction-share starting point.** The central policy opinion targets national ETS coverage of major industrial emitting sectors by 2027 and prioritizes absolute allowance caps for sectors with relatively stable emissions. MEE interpretation frames 2024-2027 as a phase for coverage expansion, foundation-building, and mechanism improvement, with 2027 still centered on intensity control, free allocation as the main approach, and paid allocation as a supplementary tool.[S2][S5] Therefore, 2027 is more likely to see small-ratio, sector-specific, floor-price or fixed-price sale/auction pilots rather than a large immediate auction program.

3. **2030 is the system-formation milestone.** The policy objective is to basically establish by 2030 a national ETS based on allowance caps and a combination of free and paid allocation. MEE commentary describes the 2027-2030 phase as a cap-based allocation system with broad coverage, stronger constraints, and more reasonable price signals.[S2][S5]

4. **The liquidity effect could be large, but it depends on whether paid allowances are incremental supply or a replacement for free allocation.** Using the 2024 compliance obligation of 8.194 billion tonnes as the scale reference, a 1% paid-allocation share equals 81.94 million tonnes, about 2.6 times the 31.8755 million tonnes traded from 2026-01-01 to 2026-05-13. A 5% share equals 410 million tonnes, about 1.7 times the full-year 2025 allowance trading volume of 235 million tonnes.[S7][S8] Regular auctions would materially increase primary-market turnover and create a price anchor; if paid allocation only replaces free allocation under a tighter cap, it would raise near-term compliance funding needs and price elasticity.

## Mechanisms Affecting Market Liquidity

- **The primary market would provide a missing price anchor.** Current pricing relies mainly on secondary trading and compliance demand. MEE commentary notes that paid allocation can provide a primary-market reference price and reduce information asymmetry and irrational volatility.[S6]

- **Trading frequency should rise.** Paid allocation through auctions or fixed-price sales makes covered entities buy allowances regularly. Combined with gradual entry of financial institutions, professional dealers, and other non-compliance participants, this should shift trading from end-period compliance concentration toward more continuous annual trading.[S2]

- **Hoarding should fall, while funding cost rises.** The 2023 and 2024 power-sector allocation plan introduced banking partly to address surplus-holder hoarding and inactive trading. If paid allocation is paired with banking and reserve mechanisms, it should further push surplus allowances into the market.[S9] For short entities, however, replacing free allowances with paid ones raises margin, cash-flow, and financing needs.

- **Short-term price direction is not determined by auctions alone.** If paid allowances come from a reserve and are released under a stabilization mechanism, available supply increases and near-term price spikes may ease. If paid allowances merely replace free allocation under a tighter cap, supply does not increase and prices and compliance costs can rise.

- **Sector differentiation will increase.** MEE has stated that future paid-allocation ratios will be differentiated by sector, considering abatement potential, cost differences, and capacity/output regulation.[S1] For utilities, power-sector allowance costs should be modeled separately from steel, cement, and aluminum smelting; a national average paid share should not be applied mechanically to power plants.

2. **Key dates:** 2025 allowances are due to be allocated by 2026-09-30; the 2027 covered-entity list is due by 2026-10-31; 2025 compliance is due by 2026-12-31.[S3]

3. **Price indicators:** If CEA stays above CNY 80/t before compliance while volume fails to expand, paid-allocation expectations may be reinforcing hoarding and shortage-covering demand. If volume rises and spreads narrow, banking and expanded-sector allowances are improving liquidity.

4. **Modeling suggestion:** For thermal-power assets, run at least 1%, 3%, and 5% paid-allocation cash-flow sensitivities under two cases: incremental auction supply and replacement of free allocation. The first is liquidity-positive; the second is cost-upward.

# 全国碳配额有偿分配：潜在时间表与流动性影响

**基准日期：2026-05-14。结论先行：监管方向已经从“是否引入有偿分配”转为“何时、分行业、以多大比例引入”，但截至本日尚未公布全国统一的有偿分配比例或拍卖日历。最可能的路径是 2026 年完成 2025 年度配额核定和清缴、夯实 2027 年名录与数据基础；2027 年起在碳排放总量相对稳定、数据基础较好的行业率先小比例试点；2028-2030 年逐步提高比例，并在 2030 年前形成以总量控制为基础、免费与有偿分配相结合的制度框架。**

## 优先结论

1. **2026 年大概率不是全国性有偿分配落地年。** 生态环境部 2026 年工作通知列明的是 2025 年度排放报告、核查、预分配、核定分配和 2025 年度配额清缴时间表：钢铁、水泥、铝冶炼 2025 年度配额须在 2026-04-10 前预分配，发电行业在 2026-06-30 前预分配；四个行业 2025 年度配额在 2026-09-30 前分配，2026-12-31 前完成清缴。[S3] 通知没有设置有偿分配或拍卖日程。

2. **2027 年是关键制度切换点，不宜等同于高比例拍卖。** 中央层面《意见》提出到 2027 年全国碳市场基本覆盖工业领域主要排放行业，并对碳排放总量相对稳定行业优先实施配额总量控制；生态环境部解读进一步把 2024-2027 年定位为“扩大范围、夯实基础、完善机制”，到 2027 年以“强度控制为核心、免费分配为主、有偿分配为辅”。[S2][S5] 因此，2027 年更可能是小比例、分行业、有底价或定价出售/拍卖试点，而不是一次性大规模拍卖。

3. **2030 年是制度成型节点。** 《意见》目标是到 2030 年基本建成以配额总量控制为基础、免费和有偿分配相结合的全国碳排放权交易市场；生态环境部解读称 2027-2030 年阶段将以总量控制为基础，形成覆盖广、约束强、价格信号合理的配额分配体系。[S2][S5]

4. **流动性影响会很显著，但取决于“有偿配额是新增供给还是替代免费配额”。** 以 2024 年度配额应清缴总量 81.94 亿吨作为规模参照，1% 有偿分配对应 8194 万吨，约为 2026 年 1 月 1 日至 5 月 13 日累计成交量 3187.55 万吨的 2.6 倍；5% 对应 4.10 亿吨，约为 2025 年全年配额成交量 2.35 亿吨的 1.7 倍。[S7][S8] 若通过定期拍卖释放，会显著增加一级市场成交和价格锚；若只是从免费配额中切出并加严总量，短期会抬升履约资金需求和价格弹性。

## 对市场流动性的机制影响

- **一级市场会补齐价格锚。** 当前市场主要依靠二级交易和履约需求形成价格。生态环境部解读指出，有偿分配可提供一级市场价格参考，减少信息不对称和非理性波动。[S6]

- **交易频次会提高。** 有偿分配通过拍卖或定价出售让控排企业定期参与买入，叠加金融机构、专业交易商等非履约主体逐步进入，有助于把交易从履约截止日前的集中成交转为更连续的年度交易。[S2]

- **惜售会下降，但资金成本会上升。** 发电行业 2023、2024 年配额方案引入结转政策，目的之一是解决盈余企业惜售和交易不活跃问题；有偿分配若配合结转和储备调节，会进一步迫使盈余配额进入市场。[S9] 但对短缺企业而言，免费配额被有偿部分替代会提高保证金、现金流和财务费用需求。

- **短期价格方向不单由“拍卖”决定。** 若有偿分配来自配额储备并按稳定机制释放，增加可得供给，短期可能缓和价格尖峰；若有偿配额只是总量收紧下的免费转付费，则供给不增加，价格和履约成本可能上行。

- **行业差异化会增强。** 生态环境部已经明确未来将综合考虑减排潜力、成本差异、产能产量调控等因素，分行业差异化设置并有序提高有偿分配比例。[S1] 对公用事业而言，需把电力行业与钢铁、水泥、铝冶炼分开建模，不能用全国平均比例直接套用电厂配额成本。

## 给公用事业分析师的跟踪项

1. **2026 年内重点看三类文件：** 发电行业 2025/2026 年度配额方案、全国配额储备和市场调节机制规则、有偿分配收入管理办法或拍卖规则。

2. **关键日期：** 2026-09-30 前完成 2025 年度配额分配；2026-10-31 前公布 2027 年度重点排放单位名录；2026-12-31 前完成 2025 年度配额清缴。[S3]

3. **价格指标：** 若 CEA 在清缴期前持续站上 80 元/吨且成交量不放大，说明有偿分配预期可能强化“惜售+缺口补库”；若成交量放大且价差收窄，说明结转和扩围配额正在改善流动性。

4. **建模建议：** 对火电资产至少跑 1%、3%、5% 有偿分配比例的现金流敏感性，并分别采用“新增拍卖供给”和“替代免费配额”两种情景；前者偏流动性改善，后者偏成本上行。
