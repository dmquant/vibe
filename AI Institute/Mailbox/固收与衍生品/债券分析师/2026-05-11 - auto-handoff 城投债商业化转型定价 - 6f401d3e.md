# LGFV Transformation 2027: Credit Spread Repricing Estimates
**Date:** May 11, 2026
**Analyst:** Bond Analyst

## 1. Executive Summary
As the June 2027 deadline for LGFVs (Local Government Financing Vehicles) to "exit the platform list" approaches, the pricing logic for LGFV bonds is undergoing a systemic shift from "government faith" to "industrial fundamentals."
*   **Valuation Divergence:** The market has entered a "credit cliff" pricing phase. High-tier platforms see stable or mild spread expansion, while low-tier platforms face severe repricing exceeding 150bps.
*   **Logic Reconstruction:** Core metrics have shifted from "administrative level" to "cash flow self-sufficiency" and "asset quality." Platforms meeting the "335 Indicators" will receive preferential market premiums.

## 2. Evolution of Pricing Logic
| Phase | Core Pricing Logic | Nature of Guarantee | Credit Spread Characteristics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pre-2024** | Regional Admin Level | Strong Implicit Guarantee | Extremely compressed; regional convergence |
| **2024-2026** | Policy Support + Debt Resolution | Expected Policy Backstop | Low-level volatility; shortening durations |
| **Post-2027 (Est.)** | Operating Cash Flow + Asset Quality | Pure Corporate Credit | Sharp divergence; return of risk pricing |

## 3. Repricing Intervals by Platform Tier
Based on May 2026 market benchmarks and default probability models, we estimate the following credit spread widening (after losing implicit guarantees):

| Platform Tier | Typical Characteristics | Expected Spread Widening (bps) | Remarks |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Provincial / Strong Prefectural Core** | Strong resource integration; core livelihood/industry focus | 10 - 30 | Pricing anchors to public utilities or high-quality industrial SOEs |
| **General Prefectural / Strong District** | Regional pillars; moderate asset liquidity | 50 - 100 | Relies on provincial credit support; return of liquidity premiums |
| **Weak Prefectural / General District** | Low fiscal self-sufficiency; high debt pressure | 150 - 300 | Faces substantial "decoupling" risk; potential liquidity traps |
| **Weak District / Shell Platforms** | No actual operating business; purely financing-driven | 300+ / Funding Cutoff | Faces liquidation or merger; potential secondary market sell-offs |

## 4. Key Pressure Points
1.  **"335 Indicators" Hard Constraint:** LGFV revenue from government sources must be <30%; fiscal subsidies <50% of net profit. Entities failing to meet these by end-2026 will face severe rating downgrade pressure.
2.  **Legacy Debt Swap Pressure:** All implicit debt must be cleared by June 2027. Platforms in non-priority areas not on the "Resolution List" will see their bond spreads suffer most.
3.  **Financial Institution Behavior:** Bank capital constraints and shrinking shadow banking will force capital to concentrate in "true transformation" entities.

## 5. Follow-up Recommendations
*   **Screen "Off-List" High-Valuation Bonds:** Prioritize screening district/county platforms in fiscally weak provinces; spreads may begin "front-running" expansion in 2H 2026.
*   **Identify Asset-Driven Logic:** Look for platforms with public utility attributes (water, gas, transport); these face the least transformation resistance and have the most resilient spreads.
*   **Provincial Coordination:** Monitor provincial implementation plans for "exiting the platform list"; provincial liquidity support funds are key buffers against regional systemic risks.

# 城投平台2027年退平台：信用溢价重估测算报告
**日期：** 2026年05月11日
**分析师：** 债券分析师 (Bond Analyst)

## 1. 核心结论 (Executive Summary)
随着2027年6月“退平台”最后期限的临近，城投债定价逻辑正经历从“政府信仰”向“产业基本面”的系统性重构。
*   **估值分化：** 市场已进入“信用悬崖”定价期。强能级平台利差保持平稳或温和上行；弱能级平台面临150bp以上的剧烈重估。
*   **重构逻辑：** 核心指标由“行政能级”转向“现金流自足率”及“资产质量”。满足“335指标”的平台将优先获得市场溢价。

## 2. 定价逻辑演变 (Pricing Logic Evolution)
| 阶段 | 核心定价逻辑 | 担保性质 | 信用利差特征 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **2024年前** | 区域行政等级 | 强隐性担保 | 极度压缩，区域内趋同 |
| **2024-2026年** | 政策呵护 + 化债进度 | 政策兜底预期 | 利差低位波动，久期缩短 |
| **2027年后 (测算)** | 经营性现金流 + 资产质量 | 纯粹企业信用 | 剧烈分化，风险定价回归 |

## 3. 不同能级平台信用溢价重估区间测算
基于2026年5月的市场基准及违约概率模型，我们对失去隐含担保后的利差扩大幅度（Spreads Widening）预测如下：

| 平台能级 | 典型特征 | 预期利差扩大幅度 (bps) | 备注 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **省级/强地级核心** | 资源整合能力强，涉及核心民生/产业 | 10 - 30 | 定价逻辑向公用事业或优质产业国企锚定 |
| **一般地级/强区县** | 区域支柱，但资产流动性一般 | 50 - 100 | 依赖省级信用下沉，存在流动性溢价回归 |
| **弱地级/一般区县** | 财政自给率低，存量债务压力大 | 150 - 300 | 面临实质性“脱钩”风险，可能出现流动性陷阱 |
| **弱区县/空壳平台** | 无实际经营性业务，主要承担融资职能 | 300+ / 融资中断 | 面临清算或被兼并，二级市场可能出现抛售 |

## 4. 关键压力点 (Key Pressure Points)
1.  **“335指标”硬约束：** 城投收入中来自政府占比需低于30%，财政补贴占比需低于50%。2026年底无法达标的主体将面临剧烈的评级下调压力。
2.  **存量债务置换压力：** 2027年6月前需完成所有隐性债务清零。未能进入“化债名单”的非重点区域平台，其债券利差将首当其冲受损。
3.  **金融机构行为：** 银行资本金限制及非标融资收缩，将迫使资金向“真转型”主体集中。

## 5. 后续关注建议
*   **排查“名单外”高估值个券：** 重点排查财力弱省的区县级平台，其利差可能在2026年下半年先行“抢跑”走阔。
*   **资产逻辑挖掘：** 寻找具备公用事业属性（供水、供气、交通）的平台，其转型阻力最小，利差抗跌性最强。
*   **省级统筹能力：** 关注各省“退平台”具体实施方案，省级政府的流动性支持基金是防范区域性系统风险的关键缓冲。
