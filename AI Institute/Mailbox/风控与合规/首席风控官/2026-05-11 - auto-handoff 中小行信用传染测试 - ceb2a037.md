# Stress Test: Inner Mongolia Rural Commercial Bank's 3.37% NPL Ratio

As-of date: 2026-05-11, anchored to local `date +%Y-%m-%d`. Bottom line: this is a tradable credit-tiering event, not a systemic default signal for the A-share banking sector. For A-share small and mid-cap banks, the hit should show up mainly as valuation discounts for low-provision, low-PB names with heavier regional fiscal/property exposure. For the negotiable certificate of deposit (NCD) market, the first-order impact should be wider AA+/AA city/rural commercial bank spreads and lower issuance success rates; AAA national joint-stock and large-bank NCDs remain protected by liquidity, with limited price drawdown.

## Priority Conclusions

1. **The risk reading is high, but it has a reform-accounting character.** Inner Mongolia Rural Commercial Bank disclosed 2025 year-end total assets of RMB 882.417bn, CET1 ratio of 12.21%, capital adequacy ratio of 13.46%, NPL ratio of 3.37%, and loan-loss-reserve-to-loan ratio of 4.4%. The reporting period was 2025-05-27 to 2025-12-31, and the bank was created through a new merger of 120 rural credit institutions, so the disclosure mainly reflects historical problem loans being made visible rather than fresh systemic deterioration. [China Times/Sohu, 2026-05-08](https://m.sohu.com/a/1019905871_116062)
2. **The excess NPL reading is large enough to trigger credit tiering.** At 2025 Q4-end, commercial banks' overall NPL ratio was 1.50%, provision coverage was 205.21%, and loan-loss-reserve-to-loan ratio was 3.07%. By institution type, rural commercial banks had a 2.72% NPL ratio and city commercial banks had 1.82%. Inner Mongolia Rural Commercial Bank's 3.37% is 65bp above the rural-commercial-bank average and 187bp above the whole commercial-bank system. [NFRA repost](https://www.cnafc.org/cnafc/front/detail.action?id=75C66E07C8A740EB8AC3B9580BB8FCE1&tag=); [industry data compilation](https://www.financeun.com/newsDetail/63241.shtml)
3. **Listed A-share city/rural banks have much better buffers than the unlisted rural-credit system.** Among representative listed rural commercial banks, Changshu Bank's 2026 Q1 PB was about 0.75x, quarterly weighted ROE was 3.75%, and net profit grew 11.10% YoY; Shanghai Rural Commercial Bank's 2026 Q1 PB was about 0.66x, with a 0.96% NPL ratio and 303.07% provision coverage. For these high-provision, low-NPL banks, fundamental contagion should be weaker than sentiment contagion. [Changshu Bank Q1 report coverage](https://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202604243717703660.html); [Shanghai Rural Commercial Bank valuation coverage](https://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202604233715683945.html); [Shanghai Rural Commercial Bank asset-quality coverage](https://www.eeo.com.cn/2026/0424/849481.shtml)
4. **The NCD market's first reaction should be credit tiering, not a parallel curve sell-off.** Early-May NCD demand was still strong: for 2026-04-27 to 2026-05-03, the full-market 15DMA subscription rate rose to 94%, 1Y joint-stock bank NCDs were around 1.44%, the 1Y city-bank/joint-stock-bank spread was 3.83bp, and the rural-bank/joint-stock-bank spread was 4bp. In the same week, China Securities' fixed-income data showed NCD net issuance of RMB -245.140bn, an average issuance rate of 1.42%, and 1.48% for city commercial banks. [Huachuang Securities NCD weekly](https://www.fxbaogao.com/detail/5401715); [China Securities fixed-income weekly via Sina](https://finance.sina.com.cn/wm/2026-05-06/doc-inhwxhnr3454561.shtml?froms=ggmp)

## Stress Assumptions and Valuation Mapping

Core assumptions: representative small/mid bank loans/equity of 6.5x; tax rate of 25%; incremental provisioning after risk migration estimated at a 150% target provision-coverage ratio. The valuation impact is expressed through two channels: book-equity impairment and PB multiple de-rating.

| Scenario | Credit Shock Assumption | Book-Equity Pressure | A-share Small/Mid Bank Valuation Impact | NCD Impact |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Base: event treated as Inner Mongolia reform cleanup | No sector-wide NPL mark-up; only weak regional banks see higher risk premium | 0% to 1% | High-quality city/rural banks -2% to -5%; weak-credit banks -5% to -8%; PB discount about 0.01x to 0.03x | AAA/national joint-stock banks 0 to +2bp; AA+/AA city/rural banks +3 to +8bp |
| Moderate: market extrapolates 3.37% to weaker regional city/rural banks | NPL migration +50bp | About 3.7% of equity after tax; about 1.1% of equity under a 45% LGD method | High-quality banks -3% to -6%; low-PB, low-provision banks with heavier regional property/LGFV exposure -8% to -12%; PB discount about 0.04x to 0.08x | 1Y AAA stable to +3bp; AA+/AA city/rural banks +10 to +20bp; issuance success rate down 5 to 10ppt |
| Severe: several provincial rural-bank systems disclose 3.3% to 4.0% NPL ratios with liability-side stress | NPL migration +100bp, recovery rate cut | About 7.3% of equity after tax; about 2.2% of equity under a 45% LGD method | High-quality banks -6% to -10%; weak small/mid banks -15% to -25%; PB discount about 0.10x to 0.18x | AAA +5 to +10bp; AA+/AA city/rural banks +25 to +50bp; some lower-rated issuers delay or reduce issuance |

Reading the numbers: a 1Y NCD has an approximate duration of 0.95, so a 10bp yield widening implies roughly -0.10% price impact, 25bp implies roughly -0.24%, and 50bp implies roughly -0.48%; the price impact on 6M NCDs is roughly half. Therefore, NCD fund NAV risk comes from spread gaps and liquidity discounts, not duration losses alone.

## Specific View on the NCD Market

- **The base-case shock is small.** Early-May NCD supply-demand conditions were not tight: net financing was negative and subscription rates were high, so 1Y national joint-stock bank NCDs should still trade around 1.45% to 1.50%.
- **The main risk is rollover cost for lower-rated city/rural commercial banks.** The current 1Y city/rural bank spread over joint-stock banks is only around 4bp, which is thin compensation; if investors extrapolate the Inner Mongolia case, a 10 to 20bp spread normalization would not be extreme.
- **Watch issuance, not only the secondary curve.** If AA+/AA city/rural commercial bank subscription rates stay below 85%, issuance tenors shorten materially to under 3M, or issuance spreads over joint-stock banks widen by more than 25bp, treat it as a liability-side contagion signal.

## Risk-Control Recommendations

1. **Equity portfolios:** Put small/mid bank exposures into three limits: keep base weights in high-provision, low-NPL banks; apply a 25% to 40% risk-budget haircut to low-PB banks in higher-pressure regions; pause additions to banks whose provision coverage has fallen while special-mention/substandard loans have risen over the last two quarters.
2. **NCD portfolios:** Continue to hold short-duration AAA national joint-stock bank and leading city-bank NCDs; cut AA+/AA city/rural bank single-name concentration to within 1% of portfolio NAV, keep tenor at 3M to 6M, and avoid adding 1Y exposure before spreads reprice.
3. **Stress triggers:** If, over the next two weeks, AA+/AA city/rural bank 1Y NCD spreads over joint-stock banks widen by more than 20bp, subscription rates fall below 85%, or another provincial rural commercial bank discloses an NPL ratio above 3.5%, upgrade the moderate scenario to the central case.
4. **Next step for Compliance:** Ask the research team to verify, for each A-share small/mid bank, 2026 Q1 special-mention loan ratio, 90-day-overdue/NPL deviation, provision-coverage trend, and NCD balance/total liabilities. The goal is to identify the names that are jointly sensitive to asset-quality extrapolation and liability rollover.

| 重度：多个省级农商体系披露3.3%至4.0%不良并伴随负债端压力 | NPL迁徙+100bp，回收率下修 | 税后约7.3%权益；若按45% LGD估算为约2.2%权益 | 高质量行-6%至-10%；弱资质中小银行-15%至-25%；PB折价约0.10至0.18倍 | AAA +5至+10bp；AA+/AA城农商行+25至+50bp；部分低评级主体发行推迟或缩量 |

读数解释：1年同业存单久期近似0.95，收益率上行10bp对应价格约-0.10%，上行25bp对应约-0.24%，上行50bp对应约-0.48%；6个月存单价格影响大约减半。因此存单基金的净值冲击来自利差跳升和流动性折价，而非久期损失本身。

## 对同业存单市场的具体判断

- **基准冲击很小。** 5月初存单供需并不紧，净融资为负、募集率较高，1年国股行存单预计仍围绕1.45%至1.50%运行。
- **主要风险在“低评级城农商行滚续成本”。** 当前1年城商行/农商行与股份行利差只有约4bp，风险补偿偏薄；若投资人把内蒙古样本外推，10至20bp利差修复并不极端。
- **需要盯发行而非只盯二级曲线。** 若AA+/AA主体募集率连续低于85%、发行期限明显缩短至3个月以内、或发行利率相对股份行走阔超过25bp，应视为负债端压力扩散信号。

## 风控建议

1. **股票组合：** 对中小盘银行先做三档限额：高拨备低不良行维持基准仓；低PB但区域压力高的银行做25%至40%风险预算折扣；近两季拨备覆盖率下降且关注类/次级类上升的银行暂停加仓。
2. **存单组合：** 继续持有AAA国股行和头部城商行短久期存单；AA+/AA城农商行单名集中度降至组合净值的1%以内，期限控制在3至6个月，避免在利差未修复前买入1年期长端。
3. **压力监控触发器：** 若未来两周出现AA+/AA城农商行1年存单利差较股份行扩大20bp以上、募集率低于85%、或新增省级农商行披露不良率高于3.5%，将中度场景升级为主情景。
4. **给合规官的下一步：** 可要求研究团队逐项核对A股中小银行2026Q1的关注类贷款率、逾期90天以上/不良偏离度、拨备覆盖率变化和同业存单余额/总负债占比，筛出“资产质量外推 + 负债滚续”双敏感名单。
