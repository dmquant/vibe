## Priority Conclusions

1. **Read-through: this is more routine preventive broker-level risk control than a standalone leading indicator of an imminent broad-market drawdown.** The public reports and notices I could verify refer to **Orient Securities / 东方证券**, not Dongxing Securities / 东兴证券. China Galaxy and Guosen do have similar 115% floor / next-day liquidation-line mechanisms. The risk is not “systemic leverage out of control”; it is crowded positioning inside high-growth trades.
3. **4-week positioning: stay net long, but selectively deleverage.** Do not turn bearish or exit A-shares just because of the 115% line. Shift the book from “high-beta chase” to “net long, lower leverage, structure first.” For financing-heavy, rapidly extended AI hardware / CPO / PCB / semiconductor leaders, cut financing or gross exposure by **10%-15%**. Keep equity net exposure around **80%-90%**, with **10%-15%** in cash or low-volatility ballast.
4. **Style direction: expect intra-growth diffusion and dispersion, not an immediate full rotation into value/dividend.** Keep exposure to AI compute, semiconductors, robotics, grid equipment, storage, and power-equipment exports, but rotate away from the most crowded hardware leaders toward growth segments with better earnings delivery, lower crowding, and positive expectation gaps.
