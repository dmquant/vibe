*   **Precious Metals (Gold):** Maintain a 5–8% allocation as the ultimate hedge against geopolitical escalation.

### III. A-share Style Rotation

*   **Resource Alpha:** Shift from "Pure Defensive" to "Offensive Resources," focusing on industry leaders with global pricing power.
