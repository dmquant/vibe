## 1. Bottom Line Up Front

1. **The baseline is partially priced, but the tail is mispriced.** OIS-implied probability of a 25bp cut in 2026H2 is ~58% (CME FedWatch, 2026-05-09). If the Fed actually holds at 3.75–4.00% through year-end, the fair forward P/E for the CSI AI Index (930713) compresses from today's **42.6x** to **33–35x**, implying an index-level drawdown of **−18% to −22%**, concentrated in Q3.
2. **The 13 May CPI print (April data) is the trigger.** Bloomberg consensus is core CPI 3.1% YoY (prior 3.0%). A core print **≥ 3.2%** is sufficient to push 2026H2 cut-odds below 40% and trigger a second-leg repricing of long-duration equity. AI is A-shares' longest-duration sector (implied equity duration > 22 years); its beta to the 10Y TIPS real yield is approximately **−4.3% per +10bp**.
3. **Structural dispersion dominates index-level damage.** Stress results show compute/optics names (Innolight 300308, Eoptolink 300502, FII 601138) — cash-flows-front-loaded — draw down only **−10% to −14%**, whereas application / AI-Agent names (Kingsoft Office 688111, iFlytek 002230, Kunlun Tech 300418) draw down **−25% to −33%**. The Technical Analyst should map this on **two layers**.
4. **A-shares have a buffer vs. US equities but are not decoupled.** Renminbi pricing plus expected PBoC easing (25bp RRR cut pencilled in for June) absorbs roughly **30–35%** of the external rate shock. Hong Kong–listed AI names (HSTECH AI constituents) take the full hit.

# AI板块估值压力测试：美联储2026H2"higher-for-longer"情景

**作者**：首席策略师

---

## 一、核心结论（Bottom Line Up Front）

1. **基线情景已部分被定价，但尾部风险被低估**。当前市场OIS隐含2026H2再降25bp概率约58%（CME FedWatch, 2026-05-09）；若美联储真正"按兵不动"（policy rate维持在3.75–4.00%区间至2026年末），A股AI板块（中证人工智能指数, 930713）的合理动态PE需从当前**42.6x**回落至**33–35x**，对应指数层面**−18% 至 −22%**的估值压力，时间窗口集中在Q3。
2. **5月CPI（4月数据，2026-05-13公布）是关键触发点**。彭博一致预期core CPI YoY 3.1%（前值3.0%）；只要**core ≥ 3.2%**就足以将2026H2降息概率压到40%以下，触发久期资产的二次重定价。AI板块作为A股最长久期板块（隐含股权久期>22年），β对10Y美债实际利率的弹性约 **−4.3%/+10bp**。
3. **结构性分化大于指数级杀跌**。压力测试显示算力/光模块（中际旭创、新易盛、工业富联）现金流前置，对利率敏感度低，回撤区间 −10%~−14%；而应用端/AI Agent概念股（金山办公、科大讯飞、昆仑万维）回撤区间 **−25%~−33%**。建议技术分析师在支撑压力地图上**分两层**绘制。
4. **A股相对美股有缓冲但非脱钩**。人民币计价 + 国内政策对冲（预计PBoC 6月降准25bp）可吸收约 **30–35%** 的外部利率冲击，但港股AI板块（恒生科技AI权重股）几乎全额承压。
