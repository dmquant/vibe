# Thematic Growth Overweight Limits

Date anchor: local `date +%Y-%m-%d` returned **2026-05-11**. All references to “current,” “last trading day,” and “this week” are resolved against that date. At the time of writing, the latest publicly verifiable cross-asset data mostly falls between 2026-05-07 and 2026-05-11.

## Conclusions First

**Treat “low-altitude economy + biotech GLP-1/AI drug discovery” as a satellite growth sleeve, not a substitute for the broad-market core.** In a 60/40 framework, the reasonable thematic basket range is **5%-8% of NAV**, with a tactical hard cap of **9% of NAV**. If the primary benchmark is the S&P 500, lower the range to **3%-6% of NAV**, because the index already embeds Lilly-style GLP-1 exposure and a high-duration growth profile.

**Manage the overweight limits in total-NAV bps, not only in equity-sleeve bps.** With a 60% equity allocation, `300 bps of NAV = 5.0% of the equity sleeve`. Recommended limits:

| Benchmark | Recommended active overweight cap (total NAV) | Equivalent inside a 60% equity sleeve | Recommended total thematic basket | Portfolio implication |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Broad A-share benchmark | **+300 bps** | +5.0% of equity sleeve | 5%-8% NAV | Keeps a growth overweight, but avoids chasing small-cap themes while margin financing is elevated. |
| CSI 300 | **+350 bps** | +5.8% of equity sleeve | 5%-8% NAV, hard cap 9% | Direct low-altitude/eVTOL exposure in CSI 300 is limited, so the cap can be slightly above broad A-share; the index still has indirect IT, industrials, and healthcare exposure. |
| S&P 500 | **+200 bps** | +3.3% of equity sleeve | 3%-6% NAV | The S&P 500 already has GLP-1 leader exposure: Slickcharts shows Eli Lilly at about **1.26%** of the S&P 500 around May 2026; adding China thematic exposure should therefore use a tighter active-risk budget. |

**Use a “three-factor, any two trigger” risk rule for trimming.** If any two of U.S. 10Y yields, DXY, and A-share risk appetite move into red-light territory, cut the thematic basket from 5%-8% to **3%-4% of NAV**. If all three turn red, reduce to **0%-2% of NAV**, retaining only names with the strongest earnings, orders, clinical, or BD evidence.

## Why Not Higher

In 2026, thematic equities are moving from “narrative diffusion” into a capex and order-delivery test. Low-altitude economy has policy support and market potential, but commercialization still depends on airworthiness, infrastructure, operating scenarios, and order delivery. GLP-1 demand is highly visible, but crowding, pricing pressure, reimbursement policy, clinical readouts, and large-pharma supply expansion can all amplify volatility. The market has catalysts; it lacks a generous valuation cushion.

# 主题成长超配上限建议

日期锚点：本机 `date +%Y-%m-%d` 为 **2026-05-11**。以下“当前”“上一交易日”“本周”均按该日期回溯；截至撰写时，可公开核验的最新跨市场数据主要落在 2026-05-07 至 2026-05-11。

## 结论优先

**建议把“低空经济 + 生物科技 GLP-1/AI 制药”定义为成长表达的卫星仓，而不是替代宽基核心仓。** 在 60/40 框架下，主题篮子合理仓位是 **5%-8% NAV**，战术上限 **9% NAV**；若以标普 500 为主基准，因指数已内含 Lilly 等 GLP-1 权重和较高久期成长暴露，建议区间下移到 **3%-6% NAV**。

**超配上限用总 NAV bps 管理，不用股票仓 bps 掩盖真实风险。** 若股票仓为 60%，则 `300 bps NAV = 5.0% 股票仓`。建议上限如下：
