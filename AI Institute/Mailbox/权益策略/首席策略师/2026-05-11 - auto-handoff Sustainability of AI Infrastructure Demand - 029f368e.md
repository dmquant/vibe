# Analysis of AI Data Center Demand for Copper and Lithium: Will Efficiency Lead to a 2027 Inflection Point?

**To:** Materials Analyst
**Date:** May 11, 2026
**Subject:** Assessment of Computing Efficiency Gains on Long-term Commodity (Copper, Lithium) Demand

---

## Executive Summary (Conclusions)

**Computing power efficiency gains will NOT lead to a demand decline for copper and lithium after 2027. Instead, they are likely to trigger further demand expansion via the "Jevons Paradox."**

Research indicates that while GPU efficiency (FLOPs/Watt) is improving significantly, these leaps lower the cost per inference, unlocking massive "latent demand" (e.g., real-time omni-modal interaction, large-scale autonomous agent swarms). This results in a continuous expansion of total AI data center power consumption and infrastructure scale.

1.  **Copper:** 2027 will be the critical inflection point where the market shifts from "surplus" to a "structural deficit." The adoption of liquid cooling and short-range copper interconnects (such as NVLink) will increase copper intensity per facility by 3x to 10x.
2.  **Lithium:** Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) for AI data centers are becoming the "third pillar" of lithium demand. AIDC lithium-ion battery shipments are projected to surge from 12 GWh in 2025 to 61 GWh by 2027, with no signs of slowing down before 2030.
3.  **The Efficiency Trap:** Although algorithmic efficiency is rising (e.g., breakthroughs like DeepSeek V4), physical power density (power per rack) is skyrocketing from 40kW to 120kW and even 1MW, leading to rigid growth in demand for underlying commodities.

---

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

## Key Drivers Analysis

### 1. Jevons Paradox and "Efficiency-Led Expansion"
- **Observation:** Nvidia's Blackwell (B200) is multiple times more efficient than Hopper, yet its single-chip power consumption (TDP) has risen from 700W to 1,200W.
- **Logic:** Efficiency makes AI inference extremely cheap, leading to an explosion in downstream applications (autonomous driving, humanoid robots, AI search). This "rebound effect" means that while material use per unit of compute decreases, the total scale of compute expands far faster than efficiency improves.

### 2. The Return of "Structural Rigidity" in Copper
- **Liquid Cooling Revolution:** By 2027, the majority of high-compute racks will be forced to switch to liquid cooling. Since liquid cooling systems require significant amounts of copper cold plates, manifolds, and piping, copper consumption per rack will increase.
- **Short-Distance Copper Interconnects:** To minimize latency and power loss, supercomputer clusters post-2027 (e.g., Stargate) will utilize copper cabling for short-range (<10m) interconnects rather than fiber optics, further locking in demand for high-purity copper.

### 3. Lithium's "New Pillar": Data Center BESS
- **Grid Strain:** Because utility grid expansion cannot keep pace with AI demand, hyperscalers (Google, Microsoft, etc.) are being forced to build their own microgrids equipped with large-scale Lithium-Iron Phosphate (LFP) BESS.
- **Chemistry Stability:** LFP has become the standard for AIDC due to safety. As energy density requirements for AIDC rise, the intensity of lithium use remains robust.

---

## Follow-up Items

1.  **Supply-Side Lag:** Copper mining cycles take 15-20 years. The demand explosion in 2027, combined with current investment lags, will create a severe price squeeze.
2.  **Edge AI:** Monitor whether a significant shift of compute to edge devices post-2030 might slow the construction rate of hyperscale data centers.
3.  **Substitution Risks:** Watch for large-scale adoption of graphene cooling or Co-Packaged Optics (CPO) after 2028, which could potentially replace copper interconnects.

# AI 数据中心对铜与锂需求趋势分析：效率提升是否会导致 2027 年需求拐点？

**致：** 材料行业分析师
**日期：** 2026年5月11日
**主题：** AI 算力效率提升对大宗商品（铜、锂）中长期需求的冲击评估

---

## 核心结论 (Conclusions)

**算力效率的提升不会在 2027 年导致铜、锂需求的下降，反而可能通过“耶文斯悖论”（Jevons Paradox）触发需求的进一步爆发。** 

研究表明，虽然 GPU 的每瓦算力（FLOPs/Watt）在显著提高，但这种效率的飞跃降低了单次推理的成本，进而激发出更庞大的“潜在需求”（如实时全模态交互、大规模智能体集群），导致 AI 数据中心总功耗和基础设施规模持续扩张。

1.  **铜 (Copper)：** 2027 年将是从“过剩”转向“结构性短缺”的关键拐点。液冷技术的普及和短距铜缆连接（NVLink 等）将使 AI 数据中心的单位铜耗提升 3-10 倍。
2.  **锂 (Lithium)：** AI 数据中心储能系统（BESS）将成为锂需求的“第三支柱”。预计 AIDC 锂电出货量将从 2025 年的 12 GWh 激增至 2027 年的 61 GWh，且在 2030 年前无放缓迹象。
3.  **效率陷阱：** 虽然算法效率在提升（如 DeepSeek V4 等模型的突破），但物理层面的功率密度（每机架功率）正在从 40kW 飙升至 120kW 甚至 1MW，对底层大宗商品的需求呈现刚性增长。

## 关键影响因素分析 (Analysis)

### 1. 耶文斯悖论与“效率带来的规模扩张”
- **现象：** Nvidia Blackwell (B200) 的推理效率比 Hopper 高出数倍，但其单芯片功耗从 700W 提升至 1,200W。
- **逻辑：** 效率提升使 AI 推理变得极其廉价，导致下游应用（如自动驾驶、人形机器人、AI 搜索）大规模爆发。这种“回流效应”意味着虽然单位算力耗材减少，但总算力规模的扩张速度远超效率提升速度。

### 2. 铜的“结构性刚需”回归
- **液冷革命：** 2027 年前，绝大多数高算力机架将强制转向液冷。由于液冷系统需要大量铜质冷板、歧管和管道，单机位铜耗将增加。
- **短距铜缆连接：** 为了降低延迟和功耗，2027 年后的超级计算机集群（如 Stargate）将在短距离（<10米）互联中大量使用铜缆而非光纤，这进一步锁定了高纯度铜的需求。

### 3. 锂需求的“新极点”：数据中心储能 (BESS)
- **电网压力：** 由于公用电网扩容速度跟不上 AI 需求， hyperscalers（谷歌、微软等）正被迫自建配备大型锂电储能系统的微电网。
- **化学体系：** 磷酸铁锂（LFP）因其高安全性成为 AIDC 的首选。随着 AIDC 能量密度要求提高，锂的使用强度保持稳健。

---

## 后续关注项 (Follow-up Items)

1.  **供给侧滞后：** 铜矿开采周期长达 15-20 年。2027 年的需求爆发与当前的投资滞后将形成价格挤压。
2.  **端侧 AI (Edge AI)：** 需警惕 2030 年后，若大量算力转移至端侧设备，是否会减缓超大规模数据中心的建设速度。
3.  **技术代换风险：** 关注石墨烯散热或光电集成芯片（CPO）是否会在 2028 年后大规模替代铜互联。
