## K-Shaped or Oil-Driven

Base case: `65%` probability that K-shaped bifurcation continues, `25%` probability of a short-lived sentiment repair from oil/geopolitical news, and only `10%` probability of a broad cyclical consumption restart.

Rationale:

- A broad cyclical rebound should show simultaneous improvement across low-end stores, discounters, department stores, restaurants, and home-related retail. Instead, three weeks of weaker low-end traffic alongside Costco at `+1.9%` is more consistent with share shift.
- A pure oil-relief explanation should show sustained pump-price declines and quick improvement in lower-income traffic. Latest EIA weekly pump prices were still rising, and the March energy CPI shock was large.
- A K-shaped framework should show necessities, value, membership, and stock-up channels outperforming high-ticket, premium, and pure discretionary categories. That matches public Placer observations on Costco, Dollar General/Dollar Tree, discretionary value retailers, and Q1 discretionary retail: value and selected low-ticket categories are resilient, while apparel, luxury, and full-price discretionary remain mixed. ## Allocation Implications

### Staples vs. Discretionary

- Maintain a modest staples overweight versus discretionary, around `+2-3pct`, until low-end traffic stabilizes for at least `2` consecutive weeks, regular gasoline moves below `$4.00/gal` and stays there, and real DPI turns positive again.
- Do not chase all staples indiscriminately. Costco/Walmart-type value, traffic, and supply-chain franchises deserve a quality premium, but valuation discipline matters; prefer stable cash flow, lower price points, and high turnover.
- In discretionary, keep only structural exposures: low-price discretionary, repair/replacement demand, affordable beauty, sports/outdoor, and off-price retail. Underweight high-ticket durables, luxury, full-price apparel, and restaurants with high dependence on lower-income customers.

### A-Share Staples vs. Discretionary

- Within staples, prioritize mass food and beverage, condiments/frozen food/snacks with lower price points, discount retail and supply-chain enablers, packaging, and selected agri-food chains that can benefit from cost relief. The key tests are turnover improvement and controlled selling expenses, not simple pricing power.
- Be cautious with premium baijiu despite its “staples” label. Its demand behaves more like business activity, wealth effect, and gifting discretionary demand, so U.S. low-end stress plus Costco strength does not directly justify adding premium baijiu exposure.
- Within discretionary, keep only value-for-money discretionary: affordable beauty, sports/outdoor, repair/replacement appliances, and selected services. Keep U.S.-exported furniture, apparel, home goods, and low-end consumer electronics underweight or hedged, because weaker U.S. lower-income traffic can hit order visibility and channel destocking first.
- Style should lean toward quality dividend and cash flow, with low exposure to pure consumption-recovery beta. If oil genuinely rolls over and translates into better real disposable income, rotate gradually from defensive staples toward discretionary beta.

## K 型还是油价瞬时反弹

基准判断：`65%`概率是 K 型分化延续，`25%`是油价/地缘消息带来的短期情绪修复，`10%`才是宽基周期性消费重启。

理由：

- 如果是宽基周期反弹，应看到低端、折扣、百货、餐饮、家居等客流同步改善；现在低端门店连续 `3` 周走弱，和 Costco `+1.9%`并存，更像份额转移。
- 如果只是油价回落，应看到汽油泵价连续回落并快速带动低收入相关门店回暖；但 EIA 最新周度泵价仍在上行，且 3 月能源 CPI 冲击很大。
- 如果是 K 型分化，应看到“必需品/低价/会员/补库存”强于“高票价/高端/纯可选”。这与 Placer 对 Costco、Dollar General/Dollar Tree、折扣可选渠道和 Q1 可选行业的公开观察一致：价值渠道与部分低价可选有韧性，但服装、奢侈和全价可选仍分化。[^placer-dollar][^placer-discretionary]

## 资产配置含义

### Staples vs. discretionary

- 维持 staples 相对 discretionary 的温和超配，幅度建议 `+2-3pct`，直到低端门店客流至少连续 `2` 周企稳、普通汽油回到 `$4.00/gal`以下并维持、实际 DPI 重回正增长。
- 但不要无差别追高 staples。Costco/Walmart 类型的“价值+流量+供应链”质量溢价合理，但高估值会降低胜率；更偏好估值纪律下的稳定现金流、低价格带和高周转。
- 对 discretionary 只做结构性配置：低价可选、维修/替换需求、平价美妆、运动户外、折扣零售可以保留；高票价耐用品、奢侈、全价服装、低端客群依赖度高的餐饮应低配。

### A 股必选 vs. 可选

- 必选内部优先：大众食品饮料、调味品/速冻/休闲零食中的低价带龙头、折扣零售/供应链、具有成本下降弹性的包装与部分农食链。核心条件是周转改善和费用率可控，而不是单纯提价能力。
- 谨慎看待“必选标签”的高端白酒。其需求更接近商务/财富效应/礼赠可选消费，不能从美国低端压力与 Costco 增长中直接推导出高端酒加仓。
- 可选内部只留“性价比可选”：平价美妆、运动户外、维修替换型家电、部分服务消费。对出口美国的家具、服装、家居用品、低端消费电子保持低配或对冲，因为美国低收入客流弱化会先传导到订单能见度和渠道去库。
- 风格上偏质量红利和现金流，低配纯消费复苏 Beta。若油价真正回落并转化为实际可支配收入改善，再从防御型必选逐步切向可选 Beta。

## 跟踪触发器

| 触发器 | 若出现 | 配置动作 |
|---|---|---|
| 低端 Placer/Earnest 客流 | 连续 `2` 周转正，且不是天气/发薪错位 | 将可选从低配调至中性，优先低价可选和餐饮。 |
| 汽油价格 | EIA 普通汽油低于 `$4.00/gal`并维持 `2` 周 | 提高低收入敏感消费链的战术权重。 |
| 收入 | 实际 DPI 连续 `2` 个月正增长，储蓄率不再下滑 | 可把消费复苏从“交易”上调为“配置”。 |
| Costco/Walmart vs. dollar stores | Costco/Walmart 强、低端店弱 | 保持“价值渠道获份额”框架，不追广谱可选。 |
| 密歇根信心 | 确认当前系列实际回到 `70+`且分项同步改善 | 才能重新评估周期性重启假设。 |

## 给 Alt Data Analyst 的下一步

1. 请先核对 `71.2`的时间戳；公开资料显示这是 2023 年 8 月初值，不是 2026 年 5 月附近的当前读数。
2. 将 Placer/Earnest 低端客流拆成：地区汽油价格暴露、收入分位、SNAP/发薪周期、篮子大小、交易次数和客单价。
3. 对 Costco `+1.9%`拆分为食品/汽油/药房/一般商品，并与 Walmart、Sam's Club、BJ's、Dollar General、Dollar Tree 做同周对照。
4. 若低端走弱来自交易次数下降而非客单价下降，优先解释为现金流压力；若交易次数持平但客单价下降，则更像折扣化和品类替代。
