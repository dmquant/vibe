## 3. Why Sub-Sector Treatment Is the Only Isomorphic Response to the House Theme

The current house theme — **"PE shell flash-injection credit chain + liquidity defense"** — is in essence two independent, simultaneously-active capital axes:

- **Axis 1 — Credit-chain flash injection:** Capital flows through PE / RTO channels into shell vehicles, creating event-driven α. This is **high β, single-point detonation, back-loaded risk**.
- **Axis 2 — Liquidity defense:** Active money, insurance accounts, and passive ETFs migrate toward low-β, cash-flow-stable sectors on macro / liquidity expectations. This is **low β, continuous, front-loaded risk**.

Healthcare contains names for *both* axes:

1. **Axis-1 beneficiaries** = innovative drugs + medical devices (= the bulk of the 15% RTO share), because
   - NewCo structures and license-out valuation anchors are already accepted by the market;
   - The accounting / regulatory path for injecting off-balance-sheet biotech assets into listed shells is comparatively mature;
   - When the primary–secondary valuation inversion needs to be unwound, shell injection is the most direct monetization route.

2. **Axis-2 beneficiaries** = TCM + pharma distribution + (secondary: traditional chemicals / blood products), because
   - Procurement / NRDL pressure is in its "late-cycle clear-out", marginal pressure is declining;
   - 3–4% dividend yield and stable cash flow match insurance / dividend-ETF preferences;
   - Correlations to staples are high, so they can absorb the same flight-to-safety flow.

**Therefore, treating healthcare as a single defensive bucket (a) *or* a single RTO bucket (b) discards one side of the α.** Option (c)'s sub-sector treatment is the only information-lossless positioning under the current theme.

## 三、为何"分子板"是与主线唯一同构的处理方式

当前主线 **「PE 壳股快闪注入信用链 + 流动性防御」** 本质是两个分别独立、共同存在的资金轴:

- **轴 2 — 流动性防御:** 主动资金、保险账户、被动 ETF 由于宏观与流动性预期,挪向低 β、高现金流稳定性板块。这是**低 β、连续、风险前置**的资金行为。

医药板块同时具备两个轴的标的:

1. **轴 1 受益分子板** = 创新药 + 医疗器械 (= 反向并购 15% 占比的主要贡献),因
   - NewCo 结构、license-out 估值锚已被市场接受;
   - 体外资产装入上市壳的会计与监管路径相对成熟;
   - 一二级估值倒挂修复时,壳化是最直接的兑现路径。

2. **轴 2 受益分子板** = 中药 + 医药商业 + (次级:传统化药/血制品),因
   - 国谈/集采政策"出清后期",边际压力下降;
   - 股息率 3–4%,现金流稳定,符合保险/红利 ETF 偏好;
   - 与必需消费的同业相关性较高,可承接同样的避险资金。

**因此,把医药打成单一防御板 (a) 或单一受益板 (b) 都会丢一边的 α。** 选项 (c) 的分子板处理是当前主线下唯一信息无损的定位。
