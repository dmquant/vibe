# SpaceX at a $2T IPO Valuation: Liquidity Siphon vs. ASTS Valuation Anchor

Report date anchor: 2026-05-12, based on shell `date +%Y-%m-%d`. This note analyzes the scenario “SpaceX IPOs at a $2T valuation”; it does not treat that scenario as already completed.

## Prioritized Conclusions

1. **The larger effect is likely the valuation-anchor uplift for ASTS and other “pure satellite / direct-to-device” names, not liquidity siphoning from the existing Aero-Defense sector.** If public markets accept SpaceX at $2T, the valuation framework for LEO satellite networks, Starlink, direct-to-device, and space AI infrastructure shifts from “concept discounted heavily” to “tradable leader benchmark.” That matters more directly for ASTS.
2. **The liquidity siphon is real but mostly short-term and event-driven.** Kiplinger’s reported $75B raise equals about **4.2%** of the U.S. Aerospace & Defense industry’s **$1.79T** market cap, but it is **2.7x** the combined **$27.7B** AUM of the three core A&D ETFs: ITA, PPA, and XAR. That can pressure high-beta space proxies and theme ETFs during the IPO window, but it is unlikely to drain core primes such as Lockheed Martin, RTX, GE Aerospace, or Boeing in a systemic way.
3. **ASTS has the higher anchor sensitivity, but it is a second-order effect.** Using the latest run-time market snapshot of about **$22.5B** market cap and company 2026 revenue guidance of **$150M-$200M**, ASTS trades at roughly **113x-150x 2026E sales**, or about **129x** at the midpoint. If SpaceX lists at **$2T** against reported 2025 revenue of about **$18B**, that is roughly **111x 2025 sales**. This would make ASTS’s high multiple less isolated, although SpaceX is also the strongest competitive anchor in direct-to-device and Starlink-related services.
4. **For A-share mapping, the more relevant beneficiaries are commercial space, satellite internet, payloads, ground terminals, telemetry/control, and low-cost manufacturing chains, not broad traditional defense primes.** Strategically, a SpaceX IPO is more a global space-beta and valuation-regime event than a simple defense-sector funding rotation.

## Quantitative Framework

## Evidence Chain

### 1. The SpaceX IPO scenario is large enough to matter, but the funding pool is broader than Aero-Defense

- Kiplinger reported that SpaceX is seeking a valuation of **>$2T** and aiming to raise about **$75B**; 2025 revenue was reportedly about **$18B**, mostly from Starlink, which has **>10M** residential subscribers, hundreds of thousands of business subscribers, and defense contracts. [Source: Kiplinger](https://www.kiplinger.com/business/the-space-sector-prepares-to-blast-off)
- TechCrunch cited Bloomberg in December 2025 that SpaceX was planning a mid-to-late 2026 IPO at about **$1.5T** valuation and a **$30B** raise; it also cited an employee secondary sale valuing the company at **>$800B**. [Source: TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/12/09/spacex-reportedly-planning-2026-ipo-with-1-5t-valuation-target/)
- Sacra’s company work describes a **>$2T** valuation target and highlights the combined business model across Starlink/direct-to-phone, Starshield, and low-cost launch. [Source: Sacra](https://sacra.com/c/spacex/)

**Strategy read-through:** $75B is a massive IPO and can crowd out capital tactically. But SpaceX’s buyer base would include global growth, AI, telecom, sovereign capital, technology-index anticipators, and retail demand, not only the A&D pool. The siphon should show up as event-window proxy selling rather than systemic capital flight from the A&D industry.

### 2. The existing Aero-Defense sector is large and has independent fundamental drivers

- StockAnalysis shows the U.S. Aerospace & Defense industry with **86** stocks, **$1.79T** combined market cap, **$560.35B** revenue, and **44.96x** weighted PE; GE Aerospace, RTX, Boeing, Lockheed Martin, Howmet, General Dynamics, and Northrop Grumman remain core weights. [Source: StockAnalysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/industry/aerospace-and-defense/)
- BlackRock’s ITA page shows **$13.84B** net assets, **44** holdings, and **38.76x** P/E, positioned as a way to express a U.S. A&D sector view. [Source: BlackRock ITA](https://www.blackrock.com/us/financial-professionals/products/239502/)
- State Street’s XAR page shows **$5.97B** AUM, **41** holdings, **31.21x** FY1 P/E, and **4.83x** P/B; its modified equal-weight index gives more mid/small-cap A&D beta. [Source: State Street XAR](https://www.ssga.com/us/en/intermediary/etfs/state-street-spdr-sp-aerospace-defense-etf-xar)
- PPA shows **$7.92B** in assets, **34.64x** PE, and **62** holdings; ETF Central shows **+$857M** YTD flows for XAR and **+$622M** for PPA as of 2026-05-08. [Source: StockAnalysis PPA](https://stockanalysis.com/etf/ppa/), [Source: ETF Central](https://www.etfcentral.com/compare-etfs/XAR-vs-PPA)

**Strategy read-through:** SpaceX pricing well may cause tactical ETF and space-proxy rotation, but it does not replace the fundamental drivers of large-cap defense and aero. The more affected cohort is the set of listed “SpaceX exposure substitutes”: RKLB, ASTS, LUNR, PL, GSAT, VSAT, and similar names closer to commercial space and satellite communications.

### 3. ASTS is more sensitive to the valuation anchor

ASTS has moved from a pure concept story toward simultaneous validation across revenue, launch cadence, regulation, and partner ecosystem:

- ASTS reported **$14.7M** Q1 2026 revenue and maintained full-year **$150M-$200M** revenue guidance; about half of full-year guidance is expected from existing contracted backlog. [Source: Business Wire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260511685431/en/AST-SpaceMobile-Provides-Business-Update-and-First-Quarter-2026-Results)
- The company targets about **45** BlueBird satellites in orbit during 2026; BlueBird 8/9/10 are expected to launch in mid-June on Falcon 9, while BlueBird 11-33 are in advanced production and assembly. [Source: Business Wire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260511685431/en/AST-SpaceMobile-Provides-Business-Update-and-First-Quarter-2026-Results)
- ASTS says its commercial ecosystem is nearly **60** global mobile network operator partners covering **>3B** subscribers; the FCC has authorized U.S. commercial SpaceMobile service using up to **248** satellites for direct-to-device broadband. [Source: Business Wire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260511685431/en/AST-SpaceMobile-Provides-Business-Update-and-First-Quarter-2026-Results)
- Q1 2026 ending cash, cash equivalents, and restricted cash were about **$3.5B**; IP and patent-pending claims were about **3,900**; the company disclosed a **98.9 Mbps** peak direct-to-smartphone data speed. [Source: Business Wire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260511685431/en/AST-SpaceMobile-Provides-Business-Update-and-First-Quarter-2026-Results)
- Full-year 2025 revenue was **$70.9M**, and aggregate contracted revenue commitments from commercial partners were **>$1.2B**. [Source: SEC / ASTS 8-K Exhibit 99.1](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1780312/000178031226000005/asts-ex99_1.htm)

**Valuation implication:**

- ASTS at about **$22.5B** market cap and **$150M-$200M** 2026 revenue guidance trades at **113x-150x** sales, or **129x** at the midpoint.
- SpaceX at **$2T** on **$18B** of 2025 revenue would trade at **111x** sales.
- Therefore, if SpaceX holds or extends the IPO valuation, public markets are more likely to accept the “low early revenue, very large terminal TAM” framework for satellite networks. That is more important for ASTS-style direct-to-device pure plays than the liquidity siphon is for traditional A&D.

## Trading and Allocation View

**Base case: valuation-anchor uplift > liquidity siphon.** The direct answer is: if SpaceX IPOs at a $2T valuation, the uplift to valuation anchors for ASTS and other pure satellite names should matter more than the liquidity drain from existing Aero-Defense. The siphon comes first, but it is mostly listing-window noise.

**Timing view:**

- **T-30 to T+5 trading days:** Watch the siphon. If the raise approaches **$75B**, high-beta space proxies may be sold to fund IPO allocations, with ASTS/RKLB/LUNR/PL likely more volatile than traditional defense.
- **T+5 to T+60 trading days:** Watch whether SpaceX holds its offer price and the **$2T** zone. If it holds, the anchor helps ASTS; if it breaks, the market will compress space proxies broadly.
- **3-6 months:** Valuation returns to execution: ASTS’s June BlueBird 8/9/10 launch, Q2 2026 revenue ramp, FCC and overseas regulatory milestones, cash burn, and subsequent launch cadence determine how much of the thematic premium is retained.

**A-share mapping:**

- Prioritize commercial space, satellite internet, phased-array/payload suppliers, ground terminals, telemetry/control, low-cost manufacturing, and launch-service chains.
- Traditional defense primes are not the highest-beta expression unless they also have satellite-internet orders, commercial-space supply-chain exposure, or dual-use communications growth.
- Treat the SpaceX IPO as a “global space beta re-rating” rather than a “defense spending upgrade.” For A-share portfolios, rank names by verifiable orders, launch milestones, and how much global valuation anchoring is not yet priced in.

## 结论优先

1. **更大的影响大概率是对 ASTS 等“纯卫星/直连手机”标的的估值锚提升，而不是对既有 Aero-Defense 板块的流动性虹吸。** 原因是 SpaceX 若以 $2T 被公开市场接受，本质上会把 LEO 卫星网络、Starlink、direct-to-device、空间 AI 基础设施的估值框架从“概念折现”改写为“可交易龙头锚”。这对 ASTS 的边际定价更直接。
2. **流动性虹吸是短期、交易性压力，不是中期主线。** Kiplinger 报道的 $75B 募资规模约等于美国 Aerospace & Defense 行业 $1.79T 总市值的 **4.2%**，但达到 ITA、PPA、XAR 三只核心 A&D ETF 合计 AUM $27.7B 的 **2.7x**。这意味着 IPO 申购期会挤压高 beta space proxy 和主题 ETF 的资金，但不太可能系统性抽干 Lockheed Martin、RTX、GE Aerospace、Boeing 这类大盘国防/航空主线。
3. **ASTS 的估值锚弹性更高，但也更“二阶”。** 以本次行情快照 ASTS 市值约 **$22.5B**、公司 2026 年收入指引 **$150M-$200M** 计算，ASTS 约为 **113x-150x 2026E sales**，中点约 **129x**。若 SpaceX 以 $2T、2025 年收入约 $18B 被市场接受，相当于约 **111x 2025 sales**。这会让 ASTS 当前高倍数不再显得孤立，但 SpaceX 同时也是 ASTS 在 direct-to-device/Starlink 生态上的最强竞争锚。
4. **A 股映射：利好更偏“商业航天/卫星互联网/星载载荷/地面终端/测控链”，而不是传统军工白马的无差别重估。** 对 A 股策略而言，SpaceX IPO 更像一次全球风险偏好和估值范式再定价，而非单纯军工板块资金搬家。

## 证据链

### 1. SpaceX IPO 情景足够大，但资金来源不会只来自 Aero-Defense

- Kiplinger 4 月报道：SpaceX 拟以 **>$2T** 估值上市、募资约 **$75B**；2025 年收入据报约 **$18B**，大部分来自 Starlink；Starlink 已有 **>10M** 住宅用户、数十万商业用户及国防合同。[来源：Kiplinger](https://www.kiplinger.com/business/the-space-sector-prepares-to-blast-off)
- TechCrunch 2025 年 12 月引用 Bloomberg 称：早前计划为 2026 年中后期 IPO，约 **$1.5T** 估值、募资约 **$30B**；同时员工二级出售估值约 **>$800B**。[来源：TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/12/09/spacex-reportedly-planning-2026-ipo-with-1-5t-valuation-target/)
- Sacra 的行业资料称 SpaceX 目标估值 **>$2T**，并强调 Starlink/direct-to-phone、Starshield 和低成本发射的复合商业模式。[来源：Sacra](https://sacra.com/c/spacex/)

**策略判断：** $75B 是超大 IPO，足以形成短期资金挤出；但 SpaceX 的买方会是全球成长、AI、通信、主权资金、科技指数预期资金和散户，不是单一 A&D 池子。因此“虹吸”会体现为事件窗口内的 proxy 减仓，而非 A&D 行业系统性失血。

### 2. 既有 Aero-Defense 板块体量大、基本面驱动独立

- StockAnalysis 的美国 Aerospace & Defense 行业页显示：**86** 只股票、合计市值 **$1.79T**、收入 **$560.35B**、加权 PE **44.96x**；GE Aerospace、RTX、Boeing、Lockheed Martin、Howmet、General Dynamics、Northrop Grumman 仍是权重核心。[来源：StockAnalysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/industry/aerospace-and-defense/)
- BlackRock 的 ITA 页面显示：ITA 净资产 **$13.84B**、持仓 **44** 只、P/E **38.76x**，用于表达美国 A&D 行业观点。[来源：BlackRock ITA](https://www.blackrock.com/us/financial-professionals/products/239502/)
- State Street 的 XAR 页面显示：XAR AUM **$5.97B**、持仓 **41** 只、FY1 P/E **31.21x**、P/B **4.83x**，且指数为 modified equal-weight，更偏中小盘 A&D beta。[来源：State Street XAR](https://www.ssga.com/us/en/intermediary/etfs/state-street-spdr-sp-aerospace-defense-etf-xar)
- PPA 页面显示：PPA 资产 **$7.92B**、PE **34.64x**、持仓 **62** 只；ETF Central 显示截至 2026-05-08，XAR 年内流入 **+$857M**、PPA 年内流入 **+$622M**。[来源：StockAnalysis PPA](https://stockanalysis.com/etf/ppa/)，[来源：ETF Central](https://www.etfcentral.com/compare-etfs/XAR-vs-PPA)

**策略判断：** 如果 SpaceX IPO 定价成功，A&D ETF 可能被短线资金调仓，但大盘防务/航空的驱动变量没有被 SpaceX 取代。真正受影响的是“用来替代 SpaceX 暴露的上市 proxy”，即 RKLB、ASTS、LUNR、PL、GSAT、VSAT 等更接近商业航天和卫星通信的标的。

### 3. ASTS 的估值锚更敏感

ASTS 的最新基本面已经从“纯概念”转向“收入、发射、监管、伙伴生态同时验证”的阶段：

- ASTS 2026Q1 收入 **$14.7M**，维持全年 **$150M-$200M** 收入指引；约一半全年收入指引预计来自既有合同 backlog。[来源：Business Wire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260511685431/en/AST-SpaceMobile-Provides-Business-Update-and-First-Quarter-2026-Results)
- 公司目标 2026 年约 **45** 颗 BlueBird 在轨；BlueBird 8/9/10 预计 6 月中旬搭载 Falcon 9 发射；BlueBird 11-33 已处于高级生产/组装阶段。[来源：Business Wire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260511685431/en/AST-SpaceMobile-Provides-Business-Update-and-First-Quarter-2026-Results)
- 公司称其商业伙伴生态接近 **60** 家全球移动网络运营商，覆盖 **>3B** 用户；FCC 已授权美国商业 SpaceMobile 服务，最多 **248** 颗卫星的 direct-to-device 宽带连接。[来源：Business Wire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260511685431/en/AST-SpaceMobile-Provides-Business-Update-and-First-Quarter-2026-Results)
- 2026Q1 期末现金、现金等价物及受限现金约 **$3.5B**；IP 和待批专利权利要求约 **3,900** 项；公司披露 direct-to-smartphone 峰值速率 **98.9 Mbps**。[来源：Business Wire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260511685431/en/AST-SpaceMobile-Provides-Business-Update-and-First-Quarter-2026-Results)
- 2025 年全年收入 **$70.9M**，签署商业伙伴累计收入承诺 **>$1.2B**。[来源：SEC / ASTS 8-K Exhibit 99.1](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1780312/000178031226000005/asts-ex99_1.htm)

**估值含义：**

- ASTS 以约 **$22.5B** 市值、2026 年收入指引 **$150M-$200M** 计算，P/S 为 **113x-150x**，中点 **129x**。
- SpaceX 若以 **$2T** 对 **$18B** 2025 年收入上市，P/S 为 **111x**。
- 因此，如果 SpaceX 上市后维持或抬升估值，市场会更容易接受“卫星网络早期收入低、但终局 TAM 大”的估值叙事；这对 ASTS 这类直连手机纯标的的估值锚提升，比对传统 A&D 资金虹吸更关键。

## 交易与配置结论

**基准判断：估值锚提升 > 流动性虹吸。** 对请求问题的直接回答是：若 SpaceX 以 $2T 估值 IPO，对 ASTS 等纯卫星标的的估值锚提升更大；流动性虹吸会先出现，但更像上市窗口的交易噪音。

**节奏判断：**

- **T-30 到 T+5 交易日：** 关注虹吸。若募资规模接近 **$75B**，高 beta space proxy 可能被卖出换取 IPO 额度，ASTS/RKLB/LUNR/PL 的相对波动会高于传统军工。
- **T+5 到 T+60 交易日：** 关注 SpaceX 是否守住发行价及 **$2T** 附近市值。若守住，估值锚对 ASTS 更有利；若破发，市场会反向压缩所有 space proxy。
- **3-6 个月：** 估值会从“SpaceX 热度”切回执行验证：ASTS 的 6 月 BlueBird 8/9/10 发射、2026Q2 收入爬坡、FCC/海外监管、现金消耗和后续发射节奏是决定估值留存率的核心。

**A 股映射：**

- 优先跟踪商业航天、卫星互联网、相控阵/星载载荷、地面终端、测控、低成本制造和发射服务链。
- 传统军工白马不是最佳弹性方向，除非同时具备卫星互联网订单、商业航天供应链或军民两用通信增量。
- 主题交易上，SpaceX IPO 更像“全球 space beta 重估”而非“国防支出上修”。A 股组合应偏向订单可验证、发射节点明确、估值尚未充分反映全球锚的公司。

## 风险与反证

| 风险 | 对结论的影响 |
|---|---|
| SpaceX 募资规模从 $75B 下修至接近 $30B | 流动性虹吸明显减弱；估值锚仍在，但冲击不如 $2T/$75B 情景。 |
| SpaceX 上市后破发或披露利润率/资本开支不及预期 | 估值锚变成反向锚，ASTS/RKLB 等高倍数标的会承压。 |
| SpaceX direct-to-phone 进展超预期 | 对 ASTS 既是 TAM 验证，也是竞争折价，可能导致“先涨后分化”。 |
| ASTS 发射延误、卫星入轨失败或监管不及预期 | 即使 SpaceX 锚提升，ASTS 自身执行折价也会覆盖主题溢价。 |
| 利率上行或成长股风险偏好下降 | 高 P/S 的卫星标的比传统防务现金流标的更脆弱。 |

## 建议给 Thematic Researcher 的下一步

1. 把研究问题拆成两个监控面板：**IPO 资金压力面板**（募资规模、基石/长线资金占比、首周成交额、ETF/space proxy 资金流）和 **估值锚面板**（SpaceX P/S、Starlink 收入/EBITDA、direct-to-device 披露、ASTS/RKLB/PL 估值联动）。
2. 对 ASTS 重点跟踪 2026 年 6 月 BlueBird 8/9/10 发射、2026Q2 收入是否从 **$14.7M** 环比加速、全年 **$150M-$200M** 指引是否上修。
3. 对 A 股主题池，避免只按“军工”标签筛选，改按“卫星互联网收入占比、商业航天订单、星载载荷/终端/测控能力、发射节奏可验证性”排序。
