## Priority Conclusions

With VIX around 17, UST10Y at 4.38%, 2s10s at +48bp, 5s30s at +93bp, and a bull-steepening curve, keep a neutral-to-mild risk-on stance, but do not buy duration indiscriminately. Over the next 4-8 weeks, the likely outperformers are “growth with earnings delivery” and “financial beta linked to active turnover,” rather than broad large-cap value or loss-making long-duration growth.

1. **Top pick: technology hardware/AI infrastructure + quality growth/earnings momentum.** Prioritize optical communications/CPO, PCB/electronic materials, memory, semiconductor equipment, and upstream AI compute. The reason is a joint signal from trading momentum and earnings momentum: during the May 6-8 week, STAR 50, ChiNext, and Shenzhen Component rose 4.42%, 3.24%, and 3.02%, while the F5G, optical fiber, CPO, 6G, and AI PC themes gained 12.04%, 10.42%, 10.06%, 9.26%, and 8.32%[1]; meanwhile, January-March 2026 profits in computers, communications, and other electronic equipment manufacturing rose 120% year on year, with the official table showing 124.5%[2].
2. **Second pick: non-bank financials/insurance/brokers + low-valuation cyclical beta.** This is a better fit than “bank value” for the current mildly risk-on setup. On the previous A-share trading day, 3,634 names rose and 1,725 fell across the A-share market and Beijing Stock Exchange, while Shanghai and Shenzhen turnover reached RMB 3.0486 trillion, staying above RMB 3 trillion for a third straight day[3]. That directly supports broker trading, margin financing, and wealth-management beta. Financial valuations remain low, with the financial sector at 7.72x PE and 0.79x PB at the end of 2026 Q1[4].
3. **Moderate overweight: defense/commercial aerospace/high-end manufacturing + event-driven growth.** Defense led on the previous trading day, while commercial aerospace, humanoid robots, CPO, and PCB were active[3]. In industrial profits, railway, ship, aerospace, and other transportation equipment manufacturing profits rose 16.7% year on year in January-March[2]. This combination is better able to absorb high long-end U.S. yields than pure software.
4. **Neutral to slight underweight: banks/high-dividend defensives.** Banks still offer low valuation and long-term allocation value, but when risk appetite rises and turnover expands, they are more of a portfolio stabilizer than an offensive leader. On the first trading day of May, when A-shares rallied, banks were among the weaker Shenwan industries, down 0.86%[5].
5. **Underweight: loss-making long-duration growth, crowded high-valuation software/themes, and coal/oil defensive dividend trades.** STAR Market and ChiNext PE ratios were 195.68x and 67.24x at the end of Q1, while the information technology sector’s PE/PB were 85.29x/5.08x[4]. With UST10Y at 4.38%, earnings certainty is essential. Coal and oil/petrochemicals had already risen 17.64% and 18.27% in Q1[4], and oil/petrochemicals fell 1.63% on the first May trading day[5], suggesting greater profit-taking pressure.

## 操作建议

建议相对基准的战术倾斜为：科技硬件/AI基础设施+6至8个百分点，非银金融/保险+3至5个百分点，军工高端制造+2至4个百分点，有色化工+1至2个百分点；银行/高股息-2至4个百分点，煤炭油气-3至5个百分点，纯长久期亏损成长-4至6个百分点。

执行上采取“成长进攻、金融确认、红利降温”的轮动顺序：先配盈利兑现的AI硬件和电子材料；若两市成交额继续稳定在2.5万亿-3.0万亿元以上，再加券商和保险；若美债10Y重新上破4.50%或VIX回到22以上，则把科技硬件降到中性、提高银行和高股息仓位。

## 风险与观察清单

- **美债风险：** UST10Y若从4.38%上行至4.50%-4.60%，高PE成长估值压力会明显上升。
- **成交风险：** A股成交额若跌回2万亿元以下，券商和题材成长的相对优势会快速削弱。
- **政策风险：** 央行当前是“削峰填谷、保持充裕”而非无约束宽松；资金利率若持续回到政策利率上方，成长估值扩张需要降速。
- **盈利验证：** 电子、AI硬件和高端制造需要5-6月订单、价格、毛利率继续确认；否则从“盈利动量”退化为“主题博弈”。
