# If the AI Sector Falls More Than 3% Tomorrow, How Much Financing Balance Would Hit the 115% Immediate Liquidation Line?

## Priority Conclusions

Date anchor: 2026-05-12. “Tomorrow” means 2026-05-13, and the latest available margin-financing data are as of the 2026-05-11 close.

**Base conclusion: no systemic forced liquidation. If the AI sector falls 3%-5% only because of a narrative rotation, the financing balance likely to touch the 115% immediate liquidation line should be below 0.5% of the current RMB 2,813.037 billion financing balance, or roughly RMB 0-14.1 billion. A more usable base range is 0.1%-0.3%, or RMB 2.8-8.4 billion.** The risk may rise to 1%-2%, or RMB 28.1-56.3 billion, only if crowded AI names gap down by more than 8%-10%, while affected credit accounts were already near a 120% maintenance collateral ratio and cannot add collateral.

The estimate should not be “AI-related financing balance × 3% price decline.” The 115% line is an account-level maintenance collateral ratio, not a stock-level drawdown trigger. Financing debt does not fall with share prices; only accounts already close to the 115%-119% tail would fall below 115% after a 3% sector drop.

## Stress Test

The simplified maintenance collateral ratio is: **collateral value / margin debt**. SSE rules also define the ratio as cash, credit-account securities value and other collateral divided by margin liabilities; CSRC rules allow brokers to apply differentiated controls based on client and market risk within regulatory requirements. [SSE rules](https://www.sse.com.cn/services/tradingservice/margin/rules/c/c_20150912_3987293.shtml), [CSRC margin financing and securities lending rules](https://www.csrc.gov.cn/csrc/c106256/c1654005/content.shtml)

Let the current maintenance collateral ratio be `M`, the AI-related collateral weight be `w`, and the AI price decline be `r`. The post-shock ratio is approximately:

`M' = M × (1 - w × r)`

To fall below 115%, the pre-shock ratio must be below:

| AI decline | AI collateral weight in account collateral | Pre-shock upper threshold |
|---:|---:|---:|
| 3% | 100% | 118.56% |
| 3% | 75% | 117.65% |
| 3% | 50% | 116.74% |
| 5% | 100% | 121.05% |
| 8% | 100% | 125.00% |
| 10% | 100% | 127.78% |

This means a **3% decline only breaks accounts that were already around 115%-119%.** If a financing purchase is opened at the minimum 100% financing margin ratio, an investor contributes RMB 100, borrows RMB 100 and buys RMB 200 of stock, resulting in an initial maintenance ratio near 200%. Even if all collateral is AI equities, a 3% decline leaves the ratio near 194%, far above 115%. Moving from 200% to 115% requires an accumulated collateral decline of roughly 42.5%.

## 压力测算

维持担保比例简化为：**担保物价值 / 融资融券债务**。上交所规则口径也是用现金、信用证券市值和其他担保物价值除以融资融券负债计算维持担保比例；证监会同时允许券商在监管要求内按客户和市场风险作差异化控制。[上交所规则](https://www.sse.com.cn/services/tradingservice/margin/rules/c/c_20150912_3987293.shtml)、[证监会《证券公司融资融券业务管理办法》](https://www.csrc.gov.cn/csrc/c106256/c1654005/content.shtml)

设账户当前维持担保比例为 `M`，AI 相关担保物权重为 `w`，AI 跌幅为 `r`，则冲击后约为：

`M' = M × (1 - w × r)`

要跌破 115%，账户冲击前必须低于：

| AI 跌幅 | AI 担保物占账户担保物权重 | 冲击前触发区间上沿 |
|---:|---:|---:|
| 3% | 100% | 118.56% |
| 3% | 75% | 117.65% |
| 3% | 50% | 116.74% |
| 5% | 100% | 121.05% |
| 8% | 100% | 125.00% |
| 10% | 100% | 127.78% |

这说明：**3% 跌幅只能击穿本来就在 115%-119% 附近的账户。** 如果账户融资保证金按最低 100% 初始建立，投资者自有 100 元、融资 100 元买入 200 元股票，初始维持担保比例约 200%；即使担保物全是 AI 股票，3% 下跌后仍约 194%，离 115% 很远。要从 200% 跌到 115%，担保物需要累计跌约 42.5%。

## AI 融资拥挤度

AI 链条的融资拥挤确实在上升，但这更多代表“波动放大器”，不等于 115% 强平规模。

## 结论区间

| 场景 | 假设 | 触及 115% 的融资余额占比 | 对应金额 |
|---|---|---:|---:|
| 基准 | AI 板块下跌 3%-5%，冲击主要局限于 AI 硬件和半导体；客户有 T+1 上午补足窗口 | 0.1%-0.3% | 28-84 亿元 |
| 谨慎 | 个别 AI 高融资余额股票跌幅 5%-8%，部分账户高度集中且本来就在 120% 附近 | 0.3%-0.8% | 84-225 亿元 |
| 极端 | AI 跌幅 8%-10% 以上并扩散到创业板、科创板担保品，流动性显著恶化 | 1.0%-2.0% | 281-563 亿元 |

**给日报总编的可用表述：** 即使 AI 板块明日下跌 3% 以上，115% 即时平仓线触发的应是尾部、个股化风险，而不是 2.8 万亿元融资盘的系统性平仓。基准估计触线融资余额低于总融资余额的 0.5%，更可能落在 0.1%-0.3%；真正需要监控的是电子、通信、计算机中融资净买入靠前且连续放量的 AI 硬件、半导体、CPO 个股。

## 后续核验项

1. 向券商或数据商索取信用账户维持担保比例分布，至少需要 `115%-120%`、`120%-130%`、`130%-150%` 三档余额。
2. 拆出采用 115% 即时平仓线的券商市场份额；该规则并非所有券商同步执行。
3. 对寒武纪、中际旭创、新易盛、海光信息、兆易创新、澜起科技、胜宏科技、东山精密等标的逐只拉取融资余额、融资买入额/成交额、可充抵保证金折算率。
4. 追加 5%、8%、10% 跌幅情景，并把创业板、科创板权限账户单独分层。
