## Mechanism

- **Forced redemption moves CB investors from “optional conversion” to “must handle the position.”** When the underlying stock trades far above the conversion price, the redemption price is usually close to par, so rational holders convert or sell the bond before the last trading date rather than accept a low redemption price. The CB balance therefore falls quickly during the redemption window and becomes new free-floating equity supply.
- **The selling window starts before the redemption date.** For the underlying stock, the impact usually clusters around the last trading date, the last conversion date and the first sellable trading day after converted shares are listed. Wangneng’s announcement also states that shares created through conversion are listed for trading on the next trading day after conversion.[3]
- **Small-cap underlying stocks are more fragile.** This forced-redemption wave is not large enough to alter aggregate market liquidity, but for Jialian, Sitech and Ruifeng, which have about `RMB 4bn` free-float market caps and daily turnover between `RMB 200mn` and `RMB 800mn`, one CB’s converted equity supply can equal 40% to 80% of a full day’s turnover. If combined with profit taking, margin-account de-risking or event-driven fund exits, intraday liquidity can thin materially.
- **A rising market cushions the pressure but does not remove it.** A-share turnover was about `RMB 3.24tn` on May 13, indicating strong market-wide risk appetite and better absorption capacity. However, high turnover also means more trading-oriented holders, so the new share supply from forced redemptions can still weigh on relative performance of the affected stocks.[6]

## Recommended Use for the Convertible-Bond Analyst

1. **Forced-redemption watch priority: Ruifeng > Jialian > Sitech > Borr > Huaxing > Infore > Wangneng.** The ranking combines share of free-float market cap, share of same-day turnover and proximity of the redemption window. Infore has the largest absolute amount, but its liquidity is also the deepest; Ruifeng, Jialian and Sitech have the higher relative shock.
2. **Track three calendar points for each name.** Watch the last trading date, the last conversion date and the first sellable trading day after the final converted shares. For Huaxing and Wangneng, the key period is `2026-05-14` to `2026-05-20`; for Jialian, Sitech, Infore and Ruifeng, the key period is `2026-05-22` to `2026-06-01`.
3. **Use “T+1 CB balance change + underlying-stock turnover” to judge whether pressure has been realized.** If the remaining CB balance falls quickly but underlying-stock turnover does not expand, potentially unsold converted shares are accumulating. If turnover rises sharply after conversion and the stock price weakens, the selling has entered the secondary market.
4. **Treat margin financing and lock-up expiry as second-order risk factors.** For small-float stocks, a rising margin balance, an extended price above moving averages or a simultaneous lock-up expiry can widen the liquidity discount. If margin balances fall and block/institutional absorption appears, the impact is more likely to behave as a one-off event.

## 机制判断

- **强赎把转债投资人从“可选择转股”推到“必须处理头寸”。** 当正股价显著高于转股价时，赎回价通常接近面值，理性持有人会选择转股或在最后交易日前卖出转债，而不是接受低价赎回。因此，转债余额会在强赎窗口快速下降，并转化为正股新增流通供给。
- **抛压出现的时间点早于赎回日。** 对正股而言，冲击通常集中在最后交易日前后、最后转股日前后，以及新增转股股份上市后的次一交易日。旺能公告也明确，转股形成的股份在转股后次一交易日上市交易。[3]
- **小市值正股更脆弱。** 本轮强赎并不大到影响全市场资金面，但对流通市值 `40亿元` 左右、日成交额 `2亿元`至`8亿元`的家联、思特、瑞丰，单只转债转股后的等值卖盘足以占到一天成交额的四成至八成，若叠加正股涨幅兑现、融资盘止盈或事件资金退出，盘中流动性会明显变薄。
- **上涨市场环境会缓冲而非消除压力。** 5月13日A股成交额约 `3.24万亿元`，全市场风险偏好较强，能够提高承接能力；但高换手本身也意味着筹码交易化，强赎带来的新增股供给仍可能压制相关正股相对收益。[6]

## 给可转债分析师的使用建议

1. **强赎预警优先级：瑞丰 > 家联 > 思特 > 博瑞 > 华兴 > 盈峰 > 旺能。** 排序综合“占流通市值”“占当日成交额”和强赎窗口远近；盈峰金额最大但流动性也最大，瑞丰、家联、思特的相对冲击更高。
2. **后续跟踪三个日历点。** 每只标的分别盯最后交易日、最后转股日、最后转股日后第一个可卖交易日；对华兴和旺能，重点是 `2026-05-14`至`2026-05-20`；对家联、思特、盈峰、瑞丰，重点是 `2026-05-22`至`2026-06-01`。
3. **用“T+1转股余额变化 + 正股成交额”判断压力是否兑现。** 若剩余转债规模快速下降但正股成交额没有同步放大，说明潜在未卖股增加；若转股后正股成交显著放量且价格承压，说明卖盘已经进入二级市场。
4. **把融资余额和限售解禁作为二阶风险。** 对小流通盘标的，若强赎窗口同时出现融资余额上升、股价远离均线、或限售股解禁，流动性折价会放大；若融资余额回落且大宗/机构承接出现，冲击会更接近一次性事件。
